# Campus and Facilities at Westchester Campus

Fordham Westchester is a three-story, 62,500-square-foot building on 32 landscaped acres with a stream and pond. The campus offers parking for 250 vehicles and access to public transportation. It is on the White Plains border, just off Interstate 287 near the Hutchinson Parkway and Route 684. The University is in year 15 of a 40 year lease with GHP Office Properties for the campus, which includes 26 designed classrooms with such technological features as smart podiums, teleconferencing capabilities, and flexible seating and learning areas.

### Dining

The Fordham Westchester Café located on the ground floor proudly serves Starbucks high-quality coffees, hand-crafted espresso beverages, teas, as well as a variety of breakfast sandwiches, pastries, Paninis and pre-packaged snacks.

Hours of Operations

- Thursdays: 8:30 a.m. - 2:30 p.m.
- Saturdays: 8:30 a.m. - 2:30 p.m.
- Closed Mondays, Wednesdays, Fridays, Sundays and Holidays.
