# Westchester Campus Library

The Fordham Westchester Library is located on the ground level and features a 30,000-volume library, access to the electronic databases of the University, and individual study/learning stations.

To contact the Fordham Westchester Library, please call:

Circulation Desk at 914-367-3060

Reference Desk at 914-367-3061

Please note that for the current academic year, the Library hours will be:

Tuesdays: 10 a.m. - 8 p.m.

Thursdays: 10 a.m. - 9:30 p.m.

Saturdays: 11 a.m. - 4 p.m.

Closed: Mondays, Wednesdays, Frdiays, Sundays and University Holidays
