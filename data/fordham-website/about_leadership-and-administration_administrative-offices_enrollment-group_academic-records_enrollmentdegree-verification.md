National Student Clearinghouse2300 Dulles Station Boulevard, Suite 300Herndon, VA 20171**Current Students** may access this service from the National Student Clearinghouse through the "Student Services" section of the "Students" page of [fordham.edu](https://www.fordham.edu/) (select "My Account" and then "Official eTranscript Request and Enrollment Verification").
