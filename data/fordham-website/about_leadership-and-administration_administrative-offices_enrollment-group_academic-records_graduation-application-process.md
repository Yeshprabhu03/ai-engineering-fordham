# Graduation Application Process

Students scheduled to graduate must apply online using the [fordham.edu](http://fordham.edu/) portal.

### To access the Graduation Application

- Log in to the
[fordham.edu](http://fordham.edu/)portal by clicking "Log In" on either the top navigation bar (desktops) or the three-bar icon on the upper-right (for tablets/phones, be sure to scroll down). - Select "My Pages" on the top of the page (desktops) or from the three-bar icon on the upper-right (for tablets/phones).
- Then, choose "Students" under "My Pages."
- Select "Student Services Dashboard" in the grey area.
- Choose "Student Profile."
- On the left side in the black column, select "Application to Graduate"; if on a tablet/phone, select "Additional Links" and then "Application to Graduate."

### To apply for graduation

- Select the term (only shows current and previous terms).
**If your graduation term is not listed, select the latest term from the drop-down menu.** - Select your primary degree, with degree type, level, college, and major.
- Select your intended Graduation Date.
- Next, select a name for your diploma (names currently on your record will appear, and you can enter a new one).
**Please make sure you enter a period (.) after a middle initial.** - Select your diploma mailing address (addresses currently on your record will appear, and you can enter a new one). Please be aware that a permanent address change request through the Fordham portal will not update the diploma mailing address. If you need to update your diploma mailing address after submitting your application, please email
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#5f3e3c3e3b2d3a3c302d3b2c1f39302d3b373e32713a3b2a). This is the only way that the diploma mailing address will be updated. We discourage international mailing.**If at all possible, please provide us with the address of a relative or friend in the U.S. to avoid your diploma being lost.** - Once you select an address, you will see a summary of your application to confirm with all of your responses.
- Submit the application, and then you will then see a confirmation message.
