# LGBT Event 2022

**Building LGBTQ+ Communities in New York: A Campus and Community Conversation**

*Wednesday, October 26, 2022, McShane Campus Center, Rose Hill*

![Panel discussion at LGBT event at JMCC Great Room](/media/home/Screen-Shot-2022-12-13-at-9.49.15-AM.png)


The panel discussion offered a space for the Fordham community to reflect on the history of our shared home, celebrating the commitment of New York City and Fordham today as communities embracing, welcoming and honoring diversity in all dimensions. Sponsored by the Center for Community Engaged Learning, the Office of Multicultural Affairs, and the Office of Equity, Inclusion, and Opportunity, the event commemorated LGBTQ+ History Month.

The panel included Joey Pressley, CEO of the [Hetrick-Martin Institute](https://hmi.org/), and representatives from from the [Ali Forney Center](https://www.aliforneycenter.org/), [Drag Story Hour NYC](https://www.dshnyc.org/), [Honest Accomplice Theater,](https://honestaccomplice.org/) and the Bronx's LGBT Center, [Destination Tomorrow](https://destinationtomorrow.org/).
