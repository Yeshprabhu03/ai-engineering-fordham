# CEL Project Profiles

**These Community Engaged Learning (CEL) projects are across disciplines and connect Fordham faculty and students with community members and organizations, producing community engaged research. Specifically, these projects involved students in activities with the local community while developing their academic skills, increasing their subject matter knowledge, and gaining hands-on experience practicing Asset-Based Community Development (ABCD).
