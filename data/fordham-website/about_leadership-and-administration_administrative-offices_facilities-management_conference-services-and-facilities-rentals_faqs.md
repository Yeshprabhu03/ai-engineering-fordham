**How far in advance should conference dates be reserved?**

Conferences are reserved on a first come first serve basis, in order to get everything you need you should start planning your reservation as soon as you can. Please visit Plan a Conference to determine submit your request and get more details on your needs.

**Can I take the Ram Van?**

Yes, conferences may use the [Ram Van](/student-life/department-of-transportation/ram-van-shuttle/) service. The Ram Van is a shuttle service that travels between the Rose Hill and Lincoln Center campuses. Reservations can be made at the Ram Van office (underneath the Fordham parking garage). For more information visit the [Ram Van website](/student-life/department-of-transportation/ram-van-shuttle/), or contact them at 718-817-4346.

**Is parking available?**[View parking information on our Rose Hill Campus](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/public-safety/services/parking-facilities/rose-hill-campus-parking/). [View parking information on our Lincoln Center Campus](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/public-safety/services/parking-facilities/lincoln-center-campus-parking/).

**Are there restaurants or stores in close proximity near the campus?**

There are multiple dining and shopping options near all of our campuses. Our Lincoln Center location lies in midtown Manhattan with close proximity to Lincoln Center, Central Park, and Columbus Circle. Our Rose Hill campus sits in the middle of the Belmont section of the Bronx. Directly off campus you will find Arthur Avenue, known as Little Italy in the Bronx, as well as the Bronx Zoo and New York Botanical Gardens.

**What forms of dining does Fordham offer?**

Visiting our [Camus Dining Services](https://fordham.campusdish.com/).

**Do you have access to WIFI?**

Yes, guest WIFI is available to guests upon arrival to campus. For more information on how to connect view the [Campus WIFI Webpage](https://www.fordham.edu/information-technology/it-services/campus-wifi/) or contact the [IT Service Desk](https://www.fordham.edu/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).
