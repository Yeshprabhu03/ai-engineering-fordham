# Rose Hill Academic Space

These spaces should only be reserved for academic purposes. To make a reservation please email the Office of Academic Records, [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e68785878294838589948295a6808994828e878bc8838293), in the Registrar’s Office. They will confirm the reservation by email. Requests should include:

- Date of Event,
- Expected Headcount
- Type of Room Required (lecture room, computer classroom, smartboard, etc.)
