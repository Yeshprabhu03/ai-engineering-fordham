# Rose Hill Athletic Faciliities

[Lombardi Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/) offers multiple recreational facilities. To make a reservation, call the Coordinator of Athletic Facilities at 718-817-4261 with your request, and they will confirm by telephone.

- 200-meter indoor running track
- Basketball and Volleyball courts
- Pole vault and high jump pits
- Swimming pool and diving facilities
- Tennis, Racquetball, Squash, and Handball
- Weight area

Undergraduate students, please submit your requests through [OrgSync](https://studentaffairs.unt.edu/student-activities-center/programs-and-services/student-organizations/orgsync.html).
