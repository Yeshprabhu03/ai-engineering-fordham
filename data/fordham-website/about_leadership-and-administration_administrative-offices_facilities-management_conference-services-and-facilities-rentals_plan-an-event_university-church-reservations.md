# University Church Reservations


If you are interested on hosting your Wedding at Fordham University Church please see [Weddings at Fordham](/about/living-the-mission/campus-ministry/weddings-at-fordham/) for more information.

Fordham University has a beautiful University Church and multiple chapels throughout campus. If you are interested in using one of our locations for a special event you can contact one of our Campus Ministry Staff.
