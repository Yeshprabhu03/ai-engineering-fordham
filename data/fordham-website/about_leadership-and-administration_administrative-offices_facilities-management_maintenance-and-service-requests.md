# Maintenance and Service Requests

## Regular Service Requests

To request normal routine maintenance and cleaning services for Lincoln Center or Rose Hill such as lighting outages, heating/cooling problems, window repairs, plumbing work, cleaning, furniture, pest control, etc., please submit a Work Request via the Facilities Management Work Request Channel.

[Log in](https://www.fordham.edu/login/?r=https%3A%2F%2Fwww.fordham.edu%2Fmy-pages%2F)and click 'My Apps' at the top of the page- Scroll down to Common Tools and click on 'Facilities Work Request'
- Under the designated campus, click 'Click here to Create a Work Request'

For Westchester - Contact Mr. Grant Grastorf at 914-367-3202 or Rafael Rivera at 718-817-3981
