# The Fordham Investment Office

![Rose Hill Campus building](/media/home/commonly-used-images/campus-beauty/KGamble-20141027-0096.jpg)

Fordham University's Investment Office provides investment management for the University's endowment and related financial assets.

Contributions have enabled industry-leading financial aid programs, groundbreaking discoveries in scientific research, and hundreds of professorships across a wide range of academic fields.
