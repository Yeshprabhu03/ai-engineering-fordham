# Fiscal Close - Transaction Services (AP)

Following Anthony Grono’s recent communication regarding the FY25 year-end close, we are providing key deadlines and guidance to ensure accurate and timely processing of payments.

**Key FY25 Deadlines**

Task |
Deadline |
| Final day to submit FY25 requisitions | June 16, 2025 |
| JPM Chase June statement closes | July 1, 2025 |
| FINBUY catalog orders submitted (held for July 1 processing) | June 25–30, 2025 |
| Final day to submit FY25 Concur expense reports | July 11, 2025 |
| Final day to submit FY25 invoices (FINPAY, FINBUY) | July 22, 2025 |


**Fiscal Year-End Guidance****Spending Prudence**

Please avoid last-minute, non-essential purchases. Responsible year-end spending supports affordability for students and strengthens our financial position for the year ahead.**Cutoff Guidance**

Goods delivered and services received on or before June 30, 2025, are required to be charged to FY25 budgets. Goods delivered and services received on or after July 1, 2025, are required to be charged to the FY26 budget.**Invoice Submission**

- Submit all FY25 vendor invoices by July 22, 2025.
- If invoices are pending, contact vendors
*immediately*.

**System-Specific Instructions****FINPAY**

Invoices from vendors not attached to purchase orders and payments to independent contractors:

- Submit FY25 related invoices as soon as possible but no later than July 22, 2025.
- Select “FY25” in the invoice detail section when requesting payments after June 30, 2025 related to goods deliver or services rendered prior to June 30, 2025.

Invoices from vendors with an existing purchase order:

- Submit FY25 requisitions to Procurement Operations by 5pm on June 16, 2025.
- Catalog orders submitted June 25–30 will be held (not approved or sent to suppliers) and processed on July 1. The period between will be used by Procurement Operations and Finance to process all FY25 purchase orders properly.
- Enter all vendor invoices for goods delivered or services rendered prior to June 30, 2025 as soon as possible but no later than July 22, 2025.
- Further detail to follow from Procurement Operations.

- June statement closes July 1, 2025.
- Submit June Concur reports no later than July 11, 2025 for supervisor approval.
- Ensure all FY25 charges in your Concur queue have been submitted by July 11, 2025.
- Do not submit July reports until the first week of August.
