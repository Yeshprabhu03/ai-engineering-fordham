# Appliances

**Products and Services Summary**

P.C. Richard & Son is primarily for procuring appliances and consumer electronics. This is distinct from the more specialized, enterprise-grade IT equipment provided by a company like CDW. Typical purchases include:

-
**Residence Life and Housing:**Refrigerators, microwaves, air conditioners, washers, and dryers for student dorms and on-campus apartments. -
**Campus Facilities & Common Areas:**Televisions and home theater systems for student lounges, common rooms, and athletic facilities. -
**Administrative & Break Rooms:**Refrigerators, microwaves, coffee makers, and other small kitchen appliances for departmental offices. -
**Technology for General Use:**Laptops, tablets, and monitors for departmental needs, often at a consumer-grade level rather than enterprise.
