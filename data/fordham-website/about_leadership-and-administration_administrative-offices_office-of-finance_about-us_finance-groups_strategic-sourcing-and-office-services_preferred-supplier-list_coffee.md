# Coffee


Staples Business Advantage provides“No Charge” commercial one-cup brewers for Fordham customers. The only qualification is to purchase coffee, paper products, and coffee accessories from S&B Office Products through FINBUY.

• Brewers typically take 7-10 business days to arrive, but this is dependent upon availability and brewer specifications.

• Installation is provided at no charge for plumbed brewers. If installation is required, please allow an additional 10-15 business days for installation.

• Service is included for all brewers on the program.
