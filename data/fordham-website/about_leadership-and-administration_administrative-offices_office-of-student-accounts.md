# Office of Student Accounts

![Dodransbicenntial Flag Banner with Student in Business Suit](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Dodransbicenntial_Flag_Banner_with_Student_in_Business_Suit_SM.jpg)


The Office of the Student Accounts issues student bills, processes receipts of payments, and assists students and parents with information regarding:

- Tuition and fees
- Billing information and frequently asked questions
- Monthly payment plans
- Refunds
