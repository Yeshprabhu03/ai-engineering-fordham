# State and Territory Licensing Board Contact Information

Students who plan to pursue professional licensure after completing their program at Fordham University should consult the appropriate licensing authority in the state or U.S. territory where they intend to seek certification. Licensure requirements vary by jurisdiction and may change at any time, and only the state or territorial licensing board can provide the most accurate and up-to-date information.

The list below includes official contact links for all 50 states and U.S. territories to support students in reviewing the requirements relevant to their goals.
