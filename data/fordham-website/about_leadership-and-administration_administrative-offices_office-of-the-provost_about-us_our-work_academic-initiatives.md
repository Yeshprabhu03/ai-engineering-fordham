# Academic Initiatives

### Academic AI Coordinating Committee


The Committee is charged with identifying faculty AI initiatives, developing support resources, and advising on the implementation of an AI Innovation Collaborative to facilitate AI adoption across university research, teaching, and administration.

[Click here for more information.](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/office-of-the-provost/about-us/our-work/academic-initiatives/academic-ai-coordinating-committee/)

### Working Group on Faculty Workload


The working group will analyze faculty workload data and peer benchmarks to recommend policy changes that enhance research productivity and instructional quality while ensuring a more equitable distribution of responsibilities.

[Click here for more information.](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/office-of-the-provost/about-us/our-work/academic-initiatives/working-group-on-faculty-workload/)

### Working Group on Benchmarking Faculty Salaries


The working group will analyze salary benchmarking data against peer institutions to recommend competitive pay structures and annual allocation strategies aimed at recruiting and retaining stellar faculty.

[Click here for more information.](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/office-of-the-provost/about-us/our-work/academic-initiatives/working-group-on-benchmarking-faculty-salaries/)
