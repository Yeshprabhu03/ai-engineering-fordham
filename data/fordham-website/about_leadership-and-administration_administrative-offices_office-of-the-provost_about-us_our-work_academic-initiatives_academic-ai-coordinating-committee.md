# Academic AI Coordinating Committee

The Committee's charge is three-fold: to identify and compile AI-related endeavors among the faculty; to shape the tools and resources that will support faculty in those endeavors; and to make recommendations on the role and scope of an AI Innovation Collaborative, a University resource to assist faculty in their potential adoption of AI applications in academic programs, research endeavors, or administrative processes.
