# 2025 Self Study

![Two students at the Lincoln Center campus walk along the plaza in the summer](/media/home/commonly-used-images/campus-beauty/Summer-on-LC-plaza-500x500.jpg)

The Middle States Commission on Higher Education reaccredited Fordham University in summer 2025 after a highly collaborative self-study process that began in fall 2022 and engaged faculty, administrators, and students across the institution.
