# Timeline

## Middle States Self-Study Timeline

### 2022

**September**

**Provost appoints Steering Committee Co-Chairs**

**October – November**

**Steering Committee Co-Chairs and MSCHE ALO complete MSCHE Self-Study Institute**

**December**

- Recommend and receive Provost/President approval of Self-Study Report approach
- Identify Steering Committee candidates

### 2023

**January – March**

- Zoom consultation with MSCHE VP Liaison, Dr. Sean McKitrick
- Schedule Self-Study Preparation Visit (SSPV) - April 25
- Assemble Steering Committee
- Draft Institutional Priorities and Self-Study Outcomes
- Community Stakeholder Engagement on Self-Study process and feedback on Institutional Priorities
- Hold first Steering Committee meeting, including Provost Charge
- Administer Steering Committee survey to begin assembling Working Groups and Teams
- Finalize Institutional Priorities and Outcomes
- Finalize Communications Plan
- Draft Self-Study Design and share with key stakeholders (President, Provost, Board of Trustees, Steering Committee, Cabinet, Faculty Senate, and Student Leaders)
- Integrate Steering Committee feedback and finalize Self-Study Design

**April**

- Implement communications strategy to raise awareness of SSPV
- Launch Self-Study Website
- Finalize logistics and agenda for SSPV
- Submit Self-Study Design to MSCHE VP Liaison
- Host Self-Study Preparation Visit (April 25) by Dr. Sean McKitrick
- Assemble Working Groups and Teams

**May**

- MSCHE VP Liaison meets with Fordham Board of Trustees Executive Committee
- Revise Self-Study Design on basis of MSCHE VP Liaison feedback
- Submit final version to MSCHE
- Finalize Working Group and Team membership

**June – August**

- MSCHE approval of Self-Study Design
- Evidence Inventory Team begins initial data/document collection

**September – December**

- Steering Committee meets monthly
- Working Groups meet regularly to address Lines of Inquiry; identify and gather evidence that supports Standard and Requirements of Affiliation under focus; collaborate internally and externally; carry out gap analyses; and draft Self-Study Chapter Reports
- Working Group Gap Analyses reports are due on December 15
- Steering Committee Co-Chairs and Communications Team continue community engagement and updates with various stakeholder groups to report on Self-Study progress

### 2024

**January – February**

- Steering Committee meets monthly
- Working Groups finalize initial Self-Study Draft Chapter Reports and share with Steering Committee by January 31
- Steering Committee reviews and offers feedback on all Working Group initial Draft Chapter Reports by end of February
- Steering Committee Co-Chairs and Communications Team continue community engagement and updates with various stakeholder groups to report on Self-Study progress

**March – April**

- Steering Committee meets monthly
- Working Groups finalize final Self-Study Draft Chapter Reports and share with Steering Committee by March 31
- Steering Committee reviews and offers feedback on final Draft Chapter Reports by end of April
- Steering Committee Co-Chairs and Communications Team continue community engagement and updates with various stakeholder groups to report on Self-Study progress

**May–August**

- Steering Committee Co-Chairs draft Self-Study Report

**September – December**

- Steering Committee meets monthly
- Steering Committee provides feedback to Co-Chairs on draft Self-Study Report
- Steering Committee Co-Chairs make final revisions and present draft to designated readers for feedback
- Self-Study Report finalized and submitted to Visiting Team Chair
- Host one-day Site Visit by Visiting Team Chair, December 6

### 2025

**January**

- Share draft with the University community
- Hold Town Halls and implement feedback mechanisms for public comment, January 16 and 22

**February**

- Submit final Self-Study Report to MSCHE
- Steering Committee Co-Chairs continue community engagement and updates with various stakeholder groups to report on Self-Study progress

**March**

- Prepare for multi-day Visiting Team Site Visit

**April**

- Visiting Team campus visit, April 6 - 9
- Receive Visiting Team's initial report
- Submit institutional response

**June**

- MSCHE meets; announces outcome
