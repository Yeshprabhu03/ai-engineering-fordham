# Customized Corporate Programs

We collaborate with organizations to create tailored learning experiences that drive results. Our customized corporate programs are designed to equip your employees with the skills and knowledge they need to excel in an ever-changing business landscape.

## Step 1: Building your custom program

We begin by understanding your organization’s unique goals and the skills your team

needs. Drawing from Fordham’s expertise across key industries, specialty areas and skill

sets, we work with you to design a program that aligns with your vision and objectives

**We develop skills such as:**

- Leadership
- Change Management
- Communications
- Project Management

- Emotional Intelligence
- Critical Thinking
- Marketing
- Data Analytics + Technology

**Across various industries and specialty areas:**

- Sustainability & CSR
- Cybersecurity
- AI
- Healthcare
- Biotechnology

- Ethics
- Humanitarian Affairs
- Education
- Arts & Media
- Marketing

## Step 2: Delivering for Your Employees

Once your program is designed, we deliver the curriculum in an engaging, impactful way via an online, hybrid, or in-person format. Our experienced faculty ensures that your employees gain practical skills they can apply immediately. Afterward, we assess the program’s success and refine for continuous improvement.
