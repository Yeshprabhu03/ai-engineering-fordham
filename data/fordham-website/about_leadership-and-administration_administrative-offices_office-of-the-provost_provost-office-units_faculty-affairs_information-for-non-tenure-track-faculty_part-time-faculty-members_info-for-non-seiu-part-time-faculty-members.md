# Info for Non SEIU Part-Time Faculty Members

Part Time faculty are an essential part of Fordham’s academic community. We have created this webpage as a resource for information that pertains to your position here at Fordham.

Many part time faculty are eligible for membership in the Service Employees International Union Local 200United. The terms and conditions of employment for those who are eligible for union membership are covered by a [collective bargaining agreement](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/office-of-the-provost/pdfs/Collective_Bargaining_Agreement_for_Part_time_Bargaining_Unit_Faculty_Members.pdf). For part time faculty eligible for union membership, please go to the Information on SEIU Part Time faculty page for more information.

## Benefits

Part Time faculty are eligible to enroll in medical insurance, as well as a tax-deferred annuity plan. In addition, adjuncts may be covered by certain state and local statutory benefits. Learn more about the [benefits for adjunct faculty](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/office-of-the-provost/provost-office-units/faculty-affairs/information-for-non-tenure-track-faculty/part-time-faculty-members/info-for-seiu-part-time-faculty-members/benefits/).

## Policies and Procedures

Part Time faculty should be familiar with the policies and procedures set forth in the [undergraduate faculty handbook](/resources/policies/undergraduate-faculty-handbook/) which contains important information. In addition, as members of the Fordham community, adjunct faculty are covered by the [University’s Code of Conduct](/about/leadership-and-administration/board-of-trustees/university-statutes/article-six-university-code-of-conduct/) and should be familiar with its content.

Many schools and colleges at Fordham also have additional guidelines that may be relevant to your employment at Fordham. You should ask your department chair, program director or dean whether there are any additional manuals or handbooks that apply to your position at Fordham.

## Job Opportunities

If you are interested in full-time faculty employment, job postings for available full-time faculty positions are listed at job opportunities. This information will be updated as new opportunities become available.
