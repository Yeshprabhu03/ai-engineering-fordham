# Guide to your Road to Tenure

This is a year by year guide to the types of reviews you are expected to complete leading up to your tenure year.

## Year 1

**First year as Tenure Track Faculty**

- Apply for Faculty Research Grant
- Apply for Merit (Annually)
- Complete Faculty Activity Report (annually)

## Year 2

**First Reappointment Review**

-
By October 1, submit all appropriate documents to RPT System for review

- By December 15, notification will be sent of the results of your first Reappointment Review

## Year 3

**Second Reappointment Review**

- By September 15, apply for Faculty Fellowship
- By February 15, submit all appropriate documents to RPT System for review
- By May 15, notification will be sent of the results of the your second reappointment review

## Year 4

**First Faculty Fellowship**

**If you applied and received a Fellowship in Year 3, you can take you fellowship starting year 4**

## Year 5

**Third & Final Reappointment**

- Final review and reappointment before tenure review
- By February 15, submit all appropriate documents to RPT system for review
- By May 15, notification will be sent of the results of the your third reappointment review

## Year 6

**Tenure Review**

- By January 15, submit all appropriate documents to RPT system for review
- By May 15, notification will be sent of the results of your tenure review.
- Additional materials may be needed for external review letters before January 15.
