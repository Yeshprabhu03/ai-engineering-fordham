# Consumer Information

## Student Right to Know

This page serves as a central hub for disclosures related to academics, student financial aid, campus safety, student life, and other essential consumer information in keeping with shared standards adopted by universities and colleges nationwide. We believe that informed decisions are crucial for prospective and current students, as well as their families.

Here, you'll find data on graduation rates, retention rates, cost of attendance, student demographics, and more. Our goal is to ensure you have the information you need to make informed choices about your education and experience at Fordham University.
