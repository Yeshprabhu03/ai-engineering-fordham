# Fordham University Discrimination Complaint and Intake Form

**This reporting tool is for non-emergency incidents only. For emergencies, please contact the Department of Public Safety, 24/7, 365, at 718-817-2222 or Dial 911.**

Fordham University takes all discrimination complaints very seriously and will make every effort to respond to each complaint promptly. When filling out the form, be sure to be as accurate and truthful as possible. The information contained in this report will be shared with the Office of Human Resources Management and any other appropriate University officials involved in any subsequent monitoring, investigation, or resolution of the incident.
