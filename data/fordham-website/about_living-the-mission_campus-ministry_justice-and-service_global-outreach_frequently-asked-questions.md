## GO! Costs & Finances

-
The cost for each GO! project can be amounted to travel, food, housing, and additional project expenses like partner costs or donations. These costs vary depending on the location; for example, the farther the location, the more expensive group flights typically are. But Fordham would like to make this experience accessible to all, so we do offer scholarship opportunities.

-
Fordham offers scholarships for those who demonstrate need. To apply, simply offer your relevant information at the end of your participant application; there is no separate form to fill out regarding initial scholarship opportunity. GO! encourages all interested students to provide any of their financial concerns to better inform the scholarship committee.

There are additional scholarship opportunities that are offered over the course of the project cycle.
