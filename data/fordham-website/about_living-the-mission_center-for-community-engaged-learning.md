The Fordham Center for Community Engaged Learning (CCEL) mission is to bridge the university with its neighboring communities and global partners through experiential learning, research, and civic engagement. It fosters reciprocal partnerships by connecting faculty, students, and staff with community organizations to integrate academic inquiry with community engagement, advancing justice and solidarity.
