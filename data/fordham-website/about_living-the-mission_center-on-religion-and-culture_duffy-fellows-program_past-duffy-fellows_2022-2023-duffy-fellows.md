# 2022-2023 Duffy Fellows

**Caitriona Delumpa (FCRH 2022)**

*Vessel: A Spiritual Art Experience* (art exhibition)

**Major:** International Political Economy

**Minor:** Peace and Justice Studies and Visual Arts

Caitriona Delumpa, through storytelling and interviews, documented how contemporary young adults experience and even reimagine religion across faith traditions. Her Duffy Fellow project concluded with a public art exhibition representing these perspectives in various media.

**Concentration:** American Catholic Studies

**Henry Sullivan (FCRH 2024)**

[ Questions on the Catholic Imagination(s)](https://youtu.be/jGyIYW7JlZ0?si=mJ-tOYVzvKOiUcvV) (documentary)

**Major:** Urban Studies and Theology

Henry Sullivan was a member of the Rose Hill Society, a John Lewis Scholar with the Faith & Politics Institute, and a past intern at the pro-bono legal department of Catholic Charities. As a Duffy Fellow, Sullivan investigated both the sincere and ironic usage of provocative Catholic imagery in fashion and art. Drawing on his background in photography, this project culminated in a documentary.
