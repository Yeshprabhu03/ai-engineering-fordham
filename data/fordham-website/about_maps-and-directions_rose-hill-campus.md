# Rose Hill Campus Map

![Rose Hill Campus Map](/media/home/admin-use-only/Rose-Hill-Campus-Map.png)


Rose Hill is an 85 acre green and gothic campus and is home to Fordham College at Rose Hill, the Gabelli School of Business, the School of Professional and Continuing Studies as well as the Graduate Schools of Arts and Sciences and of Religion and Religious Education. Rose Hill is adjacent to the New York Botanical Garden, the Bronx Zoo, and the neighborhood's Little Italy.
