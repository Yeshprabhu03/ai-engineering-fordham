# Association for the Advancement of Sustainability in Higher Education

**Research**

The Office of Sustainability has been provided with a database where faculty can submit and display their research. It will serve as a way to publicize professors’ works, pique students' interest in various subject matter, and aid in choosing mentors.

Learn more about the [STARS faculty survey](https://docs.google.com/document/d/1CwDwotfmwZEhXz5iQPyA_Emoh5YBKfsgIHCPnmgq2BY/edit#heading=h.793s3s1bkhvu).

**Courses**

Faculty will submit their courses that pertain to sustainability as defined by the UN Sustainable Development Goals. This will Increase knowledge of the sustainability courses available and serve as an efficient way of tracking for AASHE.

Read more about [AASHE's suggested keywords for sustainability courses and research inventories](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1CINYGQ-8nov8qhGx_fQxn-KTSbC4BgpMHLsBgplaQGo/edit?gid=1359647972#gid=1359647972).

**Operations**

Through data implementation with AASHE, we will have access to emissions, waste, and water data. This new data can gamify climate work and increase student spirit/confidence in Fordham’s ongoing efforts.
