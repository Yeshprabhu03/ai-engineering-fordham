# ESL Housing Alternatives

## Housing in New York City

Fordham housing and residence halls are not available to students taking courses only at the Institute of American Language and Culture.

If you would like more information about housing in the New York area, One to World offers a booklet entitled, "The International Student's Survival Guide for Living in NYC." To obtain a copy, contact [One to World](https://onetoworld.org/) at 212-431-1195.
