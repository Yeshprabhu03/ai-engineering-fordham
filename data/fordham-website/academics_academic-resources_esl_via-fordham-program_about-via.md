***Via Fordham Highlights***

• Ideal for students with TOEFL 3.5-4.5 (or 70-95) or IELTS 5.5–7.0 who meet all other undergraduate admission requirements

• You’ll spend one or two semesters in Via Fordham depending on your English scores at the time of entry.

• Earn anywhere from 3 to 9 credits toward yourFordham undergraduate degree.

• Complete your Via Fordham coursework with a 3.0 GPA and pass the English evaluation to become a full-time Fordham undergraduate. No second application is required.
