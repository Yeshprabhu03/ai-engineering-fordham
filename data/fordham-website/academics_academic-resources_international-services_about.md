# About International Services

![Office of International Services](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/OIS_Keating_Steps_Lg.jpg)

## Our Mission

Our mission is to serve our University and its international community complying with pertinent federal statutes and regulations; to develop and assist in developing programming to promote comprehensive internationalization collaborating with University-wide working group (WGIS), committee (GT) and Office of the Provost’s Office of International Initiatives; to support partnerships and affiliations that bring prestige and positive recognition to our University through NGO/DPI relationship with the United Nations; and to advocate for international education issues and development of professionalism in NAFSA.
