# Working Legally in the U.S.

F-1 international students have several employment options while studying in the United States, but most opportunities require prior authorization. Students **cannot** work without the appropriate approval unless the position is considered volunteer work or on-campus employment.

Employment authorization in the U.S. involves specific eligibility requirements and application steps.
