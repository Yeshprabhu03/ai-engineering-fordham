# Air Force ROTC Pre-Screening

Thank you for your interest in joining Air Force Reserve Officer Training Corps Detachment 560.

To be considered for the second phase (interview) of the application process, please complete the Initial Screening Application.

The application process takes three simple steps:

- Complete the
[Initial Screening Application](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/19gFOC4Rf8jWWtwmBwepNdaJpKnUPGejI955dh_gYvcM/viewform?ed). - If you meet the pre-qualifications, you will be invited for an interview by the unit admissions officer.
- After the interview, if accepted, you will receive a formal invitation to join our distinguished program.
