# Army ROTC Information for Active Duty Soldiers

## Green to Gold Program

If you are an Active Duty Soldier, you may be eligible for the Green to Gold Program. There are several options. You need to familiarize yourself with the different categories to see if you meet the criteria.


Before contacting us you should:

- Check to see if the
[college/university](/academics/academic-resources/rotc/army/participating-schools/)you plan to attend is affiliated with Fordham Army ROTC - Review the
[Green to Gold program details](http://www.goarmy.com/rotc/enlisted-soldiers.html) - Ensure you meet eligibility for the
[Green to Goldvprogram](http://www.goarmy.com/rotc/enlisted-soldiers.html)you're applying to since all have varying requirements (Hip Pocket, Non-Scholarship, Scholarship, and ADO) - Know your projected month and year of graduation
- Remember that applying to the university and applying to Green to Gold are two different and separate processes.
