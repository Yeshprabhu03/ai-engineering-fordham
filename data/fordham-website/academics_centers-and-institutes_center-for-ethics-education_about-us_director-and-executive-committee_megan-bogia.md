**About: **Megan Bogia's work centers on postsecondary policy ethics, with a special focus on financial aid and student debt. She previously worked in policy research as a senior analyst for Abt Associates, as well as in higher education administration as an IRB administrator, managing one of the medical ethics boards and their research ethics education programming. She has a Ph.D. in Education from Harvard University and an M.S.Ed. in Higher Education from the University of Pennsylvania.
