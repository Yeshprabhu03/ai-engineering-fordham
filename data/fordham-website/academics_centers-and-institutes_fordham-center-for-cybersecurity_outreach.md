# Cybersecurity Outreach

The Cybersecurity program and Fordham University sponsor and arrange for events and activities that serve the community at large. In what follows we list few of these recent activities.

- Recently the Cybersecurity Program has arranged and an
[awareness workshop](https://drive.google.com/a/fordham.edu/file/d/0B_bASLJirdwDYzZ1RXpOdGIxdnc/view?usp=sharing)for Senior Citizens at Riverdale Senior Assisted Living Center. - Recently the Cybersecurity Program also
[sponsored another event](https://drive.google.com/file/d/0B9ckbhDt33WWZXNLVzREX3BRazQ/view?usp=sharing)at the Church of the Magdalene for children at the local school. - Fordham University CLIP center has conducted
[several studies](/school-of-law/centers-and-institutes/fordham-clip/)on public schools’ education and children’s privacy.
