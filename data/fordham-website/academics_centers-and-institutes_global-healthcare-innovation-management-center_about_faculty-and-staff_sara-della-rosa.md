# Sara Della Rosa

![Sara Della Rosa](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Sara_Della_Rosa.jpg)


Sara Della Rosa, PhD is a Research Fellow at the Global Healthcare Innovation Management Center, Fordham University, USA. Before joining Fordham, she has been a Visiting Scholar at Columbia University â€“ Columbia Business School, USA in the Department of Management from 2015 to 2017. Sara earned her PhD in Business Administration from the University of Cassino and Southern Lazio, Italy where she also got her Master's Degree and Bachelor's Degree with distinction. Her research interests lie at the intersection between business management and healthcare innovation technologies. Her work focuses on quantitative analysis in the Digital Health industry and on the use of online services in healthcare. At Fordham, her work aims at leveraging the use of Social Media data to improve the effectiveness in medical research and disease management.
