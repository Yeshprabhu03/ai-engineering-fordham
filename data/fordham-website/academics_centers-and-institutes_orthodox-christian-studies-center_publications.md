# Publications

![Orthodox Christian Studies Center publications.](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/OCSC_Publications.jpg)


The Orthodox Christian Studies Center publishes two book series with Fordham University Press: *
Orthodox Christianity and Contemporary Thought*, edited by George Demacopoulos and Aristotle Papanikolaou, and

[Christian Arabic Texts in Translation](/academics/centers-and-institutes/orthodox-christian-studies-center/publications/christian-arabic-texts-in-translation/), edited by Stephen J. Davis.

In 2018, the Center launched the *
Journal of Orthodox Christian Studies*, a double-blind peer-reviewed scholarly journal publishing leading scholarship on all aspects of Orthodox Christianity.
