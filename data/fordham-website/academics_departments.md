### New York as Your Classroom

Each of our departments and programs makes our home in New York City part of the curriculum.

Theatre major? You might stage an off-Broadway production. Studying the sciences? Our Rose Hill campus is literally across the street from the New York Botanical Garden. Pursuing a graduate degree in business or a law degree? A dedicated network of alumni on Wall Street and in the local, state, and federal courts serve as mentors in our classrooms.
