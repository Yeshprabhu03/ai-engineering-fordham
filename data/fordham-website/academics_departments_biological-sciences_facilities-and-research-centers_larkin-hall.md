# Larkin Hall

![Male student filling test tube - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/CShinn_100112_1076.jpg)

Larkin Hall is a 27,000-square-foot building with state-of-the-art facilities that include 16 modern laboratories and a central Animal Care Facility. The laboratories are equipped for broad-based research employing modern technologies for research in cell and molecular biology, entomology, cytogenetics, ultrastructure, microbiology, and neurobiology. Items of particular interest for graduate research include: tissue culture facilities, fluorescence microscopes, confocal microscope, PCR instruments, ultracentrifuges, fluorometer, high performance liquid chromatography equipment, liquid scintillation counters, video densitometer, DNA sequencing equipment, oligonucleotide synthesizer, electroporators, transmission and scanning electron microscopes, and X-ray film developer.
