# Biological Sciences Graduate Program Tuition and Financial Aid

## Program Costs

Over the past three years, students earned an average of 11 credits per term, while in coursework. Visit our Tuition and Fees page to view the current tuition fees.

### GSAS Funding

In academic year 2012 - 2013, 52 students were enrolled in the biological sciences program.

- PhD Students: 37
- MS Students: 15

In the biological sciences program, 50 (96%) students had stipends and/or scholarships:

- Distinguished Fellowships: 3
- Graduate Assistantships: 40
- Teaching Associates: 0
- Teaching Fellows: 1
- External Funding: 6
- Tuition Scholarships: 0

To apply for financial aid, please visit the [Graduate Financial Aid](/student-financial-services/graduate-financial-aid/) page.
