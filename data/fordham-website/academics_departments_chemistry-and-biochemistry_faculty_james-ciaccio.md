-
My primary research interests are in the following areas: 1) devising novel, project-oriented and/or discovery-based experiments for the undergraduate organic teaching laboratory that combine synthesis or natural product isolation, spectral analysis and mechanistic discovery; 2) synthetic organic chemistry, including the development of new or improved organic synthetic methods with emphasis on chemo- and regioselective reactions of epoxides and epoxide synthesis.
