# Chemistry Department Research Facilities

The Chemistry Department, located in John Mulcahy Hall on the Rose Hill Campus, has twelve full time faculty and four laboratory technician. A machine shop and one skilled machinist are shared with the Physics Department. Modern instrumentation to support research in all areas of chemistry is maintained.
