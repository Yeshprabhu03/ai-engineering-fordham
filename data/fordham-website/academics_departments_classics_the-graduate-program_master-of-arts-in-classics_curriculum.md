# Master of Arts in Classics Curriculum

**Thank you for your interest in the Master of Arts in Classics. The program has been placed on hiatus and will not be accepting applications at this time.**

College-level proficiency in Greek and Latin is expected for admission to the M.A. in Classics.

- 10 courses must be successfully completed, which may be taken in Greek, Latin, or Greek and Latin.
- Greek Composition is required for the Greek M.A., Latin Composition for the Latin M.A., and both Greek and Latin Composition for the Greek and Latin M.A.
- The candidate must demonstrate in a proficiency exam competency in German, French, or Italian.

There is no thesis, but the student must pass written comprehensive examinations in
[translation](/academics/departments/classics/the-graduate-program/reading-lists/) and history of literature after completing the above requirements.
