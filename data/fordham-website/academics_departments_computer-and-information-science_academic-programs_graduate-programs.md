### STEM Designation

The Ph.D. in Computer Science, M.S. in Computer Science, M.S. in Cybersecurity, and M.S. in Data Science are registered STEM designated programs, which permits a 24-month extension of post-completion Optional Practical Training (OPT), for a total eligibility of up to 36 months. Please consult Fordham’s [STEM Extension](/academics/academic-resources/international-services/students/post-graduation/stem-extension/) page for more information.
