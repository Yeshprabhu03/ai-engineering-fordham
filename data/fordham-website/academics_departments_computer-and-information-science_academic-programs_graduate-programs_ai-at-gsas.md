![GSAS-Network-Abstract-Technology](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-arts-sciences/GSAS-network-abstract-technology.jpg)


### Embrace the Future of AI at Fordham

In the rapidly evolving landscape of Artificial Intelligence (AI), staying ahead means being prepared. At Fordham, we're committed to equipping you with the knowledge and skills to thrive alongside this cutting-edge technology. Our selection of computer science-based graduate degree programs is designed to put you at the forefront of your field, whether integrating AI into your projects or pioneering new advancements.

### Explore our Programs and Find your Fit

M.S. in Data Science

Delve into the world of AI as you learn to construct behind-the-scenes models and deliver core functionality. This program hones your expertise in data analysis, machine learning, and computational models, preparing you for a future where data is king.
