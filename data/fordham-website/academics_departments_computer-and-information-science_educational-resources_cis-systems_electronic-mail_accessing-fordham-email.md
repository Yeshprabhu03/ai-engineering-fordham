# Accessing Fordham Email

All students at Fordham University are automatically given a `fordham.edu` email account which is powered by Google. You should be aware that the University administration will send official messages only to your Fordham address. Therefore you should check this account regularly.

[Learn more about your Fordham email](https://www.fordham.edu/information-technology/standard-software/google-workspace/gmail/), including how to access it from a smartphone.
