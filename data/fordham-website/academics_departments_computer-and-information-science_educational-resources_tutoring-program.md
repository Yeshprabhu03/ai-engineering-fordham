Free peer tutoring services available through Knack

Fordham has a partnership with Knack, a platform which allows students in need of tutoring services to book free sessions on-demand from verified Fordham peer tutors. You can view the full list of supported courses [here.](https://www.joinknack.com/sponsorship/eOpBG5/tutor)
