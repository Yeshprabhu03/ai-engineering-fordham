# CIS Lecturer and Adjunct Positions

Department of Computer and Information Sciences- Full-time Lecturer position and multiple Adjunct positions.

The Department of Computer and Information Science at Fordham University offers undergraduate, masters, and doctoral programs in computer science and related fields in the Bronx and Manhattan campuses. For information about the department, visit [our website](https://www.fordham.edu/cis).

The department invites applications for a full-time lecturer position and multiple adjunct positions to start in Spring 2023.

## Full-time Lecturer Position:

- Incumbent is expected to teach 4 courses on-campus during Spring 2023. The course list includes:
- Undergraduate classes: C++ Programming, Data Structures, Operating Systems
- Graduate classes: Data Structures, Internet and Web Programming
*All graduate courses and some undergraduate courses are offered in the evening and meet once a week. All classes are capped at thirty.*


There is no research expectation as this is primarily a teaching position. Additional expectations include service to the program, department, and the School as assigned by the Chair.

- The position requires a M.S. or higher degree in Computer Science or related fields, a commitment to teaching excellence, and good communication skills.

## Adjunct Positions:

- Incumbents are expected to teach up to 2 courses per semester (on-campus or non-online or in dual modality) during Spring 2023. The course list includes:
- Undergraduate classes: C++ Programming, Data Structures, Operating Systems
- Graduate classes: Data Structures, Internet and Web Programming
*All graduate courses and some undergraduate courses are offered in the evening and meet once a week. All classes are capped at thirty.*

- The positions require a M.S. in Computer Science or related fields, or B.S. with relevant industrial or teaching experience, a commitment to teaching excellence, and excellent communication skills.

Compensation: **As per the current Collective Bargaining Agreement, $7,000 per course (subject to change upon ratification of a new CBA)**.
