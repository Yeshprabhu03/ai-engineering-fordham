# Ailey/Fordham BFA in Dance Program Admission

![Student supervising dance class - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/KGamble_6197__SM.jpg)

Students applying to the Ailey/Fordham BFA in Dance program must apply by the Early Action deadline and must complete 2 applications: Fordham University’s Common Application and The Ailey School’s Dance Application.

The deadline to apply to the Ailey/Fordham BFA in Dance is November 1st. (Please note: Theatre and Dance applicants should select "Early Action." Decisions will be rendered on a rolling basis after March 1st. These programs have additional audition/interview requirements.)

Ailey Dance Application and further information on the application process can be found on their [website](https://www.alvinailey.org/school/professional-division-ages-17-25/aileyfordham-bfa-program).
