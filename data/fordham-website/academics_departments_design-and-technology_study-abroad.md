# Design and Technology Study Abroad

NMDD students are * encouraged* to enhance their design perspective and educational experience by studying in other countries and cultures -- for a semester, a year, a summer or as part of a faculty-led special program.

Fordham offers over 125 study abroad programs, in 52 countries, on six continents world-wide. Many of these universities around the world offer coursework highly compatible with and complementary to the NMDD program.

NMDD students interested in [Study Abroad](/study-abroad/) typically will proceed by:

- Contacting the International and
[Study Abroad Office](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#c6b5b2b3a2bfa7a4b4a9a7a286a0a9b4a2aea7abe8a3a2b3)for information; - Visiting the
[Study Abroad Portal](https://fordham-ip.terradotta.com/index.cfm?FuseAction=Security.LoginWizardStepOne)online; - Gaining approval for the proposed course of study from the Program Director.
