David A. Rosenkranz, Ph.D., is an Assistant Professor of Economics. He is an applied microeconomist specializing in health economics and industrial organization and studying the determinants of health care access, capacity investment, and utilization. He completed his postdoctoral training at the Leonard Davis Institute of Health Economics, the Wharton School, and the Perelman School of Medicine at the University of Pennsylvania in 2023. Previously, he completed his Ph.D. in economics at Columbia University in 2022 and received his B.A. from Vassar College in 2014.
