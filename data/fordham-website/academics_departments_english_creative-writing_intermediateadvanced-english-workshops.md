# Intermediate and Advanced Creative Writing Workshops

Admission to Intermediate and Advanced Creative Writing Workshops is available through writing sample submission application only. Writing sample applications are available from mid-September to mid-October (Fall semester) and early February to early March (Spring semester).

If no courses are listed, the call for applications has passed.
