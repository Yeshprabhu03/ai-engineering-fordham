# Prizes

## Scholarships

**The Peter and Kitty Quinn ScholarshipEligibility:** Full time Junior or Senior that has declared major in English Literature attending Fordham College at Rose Hill or Fordham College at Lincoln Center. Must demonstrate financial need and demonstrates academic merit (minimum 3.0 grade point average).

**Guidelines:**The Application should consist of: A two page narrative explaining why the candidate chose to major in English, filed with the English Department; a brief statement of financial need, also filed with the English Department; A letter from an English Department faculty member nominating the candidate; A FAFSA form, filed with Financial Aid (NOT with the Department).

**Prize:**TBA

### Awards

**The Margaret Amassian AwardEligibility: **Any graduating English major who has achieved the highest grade point average in the course of his or her college career.

**Guidelines:**This Award is made possible by the generosity of Dr. Amassian's family, friends and former students, honors the memory of a beloved member of the English faculty. It is given to the graduating English major who has achieved the highest grade point average in the course of his or her college career.

**Prize:**$1,000
