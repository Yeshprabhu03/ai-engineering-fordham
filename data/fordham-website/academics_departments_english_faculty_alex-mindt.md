# Alex Mindt

## Senior Lecturer

![Alex Mindt](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Alex_Mindt.jpg)

MFA., Columbia University

Areas of Interest: creative writing (fiction, non-fiction, poetry & plays); modernism; contemporary fiction; the short story; wilderness literature; voices from the gaps.

-
Alex Mindt is the author of

*Male of the Species,*which was a finalist for the PEN/Bingham award, the Williams Saroyan Prize and the Washington State Book Award. His stories, published widely, have been recognized with numerous honors, including the Pushcart Prize and the Charles Angoff Award. He also writes plays and screenplays.
