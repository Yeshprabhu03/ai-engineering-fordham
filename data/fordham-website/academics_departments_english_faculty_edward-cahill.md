# Edward Cahill

![Prof. Edward Cahill, English Department](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/english/Cahill,-Edward.jpeg)

### Professor

B.S., California State University, Fresno; M.A., Claremont Graduate School; Ph.D., Rutgers University, New Brunswick

Research and Teaching Interests: Modern and contemporary fiction; queer literature.

-
[Professor Cahill](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#27424344464f4e4b4b67414855434f464a09424352)is the authorwhich was short-listed for the VCU-Cabell First Novelist Award and won BIBA and IPPY awards for LGBTQ fiction. He is also the author of[Disorderly Men](https://www.fordhampress.com/9781531504441/disorderly-men/),and his scholarly essays have appeared in such journals as[Liberty of the Imagination: Aesthetic Theory, Literary Form, and Politics in the Early United States](http://www.upenn.edu/pennpress/book/14968.html),*American Literature**,*,*Early American Literature**ELH**,*, and*Early American Studies*. His teaching interests include modern and contemporary fiction and queer literature.*Common-place*
