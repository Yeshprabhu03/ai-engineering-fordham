# Kathryn Blakely

![Picture of Kathryn Blakely](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/english/Blakely.jpg)


### Lecturer

BA, New York University; MA, Brooklyn College (MFA); MPhil, PhD, Rutgers University, New Brunswick

Areas of Interest: 18th-Century women writers; ethics; gender studies

-
Kathryn Blakely studies eighteenth-century British women writers with a focus on the intersection of ethics and narrative form. Her research has been published in

*SEL Studies in English Literature 1500-1900*;*Persuasions: The Jane Austen Journal*; and*Eighteenth-Century Fiction*. Her poetry has been published in*West 10th*.
