# Michael Natriello

![Picture of Michael Natriello](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/english/Natriello-Photo.jpg)


### Lecturer

MFA: The New School, Fiction; MA: Teachers College, Columbia, Applied Linguistics

Areas of Interest: Rhetoric and Writing, Language Acquisition, Linguistics, Translingualism, Creative Writing, Contemporary Literature

-
Michael Natriello is a lecturer in the English Department. He researches and teaches courses on rhetoric, college composition, language acquisition, and contemporary literature. His teaching has been recognized with an

[Alpha Chi Omicron Teaching Award](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/departments/english/undergraduate/alpha-chi-omicron/teaching-awards/), and a Beacon Exemplar Award from the United Student Government. Natriello has an MFA in Fiction from the New School, and has been selected to participate in the Kenyon Writers Workshop.
