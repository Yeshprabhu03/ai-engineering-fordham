# Sarah Gambito

![English Faculty Profile Picture](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Gambito_240.jpg)

### Professor; Director of Creative Writing

BA, The University of Virginia; MFA, Brown University

Research and Teaching Interests: creative writing, comparative & postcolonial literature, contemporary literature

-
[Sarah Gambito](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#3750565a555e435877515845535f565a19525342)is the author of the poetry collections*Matadora*(Alice James Books) and(Persea Books). Her poems have appeared or are forthcoming in[Delivered](https://www.perseabooks.com/delivered/?rq=delivered)*The Iowa Review*,*The Antioch Review*,*Denver Quarterly*,*The New Republic*,*Field*,*Quarterly West*,*Fence*and other journals. Current research focuses on post-modern U.S. immigration via Internet-based poetics. She is co-founder of[Kundiman](http://kundiman.org/), a non-profit organization that promotes Asian American literature. Her website is[sarahgambito.com.](http://sarahgambito.com/)
