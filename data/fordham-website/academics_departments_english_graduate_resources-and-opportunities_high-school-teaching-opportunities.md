# High School English Teaching Opportunities

Are you considering a teaching career at a private high school? Many Fordham graduate students have gone on to teach at the high school level. Most independent schools do not require state certifications or an Education degree, and starting salaries are on average comparable to (or higher than) those of colleges and universities.
