# About Environmental Studies

Our program offers the [major and minor](/academics/departments/environmental-studies/major-and-minor/) at [Fordham College at Rose Hill](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/), [Fordham College at Lincoln Center](/fordham-college-at-lincoln-center/), and the [School of Professional and Continuing Studies](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/).

Environmental Studies uses the natural sciences, social sciences and humanities to study the physical and societal causes and effects of environmental problems, integrating these disciplines in economic, political, and societal policy solutions.

Environmental Studies offers a broad range of career choices from conservation biology to work in organizations, business, and government.
