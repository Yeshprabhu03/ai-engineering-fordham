# Environmental Studies Study Abroad

Students are encouraged to pursue study abroad to gain international experience and knowledge of international environmental policies. Study abroad credits are accepted toward the environmental policy major. Please contact the [study abroad office](/study-abroad/) for current opportunities and policies.

Environmental courses are available at major universities around the world that can be attended through the study abroad office. The office lists programs in environmental studies, such as the following ones, which majors have successfully completed.

Denmark, [Sustainability in Europe](http://disabroad.org/copenhagen/programs/sustainability/)

Berlin, Environmental Studies

Costa Rica, Tropical Ecology and Conservation

Costa Rica, Sustainable Development Studies

Brazil, Amazon Resource Management and Human Ecology

Ecuador, Comparative Ecology and Conservation

Panama, Development and Conservation

Mexico, Social and Environmental Justice

Tanzania, Coastal Ecology

Botswana, Madagascar, Ecology and Conservation

Tanzania, Wildlife Ecology and Conservation

Mekong Delta, Natural and Cultural Ecology

Australia, Natural and Cultural Ecology

Australia, Sustainability and the Environment

ChinaGreen, Environmental Studies

[Conservation Biology Research Around the World](http://opwall.com/)
