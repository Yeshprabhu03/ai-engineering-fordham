# Ethics and Society Alumni

Alumni of the Ethics & Society Master's Degree Program go on to future studies in doctoral and professional degree programs (such as law and medical school), begin new or advance existing careers, and, most importantly, contribute to society as ethically educated citizens and professionals with specialized knowledge and skills.
