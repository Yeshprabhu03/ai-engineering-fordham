**Fashion for Philanthropy (LC)**

Fashion for Philanthropy (FFP) works to promote interest and knowledge of the fashion industry and its various components. FFP seeks to use the medium of self-expression, a fundamental element of fashion, to express the Jesuit ideals of social responsibility and creative individuality. Follow them [@FFP_LC](https://www.instagram.com/ffp_lc/) on Instagram.

**Fashion for Philanthropy (RH)**

Fashion for Philanthropy (FFP) is a group of students who are interested in both fashion and service, whether it be as a hobby or a potential career interest. As a club, we organize and host fundraisers throughout the year to benefit the Make-A-Wish Foundation of the Metro New York Area. Our biggest fundraiser is the annual student-run fashion show, where our members work together to collect donations and run the event as a whole. Throughout the year, FFP holds various career oriented events such as panels, speakers, and round tables, that provide our members with resources to cultivate their interest in the fashion industry. Follow us at [@fordhamffp](https://www.instagram.com/fordhamffp/) and e-mail [fashionforphilanthropyrh@ fordham.edu](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#5137302239383e3f373e232139383d303f2539233e2128233911373e233539303c7f343524) to join!
