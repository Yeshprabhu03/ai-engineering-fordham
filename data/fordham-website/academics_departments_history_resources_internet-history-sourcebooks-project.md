# Internet History Sourcebooks Project

Designed and maintained by Paul Halsall, the [Internet History Sourcebooks Project](https://sourcebooks.fordham.edu/index.asp) is one of the most visited pedagogical sites in the world and has received numerous accolades from teachers and scholars. Navigate through Halsall's site for each of the main and subsidiary sourcebooks.
