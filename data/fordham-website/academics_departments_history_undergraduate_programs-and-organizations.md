# Programs and Organizations for History Majors

Undergraduate students may join the Department of History either as a
[major or a minor](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/declaring-major-or-minor/). History students may also participate in numerous programs while in the History Department, including either the
[university](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/honors-program-students/) or
[departmental honors programs](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/honors-in-history/) and organizations such as the
[Mannion Society](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/mannion-society/) and
[Phi Alpha Theta](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/phi-alpha-theta/). Undergraduate history students interested in continuing with graduate work at Fordham may also be interested in the
[Early Admissions MA program](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/programs-and-organizations/accelerated-ma/). Seniors may also pursue the
[Secondary Certification Program in Education](/academics/departments/history/undergraduate/opportunities-and-employment/secondary-certification-program-in-education/).
