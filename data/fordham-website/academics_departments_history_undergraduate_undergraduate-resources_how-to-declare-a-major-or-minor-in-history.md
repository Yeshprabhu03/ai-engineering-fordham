# How to Declare a Major or Minor in History

The undergraduate chairs ([Dr. David Myers](/academics/departments/history/faculty/w-david-myers/) for Rose Hill or [Dr. Thierry Rigogne](/academics/departments/history/faculty/thierry-rigogne/) for Lincoln Center) will be able to help you with declaring your major or minor. Please contact them by email to make an appointment or visit during their office hours to discuss requirements for the major or minor in history.
