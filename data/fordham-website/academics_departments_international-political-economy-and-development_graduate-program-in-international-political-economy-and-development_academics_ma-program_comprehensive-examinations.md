# Graduate IPED Comprehensive Examinations

In addition to the required course work, the IPED MA also requires the passing of a comprehensive examination. The IPED Comprehensive Examination is taken during the last semester of course work. The material tested on the Comprehensive Exam is taken from the Core Economics and the Core Political Science courses.

Download the Questions from the latest comprehensive examinations. These are in PDF format and you will need an application like Adobe Acrobat Reader.
