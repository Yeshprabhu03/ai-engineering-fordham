# Graduate IPED Careers

![Students at a career networking event](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/international-political-economy-and-development/Careers_MD.jpeg)

The multidisciplinary approach of the Graduate Program in International Political Economy and Development prepares its students for a wide-range of careers after graduation.

Explore [Alumni Placement](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/careers/alumni-placement/) to see our strong placement record. The IPED Program has produced many graduates who have gone on to satisfying careers in international banking and finance, international government service, and in international humanitarian assistance as well as a few who have pursued careers in academia.

Also explore [Career Services](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/careers/career-services/) to learn about the many programs we have to support your professional development and career search.
