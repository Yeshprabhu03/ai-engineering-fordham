# University Placement Services

![Rose Hill campus in Fall](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/international-political-economy-and-development/Rose_Hill_Fall_View.jpeg)

Students in the Graduate Program in International Political Economy and Development (IPED) are entitled to use the resources of the University's Career Planning and Placement Office. The Career Planning and Placement Office is located in Room 224 of the McGinley Center at the Rose Hill Campus. Their telephone number is 718-817-4350.

Throughout the year students are urged to use the resources of the Office for conducting job searches. The University's Career Planning and Placement Office sponsors a number of Job Fairs and on-Campus Recruitment. It maintains an extensive list of internship possibilities. Finally, students can obtain assistance in writing resumes and in conducting interviews.
