Brian Reilly’s interests include medieval francophone literature, contemporary French thought, and language teaching. He has published on Chrétien de Troyes and Jacques Derrida and their relation to the history of science and skepticism. His monograph titled

*Getting the Blues: Vision and Cognition in the Middle Ages*explores color in medieval language and literature. He is currently preparing a second book on the Holy Grail. -
