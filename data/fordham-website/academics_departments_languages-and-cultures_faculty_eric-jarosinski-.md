# Eric Jarosinski

-
PhD. University of Wisconsin

-
Eric Jarosinski teaches courses in German language and culture at all levels, modern and contemporary literature, philosophy and critical theory, theater and television. Recent courses have included “Culture and Critique: Marx, Nietzsche, Freud," "The Book was Better: German Literature from Page to Screen," and "Dramatic Shifts: German Theater in the 20th and 21st Centuries."

In addition to teaching, he has been active as a translator (Jewish Museum Berlin), humor columnist (

*Die Zeit, NRC Handelsblad*), book author (*Nein: A Manifesto*), Internet aphorist (Twitter: @NeinQuarterly), and touring performer in an internationally acclaimed one-man show (“The Failed Intellectual Goodwill Tour”).
