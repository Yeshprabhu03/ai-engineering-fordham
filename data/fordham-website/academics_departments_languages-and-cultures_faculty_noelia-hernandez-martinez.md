Noelia Hernandez-Martinez is an experienced teacher and scholar at Fordham University, with a diverse academic background spanning multiple countries. She holds a BA from the Universidad de Murcia in Spain, a BA from Staffordshire University in the UK, and an MA from the University of Hull, also in the UK. Noelia has earned a certificate in teaching and is dedicated to ongoing professional development in the field of language education. Her areas of specialization include Spanish Language Pedagogy, Applied Linguistics, Teaching Methodology, Translation and Interpreting, and Spanish Language for Specific Purposes. In addition to her teaching expertise, Noelia has extensive experience working with applied languages and technologies, and she has a strong background as a translator in the legal, medical, and business fields. Her work reflects a deep commitment to enhancing language instruction and fostering cross-cultural communication.
