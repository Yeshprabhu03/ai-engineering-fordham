**Francesca Parmeggiani**

Dr. Francesca Parmeggiani and her team of translators, including Fordham students and colleagues in Italy and the U.S., have been collaborating with historian Patrizia Guarnieri (University of Florence, Italy) on the portal, [Intellettuali in fuga dall'Italia fascista / Intellectuals Displaced from Fascist Italy](https://intellettualinfuga.com/) by translating selected biographies of Jewish intellectuals from Italy and other parts of Europe, who studied, worked or conducted research in Florence in the 1920s and 1930s and whose lives were upended by the Fascist regime. Translated biographies include: [Silvano Arieti](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Arieti/Silvano/8), [Elia Samuele Artom](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Artom/Elia%20Samuele/11), [Camillo](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Berneri/Camillo/20) and [Giliana Berneri](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Berneri/Giliana/177), [Carla Coen Pekelis](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Coen%20Pekelis/Carla/132), [Mario Fubini](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Fubini/Mario/61), [Trudy Goth](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Goth/Trudy/242), [Giuseppe Levi](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Levi/Giuseppe/97), [Rita Levi-Montalcini](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Levi-Montalcini/Rita/103), [Renzo Luisada](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Luisada/Renzo/185), [Aldo Mieli](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Mieli/Aldo/112), [Attilio Momigliano](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Momigliano/Attilio/116), [Rudolf Levy](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Levy/Rudolf/327), [Willy Oppler](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Oppler/Willy/127), [Gaetano Salvemini](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Salvemini/Gaetano/148), [Angiola Sartorio](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Sartorio/Angiola/280), [Piero Treves](https://intellettualinfuga.com/en/Treves/Piero/198), and others. To date, they have translated over 50 “lives on the move”. This project and the experiences it documents were featured on the podcast, [“Idee (e corpi) in fuga”](https://www.raiplaysound.it/audio/2024/01/Expat-del-27012024-7a0396c5-5615-4100-ab30-2eb7c487938f.html), *Expat*, RAI Radio 3, on January 27, 2024.
