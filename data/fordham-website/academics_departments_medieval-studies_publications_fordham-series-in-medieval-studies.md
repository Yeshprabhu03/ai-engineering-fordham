# Fordham Series in Medieval Studies

The Fordham Series in Medieval Studies offers original studies and essay collections, as well as source materials on late antique and medieval culture, with an openness to interdisciplinary work.

Particular concentrations in the newly revised series include:

- Understudied fields, especially linguistic studies
- Race and gender studies
- Religion, including spirituality, theology, ecclesiastical institutions and interfaith experience
