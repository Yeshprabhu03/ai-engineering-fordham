# Student Composer Opportunities

Classes for student composers include:

- Music Composition (MUSC 2222)
- Jazz Composition & Arranging (MUSC 2234)

Students composers can also:

- learn about orchestration
- have access to music technology software
- get feedback from professional musicians from the New York City area
- hear their compositions read by professionals
- have an opportunity to get their music publicly performed

Find more information about the [student composer program](https://www.exponentialensemble.com/fordham-composers-program).
