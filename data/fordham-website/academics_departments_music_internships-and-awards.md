# Music Awards

## Student Performance Awards

The Music Department offers several competitive awards that cover the cost of [private lesson](/academics/departments/music/private-lessons/) tuition (voice or instrument) with one of our talented music teachers. The available awards (described below) are: The Fordham Music Alumni Award, The James Boyce Award, The Edythe Sheehan Dineen Award, The Frank Patterson Memorial Award, The Frank Buckhout McShane Memorial Music Award, and the Orchestra Awards.

### Eligibility

- Awards are made on the basis of competitive auditions held annually in the spring semester in early March. Contact Angela Michalski for more information -
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#24494d474c4548574f4d64424b56404c45490a414051). - To audition, a student must be enrolled at Fordham as a current undergraduate at the time of audition.
- To hold the award, a student must be enrolled at Fordham as a current undergraduate during the lesson term; lessons must be taken in the academic year following the audition.
- Only
[lessons](https://www.fordham.edu/info/29354/private_lessons)taken through Fordham's music program are eligible for award monies. - Students may audition in multiple years.

For specific terms of each award, see below.

**The Fordham Music Alumni Award**

Funded by the generous donations of our music alumni, the Fordham Music Alumni Award covers the cost of lessons for one semester. Music majors and minors at both Lincoln Center and Rose Hill are eligible for this award.

**The James Boyce Award**

Established in memory of Rev. James Boyce, O.Carm (1949-2010), a member of the Carmelite Order, an accomplished scholar, pianist and Professor of Music at Fordham University. The Boyce Award is granted to Rose Hill students, and covers the cost of lessons for one semester. Preference is given to music majors and minors.

**The Edythe Sheehan Dineen Award**

Established in memory of Edythe Sheehan Dineen, Dineen awards are granted to Lincoln Center students, and cover the cost of lessons for one semester. Preference is given to music majors and minors.

**The Frank Patterson Memorial Scholarship**

Established in memory of the internationally renowned Irish tenor Frank Patterson, this award covers the cost of private lessons, and in some cases may be renewed for mulitple semesters. Eligibility is restricted to music majors.

**The Frank Buckhout McShane Memorial Music Award**

Originally established at the former Marymount College of Fordham University, this award is granted to Rose Hill women, and covers the cost of private lessons for one semester.

**Orchestra Awards**

Additional stipends are offered to cover the cost of private lessons for one semester, on the basis of annual competition, to students who participate in the Rose Hill Orchestra, the Lincoln Center Chamber Orchestra, and the Jazz Orchestra. For more information, contact Dr. Andy Bhasin, Director of Orchestral Ensembles ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)).

### Student Academic Awards
