[Applied Mathematics](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/departments/applied-physics-mathematics)(not allowed if one obtains a Fordham math degree)[Applied Physics](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/departments/applied-physics-mathematics)(not allowed if one obtains a Fordham physics or engineering physics degree)**Biomedical Engineering****Chemical Engineering****Civil Engineering and Mechanics**[Computer Engineering](https://compeng.columbia.edu/)[Computer Science](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/departments/computer-science)(not allowed if one obtains a Fordham CS or math-cis degree)**Earth and Environmental Engineering****Electrical Engineering**[Industrial Engineering and Operations Research](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/industrial-engineering-and-operations-research-department)[Materials Science and Engineering](https://www.engineering.columbia.edu/departments/materials-science)**Mechanical Engineering**- Operations Research:
[Engineering Management Systems](http://ieor.columbia.edu/undergraduate/operations-research-engineering-management-systems)or[Financial Engineering](http://ieor.columbia.edu/undergraduate/operations-research-financial-engineering)
