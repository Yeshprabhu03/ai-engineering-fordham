# Physics and Engineering Physics Internship Opportunities

**External Opportunities**[DOE National Laboratory Internships](http://ed.fnal.gov/interns/): The Department of Energy (DOE) offers competitive internships at all of its national laboratories, including Los Alamos, Brookhaven, Lawrence Livermore, and the SLAC.

[ DAAD Rise:](http://www.daad.de/rise/en/) An internship for undergraduate students to work with research groups at universities and research institutions in Germany for a period of two to three months during the summer. RISE interns are matched with doctoral students whom they assist and who serve as their mentors.


Other internships opportunities can be found at individual institutions and companies.
