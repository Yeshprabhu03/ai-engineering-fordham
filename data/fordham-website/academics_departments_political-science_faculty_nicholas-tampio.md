# Nicholas Tampio

-
PhD, Johns Hopkins University


MA, Indiana University

BA, New College of Florida -
Nicholas Tampio is a Professor of Political Science at Fordham University. Tampio researches the history of political thought, contemporary political theory, and education policy. He has published books on the German philosopher Immanuel Kant, the French philosopher Gilles Deleuze, national education standards, and teaching political theory. Tampio serves as an editor of the journal Comparative Political Theory and as the faculty mentor of the Fordham Political Review and the department's Pi Sigma Alpha chapter. He regularly writes for the public in outlets such as Aeon, the Boston Globe, the Conversation, and the Washington Post. Click here for
