# Mood and Behaviors Sleep and Suicide Risk Study

### Are you interested in participating in a study about sleep and suicide risk?

Participants will attend a 3-hour appointment with our research staff. You will also wear a motion tracker for 21 days and complete brief surveys on your phone twice a day. You will need a smartphone with a data plan or WIFI access to participate. You can receive between $100 and $180 for participating in the study.

If you are between 18-34 years old, have had suicidal thoughts in the past 30 days or a suicide attempt in the past six months, and speak and read fluent English or Spanish, you may be eligible to participate.

Interested in participating or would like to learn more? Please leave your contact information, and a member of our research team will get back to you!
