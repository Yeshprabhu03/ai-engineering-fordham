## Award and Grant Spotlight

**Lucy Kochman-Sabbatino** was awarded a Spring 2025 Fordham College Rose Hill Undergraduate Research Grant for her project on sleep, memory consolidation, and symptoms of anxiety.

**Alyse Peduto** was awarded a Summer 2022 Fordham College Rose Hill Undergraduate Research Grant for her project on spatial awareness, spatial anxiety, and preferences for 2D or 3D maps.

**Jordan Stiver **was awarded the Laird S. Cermak award from the International Neuropsychological Society in 2019 for "The best research presented in the area of memory or memory disorders." The title of his work was "Variable Sleep Quality Predicts Poorer Spatial Learning and Memory."
