# Psychology Department Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion

![Psychology Diversity Equity and Inclusion](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/psychology/Psychology-Diversity-Equity-and-Inclusivity.png)

The Department of Psychology at Fordham University is committed to fostering an equity, inclusion, and opportunity atmosphere for all students, faculty, and staff. Please navigate through the pages below to learn more about our democratically elected Advisory Council within the department, which helps to oversee a number of initiatives and compile resources related to creating equitable and inclusive spaces within our department.
