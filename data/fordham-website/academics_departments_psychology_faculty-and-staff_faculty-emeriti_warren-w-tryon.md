# Warren W. Tryon

![Dr. Warren Tryon](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Tryon240_1.jpg)

Professor Emeritus

Department of Psychology

Email: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#354241474c5a5b75535a47515d54581b505140)

I began teaching at Fordham University in September of 1970. As of May 2015 I am fully retired after 45 years of teaching. I wrote **Cognitive Neuroscience and Psychotherapy: Network** **Principles for a Unified Theory** to improve our understanding of how cognition and affect interact to produce behavior. The chapter supplements and blogs listed below show that I continue to think and write about the topics covered in my book.

This web page contains [a color version of Figure 5.6](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/pdfs/jadu-single-folder-pdfs/Color_Version_of_The_Emotion_Solid_Appearing_on_Page_277.pdf) that appears on page 277 of my book.

I represent important points and themes of my book in 77 blogs that are posted on the [SciTechConnect Elsevier site](http://scitechconnect.elsevier.com/page/10/?s=warren+tryon) and automatically reposted on [Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/elsevierpsychology) and [Twitter](https://www.twitter.com/elspsychology).
