Destiny Lilly is a casting director based in New York. She recently cast the independent feature film, The Subject, which won the Best Narrative Feature award at the 2020 Art of Brooklyn Film Festival. She works at The Telsey Office where she casts plays for Classic Stage Company, New York Stage & Film, MCC Theater, Atlantic Theater Company, Second Stage Theater, and the Williamstown Theatre Festival. Her recent TV/film credits include the independent feature film Testament and the new Hulu series “Only Murders in the Building”. Destiny is an advocate for actors and artists and she currently serves on the board of directors of the Casting Society of America.
