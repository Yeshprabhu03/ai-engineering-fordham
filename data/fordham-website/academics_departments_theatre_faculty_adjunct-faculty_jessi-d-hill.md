**About**

Jessi D. Hill (she/her) Director of SMALL - 2024 Nominations for Drama Desk and Outer Critics Circle Award. New York Theatre includes: New York Theatre Workshop, Public Theater/Joe’s Pub, Playwrights Horizons, Primary Stages, LAByrinth Theater Company, Rattlestick, The Women’s Project, 59E59 Theaters, P73, The New Group, Keen Company, Clubbed Thumb, Ensemble Studio Theatre, wild project, Musical Theatre Factory, The Acting Company, The Barrow Group, New Dramatists, Playwrights Realm, New Georges. Regional Theatre includes: A.R.T, Old Globe, George Street Playhouse, People’s Light, Contemporary American Theatre Festival, Diversionary, TheatreSquared, Orlando Shakes and many others. Her work has been seen internationally in Edinburgh, London, New Zealand, Berlin, Hamburg, Brussels, Ukraine and Rome. She currently teaches directing at Fordham, NYU Tisch/Playwrights Horizons and Barnard/Columbia University. MFA Directing: Yale. Website: [www.jessidhill.com](http://www.jessidhill.com)
