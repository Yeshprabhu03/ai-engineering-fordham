# Kara Gordon

### Education

B.F.A. - Washington University in St. Louis, 2014

### Biography

Kara is a first year master’s student, with a concentration in Modern and Contemporary Theology. She is primarily interested in embodiment, trauma and grief, and their intersection with womanist and liberationist theologies.

Before pursuing her MTS, she received a BFA in Communication Design and English Literature from Washington University in St. Louis. She spent a decade designing and art directing for publications including *The Atlantic, Commonweal, *and *Harvard Divinity Bulletin, *as well as creating typefaces for clients including Google and Yahoo.

She has over 500 hours of yoga teacher training with a focus on alignment, integration, and trauma-informed practices. She now lives in Brooklyn, NY with her partner, Matthew.
