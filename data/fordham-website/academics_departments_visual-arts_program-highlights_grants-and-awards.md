# Visual Arts Grants and Awards

### Grants

We offer the following grants to Visual Arts Majors and Minors. Please read the descriptions for eligibility. Applications are reviewed by the entire Visual Arts faculty. Contact the [Visual Arts Program Head](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#7f1e0f161c1a13131e17160b3f19100d1b171e12511a1b0a) or other faculty for application deadlines, help with compiling your portfolio, submitting your itinerary, budget, and other required information.

#### Susan Lipani Travel Grant

Provides up to two outstanding Sophomore or Junior Visual Arts Majors with grants of $1,500 each, for independent travel during the summer or winter breaks. ELIGIBILITY: Sophomore and Junior Visual Arts majors studying Art & Engagement, Built Environment (Architecture), Graphic Design, Painting & Drawing, and/or Film/Video. [Applications](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScS7w0gbe6pF614LSdrw6nwv7fuhClSxi6fqhRf8KrM1jYh8g/viewform) are due in April.

#### William F. Di Pietra Film/Video Production Grant

This grant is for any Visual Arts Major or Minor who has a film project in mind, regardless of their area of study. Up to three grants of $1,000 each will be given to outstanding Visual arts majors or minors to work on a film. Funds can be used to rent equipment, hire actors, pay for craft services, or defray any expenses to the making of their film. ELIGIBILITY: Any Visual Arts Major or Minor. [Applications](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLScS7w0gbe6pF614LSdrw6nwv7fuhClSxi6fqhRf8KrM1jYh8g/viewform) are due in April.

#### Ildiko Butler Travel Award for Excellence in Photography

Up to three grants of a maximum amount of $3,000 each will be given to photographers of promise to make work independently during the winter, spring, or summer breaks. ELIGIBILITY: Sophomore, Junior, and Senior Visual Arts majors in Photography. Applications are due in December.
