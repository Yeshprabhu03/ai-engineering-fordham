# Mental Health and Crime: A Path to Safety for All

**A joint symposium with the Citizens Crime Commission of New York City, Fordham University, and Northwell Health**

**Date and Time:** January 16, 2025 | 9 a.m.**Location**: Fordham Law School | Skadden Arts Conference Center | 150 West 62nd Street, New York, NY 10023

**This symposium is sold out.**

## About This Symposium

Bringing together mental health professionals, legal experts, educators, and law enforcement to improve outcomes and enhance public safety, the symposium's goal is to emerge with an actionable path forward to this critical challenge.

There is no registration fee, but registration in advance is required. Seating is limited.
