# Faculty Research

![Research Faculty](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/research_02.jpg)

Faculty research funds are provided to tenured and tenure-track faculty in accord with University Statutes and fall under the following fund types:

[Faculty Fellowships](/academics/research/faculty-research/faculty-fellowships/)[Faculty Research Grants](/academics/research/faculty-research/faculty-research-grants/)[Faculty Research Expense Program](/academics/research/faculty-research/faculty-research-expense-program/)[Other Internal Funding Opportunities](/academics/research/office-of-research/initiatives-and-infrastructure/internal-funding-opportunities/)

Each has specific applications and requirements for submission. Please review each section for more information.
