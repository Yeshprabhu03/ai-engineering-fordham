# Rebecca Sanchez

![Rebecca Sanchez](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Screen_Shot_2020_06_15_at_4.28.18_PM.png)

Associate Professor

**Bio:**

[Rebecca Sanchez](/academics/departments/english/faculty/rebecca-sanchez/) is associate professor of English at Fordham University where she also co-directs the disability studies program and the faculty working group on disability. Her first book, *Deafening Modernism: Embodied Language and Visual Poetics in American Literature*, was published in 2015 with New York University Press and in 2016 she co-edited the republication of Pauline Leader's *And No Birds Sing *(Gallaudet University Press). She was a recipient of a 2015-16 AAUW American fellowship, and her work on disability, modernism, and poetics has appeared in numerous journals and edited collections.
