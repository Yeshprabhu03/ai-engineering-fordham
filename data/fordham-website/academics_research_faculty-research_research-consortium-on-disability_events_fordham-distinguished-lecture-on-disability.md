# Fordham Distinguished Lecture on Disability

**The 2024-2025 Fordham Distinguished Lecture on Disability presents: **

**Vent: Making and Debating the New York State Ventilator Allocation Protocols **

**Speaker: Dr. Mara Mills**

**Tuesday Match 25th 2025, ****from ****5:00 pm to 7 pm (in person and online)**

*The Fordham Distinguished Lecture on Disability is organized by the Disability Studies Program and the Research Consortium on Disability. This event is cosponsored by the Office of Equity, Inclusion, and Opportunity, the Graduate School of Education, the Graduate School of Social Service, the Communication and Media Studies Department and the Computer and Information Science Department.*

[ Dr. Mara Mills](https://links.crm.fordham.edu/els/v2/v7ymtg8ALLJV/TGdNVkRWdUExT0R1aGtzc05tclc5VXBMVGtmQlhtZmtmZjVHWFFEcnRja3lSbkdNVUJHQ2c3ZXcrbXBzSDRUTHFva1NFKzJHVFgrd053OENsck83ZlJVeFo4clhqRkQxazV1RlVvWTZzZWc9S0/), Co-Founder & Director of the New York University Center for Disability Studies, will present research from her new co-edited book,

*How to Be Disabled in a Pandemic*, which addresses the experience of disability communities across NYC during the first 4 years of the COVID-19 pandemic.
