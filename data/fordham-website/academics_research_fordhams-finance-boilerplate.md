# Fordham’s Finance Boilerplate

Fordham’s Finance division is overseen by the Senior Vice President/Chief Financial Officer, along with the Vice President of Finance. This department consists of the Budget Office, Treasury, Controller's Office, Procurement, Internal Audit, University Collections and Human Resources. Internal processes and policies are in place to assure a smooth and timely processing of all financial matters.
