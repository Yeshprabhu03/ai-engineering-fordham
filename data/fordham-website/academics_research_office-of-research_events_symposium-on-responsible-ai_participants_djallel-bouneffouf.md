# Djallel, Bouneffouf

Dr. Djallel Bouneffouf has dedicated many years to the field of online machine learning and data mining, with a primary research focus on developing autonomous systems that can learn, adapt, and make decisions in uncertain environments. His work spans both the public and private sectors, contributing to advancements in artificial intelligence, reinforcement learning, and trustworthy AI.

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research/images.jpg)

Over the past decade at IBM in the USA and Ireland, Dr. Bouneffouf has contributed to a wide range of projects involving reinforcement learning, brain modeling, and the development of AI systems with enhanced trustworthiness and interpretability. According to Google Scholar, Dr. Bouneffouf is the 10th most cited scientist in his field, with over 100 publications in top-tier conferences, more than 3,000 citations, and service as a program committee member for over 20 conferences.
