# Christopher Dietrich

Associate Professor, History Department, Arts and Sciences

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research-consortium-on-global-studies/Christopher_Dietrich.jpg)

Areas of Research:

History of International Political Economy, U.S. Diplomatic History, History of Decolonization

Dr. [Chris Dietrich](/academics/departments/history/faculty/christopher-dietrich/) is the author of Oil Revolution (2017) and editor of Diplomacy and Capitalism (2022). A graduate of Grinnell College and the University of Texas at Austin, he is the co-editor of the Power, Politics, and the World series at University of Pennsylvania Press.
