# Jordan Stein

Professor, English Department & Comparative Literature Department & African and African American Studies Department (Affliliated), Arts and Sciences

FCLC Honors Program Director

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research-consortium-on-global-studies/Jordan-Stein.jpg)

Areas of Research:

Colonial and early US literature; the eighteenth century (US, British, French, Caribbean); African American literature; literary theory; book history; queer studies

Dr. [Jordan Stein](/academics/departments/english/faculty/jordan-alexander-stein/) researches how aspects of social identities (race, sexuality, gender) inflect the material practices associated with literary production (reading, printing, editing, archiving). He's currently writing a media and book history of pre- and post-colonial Haiti (1764-1821).
