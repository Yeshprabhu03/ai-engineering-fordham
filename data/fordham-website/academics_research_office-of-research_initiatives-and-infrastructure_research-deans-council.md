# Research Deans' Council

Fordham Research Deans' Council (RDC) was established to 1) improve communication between the Office of Research and schools/colleges; 2) exchange ideas and information about university and schools strategic research initiatives; and 3) coordinate research initiatives and priorities with the [University Research Council](/academics/research/office-of-research/initiatives-and-infrastructure/university-research-council/) (URC).
