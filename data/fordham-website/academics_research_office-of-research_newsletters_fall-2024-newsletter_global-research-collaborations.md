# Global Research Collaborations

Faculty Research Abroad Program 2024

The Fordham University Faculty Research Abroad Program (FRAP) 2024 is designed to facilitate the development of research collaborations between Fordham University and international Jesuit universities or entities.

Research with Sophia University

The ongoing connections with Sophia University, Tokyo, Japan continue to grow. During the Spring 2024 semester, a series of events took place between the two universities. Here is a list:

March 26-28, 2024 – Fordham delegation goes to Sophia University, Tokyo

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research/GRC---Sophia.jpg)


May 2, 2024 – Fordham hosted the President of Sophia University Yoshiaki Terumichi at Rose Hill who met with President Tania Tetlow, Provost Dennis Jacobs, and Chief Research Officer George Hong

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research/GRC---President.jpg)


May 12, 2024 – Fordham hosted the NY Alumni Association for Sophia University at Fordham Law School. The Sophia NY Alumni Association members celebrated their organization’s 60th anniversary.

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research/Sophie-May-12-photo-2.jpg)


May 31, 2024 – Fordham hosted at the Lincoln Center Campus Vice President for Global Affairs Tetsuo Morishita and Wakasa Nagakura, a staff member of the New York Alumni Association for Sophia University
