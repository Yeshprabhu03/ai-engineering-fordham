# Spring 2025 Newsletter GSAS AI Summer Research Fellowships

## GSAS 2025 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Summer Research Fellowships

The Fordham University Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS), with the support of the Office of Research, is pleased to invite proposals for 2025 Artificial Intelligence (AI) Summer Research Fellowships. Grants of $5000 will be awarded to GSAS students conducting AI research individually or under the guidance of a Primary Investigator (PI) on topics related to AI technologies and their relationships to society. Proposals will be submitted via Jotform and are **due February 26, 2025**.
