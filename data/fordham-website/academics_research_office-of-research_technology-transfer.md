# Technology Transfer Services

Fordham is committed to the discovery of knowledge and the transmission of learning through undergraduate, graduate, and professional education of the highest quality. The University has an interest in managing intellectual property and reward members of the University community who create such intellectual property. The Office of Research is responsible for implementation of technology transfer services at the University.
