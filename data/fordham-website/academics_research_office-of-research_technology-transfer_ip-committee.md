# Intellectual Property Committee

The University Intellectual Property Committee UIPC is charged with

- reviewing and determining the release of a University-owned invention to the inventor;
- providing guidelines for the implementation of this Policy;
- interpreting and applying this Policy in individual cases;
- resolving disputes concerning the interpretation and application of this Policy; and
- recommending whether University resources should be allocated to the external marketing of the invention.
