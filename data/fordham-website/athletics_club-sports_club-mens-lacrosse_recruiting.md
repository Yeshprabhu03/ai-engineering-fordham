# Recruiting

## Want to play lacrosse for the Rams?

Whether you are a prospective undergraduate, a current student at Fordham, or a recent transfer student, the Rams are always looking for new talent. Please send an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#84e9e8e5e7f6ebf7f7e1c4e2ebf6e0ece5e9aae1e0f1) with the subject line "Prospective Player." Include your position, high school, and years of experience in the email. Highlight videos are also welcome.
