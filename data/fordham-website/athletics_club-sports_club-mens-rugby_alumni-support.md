# Men's Rugby Alumni Support

Many Fordham Ruggers who remain in the New York area continue their rugby careers with the *Old Maroon* Men's Club that shares Murphy Field as home turf with FURFC. Alumni Relations is a priority for Fordham Rugby centered on the Alumni Game played every Spring that brings current and former ruggers together in competition and fraternity. Alumni have also proved to be a valuable coaching resources for the current team.
