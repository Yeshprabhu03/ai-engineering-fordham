# Uniforms Policy

## Intramural Sports Uniform Policy

-
- All players are required to have similarly colored uniforms
- All players are required to have a clearly visible number on the front and back
- All players must have unique numbers
- The numbers on the uniforms may be drawn on, stitched on, sewn on, pressed on, etc., but they cannot be taped on
- Team members are not permitted to share uniforms
- All players must wear shorts/pants without pockets. Shorts/pants with zippers are permitted, but they must stay zippered the entire time. Players are not permitted to turn their shorts/pants inside out, tape the pockets, play in spandex or share shorts/pants with a teammate.
