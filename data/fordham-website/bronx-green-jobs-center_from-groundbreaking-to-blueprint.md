# From Groundbreaking to Blueprint: A Strategic Session for the Bronx Green Jobs Center

**Date:** Monday, August 11, 2025**Time:** 12:00 PM – 4:00 PM**Location:** McShane 303, Fordham University

This convening marks the first gathering following the July 10 groundbreaking of the Bronx Green Jobs Center. Designed as a strategic planning session, it offers a unique opportunity to co-create the vision and operational strategies for a successful Fall 2026 launch. Together, participants will shape key areas of development including organizational planning, curriculum design, career pathways, fundraising approaches, and community engagement strategies.
