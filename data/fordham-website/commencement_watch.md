# Watch

## Watch

The University Commencement Ceremony and each of the college and school diploma ceremonies will be livestreamed.

![Graduates cheering at Commencement 2022](/media/home/commonly-used-images/events-university-wide/commencement/BGilbert-20220521-0540.jpg)


### Main Ceremony

The main ceremony will be livestreamed at [fordham.edu/commencement](http://fordham.edu/commencement) **May 16 at 10 a.m.** At this ceremony, degrees are officially conferred by the president, and students are recognized as a group by the dean of their college or school.

### Awards Ceremonies

Visit our [Schedule at a Glance](/commencement/events/schedule-at-a-glance/) page for a listing of times, locations and livestream links.

### Diploma Ceremonies

At the college and school diploma ceremonies that follow the University Commencement ceremony, each student’s name is announced and they receive a ceremonial scroll from the dean.
