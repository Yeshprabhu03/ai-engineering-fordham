# FCLC Administration and Staff

![Fordham at at Lincoln Center](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Fordham_at_Lincoln_Center___MD.jpg)

The Fordham University administration and staff that lead Fordham College at Lincoln Center are key to our student-centered educational environment. From even before your first day on campus, we are here to provide ongoing guidance and advisement to support progress towards your degree and a fulfilling academic experience. Our team provides advising on course and major/minor selection, how to access special offerings and support, and forging connections to the array of resources on campus and in our beautiful city. Please see below for a complete list of our Arts and Sciences and FCLC Dean's Office team. We can't wait to meet you!
