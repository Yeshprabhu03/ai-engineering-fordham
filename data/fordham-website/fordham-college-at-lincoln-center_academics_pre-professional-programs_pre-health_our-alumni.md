## Michael Singer - Graduate Student Researcher at UC Berkeley

I am a graduate student pursuing a PhD in a field that did not exist 20 years ago. My studies focus on structural changes to bat genomes that have enabled them to evolve some of the most powerful immune mechanisms in nature. This exciting field has emerged from a confluence of innovations in genetic engineering, immunology, and sequencing technologies. I credit my education in the Natural Sciences Department at Fordham University with empowering me to believe graduate school could be right for me, and providing me with the skills to pursue a career in research.
