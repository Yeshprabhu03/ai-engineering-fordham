Mentor: Dr. Mark Botton

Abstract: In summer 2020, I worked as an intern and environmental education assistant with Genesee County Parks and Recreation in Western New York. My responsibilities included working in visitor services, greeting hikers and guests, caring for the resident animals at the Interpretive Nature Center, and answering questions about the history, ecology, and layout of the park. I also assisted with the educational programs, excursions, and classes, including outlining, writing, and conducting my own original educational program. I worked to improve digital access to the interpretive signage in the park and designed and created a set of over 20 signs to educate visitors in the Outdoor Learning Center trails.
