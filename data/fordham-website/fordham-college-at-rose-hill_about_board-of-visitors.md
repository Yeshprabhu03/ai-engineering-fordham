# Board of Visitors for Fordham College at Rose Hill

Fordham College at Rose Hill convenes an advisory Board of Visitors, which meets three times a year. The board is composed of 20 influential and successful Fordham graduates who advise [the dean](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/about/message-from-the-dean/) on the goals and priorities of the college and assist her in realizing them.
