As a FCRH student, you have access to:

- An exciting, challenging
[liberal arts curriculum](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/academics/core-curriculum/). - Engagement with first-rate faculty and small class sizes.
- In-depth
[academic and professional advising](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/academics/pre-professional-programs/)with expert faculty and administrators. - Thought-provoking
[research options](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/undergraduate-research-and-internships/research-opportunities-and-support/)within all disciplines. - All the opportunities New York City has to offer: countless cultural excursions,
[internships](/resources/career-resources/career-center/), and[service opportunities](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/external-affairs/center-for-community-engaged-learning/).

We encourage you to consider broader questions of [social justice](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/external-affairs/center-for-community-engaged-learning/) and personal responsibility. As you learn to discern and prepare for your own professional and personal callings, we'll urge you to enrich the lives of others, at home and abroad.

**Life at the Rose Hill Campus**

Beyond our commitment to high standards in academics and personal growth, you also enjoy:

- A
[beautiful campus](/about/campuses/rose-hill-campus/)with expansive green spaces where you can study and reflect on your experiences. - A
[modern library](/resources/libraries/about-us/locations-and-hours/)that contains over 1,000,000 volumes and 380,000 government documents, archives, special collections, computer labs, and plenty of comfortable spaces to read, write, and research. - An array of clubs and student organizations that reflect your interests, both academically and personally.
- Exposure to others who share your excitement of learning and who are seeking lifelong friendships.

We invite you to examine our [majors, minors, certificates, and pre-professional programs](/fordham-college-at-rose-hill/academics/majors-and-minors/). We also invite you to come visit us at Rose Hill in our vibrant Bronx neighborhood, where we can answer all of your questions in person.
