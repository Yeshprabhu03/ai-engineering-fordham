# Campus and Community Involvement

FCRH offers many opportunities for enrichment and developing a well-rounded application for the health professions. At Fordham,
[the Center for Community Engaged Learning](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/external-affairs/center-for-community-engaged-learning/) is a great

![Faith mount sinai](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Faith_Mount_Sinai.jpg)


resource for helping students to identify volunteer opportunities that align with their interests.

There are dozens of relevant clubs on campus for pre-health students to participate in. Below, please see a listing of just a few that connect closely to pre-health interests.

- Integrative Neuroscience Student Association
- Laennec Society
- Minority Association for Pre-Medical Students
- Pre-Dental Club
- Psychology Club
- Colleges Against Cancer
- Fordham University Emergency Medical Services
- Partners in Health
- Project Sunshine
- Special Olympics Club at Fordham
- Up 'Til Dawn
- Autism Speaks U
