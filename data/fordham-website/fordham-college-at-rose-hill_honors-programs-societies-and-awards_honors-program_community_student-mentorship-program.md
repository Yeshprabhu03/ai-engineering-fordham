# Rose Hill Honors Student Mentorship Program

![Student Wearing Headphones in Library](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Student_Wearing_Headphones_in_Library___MD.jpg)

Before school begins, each first-year student is assigned an upper-level student mentor in the program. Students meet their peer mentors in person at our annual Mentorship Reception in early September, when they join multi-year mentor families of Honors students across all four years.

Students are matched based on intended major, high school, hometown, and extracurricular interests. Student mentors provide information, offer advice, and help first-year students navigate their way through the program and the college.
