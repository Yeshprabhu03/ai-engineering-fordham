# Rose Hill Honors Faculty

![Female Faculty Member in Front of Black Board - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/CShinn_100112_0769__SM.jpg)


Our distinguished faculty members are committed to the intellectual and ethical growth of Honors students. Perennially available in office hours and at Honors events, Honors professors create and maintain mentor-like relationships with our students, contributing to the community that is a hallmark of the FCRH Honors Program.
