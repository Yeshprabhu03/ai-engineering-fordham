# FCRH Honors Program Junior Year Experience

![Female Student Intensely Focused in Class](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/RLozier_090814_5857_1.jpg)

In their junior year, students continue in-depth explorations of particular topics through their Focused Study courses. Students also participate in the Ignatian Education Seminar. Some take this opportunity to study overseas, broadening their understanding of our modern world.

## Junior Courses

-
Designed with an awareness of what students learned in the Foundational Texts courses, the Focused Study: Literature course delves into a particular topic in literature. Students choose among several course topics offered.

-
Designed with an awareness of what students learned in the Foundational Texts courses, the Focused Study: Theology course delves into a particular topic in theology. Students choose among several course topics offered.

-
One credit, pass/fail


The goal of this course is to give students the opportunity to learn about and reflect on the distinctive nature of a liberal arts education in the Ignatian tradition, both historically and in their own experience at Fordham.*Students may take this course in either semester of their junior or senior year*.
