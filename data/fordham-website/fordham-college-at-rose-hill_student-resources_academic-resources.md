# Academic Resources for FCRH Students

## Thriving at FCRH!

Fordham College at Rose Hill is dedicated to offering academic support that will enhance the intellectual life of all students.

We are also committed to providing students with the information and services they need to fulfill their academic goals while at Fordham and beyond.
