# Andrea Mennillo

![Andrea Mennillo](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Andrea_Menillo_headshot.jpg)

Andrea Mennillo is the managing director and founding partner of IDA Capital Ltd., London. He also is chairman of Riviera Airport S.p.A.

Mennillo holds a B.S. in business from Federico II University in Naples and a B.S. in international banking from the University of Lecce. He also is an alumnus of INSEAD Graduate School. He has almost 30 years of leadership experience, primarily in financial institutions and infrastructure funds worldwide.

He has acted as chairman, managing director, and board member in banks, insurance companies, and financial conglomerates in Italy, Spain, France, Germany, Portugal, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland. He joined the staff of the secretary of finance of Italy, and then he was appointed by the secretary as a general manager of Cassa Depositi and Prestiti, the largest infrastructure bank in the world. Afterward, he founded IDA Capital, an investment firm that provides a unique combination of industry acumen, practical experience, and knowledge of foreign business environments. Riviera Airport is a business/general aviation infrastructure that serves plane owners and customers going to the Italian Riviera, Côte d’Azur, and the Principality of Monaco.
