# Annie Penachio, GABELLI ‘18

Annie Penachio is a vice president at Rialto Capital Management. She joined Rialto in 2018 and in her current role, she focuses on investor services for Rialto-managed funds, co-investments, and managed accounts. She is also involved in the development and launch of new products and in supporting Rialto’s capital raising efforts. ![Annie Penachio](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Annie-Penachio.jpg)


She graduated from Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business in 2018 with a B.S. in Finance.
