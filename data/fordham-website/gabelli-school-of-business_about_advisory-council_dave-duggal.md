# Dave Duggal, GABELLI ‘08

![Dave Duggal](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Dave_Duggal_headshot.jpg)

Dave joined Macquarie in 2009 and currently leads the North American Energy Sales & Origination team. The team originates risk management, working capital, financing and physical solutions with Macquarie’s corporate and financial sponsor clients. Dave began his career with Pall Corp as an applications engineer in refinery phase-separations technologies. Subsequent to Pall, he joined Mitsui in energy derivatives sales before joining Macquarie. Dave holds a Bachelors in Chemical Engineering from Villanova University and an MBA from the Gabelli School of Business at Fordham University. Dave sits on the Engineering Advisory Board at Villanova University, the Gabelli School of Business Advisory Council at Fordham University and holds a board seat at SquareOne, a Delaware based waste oil recycling company.
