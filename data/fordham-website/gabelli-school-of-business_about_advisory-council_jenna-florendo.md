Jenna Florendo is a senior associate at TPG on the Client & Capital Formation team. In her current role, she focuses on product development and fundraising within the private wealth channel. She currently serves on the Gabelli School of Business Advisory Council. Florendo earned a B.S. in Finance and graduated *summa cum laude* from Fordham University's Gabelli School of Business.
