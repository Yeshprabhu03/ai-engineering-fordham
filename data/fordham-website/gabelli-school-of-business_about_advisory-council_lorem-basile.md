# Lorem Basile, GABELLI ‘18

Lorem Basile is a vice president in BNP Paribas’ Sales & Trading Division. As part of the growing Prime Brokerage team, she manages the securities lending relationship with several US and London-based hedge funds. She joined BNP Paribas in 2022 from Credit Suisse. There she was on the securities lending team, primarily focused on managing relationships with the large agent lenders.![Lorem Basile](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Lorem-Basile.jpg)


Basile currently serves on the Gabelli School of Business Advisory Council. She earned a B.S. in Applied Accounting and Finance with a concentration in International Business and graduated summa cum laude from Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business.
