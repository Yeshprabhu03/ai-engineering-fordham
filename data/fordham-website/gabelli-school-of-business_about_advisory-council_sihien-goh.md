# Sihien Goh, GABELLI ‘13

Sihien Goh is an executive director at Nordic Capital, where he is responsible for relationship management and fundraising within North America.

Before joining Nordic, Goh was a vice president at Goldman Sachs Asset Management. He was a senior member of the Client Solutions Group (CSG), which engages with institutional allocators and pension plans to identify public/private asset strategies to complement existing portfolios. He led the healthcare endowment effort within CSG and fund formation for Goldman’s private markets, a nature-based carbon solution.

Goh joined Goldman Sachs in the Investment Banking Division (IBD) as an analyst on the corporate derivatives team in 2014. He worked with corporate clients to manage and mitigate macro risks related to foreign exchange and interest rates.

Goh earned a B.S. in finance from Fordham University, graduated *magna cum laude*, and is an executive committee member of Fordham’s Gabelli School of Business Advisory Council.
