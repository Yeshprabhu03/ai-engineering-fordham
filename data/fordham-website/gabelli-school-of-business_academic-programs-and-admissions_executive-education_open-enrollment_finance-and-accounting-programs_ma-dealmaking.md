# M&A Dealmaking

## Understanding the Private Equity Middle-Market Ecosystem

Middle-market companies may not sound as exciting as the biggest firms that bring in billions at a time or the flashy startups that thrill and awe.

But the strength of the overall economy depends on the health of the middle market.

Itâ€™s also where you can find great deals.

Offered in partnership with the Association for Corporate Growth (ACG New York), this eight-week course immerses ACG New York members and non-members alike into the middle-market ecosystem and operating dynamics of middle-market companies.

Up-and-coming M&A dealmakers will learn the skills and knowledge needed to navigate the middle market with ease and make the most of this growing world.

**Topics covered**

- Private Equity
- Relationship Building
- Raising Capital & Managing the Business
- Leveraged Buyout Modeling & Analysis
- Financing
- Growth Strategies
- The M&A Process and Management
- Exiting the Investment
- Accounting and Legal Matters
- Credit Markets
