# Program Details

In this unique program, leaders will be given the tools to transform their career trajectory through traditional classroom teaching paired with personalized leadership and performance coaching.

**Program Benefits:**

- Personalized and tailored coaching approach; providing the tools and self-discovery for participants to better themselves and their work environments.
- Sharpen business acumen through an astute understanding of the global business landscape and changing trends across industries.
- Receive a personalized growth strategy, geared at both improving your performance and boosting your trajectory.

**Topics Covered:**

- Building Your Personal Performance Growth Strategy
- Setting and Measuring Goals, Strategies, and Tactics
- Developing Your Personal Leadership Impact
- New Directions in Marketing
- Financial Market Impact
- Global and Local Strategic Thinking
- Technology and Management
- Corporate Governance
- Ethical Leadership

**Program Dues:**

Individual Program Dues: $4,950 (due at registration)

Includes meals, snacks and course materials
