The Full-Time M.B.A. is the Gabelli School of Business’ flagship two-year program in management and leadership. Selectively enrolling approximately 60 students per year, the Full-Time M.B.A. combines industry-inspired hands-on coursework, individualized attention, and extraordinary network development in the heart of New York City.

**This is a true New York City M.B.A. – and the Gabelli School’s program is also STEM-designated.**

## A World Capital for Business and Learning

Attending business school in New York is a decision that can have a profound impact on your future. A New York M.B.A. offers unique advantages: opportunities to build your résumé year-round, executive-level professors who teach at our Lincoln Center campus during their off-hours, and direct recruiting connections to companies that seek new hires for their New York offices.

Full-Time M.B.A. students at the Gabelli School of Business have access to all of this and more.

**Gabelli M.B.A. graduates recently have secured post-graduation placements at Barclays, Chase, Citigroup, Comcast NBCUniversal, Ernst & Young, Google, IBM, JP Morgan, Merck & Co., Microsoft, PepsiCo, Seeds of Peace, Unilever, and dozens of other firms—a testament to the value of studying right where the world’s largest companies do business.**


### Industry-Inspired Academics

The Gabelli School of Business Full-Time M.B.A. program is taught by academic experts who maintain strong ties to industry and continually adapt the M.B.A. curriculum to meet the demands of today’s employers.

**From the Gabelli Launch orientation program that serves as the start of the program, to virtual or in-person applied learning projects abroad or domestic that develop students' management and consulting skills, the STEM-designated Full-Time M.B.A. curriculum is designed to over-prepare students for the challenges of the business world. **

Full-time MBA students will:

- Travel globally to act as consultants to nonprofits, NGOs, and Fortune 500 companies.
- Complete a rigorous
[curriculum](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/full-time-mba/full-time-mba-structure/)that balances teamwork, experiential and active learning in the key business areas of economics, statistics, accounting, finance, management, ethics, and strategy as well as individually chosen electives across a variety of[concentrations](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/full-time-mba/concentrations-and-secondary-concentrations/) - Visit companies in New York Cities and meet high-level executives with real-world insights
- Receive individual coaching and counseling for career development and growth.
- Understand the role of social innovation and sustainability in the business world and the society
- Develop social networks within the Gabelli School of Business, Fordham community, and beyond
- Flourish in the closely-knit FTMBA student body
- Use Augmented Reality (AR) and Virtual Reality (VR) tools that enhance the classroom experience

### STEM Designation

The Gabelli School of Business is proud to offer a STEM-designated full-time M.B.A. program. The interdisciplinary nature of the Gabelli School’s M.B.A. program blends together foundational business disciplines and incorporates expertise in technology, engineering, and science to help graduates make better business decisions.

According to the [Bureau of Labor and Statistics](https://www.bls.gov/opub/btn/volume-10/why-computer-occupations-are-behind-strong-stem-employment-growth.htm), careers in science, technology, engineering, and math are expected to grow over the next ten years due to continued growth in the digital economy. STEM-designated programs will help students remain competitive and contribute to the economic growth of the U.S.

Students who earn a degree in select U.S. government-designated STEM programs may be eligible to apply for a 24-month STEM OPT extension.

### A Network Above All Business Schools in New York

Securing the job you want after graduation is paramount, especially for students who commit to a full-time business program. Our Career Development Center (CDC) provides end-to-end career services with the goal of helping you translate your academic experience into meaningful, productive academic-year internships and post-graduation jobs. Career-search training begins from the very first week of the first year, with continuing support offered to Fordham graduates once they receive their Gabelli School M.B.A.

**The Gabelli School of Business provides additional layers of career coaching and job-search support built around key milestones that are specific to the Full-Time MBA to help prepare students for their next big move. Every student is assigned a career advisor who will guide them through the CDC’s six-step Job Search Model which is designed to take students from crafting a resume to closing on the job of their dreams. **
