# Concentrations and Secondary Concentrations - Gabelli

Fordham offers more than a dozen ways to make your MBA *yours*.

Five-course **concentrations **and three-course **secondary concentrations** are the way to customize your degree.

Guided by their academic advisors, MBA students may choose one or more of these to fit their academic and career goals.

## Primary Concentrations

[Accounting Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/accounting/)[Accounting Technology Analytics Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/accounting-technology-analytics/)[Communication and Media Management Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/communications-media/)[FinTech Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/fintech/)[Finance Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/finance/)[Information Systems Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/information-systems/)[Management Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/management/)[Marketing Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/marketing/)[Public Accountancy Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/public-accountancy/)

With the exception of Public Accountancy, all primary concentrations can also be pursued as secondary concentrations.

## Secondary concentrations

[Blockchain Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/blockchain/)[Compliance and Risk Management Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/compliance-risk-management/)[ESG Finance Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/esg-finance/)[Electronic Business Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/electronic-business/)[Entrepreneurship Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/entrepreneurship/)[Global Sustainability Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/global-sustainability/)[Healthcare Management Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/healthcare-management/)[International Business Secondary Concentration](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gabelli-graduate/mba/concentrations/international-business/)

One-on-one meetings with graduate academic advisors will be indispensable as you make decisions about whether to pursue a primary concentration or secondary concentration(s) and if so, which. Our advisors offer special expertise in navigating requirements and ensuring that your academic program matches your post-MBA plans.
