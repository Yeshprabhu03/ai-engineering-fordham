### Tuition & Fees

The tuition cost of the Dual Master of Business Administration (M.B.A.)/Master of Studies in Law (M.S.L.) program is **$2,032 per credit.**

The total cost of the degree is **$105,664**. However, students also are responsible for paying yearly student enrichment fees and semester fees as listed below.

Year One: $2,000

Year Two: $1,950 (if participating in the international experience)

### Scholarships & Aid

In addition to receiving a reduced tuition rate for two degrees, students may be eligible for financial support through scholarships, which are awarded to exceptional candidates prior to matriculation.

Student loan options are available through the University for U.S. citizens and permanent residents and non-U.S. citizens.
