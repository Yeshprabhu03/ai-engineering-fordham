# Master’s (M.S.) in Finance

## On-site and Online Study

## #52 Master's in Finance Pre-Experience

*Financial Times *– 2024

The world of finance is rapidly evolving. It’s more global and data driven than ever before. Conventional mindsets and methods no longer suffice. To compete and succeed, you need to be agile and attuned to new technologies, the complexities of global finance and the ways they can influence what comes next by driving innovation now.

**Fordham University‘s Gabelli School of Business STEM-designated, 30-credit ****M.S. in Finance**, offered on-site in NYC and fully online, prepares you for the finance careers of the future by grounding you in traditional topics such as financial theory, raising capital, and managing assets, while exposing you to emerging industry trends through electives and specialized tracks.

This AACSB accredited degree provides the skill sets and insights that address your career goals and objectives, while increasing your knowledge in growing fields including:

- ESG
- Data Analytics
- Fintech
- Corporate Finance
- Investment Management
- Risk Management

**Two Convenient Study Options**

On-site Master’s in Finance

- Study in New York City, the business capital of the world, at Fordham University’s Lincoln Center Manhattan campus—minutes from Wall Street, NYSE and NASDAQ.
- Complete your degree in as little as 12 months.
- Benefit from unsurpassed networking opportunities through a broad array of industry focused events and site visits
- Gain experience through internships at top NYC-based financial firms
- Reduce the cost of your education through
**merit-based scholarships**for those who qualify - GMAT/GRE Optional
**STEM Designated - Students who earn a degree in select U.S. government-designated STEM programs may be eligible to apply for a 24-month STEM OPT extension.**- Prior exposure to economics, finance, statistics, and accounting can be helpful, but are not necessary to apply
*****

**IDEAL FOR**:**Recent college graduates** and **early-career professionals** who wish to pursue a career in finance, but who need to gain advanced knowledge and skills in order to progress to mid- to high-level finance jobs.


Online Master’s in Finance

![Online Masters in Finance student at desk](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Online-Masters-in-Finance-2.jpg)


- Study online, benefiting from a combination of live, synchronous teaching and learning, and asynchronous study
- Gain greater flexibility through spring and fall terms that are split into two 7 – 8 week sessions; and a summer term that consists of courses that can range from 7 – 15 weeks
- Benefit from opportunities to interact and network online with students from across the country and around the world
- Gain the deep knowledge, skills and technical abilities to set yourself apart in this growing and evolving field
- Reduce the cost of your education through
**merit-based scholarships**for those who qualify - GMAT/GRE Optional
**STEM Designated**– preparing you to gain expertise in the more technical areas of business.*Please note that international students pursuing the Online MSF degree are not eligible for a F-1 visa and cannot receive STEM OPT through program completion.*- Prior exposure to economics, finance, statistics, and accounting can be helpful, but are not necessary to apply
*****

**IDEAL FOR**:

Early-career professionals with some experience who require greater flexibility to balance work obligations and educational pursuits, as well as those who wish to advance in their current career or to change careers by gaining invaluable skills.
