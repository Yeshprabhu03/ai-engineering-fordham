# M.S.F. Careers

**A global perspective. A world of opportunity. **

Global corporations face a unique set of challenges when it comes to navigating international regulations, governance, communication practices, and cultural divides.

The Gabelli School’s Master of Science in Finance* (M.S.F.) prepares you to contribute in all of these areas. The rigorous curriculum is taught by leading finance experts who teach skills in the context of current, real-world situations. And, thanks to the rich networking opportunities afforded by Fordham’s Manhattan location, you’ll hit the job market with a solid advantage over your peers.

## Job opportunities

Graduates of the Gabelli School’s MSF program can choose from a wide variety of job opportunities around the world, in fields such as:

- Financial analysis
- Treasury
- Investment banking
- Venture capital / private equity
- Wealth management
- Private banking
- Asset management
- Mutual fund management
- Hedge fund management
- Sales and trading
- Risk management
- VaR analysis
- Compliance
- Securities
