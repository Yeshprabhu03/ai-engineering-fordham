# Industry Connections, Career Pathways, Leadership Opportunities

## Our Network, Your Advantage

### Deep Marketing Communications Industry Connections

The M.S. in Strategic Marketing Communications offers students critical connections to the advertising, marketing, and media sectors. Faculty members and guest lecturers come from top agencies and organizations such as:

Ogilvy and Mather • Ketchum PR • BBDO • Ad Club of New York

They share their knowledge through case studies and through their own professional experience, providing an insider’s look at business and creative operations that drive innovation and change, and that capture our attention and imagination through the development of cutting-edge content. Their connections, along with the Gabelli School's [Career Development Services](/gabelli-school-of-business/student-and-career-resources/graduate-student-success-center/graduate-career-development/), can be leveraged to help you build your network, secure interviews, and gain the competitive advantage in launching or advancing your career.

### A Commitment to Responsible Business Leadership

All students pursuing a graduate degree at the Gabelli School of Business can enroll in the [Responsible Business Leadership Certification](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/ms-programs/responsible-business-leadership-certificate/) program, which prepares them to be an effective leader by helping to identify their leadership style and strengths, developing problem-solving and critical-thinking skills, and building personal and professional connections.
