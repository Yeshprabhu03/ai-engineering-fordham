# Internship and Industry Projects

We have a set of highly trained finance professionals who are ready to work on various industry projects. Past employers for internships and projects include top financial institutions like AQR, AIG, Bank of America, CITI, Finastra, Goldman Sachs, Guggenheim Partners, HSBC, JP Morgan, Morgan Stanley, Neuberger & Berman, Numerix, RBC etc. to name a few and various fintech firms.

Some of the projects MSQF students recently worked on (to name a few)

- Asset management with machine learning
- ESG
- FOMC signal and asset prices
- FRTB
- Hedge Fund Strategies
- Interest rate derivatives
- LIBOR transitions
