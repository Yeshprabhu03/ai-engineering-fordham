# Learn More and Apply

## Applying to the Gabelli Business Bridge

The Gabelli Business Bridge inaugural program will be offered in June 2022.

Interested in applying to Gabelli Business Bridge? Please review the participant eligibility and application requirements for the program.

PROGRAM DATES: June 13-24, 2022

PROGRAM COST: $6,500 (Includes materials)

-
The following application items are required:

- An online application and an essay
- Undergraduate transcript(s)
- International students must submit TOFEL scores (waived if studying at an American university)
- Current resume
- One faculty letter of recommendation
- GPA of 3.0 or above
- Additional letter of recommendation from a non-faculty member (employer or coach) (Optional)

-
Application for Summer Session will open on February 1, 2022. Application for Summer Session will close on April 30, 2021. Late submissions may be accepted based on the circumstances.

We review each complete application as it comes in and respond within two weeks of submission.

Once accepted and enrolled, we require a

**non-refundable deposit**of $500 to secure your place. This deposit will confirm your seat. Cancellations 30 days before the start of GBB are eligible for a refund minus the deposit. -
Required:

- Computer with an updated operating system (Windows or Mac)
- Webcam for video conferencing

Recommended:

- Headset with microphone
- Earbuds
- Wired internet connection (WiFi can be unstable)
- Monitor large enough to comfortably follow the program
