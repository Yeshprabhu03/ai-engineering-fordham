# Gabelli STEP

![Students in circle](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/gsb_gabelli_talk.jpg)


**STEP = Succeed. Engage. Prepare.**

Your first step toward grad school success starts here.

Gabelli STEP is a one-week summer experience designed exclusively for incoming international MS students at the Gabelli School of Business. This immersive program is your launchpad — helping you start graduate school with confidence, clarity, and connection.

**Virtual Week: July 21–25, 2025****In-Person Week in NYC: August 4–8, 2025**

## What You’ll Gain

**Start Strong, Stay Ahead**

Step into the graduate classroom ready to contribute. Our dynamic sessions simulate real academic expectations so you’re never caught off guard.

**Accelerate Your Career Strategy**

Access career coaching from Gabelli’s Career Development Center. Build your U.S.-style résumé, craft your pitch, and start networking early.

**Explore NYC Through a Business Lens**

Visit top companies and cultural hubs to see how New York operates as a global capital of innovation and leadership.

**Build Your Global Network**

Meet ambitious peers from around the world — and form a strong community before your program even begins.

## Who It’s For

Gabelli STEP is ideal for globally minded, future-focused students who want to:

- Gain early insight into U.S. business school expectations
- Sharpen their academic and professional toolkit
- Experience New York City as more than a tourist
- Start their program with purpose and momentum
