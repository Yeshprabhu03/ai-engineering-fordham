# Curriculum

Gabelli STEP is built around four integrated learning tracks:

**Academic Readiness Lab**

Master case analysis, team collaboration, and U.S. academic norms through fast-paced, discussion-driven sessions.

**Career Accelerator Series**

Refine your résumé, LinkedIn, and personal brand. Practice pitching your story and prepare to stand out in recruiting.

**Think Globally, Act Locally**

Discover American society through immersive site visits that explore culture, equity, innovation, and identity.

Featured excursions include:

- Ellis Island: Immigration & American identity
- Harlem: Urban development & cultural resilience
- Museum of the City of New York: NYC history & diversity
- Brooklyn Navy Yard: Innovation & sustainability in action

**Community & Leadership in Action**

From team projects to real-time feedback, you’ll practice showing up as a collaborator, leader, and global citizen.
