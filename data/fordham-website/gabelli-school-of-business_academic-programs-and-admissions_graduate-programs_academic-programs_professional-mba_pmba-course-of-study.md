# Course of Study

## An M.B.A. That Offers Maximum Flexibility

The Professional M.B.A. curriculum provides maximum flexibility to ensure that your course of study aligns with your career path. This** 45-credit program** is designed to build the immediately applicable core skills and knowledge needed for success, while providing concentrations in both traditional and emerging sectors to develop an area of specialty that will enhance your résumé and increase your marketability.
