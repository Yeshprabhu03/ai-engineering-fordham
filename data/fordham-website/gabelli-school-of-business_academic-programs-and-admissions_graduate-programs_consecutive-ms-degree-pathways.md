# Consecutive MS Degree Pathways

Earning consecutive MS degrees will help your career by giving you an increased edge in today's business world. Now, every Gabelli School MS program is offering an accelerated pathway for existing Gabelli School MS students.

There are more reasons than ever to complete a second MS degree:

- Fast-track application process
- Application fees waived.
- Beginning fall 2025, the enrichment fee for consecutive degree students will be reduced to 33% of the current enrichment fee.
- Up to six credits from 1st degree may double count toward 2nd degree**
- More time for career development in the U.S.
- Increased employment opportunities are available for students in interdisciplinary program pairings

For example:

- MSIT + MSF as a potential pathway for careers in FinTech
- MSBA + MSF as a pathway for a career as a financial analyst
- MSM + MSIT as a pathway for a career as an IT manager

Are you a current MS student interested in a Consecutive MS degree? [Apply for the Consecutive MS degree](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/apply/?sr=2b9e1250-a095-45ac-8512-f1e4f0b3ed92).
