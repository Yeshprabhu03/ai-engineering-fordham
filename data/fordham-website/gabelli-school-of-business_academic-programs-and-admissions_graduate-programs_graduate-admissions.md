# Welcome to Graduate Admissions!

## Taking That First Step

The Gabelli School of Business provides a graduate business education that is second to none. From our Lincoln Center location in the heart of New York City—the business capital of the world—to our cutting-edge curricula, unsurpassed networking opportunities, responsible business focus, and global perspective, we are the preference for those who seek to gain the competitive advantage while leveraging the knowledge they acquire and the connections they forge to make the world a better place.

#### A Location at the Very Center of the Business World

Studying business in New York City is a transformative experience—one that opens your eyes to new possibilities while building your connections to industry. The Gabelli School learning environment offers a breadth and depth of viewpoints that reflect the City’s incredible diversity. Access to the world’s top financial services companies, big four accounting firms, world-renowned marketing and advertising agencies, and communications conglomerates gives you the New York edge that will serve you for a lifetime!

At the Gabelli School, our approach to graduate education connects the dots between classroom learning and career outcomes. #### A Responsible and Global Business Perspective

As industry becomes increasingly global, and the world focuses on conserving precious resources, providing equal opportunity, and utilizing business as a catalyst for change, it is more critical than ever before to acquire the knowledge and skills to navigate in this new environment and leverage the insights you gain to truly make a difference. While many business schools are beginning to embrace this ideology, it has been deeply ingrained in the Gabelli School’s teaching and learning for decades and will serve you well throughout your business career.[ Learn more about the Responsible Business Center](/gabelli-school-of-business/industry-collaborations/responsible-business-center/), the heart of much of this activity.
