# Graduate Admissions Events & Options to Connect

## We Can’t Wait to Get to Know You!

Making the decision to earn a graduate business degree is a critical step in your career and your life. Learning as much as you can about the institution you hope to attend, the admissions process, and the types of programs available, will be key to your success. Throughout the year, the Gabelli School Graduate Admissions team offers a wide variety of options for you to get to know us and for us to get to know you. From on-site, virtual, and international information sessions to one-on-one coffee chats, campus visits, and meetings with our student ambassadors—we’ve got you covered.

![Admissions Event Dinner India](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Admissions_Event_Dinner_India.jpg)


### On-site and Virtual Information Sessions

To meet the needs of students from Brooklyn to Bangalore, we offer information sessions on-site at Lincoln Center campus in Manhattan and at venues across the city, the country, and the world; and online. Some sessions are more generalized, and provide information about application deadlines, writing your essay, tuition costs, and financing your education. Others will provide an overview of the program to which you are interested in applying. Don’t miss out on this incredibly helpful opportunity to learn as much as you can, meet admissions officers, and professors and ask questions.

### Information Sessions Across the U.S. and Around the World

In addition to on-site and online information sessions, the Gabelli School Graduate Admissions team travels the world, making it convenient for you to meet in-person with admissions officers in your own hometown. These events are great opportunities to get acquainted with the Gabelli admissions staff, as well as with other individuals who are considering earning a graduate business degree, to learn about admissions and visa requirements, and to hear directly about the benefits of earning your degree and networking in NYC—the business capital of the world.

[Sign up Now for On-site Information Sessions Around the World](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/portal/discovergabelli).

### Coffee Chat Admissions Counseling Session

Our highly popular Zoom 1:1 admissions counseling session provide the opportunity to talk, one-on-one, with an admissions officer about a specific degree program, discuss your personal academic and career goals and objectives, ask questions about the application process before you submit. Session run 25 minutes in duration.

![Lincoln Center Plaza](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Lincoln_Center_Plaza_1.jpg)


### Fordham Lincoln Center Campus Tours

Graduate classes are held at Fordham University’s Lincoln Center campus in Manhattan, an urban oasis that features state-of-the-art facilities and classrooms combined with spectacular natural outdoor spaces for studying and relaxing. Nothing beats seeing the campus and in-person, and we are delighted to offer guided tours. If you are bringing a family member or friend, be sure that they register as well. Tours start promptly at the times they're scheduled, so plan to arrive 10-15 minutes early to ensure you have enough time to check in with security.

### Connect With a Student Ambassador

There is no one better to chat with about the Gabelli School Experience than our student ambassadors. They provide an authentic view of all that we have to offer and are happy to share their experiences and answer any questions you might have. We highly recommend you engage with them to gain an insider’s view of the learning adventure that awaits you.
