# Graduate Admissions FAQs

## We’ve Got You Covered!

The admissions process can be complex. Prospective students often have similar questions. While we are happy to answer your questions by phone, email, or during one of the many information sessions we hold during the year, for your convenience, we have provided a list of frequently asked questions and answers that may help you immediately.

**When are the application deadlines? Can I submit materials past the deadline and still be considered for admission?**- The deadlines vary depending on the program you are applying to.
[Learn more about the deadlines for your specific program here](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/graduate-admissions/graduate-admissions-deadlines-/)**.** - Students have a one-week grace period following the deadline to submit any outstanding supplemental documents.

**Why are the deadlines different for international students?**- International students are encouraged to submit all materials at least one month prior to their program’s deadline to allow enough time to apply for a student visa.

**When can I expect to hear back about an admissions decision, and how will I be notified (phone/email/mail)?**- All information regarding your application will be sent to you via the email address you initially provided. Each application submission deadline has its own designated decision date.
[Find the date by which your decision will be released](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/graduate-admissions/graduate-admissions-deadlines-/)**.**

**How can I check to see what documents are missing from my application?**- You can log into your application’s Status Page using the link provided via email when you first began your application.

**Can I defer to the next academic year?**

Admitted students who wish to defer their start date to a later term may request an admission deferment. Requests to defer enrollment will be considered on an individual basis, and admitted students are encouraged to carefully weigh all options before submitting deferral requests. Please note, students may defer their admission for one year only except in the case of military or professional obligations, extreme medical cases, or global health and/or travel restrictions.**Review deadline dates on your admitted****student portal.**To request a deferral:- Complete the
[Admissions Deferral Request Form](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/register/?id=f6c1028d-336d-49c2-8654-a2a030d8158a). - If the deferral request is approved, you will be required to pay the non-refundable enrollment deposit outlined in your letter of admissions and an additional non-refundable enrollment deposit fee of $1,500.

Please note, both payments will be credited to the tuition bill at the start of your new term.
