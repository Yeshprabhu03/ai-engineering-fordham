# Graduate Admissions Team

## Partners in Your Success

At the Gabelli School of Business, we believe that the admissions process is more than just requirements and deadlines. It is about understanding who you are as a person and a professional, and ensuring that the path you choose to take provides the academic and career outcomes you desire. From reviewing your application to providing you with the information you need to make informed decisions, we are at your service. Our combined expertise affords the resources and guidance to make your admissions journey seamless and successful. How can *we* help *you*?

![Silvia McCallister-Castillo](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Silvia_McCallister_Castillo.jpg)

### Silvia McCallister-Castillo

**Senior Assistant Dean for Graduate Admissions**

Silvia McCallister-Castillo joined Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business in 2023 as the senior assistant dean for graduate admissions. Her previous roles have spanned student recruitment and admissions, student affairs, curriculum innovation, career development, and alumni relations for master’s degree and M.B.A. programs at IE Business School, London Business School, Yale School of Management, the University of Hong Kong, and the University of California, San Diego. McCallister-Castillo graduated with a B.A. in Classics from Columbia University, an M.Sc. in Corporate Communication from IE Business School, and an M.A. in Global Diplomacy from SOAS, University of London.

#### Nikki de Castro

*Assistant Director of Admissions*

*Admissions Areas: Full-time M.B.A. and M.S. in Management*

Nikki de Castro currently serves as an assistant director of admissions at Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business, primarily overseeing the recruitment and evaluation of the Full-time M.B.A. and M.S. in Management candidates.
