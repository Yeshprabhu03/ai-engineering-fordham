# Global Business Honors Program Research

As a Global Business Honors Program student, what will you discover that will enlighten the business world?

![Global Business Honors Program Research](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Global_Honors.jpg)

GBHP students work alone or in pairs to conduct original research in business. This is a chance to look into a topic that really interests you, whether it’s the purchasing behavior of sports fans or the impact of new U.S. tax regulations on multinational companies.
