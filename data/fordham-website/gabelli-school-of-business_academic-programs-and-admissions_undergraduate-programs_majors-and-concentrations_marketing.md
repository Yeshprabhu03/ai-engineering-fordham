# Marketing Area

## #20 Best Undergraduate Marketing Programs

*U.S. News & World Report Best Colleges 2026*

Today’s marketing landscape would be nearly unrecognizable to an advertising executive from the “Mad Men” era. Marketing is now at the center of* any* successful enterprise—from financial institutions and professional sports teams to retail stores and celebrity endorsements. The Gabelli School’s B.S. in Marketing is designed to prepare students for careers across numerous marketing career paths. Visit our [Careers in Marketing](/gabelli-school-of-business/faculty/academic-areas/marketing/careers-in-marketing/) website for an expansive list of marketing careers and professional options

This comprehensive undergraduate marketing degree program challenges students to think strategically and to make smart business decisions that benefit not only companies and individuals, but also society at large. Students gain the foundational knowledge and learn about the latest marketing trends and advances, with special attention given to consumer and societal well-being.

The field of marketing calls for creative thinkers with diverse interests and backgrounds. It encompasses the application of creativity, economics, psychology, sociology, anthropology, statistics, and analytics to contribute to the growth of the economy and society by identifying patterns in consumer behavior and fulfilling customer needs and desires.
