# Invest in Her: Financial Literacy and Leadership for Women

*For High School Students*

![Group of female high school students](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Invest-in-Her-program.jpg)


The Gabelli School of Business is pleased to offer a women in business program geared towards female high school students curious about the possibility of studying business and holding leadership positions in their future. This program could put you significantly ahead and give you the confidence to succeed!

**Program Benefits**

- Introduce fundamental financial literacy, including a basic understanding of financial markets, financial statements, and accounting principles.
- Engage in collaborative work that will foster critical thinking and analytical skills through problem solving activities.
- Expose participants to the concepts of professional behavior and executive presence, helping them cultivate confidence and poise in workplace settings.

Professional speakers, including Gabelli School of Business Alumni from top firms, will visit and network with participants.

**Who should attend?**

- Any female high school student interested in learning more about being a woman in business related fields
- Any female high school student curious about strengthening their leadership skills
