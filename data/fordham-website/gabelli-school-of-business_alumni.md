# Gabelli School of Business Alumni

## Leading the Global Future of Business

Part of the benefit of joining the Fordham University Gabelli School of Business community is the incredible network of alumni who are our pride and joy. Their collective success as business leaders, innovators, entrepreneurs, and changemakers is a testament to the teaching and learning environment that we provide, as well as their grit and determination to succeed.

### Part of a Worldwide Alumni Network

More than 45,000 Gabelli School of Business alumni live and work around the globe—utilizing the business knowledge and skills they have acquired here in New York City to change the world and the way we do business. They leverage their alumni connections to advance their careers and build meaningful relationships that provide professional pathways for doing well and doing good.

As part of the larger [Fordham University Alumni Network](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=4188) of more than 200,000 worldwide, our alumni are incredibly well-connected and benefit tremendously from Fordham’s global reputation and comprehensive resources.
