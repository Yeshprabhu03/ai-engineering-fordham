# Matthew T. Lee

![Matthew T. Lee](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Matthew-T.-Lee,.jpg)

Matthew T. Lee, Ph.D., is professor of the social sciences and humanities at the [Institute for Studies of Religion](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__www.baylorisr.org_&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=VfO4Jqwcw5_TR5XgM4w1RTXXIWXX3uvoHYKbCz9RJYQ&e=) at Baylor University. He also is a research associate and director of the Human Flourishing Program’s [Flourishing Network](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__hfh.fas.harvard.edu_flourishing-2Dnetwork-2DHFH&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=j5yWjQxuVwM7tigexktuk-IuveKa1g3CEbJ1nR7aFVM&e=) at the [Institute for Quantitative Social Science at Harvard University](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__www.iq.harvard.edu_people_matthew-2Dt-2Dlee&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=S2EnyRCo7MgABbzDrjPLrLqScON7mEfRAFC5Cp3EQew&e=). In addition, he is a member of the [Global Study of Human Flourishing](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__www.baylorisr.org_baylor-2Dand-2Dharvard-2Dresearchers-2Dpartner-2Din-2Dlong-2Dterm-2Dglobal-2Dstudy-2Dof-2Dhuman-2Dflourishing_&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=E1pQFk-2LZClTAzpDQWBFMS-G5tjg_3gTHxS04O8bdQ&e=) research team, an associate editor of [Humanistic Management Journal](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__link.springer.com_journal_41463&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=wFY-KLbZzlQjsIXSCrMfQPLfkzARJdAn5_RqhSw8KtU&e=), a distinguished visiting scholar of health, flourishing, and positive psychology at Stony Brook University’s [Center for Medical Humanities, Compassionate Care, and Bioethics](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__www.stonybrook.edu_commcms_bioethics_people_staff.php&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=ZT9mu7A3atV-ICcu93q1oqDufdg3sh3dbegb5DKIR38&e=), as well as a visiting scholar at the [Benson-Henry Institute for Mind Body Medicine](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__bensonhenryinstitute.org_&d=DwMGaQ&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=BqgPiBDe9pPN685ACFyIjMUXG3rfDtPlGl1RuVsAUwU&m=aKvpRatFMGwd39t0Cf6sydSOzZvmyEiJD1UkGhUqW8m9RbUB59PddTyn6kGnYVdQ&s=EG_3PBDh4eaAmcrpmlMhAZrUJAmWEEcJiYrbhlDDBMk&e=) at Massachusetts General Hospital. In April 2023, he was designated as an inaugural “Lifetime Community Engaged Scholar” by the EXL Center for Community Engaged Learning at the University of Akron.
