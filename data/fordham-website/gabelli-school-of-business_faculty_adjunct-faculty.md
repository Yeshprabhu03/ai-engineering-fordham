# Gabelli Adjunct Faculty

Gabelli School of Business adjunct faculty are selected from the New York City professional community.

They not only represent high levels of achievement in their industries, but also have the unique gift of being able to pass on their knowledge to undergraduate and graduate students.

The table below offers a selection of our current adjunct faculty, who number close to 100 in total.
