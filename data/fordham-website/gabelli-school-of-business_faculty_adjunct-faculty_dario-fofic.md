# Dario Fofic

## Adjunct Professor of Information, Technology and Operations

![Dario Fofic](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Dario_Fofic.jpg)

Fofic carefully chose his career path by focusing on gaining experience across different business segments, which helped him to connect the dots across complex organizational structures. This experience enabled him to bridge the gap between business and technology departments.
