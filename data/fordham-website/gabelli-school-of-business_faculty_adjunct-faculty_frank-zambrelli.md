# Frank Zambrelli

## Adjunct Professor of Marketing

![Frank Zambrelli](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Frank_Zambrelli.jpg)

Frank Zambrelli is an expert in ESG/sustainability with C-suite experience in retail, luxury, and branding. His approach overlays the multiple lenses of P&L priority and the mitigation of negative environmental and social impacts, demonstrating the value sustainable systems change creates across organizations and supply chains.

After more than a decade working for world-class brands, he launched the award-winning consultancy Design Quadrant, with a client roster including Chanel, Calvin Klein, Estée Lauder, Restoration Hardware, and Gucci. Today, Zambrelli serves as managing director and co-lead of Accenture’s global ESG retail practice.

An adjunct professor of marketing at Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business, Zambrelli is regularly quoted in the media and featured on panels discussing responsible business and the acceleration of ESG. He serves as the vice chair of Fashion Makes Change; the chairman of the Accessories Council; and an executive alumni board member at the Fashion Institute of Technology, where he earned a B.S. in Marketing Communications. Zambrelli also holds an M.S. in International Marketing with a concentration in sustainability from Glasgow Caledonian College New York.
