# Joseph N. Juliano

## Adjunct Professor of Marketing

![Joseph N. Juliano](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Joseph_Juliano.jpg)

Joseph Juliano is a Gabelli School alumnus and an adjunct professor of marketing, entering his 10th year teaching at Fordham University. Juliano brings over 30 years of real-world experience in marketing and sales to the classroom. He began his career in key account sales at Anheuser-Busch and then transitioned into brand management at Pepperidge Farm. Juliano then accepted a role at Nestlé, where he helped to drive significant growth across both the domestic and international beverage portfolio. Juliano led brands such as Poland Spring, Perrier, San

Pellegrino, and Aqua Panna. He spearheaded the Innovation group, created the division’s first powdered vitamin supplement, and helped to launch Nestlé’s first health and hydration retail stores. He then transitioned into the OTC/supplement industry as the vice president of marketing and general manager at Prestige Consumer Healthcare, where he managed leading brands such as Clear Eyes, Dramamine, Gaviscon, Dentek, Beano, BC/Goody’s, Chloraseptic, and Luden’s. Juliano is currently vice president of marketing and sales at Applied Biological Laboratories. He earned both his undergraduate and M.B.A. degrees at Fordham University and lives in Westport, Connecticut.
