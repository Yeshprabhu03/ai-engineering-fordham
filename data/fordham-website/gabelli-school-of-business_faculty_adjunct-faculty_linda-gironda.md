# Linda Gironda

## Adjunct Professor of Business Law

![Linda Gironda](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Linda_Gironda.jpg)

Linda Gironda works as an attorney for the Office of General Counsel at Fox Rothschild. She earned a B.A. in philosophy at Iona College, an M.B.A. in marketing with a certificate in international business from Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business, and a law degree from Pace University. In addition, Gironda holds a doctorate in education from Columbia University, where she researched how universities use online technology to connect students from different countries to develop intercultural competencies and promote global diversity. At the Gabelli School, Gironda teaches Legal Framework of Business and the Ground Floor course for first-year students. She also has taught undergraduate legal studies courses at Purchase College (SUNY) and graduate level leadership courses at Manhattan College. In 2018, Gironda was awarded the Chancellor’s Award for Excellence in Adjunct Teaching at Purchase College.
