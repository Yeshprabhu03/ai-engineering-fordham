# Logan West

## Adjunct Professor of Communications

![Logan West](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Logan_West.jpg)

Logan West is the marketing manager for digital and display advertising on the Amazon Digital Advertising team. He manages the partnership with the NFL and oversees first-party advertising for Thursday Night Football and other live sports. West joined Amazon after managing digital marketing for *The New York Times* and* *Sotheby's Auction House on their education products. He has been featured in *Apartment Therapy *and worked with influencers like Elena Taber to show off his unique apartment in the city. Most recently he was featured on oHouse, an interior design platform that integrates content, community, and commerce.
