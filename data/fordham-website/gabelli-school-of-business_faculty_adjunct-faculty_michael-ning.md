# Michael Ning

## Adjunct Professor of Finance

![Michael Ning](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Michael_Ning.jpg)

Michael Ning is a managing director at MacKay Shields. Ning has over 20 years of experience in the financial industry, and he has held senior positions at some of the world's leading investment firms. Ning is an expert in global macro investing and has a deep understanding of trading, portfolio optimization, tail-hedging, and global-macro fundamentals. He also is an adjunct professor at Fordham University, where he teaches quantitative finance and machine learning. He holds a Ph.D. from Oxford University and is a Chartered Financial Analyst (CFA).
