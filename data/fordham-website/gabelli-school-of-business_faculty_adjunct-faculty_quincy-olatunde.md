# Quincy Olatunde

## Adjunct Professor of Communications

![Quincy Olatunde](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Olatude_headshot.jpg)

Quincy Olatunde is a dynamic product management executive with a passion for people and human-centric solutions, backed by the power of data AI.

He is an industry-agnostic visionary, whose unwavering focus on people-centric products and data fuels groundbreaking solutions. With a profound understanding of human needs and desires, he has mastered the art of crafting products that resonate deeply with businesses and consumers. "It’s not just about features and functionalities,” he notes “it's about the emotional connection between users, businesses and their products, and a focus on making sure that the right output and outcome stay mutually aligned.”

At the core of Olatunde’s approach lies a genuine passion for people—both consumers and teams alike, with an unwavering belief that success thrives on empathy, collaboration, and trust. By fostering an inclusive and innovative environment, he empowers teams to deliver their best work, resulting in products that surpass expectations.

But it doesn't stop there. Olatunde is not content with just intuition and emotions. He knows that data is the heartbeat of every successful product. With an exceptional ability to harness the power of data-driven insights, he helps drive informed decisions, measure outcomes, and optimize products for growth and scalability. This unique blend of people-centricity and data-driven acumen positions him at the forefront of industries, both core and emerging.

Olatunde is on a transformative journey, where products are more than just technology; they are the threads that weave connections, spark joy, and leave a lasting impact on the lives of users. Building a future where humanity and innovation converge, leading the way to a new era of people-powered products.
