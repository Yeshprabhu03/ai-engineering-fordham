# Will Rockett, CFA

## Adjunct Professor of Finance

![Will Rockett](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Will_Rockett.jpg)

Will Rockett is an executive director of Eaton Vance Distributors, part of Morgan Stanley Investment Management (MSIM). Rockett leads RIA and family office distribution efforts across Eaton Vanceâ€™s largest national RIA relationships. He is responsible for working closely with RIA home office professionals in product, asset allocation, and research positions. He joined Eaton Vance in 2019. Morgan Stanley acquired Eaton Vance in March 2021.

Rockett began his career in the investment management industry in 1998. Before joining Eaton Vance, he was a managing director for Charles Schwab and led its fixed-income organization. Previously, he was a client portfolio manager and analyst at Symphony Asset Management, Cypress Management, and U.S. Trust Company. Rockett earned a B.A. from Rollins College and an M.B.A. from the NYU Stern School of Business. He is a CFA charterholder.
