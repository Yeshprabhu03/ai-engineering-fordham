Aniko DeLaney is an advanced lecturer in the Marketing Area of the Fordham University Gabelli School of Business. She is a brand consultant at Oppenheimer & Co., Inc. where she is collaborating with the CMO on a strategic brand initiative. Prior to this role, DeLaney was head of Ultra High Net Worth Marketing for Bank of America’s Private Bank and Merrill's Private Wealth Management. She completed the rebrand of US Trust to Bank of America Private Bank. Previously, DeLaney was chief marketing officer and global head of corporate marketing for BNY Mellon. She was responsible for BNY Mellon’s enterprise-wide corporate brand strategy, digital marketing, and market research. She also led the rebranding of The Bank of New York Mellon to BNY Mellon.

DeLaney attended Middlebury College and the NYU Stern School of Business. She was the vice chair of the Borough of Manhattan Community College Foundation Board for 10 years.
