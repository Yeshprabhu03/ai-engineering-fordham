Jie Ren is an associate professor in the ITO area at the Gabelli School of Business. She received her Ph.D. in information management from Stevens Institute of Technology, and she earned her M.S. in management science and engineering and her BS in e-business from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications.

Professor Ren's research explores the business impact of collective online behaviors: How the crowd helps organizations innovate, market, and make financial decisions. Specifically, she studies crowdsourcing, online reviews, and social media.

Proffessor Ren won the best paper in the track of social computing at AMCIS 2020 and the best paper second runner-up for AMCIS 2018.
