Dr. Navid Asgari is the Grose Family Endowed Chair in Business and Associate Professor of Strategy and Technology Management at the Gabelli School of Business, Fordham University, where he also serves as Associate Director of the Global Healthcare Innovation Management Center.

His research examines how technological change reshapes firm strategy, structure, and value creation, with current projects exploring organizational form, knowledge flows, and the strategic implications of artificial intelligence and biopharmaceutical innovation.

Navid serves on the Editorial Review Board of Organization Science, the premier INFORMS journal in the field of strategic management. He is also a Visiting Academic at the Institute of Operations Research and Analytics (IORA) at the National University of Singapore and a former Research Fellow at the IBM Watson Research Center.

He teaches courses in innovation, AI strategy, and organizational transformation, and coaches executives and global leaders on how to navigate the challenges of technological change.
