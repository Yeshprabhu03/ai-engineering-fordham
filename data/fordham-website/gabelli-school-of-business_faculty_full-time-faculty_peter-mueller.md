Peter Mueller joined the Gabelli School of Business in September 2023 as an assistant professor of Finance. He earned a Ph.D. from Price College of Business at the University of Oklahoma. His research interests lie primarily in the area of blockchain economics and decentralized finance, but also include general corporate finance. His research has been presented at many prominent finance conferences, including the annual meetings of the Midwest Finance Association, Northern Finance Association, and Financial Management Association.
