Steven Raymar is an associate professor emeritus of finance at the Gabelli School of Business. He previously taught at Indiana University - Bloomington. He received his undergraduate degree from Yale University and his Ph.D. in finance from Columbia.

Professor Raymar teaches and conducts research primarily in the field of options, futures and derivatives. He has published his work in the Journal of Quantitative Finance, Journal of Derivatives and Journal of Fixed Income, among others. He has also taught a range of classes from Introductory Finance, to Credit Derivatives.

Professor Raymar recently reached a term limit, after 6+ years, as Area Chair – GSB Finance and Business Economics. Prior to that, he was Associate Finance Chair for GSB Undergraduates for 6 years, and Faculty Coordinator for our GSB Global Business Honors Program.
