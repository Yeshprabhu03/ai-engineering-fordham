Yang Liu joined Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business in 2023. Her research focuses on innovation strategy and entrepreneurship. She also is interested in research methods such as supervised and unsupervised machine learning. Liu holds a B.A. in Economics from Peking University and a Ph.D. in Management from Cornell University.
