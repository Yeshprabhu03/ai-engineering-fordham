# Conference Introduction & Welcome Remarks

Fordham’s Gabelli School of Business will be hosting the International Conference on Cyber Security for the first time in its history. Two of the School’s senior officials will deliver the Conference Introduction and Welcome Remarks, providing an overarching view of event content, as well as the critical intersections between cyber security and business.
