# Fireside Chat: Responding to a Catastrophic Ransomware Attack

![Ed Stroz](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Ed_Stroz.jpg)


**Moderator: Ed Stroz**, Co-Founder, Consilience 360

Responding to a ransomware attack is demanding and stressful. It requires training, practice, and skills in a dynamic setting. Lisa Black led the response to a devastating ransomware attack targeting Suffolk County, NY in 2022 as the then chief deputy county executive. She will share her experience, advice, and lessons learned in a fireside chat that promises to be enlightening.
