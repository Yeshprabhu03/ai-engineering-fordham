# Hotels & Travel

Traveling to New York City for ICCS 2025? We’ve got you covered!

The Conference is being held at Fordham University’s Lincoln Center Campus located on Manhattan’s Upper West Side. It is a short distance from **JFK International Airport, Newark Liberty International Airport, Port Authority Bus Terminal, Grand Central Station**, and **Penn Station**. In addition, it is surrounded by some of the top hotels in the world!

For your convenience, a special conference rate is available if you choose to stay at the Empire Hotel, which is located just blocks away from the conference venue. **The number of rooms is limited.**
