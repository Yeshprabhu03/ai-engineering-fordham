# How Media Reports on Cyber Security

![Steve Hill](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Steve_Hill.jpg)


**Moderator: Steve Hill, **Strategic Risk and Cybersecurity Adviser

Public perception of incidents and cyberattacks is shaped by media coverage, which can influence public confidence and market stability. Media reporting on cyberattacks plays a vital role in educating the public, raising awareness, and alleviating uncertainty. However, covering cyber events presents unique challenges. The media not only informs the public but also holds organizations and platforms accountable when they experience cyber intrusions. It is essential for media coverage to be fair, balanced, and accurate.

Hear from leading journalists reporting on cyber and what they see and what they have learned.
