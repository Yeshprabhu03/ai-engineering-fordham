# Faculty

**Iftekhar Hasan, Co-Director**[Iftekhar Hasan](/gabelli-school-of-business/faculty/full-time-faculty/iftekhar-hasan/) holds the title of university professor at Fordham University, where he also serves as the E. Gerald Corrigan Chair in Finance at the Gabelli School of Business and co-director of the Center for Research in Contemporary Finance. He serves as a scientific advisor to the Central Bank of Finland and as a research fellow at the IWH Institute in Halle, Germany. He is the managing editor of the *Journal of Financial Stability*.

**An Yan, Co-Director**[An Yan](/gabelli-school-of-business/faculty/full-time-faculty/an-yan/) is a professor of finance and the associate dean of research and faculty development at the Gabelli School of Business. He received his undergraduate degree from Tsinghua University, China, and his PhD in finance from Boston College. His main research interest is in theoretical and empirical corporate finance. An Yan has published his research in the *Journal of Financial Economics*, the *Journal of Financial and Quantitative Analysis*, the *Journal of Banking and Finance*, the *Journal of Financial Intermediation*, and *Financial Management*. He teaches graduate-level finance courses, acts as a faculty advisor for Executive MBA consulting projects, and supervises MS in Quantitative Finance internship projects.
