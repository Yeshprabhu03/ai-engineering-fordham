# Responsible Business Center

Fordham University | Gabelli School of Business

## A Platform for Responsible Leadership

**The Responsible Business Center** at Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business is a dynamic platform for interdisciplinary engagement in the fields of sustainability and business responsibility. We work to **advance cross-sector collaborations**, **generate academic research**, and **enrich business school learning**, all with the goal of inspiring the next generation of responsible leaders.

![Fordham Gabelli Responsible Business Logo](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/fordham-gsb-rbc-logo-25.jpg)


## How We Achieve Impact

Through our programs, partnerships, and research, we empower the business community, academics, and students with the knowledge and tools to make values-driven decisions that support **long-term value creation**.

## Our Areas of Focus

Our Areas of Focus reflect a holistic approach to advancing responsible business practices by integrating transparency, environmental stewardship, and human wellbeing to support long-term societal and economic resilience.

**Responsible Reporting & Disclosures **

Focused on transparency and accountability in business disclosures, this focus area advances responsible reporting practices by exploring how sustainability and ethics intersect within financial accounting

**Natural Capital & Environmental Stewardship **

Committed to the stewardship of natural capital, this focus area advances the development and adoption of standards that support long-term environmental sustainability through the Natural Asset Accounting Standards Board (NAASB)

**Sustainable Wellness & Societal Health**

Grounded in the Jesuit principle of *Cura Personalis* (care for the whole person), this focus area promotes mental, physical, and spiritual wellbeing to support future quality of life and foster societal and business resilience
