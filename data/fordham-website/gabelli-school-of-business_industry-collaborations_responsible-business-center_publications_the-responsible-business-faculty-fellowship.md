# Faculty Fellowships

The Responsible Business Center supports faculty research that leads to **positive global change**. The **Responsible Business Faculty Fellowship** is a signature initiative that underscores our deep commitment to responsible leadership, sustainability, and academic excellence.

Each year, we award **three research fellowships** to faculty whose work explores the evolving challenges and opportunities in **responsible business**. Fellows contribute to shaping a more just and sustainable world while advancing top-tier scholarship and real-world impact.
