# 2023 Graduate Award Winners

These awards are bestowed each year upon students who have excelled in and outside of the classroom, and faculty members and alumni who have made a difference in the education and careers of our graduate students.

-
The 2023 Graduate Student Award Winners represent the breadth and depth of the members of our diverse student body, their many talents, and accomplishments. They exemplify the dedication and hard work required to achieve academic, professional, and personal success, setting a standard of excellence for those who will follow in their footsteps.

Congratulations to all of this year’s winners!

### Gabelli School of Business Alumni Award

This award is bestowed upon a Gabelli School of Business alumnus/na who has advanced graduate business education during the past academic year.

Louis Romeo, GABELLI '98, '99

Lou earned a B.S. in public accounting and an M.B.A. in corporate finance from Fordham University. He is a partner in Deloitte & Touche LLP’s Audit & Assurance practice and currently leads its U.S. banking audit practice, which includes responsibility for market strategy, eminence activities, and human resources management and development.

Lou is deeply committed to the Gabelli School of Business and its mission of educating the next generation of leaders. He played an important role in the School to collaborate with Deloitte on the Deloitte Foundation Accounting Scholars Program, which supports students with a record of commitment to diversity, equity and inclusion by providing a number of full scholarships for students in our M.S. in Accounting and M.S. in Taxation programs.
