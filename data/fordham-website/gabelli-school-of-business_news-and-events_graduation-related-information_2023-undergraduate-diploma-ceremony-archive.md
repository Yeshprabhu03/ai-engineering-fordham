# 2023 Undergraduate Diploma Ceremony Archive

The Class of 2023 graduated on a rainy day, but spirits soared as graduates received their ceremonial diploma scrolls from Dean Lerzan Aksoy. Relive the memories and your time at the Gabelli School of Business by browsing through the archival content on this page.

Also, remember to stay involved as an alumnus/na and take advantage of all that the Gabelli School has to offer personally and professionally.

The Gabelli School of Business Undergraduate Diploma Ceremony, which occurred directly after the Fordham University Commencement Ceremony on Saturday, May 20, 2023, was a happy occasion despite the rain. Nothing could dampen the spirits of students who had completed four years of work in and outside the classroom, readying themselves for careers across industries or for graduate school. Family members and friends were there to cheer them on.
