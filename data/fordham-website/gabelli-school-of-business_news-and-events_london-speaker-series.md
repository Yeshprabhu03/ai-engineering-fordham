# Gabelli School London Speaker Series: Business Perspectives From Europe

## Hosted by the Gabelli School of Business at Fordham London

Business is changing with the blink of an eye. Are you ready for what comes next? In partnership with Fordham London, the **Gabelli School London Speaker Series: Business Perspectives From Europe**, connects students with our partners in industry and at universities in the United Kingdom and across Europe, offering insights into the future of business and the driving forces behind the transformations that are occurring.

Gain a unique global perspective and the chance to network with professionals across the continents. These virtual events are open to the entire Gabelli School of Business and Fordham University community, including more than 45,000 Gabelli School Alumni worldwide.
