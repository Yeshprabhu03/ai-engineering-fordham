# Gabelli School London Speaker Series

## On the Cutting Edge of What Comes Next

From Environmental Sustainability to Equitable Outcomes to Emerging Technologies, the **London Speaker Series** covers the issues and opportunities that are transforming the way we do business. Gain a unique perspective on what’s happening in business now, as well as what the future holds from thought leaders and influencers who are on the cutting edge.
