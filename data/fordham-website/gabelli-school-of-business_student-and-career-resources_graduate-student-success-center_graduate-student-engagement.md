# Graduate Student Engagement

## Get Involved!

Student Engagement programs at the Gabelli School of Business enhance your education by providing you with the Leadership; Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion; and Community Service skills that hiring managers seek. The experience they provide complements your development as a business professional and serves as an invaluable addition to your résumé.

While you will engage in countless career and leadership-related activities, we also want your graduate experience to be fun and memorable. Participating in student life and getting involved in the Gabelli School community will help you to build critical personal and professional connections that will serve you for a lifetime.


Leadership Development

As a student enrolled in any of our graduate degree programs, you’ll have access to a suite of leadership resources that can help to launch or advance your career, while building the critical leadership qualities that will set you apart. We provide the “toolkit” that will help you to understand and address pressing issues—from ethics and sustainability to equitable outcomes and corporate responsibility—building the value you bring to any company or organization.


Student Life –Orientation/Activities/Organizations/Events

When looking back on their careers, many of our alumni cite their involvement in student life at the Gabelli School as being paramount in making their educational experience a success. Through the wealth of activities, organizations, and events we offer, you’ll bond with your classmates, discover new interests, and build critical networks for moving forward.
