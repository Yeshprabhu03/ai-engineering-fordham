# Industry Resources

In searching for a job, certain truths apply across the board. Do thorough research on a company before deciding to apply. Use your résumé to give an honest, concise description of what you can bring to an employer. Give specific examples in interviews to support statements about your strengths.

Beyond these, though, each business field has specialized approaches to hiring—which it pays to know as you seek an internship or job.
