# Sophomore Advising: Guide to Registration

## Overview of the sophomore year integrated core

The integrated core is made up of two major sections, typically taken during sophomore year.

### Business core phase one (typically fall semester of sophomore year):

- ACBU 2222 Principles of Financial Accounting (3 credits)
- MKBU 3225 Marketing Principles (3 credits)
- INSY 2299 Information Systems 1 (1.5 credits)
- CMBU 2664 Business Communication 1 (1.5 credits)
- LPBU 3223 Principles of Management (3 credits)
- CBBU 1002 IP 1 Consulting Project (1 credit)
- SABU 2142 Statistical Decision Making (3 credits)

### Business core phase two (typically spring semester of sophomore year):

- ACBU 2223 Principles of Managerial Accounting (3 credits)
- CMBU 2666 Business Communication 2 (1.5 credits)
- FNBU 3221: Financial Management (3 credits)
- INSY 2301 Information Systems 2 (1.5 credits)
- SABU 4441 Strategy (3 credits)
- One Elective (3 credits)

*During phase two, students complete business core courses plus one additional course of their choice.*
