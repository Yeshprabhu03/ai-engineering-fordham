# Student Organizations

Why join a student organization? Business student groups are a way to make friends and have fun, of course—but in business school, student organizations are something more.

## Build Your Résumé

Student club leaders and members make direct connections with Fordham alumni to hold events, connections that become valuable during the career-search process. They organize club site visits to companies that are likely to remember them when application season begins. And officer positions in student organizations are a potential way to demonstrate leadership, a character trait that many employers seek.
