**Overview:** Ascend is a premier nonprofit professional association that enables its members, corporate partners, and the community to realize the leadership potential of Pan-Asians in global corporations.

Ascend reaches 13,000 people with 37 professional and student chapters located in major business hubs and on university campuses. **Membership is open to individuals of all ethnicities and business backgrounds**.

The group’s services include mentoring programs, leadership training sessions, networking events, résumé workshops, mock interviews, other professional development events and more.
