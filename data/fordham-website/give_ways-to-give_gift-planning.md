# Gift Planning

![Three Fordham alumnae posing for a photo at a recent event](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/daur/give_pg_banner_image.jpeg)


Making a planned gift is an accessible and impactful way to show your support for Fordham University while accommodating your own personal, financial, estate-planning, and philanthropic goals. With proper planning, you may actually increase the size of your estate and/or reduce the tax burden on your heirs. Your contribution will not only benefit Fordham for years to come, but also uphold your legacy as an important member of our community.
