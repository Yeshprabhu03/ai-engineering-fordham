# Tracy O'Neill

**Tracy O’Neill, FCRH ’87**, began her career working at the BBC TV newsroom in London. She then managed media relations and communications for IBM, including their sponsorship of the Winter Olympics in Lillehammer, Norway. She was on the Board of Directors for the Charlotte Catholic High School Foundation and Habitat for Humanity. Tracy has also created a Freedom School Teen Board and helped transition homeless families into stable housing through the Supportive Housing Innovative Project in Charlotte, North Carolina.![Tracy O'Neill pioneering in philanthropy](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Tracy_ONeill.jpg)


As a Fordham graduate, a Fordham spouse, and a parent of Fordham alumnus Ryan Begley, FCRH ’15, Tracy maintains a strong commitment to the University. She and her husband, Terry Begley, GABELLI ’86, have created the Terence P. Begley and Tracy A. O’Neill Family Endowed Scholarship Fund at Fordham.

Tracy also serves on the Fordham College at Rose Hill Board of Visitors and on the President’s Council. She is a former member of the Parents’ Leadership Council.
