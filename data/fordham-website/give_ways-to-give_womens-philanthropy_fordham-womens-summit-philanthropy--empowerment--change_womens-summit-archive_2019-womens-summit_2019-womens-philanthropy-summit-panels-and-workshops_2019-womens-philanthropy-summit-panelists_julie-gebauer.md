# Julie Gebauer

![Panelist Julie Gebauer](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Julie_Gebauer.jpg)

Julie Gebauer, PAR

Prior to this role, Julie held a number of other leadership positions at Willis Tower Watson, including global head of talent and rewards line of business, business leader of the Eastern U.S. division, leader of the global workforce effectiveness practice, and leader of the employee survey line of business. She has been with the firm for 32 years.

Julie also consults with leading global companies on a broad range of HR issues. As both a business leader and consultant, she *has a deep appreciation for the value and risk that talent **bring** to an organization, and she is passionate about working with clients to optimize the talent equation. Julie is a frequent speaker on global talent trends, employee engagement, and total rewards. She is also co-author of Closing the Engagement Gap: How Great Companies Unlock Employee Potential for Superior Results*.

Julie received a B.S. in mathematics from the University of Nebraska, where she was honored as a Chancellor’s Scholar. She is a member of Phi Beta Kappa and a fellow of the Society of Actuaries, and she was inducted into the YWCA’s Academy of Women Achievers. Julie is a member of Fordham University’s President’s Council. She is also the parent of two Fordham grads—Dane, FCLC ’14, and Jeffrey, FCRH ’18.
