# Workshop: How to Show Up Authentically & Stand Out

In both our work and personal lives, we all want to feel fulfilled and happy. But how do we actually make that happen, while building our unique brand, and carving out our own paths? And the big question: How do we stay true to ourselves while aiming for success? Develop your personal action plan to do just that with marketing and branding expert, Monique de Maio, GABELLI ’94, PAR.

## Monique de Maio, GABELLI ’94, PAR, Founder & CMO of OnDemandCMO

Monique de Maio is a trailblazing female leader who has dedicated her life to helping women succeed in their personal and professional lives.

Monique is the Founder & CMO of OnDemandCMO, a 25+ year-old consultancy that operates at the intersection of marketing and sales. She works with Fortune 500 to mid-market technology, tech services, nonprofits and educational clients like Intel, Spectrum, Cox Communications, Iron Mountain, Jabra and others with their branding, messaging, positioning, content development and go-to-market strategies and implementations.

Monique is a multi-lingual immigrant from France, a mixed martial arts/ karate black belt, a fitness/wellness enthusiast, an empowering mentor, a podcaster (called Possibilities with Monique de Maio), and award-winning and top-rated author of *7 Secrets to Creating a Life You Love: A Practical Guide for Women in Leadership*.
