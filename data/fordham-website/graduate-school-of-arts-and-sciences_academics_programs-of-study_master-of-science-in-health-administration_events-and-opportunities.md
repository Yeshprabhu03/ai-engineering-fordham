# M.S. in Health Administration Events and Opportunities

## Discover How Fordham’s M.S. in Health Administration Can Advance Your Career

Join us for an **M.S. in Health Administration (MSHA) Information Session** to:

- Understand how you can
balance full-time work and earn Fordham’s CAHME-accredited MSHA in just one year. - Learn how you will benefit from our classes, held approximately twice per month, at our centrally located Lincoln Center campus (in midtown-Manhattan).
- Get your questions answered and see how the program equips you for success in the rapidly evolving healthcare industry.

Whether you’re just beginning to explore graduate school or are ready to apply, this session will give you the clarity and confidence to take the next step.
