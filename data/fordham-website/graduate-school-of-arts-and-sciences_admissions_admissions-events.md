# GSAS Admissions Events

![Female working on laptop - Stock photo](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/GSAS_female_working_on_laptop_virtual_info_sessions.jpg)

Fordham’s Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS) offers a variety of information sessions and events, both virtual and in-person, throughout the year. We invite you to register for an upcoming event so you can better learn about our graduate program offerings and why we may be the right fit for you.

To schedule a virtual appointment with an Admissions Counselor, please email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e98f9c8e88a98f869b8d818884c78c8d9c) with your program(s) of interest and availability.

*Visit this page often. We schedule events throughout the year*.
