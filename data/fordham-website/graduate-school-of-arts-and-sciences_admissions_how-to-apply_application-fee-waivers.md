# GSAS Application Fee Waivers

## Eligibility for fee waivers


GSAS may grant an application fee waiver to U.S. citizens or permanent residents who meet at least one of the criteria listed below. Applicants who are citizen residents of a country on the [United Nations’ List of Least-Developed Countries](https://www.un.org/development/desa/dpad/least-developed-country-category/ldcs-at-a-glance.html) (LDC) may be eligible for an application fee waiver (Proof of citizenship must be submitted with the fee waiver request). International students who are not citizen residents of an LDC country are not eligible for a waiver of the application fee.

**Please follow the application fee waiver request process outlined below. DO NOT email GSAS to request a fee waiver. All ****application**** fee waivers are ****considered**** on a case-by-case basis. Fee waivers ****are**** granted at the discretion of GSAS Admissions and are not guaranteed.** **Only**** one fee waiver per eligible applicant is permitted.**

-
Applicants who are currently serving in the military or who are veterans may be eligible for an application fee waive. Submit copies of any one of the following with your fee waiver request:

- Active Duty Orders
- Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty (DD Form 214, 215 or 256)
- Military ID Card (active duty, National Guard, Reserves, IRR, or retiree).
- VA Issued ID Card for Health Care
- Veterans ID Card
- Driver’s License with Veterans Designation or State Veterans ID Card
- Veterans Group Membership Card

-
GSAS may grant an application fee waiver to U.S. citizens or permanent residents who are currently enrolled in an undergraduate or graduate program and who submit official documentation that demonstrates financial need. Applicants can submit one or more of the following to demonstrate financial need:

- An official letter that verifies your
[Expected Family Contribution (EFC)](https://studentaid.gov/help-center/answers/article/what-is-efc), signed by you and a financial aid officer at your current institution. - A copy of your
[FAFSA Submission Summary](https://studentaid.gov/apply-for-aid/fafsa/review-and-correct/fafsa-submission-summary)(available from the[FAFSA website](https://studentaid.gov/h/apply-for-aid/fafsa)). - A copy of your most current U.S. federal income tax return (Form 1040).

GSAS reserves the right to request additional information beyond the above.

GSAS may also grant an application fee waiver to applicants who are citizen residents of countries on the

[United Nations List of least-developed countries (LDC)](https://unctad.org/topic/least-developed-countries/list). Applicants must provide documentation of proof of citizenship/residency. Acceptable forms of documentation include:- Copy of Photo Page of Passport from an LDC.
- Government-issued National Identity Card (must clearly show country of citizenship).
- Birth Certificate (must be issued by the civil authority of the LDC country, and accompanied by an official English translation if not in English).
- Consular Registration or Certificate from your country’s embassy/consulate abroad.
- Government-issued refugee travel document (if applicable and lists country of origin).


### How to request an application fee waiver

- Submit your completed
[online application](http://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/apply/?_ga=2.126132495.302961776.1599054296-795095001.1583966634). DO NOT PAY THE APPLICATION FEE. Please note that if you pay the application fee prior to receiving approval, we are**unable**to issue a refund. - Log into your
[Application Status Page](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/apply/status)and click the link to the Application Fee Waiver Request form. - Complete the form as directed and upload all verification documentation by the
[application deadline](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/admissions/how-to-apply/application-deadlines/)for your program of interest.

All completed fee waiver requests will be reviewed by GSAS Admissions. Applicants will be notified of the decision only when all necessary documentation has been submitted. GSAS reserves the right to request additional information beyond the above. Failure to submit the completed request form with the proper documentation by the application deadline will result in an incomplete fee waiver request and require payment of the application fee. Fee waivers are not guaranteed. Applicants not approved for fee waivers must submit the non-refundable application fee online.
