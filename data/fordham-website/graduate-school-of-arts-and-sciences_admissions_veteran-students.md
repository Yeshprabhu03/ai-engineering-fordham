# GSAS Veteran Students

![Fordham class of Veterans Standing Front of Keating Hall](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/GSAS_Fordham_Class_of_Veterans_Standing_Front_of_Keating_Hall_975.jpg)


Fordham is the only New York City university to be among those ranked


“Best For Vets” byMilitary Times

Fordham has been ranked as a top Military-Friendly [Yellow Ribbon University](/veterans/about-us/yellow-ribbon-commitment/) by Military Times for five consecutive years and is extremely proud of its active military, veteran, and dependent community for all students who have served our country.

An approved Yellow Ribbon Program university, Fordham is supportive of those who have served our country and we fully support their educational journey. We are pleased to offer a streamlined admissions application process for veterans, and guarantee that all tuition and fees are fully covered for admitted veterans, active military, and eligible family members who fall in the VA's maximum benefit category.

Why veterans love Fordham’s Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS):

- Fordham is a top-ranked, nationally recognized accredited university
- GSAS offers over 40
[graduate degree programs](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/academics/programs-of-study/), including 24 master’s programs, 10 doctoral programs, and 10 advanced certificate programs - Our students may take classes at our beautiful Rose Hill campus, located in the Bronx, NY, or at our Lincoln Center campus in midtown-Manhattan
- Small classes taught by distinguished faculty
- Active military, veteran and dependent community with dedicated advisors, alumni chapter, and student association
- Fordham offers transition workshops and networking opportunities
- GSAS offers Yellow Ribbon seats to qualifying veterans
