**Payment Explanation**

Graduate Assistant, Teaching Fellow, and Senior Teaching Fellow payments are processed in the HRIS system on a per-semester basis, totaling 21 installments. Payments will begin with the **August 8, 2025**, payroll period and end with the **May 15, 2026**, payroll period. Note that the first payment will cover one week only. The first payment reflects a one-week period because the assignment begins in the middle of a payroll cycle. All subsequent payments will cover two weeks.

Students appointed as a GA or TF for the fall 2025 semester only will receive their final paycheck on **December 26, 2025**. This payment will cover one week only.

Students appointed as a GA or TF for the spring 2026 semester only will receive their first paycheck on **December 26, 2025**.
