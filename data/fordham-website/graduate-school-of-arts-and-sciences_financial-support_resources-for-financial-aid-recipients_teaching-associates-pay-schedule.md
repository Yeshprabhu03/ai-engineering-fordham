**Payment Explanation**

Students who receive a Teaching Associateship for the fall semester only will receive their last check on December 26, 2025, which will be for one week only. Students receiving a Teaching Associateship for the spring 2025 semester only will receive their first check on December 26, 2025, which will be for one week only.

*****Fall 2025 semester payroll period ends on 12/19/2025. Spring 2026 semester payroll period begins on 12/20/2025.
