# GSAS Diploma Ceremony

Save the Date - Saturday, May 16, 2026

![Keating Hall at Commencement](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-arts-sciences/Keating_Hall_Commencement.jpg)


The main University Commencement ceremony will be held at the **Rose Hill campus on Saturday, May 16, at 10 a.m.** During this ceremony, the deans of the University will present their graduates as a group and President Tetlow will officially confer the degrees on our graduates. A guest speaker and honorary degree recipient will also address the Class of 2026.

All graduates are welcome and encouraged to join the University Commencement ceremony at **10 a.m. on Edwards Parade**.

The GSAS Diploma Ceremony information is forthcoming.
