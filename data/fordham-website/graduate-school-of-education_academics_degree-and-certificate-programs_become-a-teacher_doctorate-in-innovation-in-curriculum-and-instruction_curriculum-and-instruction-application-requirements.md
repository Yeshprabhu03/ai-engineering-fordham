# Doctorate in Innovation in Curriculum and Instruction Application Requirements

**Application Deadline: May 15**

- Online Application
- Applicants must have a Master's degree
- A minimum of two years of teaching or equivalent teaching experience with children, youth, or adults
- Minimum 3.0 undergraduate GPA and minimum 3.5 graduate GPA
- Official transcripts of all undergraduate and graduate coursework
- Graduate Record Exam (GRE) OR the Miller Analogies Test (MAT) with test scores less than five years old (Optional).
- For international applicants: English language competence TOEFL (minimum score of 90) or IELTs (minimum general score of 7 and no subtest can be lower than 6)
- C.V. or Resume
- Personal Statements (Part 1: Your background in teaching, learning, and research and how it relates to the ICI program; Part 2: Your professional goals and research interests)
- Writing sample (thesis, literature review, publication, course paper, etc.)
- Two Letters of Recommendation
- $80 Application Fee
- Interview with Program Faculty
