# GSE Admissions

## Welcome to Fordham’s Graduate School of Education (GSE)

Thank you for considering Fordham GSE as the next step in your professional journey. Our graduates are comprised of experts in the fields of [teaching](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/), [leadership](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/), [psychology](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-school-psychologist/), and [counseling](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-counselor-/). We make meaningful contributions to our communities and beyond, and we're delighted to engage with you to discuss your future.

Why Fordham GSE?

![Fordham students cross the street near Grand Central Station](/media/home/commonly-used-images/classroom-and-campus-life/KGamble-2430.jpg)

At Fordham, we don’t just educate—we empower. Here’s why our Graduate School of Education is the right place for you:

**Diverse and Comprehensive Programs:**Whether you're starting your career or advancing to the next level, our programs are designed to meet your goals.**Expert Faculty:**Learn from leaders in education who bring a wealth of experience, research, and industry connections to the classroom.**Hands-On Experience:**Prepare for real-world challenges with internships, field placements, and research opportunities that give you practical experience.**Supportive Community:**Join a vibrant and inclusive community that offers robust support services, from academic advising to career counseling, ensuring you succeed both personally and professionally.**Prime Location and Network:**Benefit from our location in New York City, with access to an extensive network of schools, organizations, and opportunities that enrich your educational experience.

## Become a Non-Matriculated Student

Whether you are deciding if you want to apply for a degree program, need prerequisites before applying, or are pursuing professional development, applying as a non-matriculated student allows you to take courses without enrolling in a degree program.
