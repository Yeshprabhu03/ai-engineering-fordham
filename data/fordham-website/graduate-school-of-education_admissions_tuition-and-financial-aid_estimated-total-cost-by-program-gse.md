## Additional Fees & Financial Aid

**Please Note: Additional Program Fees**

Depending on your chosen program and course model, additional fees may apply. These could include charges for workshops, labs, portfolio reviews, field work, research apprenticeships, dissertation seminars, and other related activities.

**One-Time Fees**

Students are required to pay a one-time assessment fee of $190 and a one-time malpractice insurance fee of $72. Please note that some fees may vary based on the student course load and will adjust accordingly with your individual course of study.

**Detailed Fee Schedule**

For a comprehensive listing of all applicable fees, please consult the [GSE Tuition and Fees Schedule](/student-financial-services/tuition-and-payments/graduate-tuition/graduate-school-of-education/).

**Financing Your Fordham Degree**

Students interested in financing their Fordham education may explore federal-based financial aid options, including student loans. Fordham offers several graduate loan options. For detailed information on how to proceed with financial aid, please refer to the [Graduate Financial Aid Guide](#).
