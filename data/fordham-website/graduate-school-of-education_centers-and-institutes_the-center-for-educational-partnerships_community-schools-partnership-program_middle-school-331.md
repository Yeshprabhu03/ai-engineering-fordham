# Community Schools Partnership Program MS 331

New York City's MS 331 was recently elevated from the city's Renewal Program for schools to become a *RISE School*, after meeting progress benchmarks and demonstrating a sustainable school improvement structure. [Read about one of our projects here!](https://now.fordham.edu/education-and-social-services/fordham-celebrates-opening-of-revitalized-school-playground/)

## Academic Support

As the school's Partner Community Based Organization (CBO), we provide:

- Math Coaches for Teachers
- Tutoring

We also organize and administer programs and supports in the following areas:

### Extended Learning Time

- Footprintz Basketball Academy;
- Mentoring in Medicine;
- Salvadori Center.

### Mental Health

- Full-time Social Worker (Morris Heights)

### Additional Support Services

- Fordham University Feerick Center for Social Justice LEEAP assistance with high school applications
- Fordham University's The Dorothy Day Center for Service and Justice
- College and Community Forum
