Merle Keitel is a licensed psychologist. She has taught counselors and psychologists in training for more than 30 years. Keitel has also worked in various mental health settings (e.g., a college counseling center, VA hospital, a child and adolescent clinic, a comprehensive cancer hospital and research center, and in private practice).

She has coauthored (with Mary Kopala) *Counseling Women with Breast Cancer* (2000), and co-edited (with Mary Kopala) *The Handbook of Counseling Women* (2003, 2017). Keitel has written numerous book chapters and articles, and has presented at national conferences primarily in the areas of health psychology, stress and coping, and women's issues.

Most recently, her areas of interest within health psychology include infertility, polycystic ovary syndrome (PCOS), and family responses to anorexia and eating disorders, plus women's mental health broadly defined.
