# GSE Course Schedules and Descriptions

Our course schedules and descriptions are searchable online without a Fordham login via our course registration system. Follow the instructions below to search the database. [Course descriptions](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gse/) are also housed in the [GSE Bulletin](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gse/).

## How to search Course Schedules and Descriptions

- On
[fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/), select Class Schedule or Course Catalog from the top right corner. - Select term (e.g. Fall 2021)
- Choose your subject area(s)
- Teaching courses are listed under Curriculum and Teaching
- Counseling Psychology and School Psychology courses are listed under Psych and Educational Services
- Educational Leadership courses are listed under Administration and Supervision
- Interdisciplinary Research (CLAIR) courses are listed under Contemp Learn and Interdisc Rsch
- Cross-disciplinary courses are listed under Urban Education

- Select College: Graduate Sch of Educ
- Click the course title to view additional details, including descriptions, meeting times, and instructors.

If you have trouble accessing the information you need, please email the Dean's Office at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#a9cedacccdccc8c7dae9cfc6dbcdc1c8c487cccddc) for assistance. If you are interested in classes outside the Graduate School of Education, please contact the [Admissions Office](/admissions-and-aid/) for information about that school.
