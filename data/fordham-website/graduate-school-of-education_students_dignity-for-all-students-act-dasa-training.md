# Dignity for All Students Act (DASA) Training

*Training in Harassment, Bullying, Cyberbullying, and Discrimination in Schools: Prevention and Intervention*

Effective December 31, 2013, all individuals applying for certification in New York state schools are required to complete six clock hours of training in harassment, bullying and discrimination prevention under the Dignity for All Students Act (DASA).

**Saturday, October 5th**| 9 a.m. - 4 p.m. | CRN: 40912 (EDGE 0260 - L01)**Sunday, November 3rd**| 9 a.m. - 4 p.m. | CRN: 40913 (EDGE 0260 - L02)

To register, please fill out an ADD form and bring it to the Associate Dean's Office for a signature to the Lowenstein Building, 113 West 60th Street, Room 1121, no later than five days before the workshop.

If you register and then decide not to attend the DASA workshop, you must complete a DROP Form before the date of the workshop. If you have questions about the DASA workshop requirements, please contact your academic advisor.

The workshop is open to Fordham students only.
