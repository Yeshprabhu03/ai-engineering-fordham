# GSS at Lincoln Center Campus

## A Quick Look: Fordham's Manhattan Campus

A degree with heart, in the heart of the city! Fordham's Manhattan campus is a prime location for a social work education. Surround yourself with different cultural perspectives, networking opportunities, and endless activities to do around your class schedule. Watch this short video to get a feel for your new home in the city that never sleeps.

We offer B.S.W., M.S.W., and Ph.D. programs at Fordham University's Lincoln Center campus.

At our Lincoln Center campus, you'll find yourself in the heart of Manhattan, with Broadway one block away and Central Park close by.

Enrich your experience with historic social work settlement houses and New York City's treasured culture. Immerse yourself in the art, museums, plays, and restaurants the city offers, along with the innovation and excitement that attracts the best minds in the world.
