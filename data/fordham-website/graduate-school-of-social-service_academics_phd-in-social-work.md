## The Desire to Grow

Doctoral graduate Margaret Knapp, Ph.D., pursued doctoral education at Fordham out of a desire to grow—both professionally and personally. Through Fordham's Ph.D. in Social Work program, Knapp has had the opportunity to teach master's students alongside her research, affording her countless development opportunities for her future. If you're interested in taking your social work education to the highest level, a Ph.D. in Social Work is an outstanding path.
