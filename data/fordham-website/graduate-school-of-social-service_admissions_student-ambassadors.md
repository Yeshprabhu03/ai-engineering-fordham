# GSS Student Ambassadors

![](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-social-service/Student_AMBASS_SMALL_9.jpg)


Our GSS Student Ambassadors are here to help you get a better sense of what it is like to be an MSW student here at Fordham. Whether you are a prospective applicant or a recently admitted student, our ambassadors are here to answer whatever questions you have about the Fordham student experience. Reach out to them by emailing [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#afc8dcdccec2cdcedcdccecbc0ddefc9c0ddcbc7cec281cacbda) or by signing up for a [campus tour](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/portal/gss_campus_tour).
