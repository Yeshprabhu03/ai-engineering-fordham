# GSS Centers and Institutes

![Students Walking in Front of Lowenstein Center](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/CShinn_100112_1576__SM.jpg)

Our Centers and Institutes help to further our mission to promote human rights and social justice:

[Children and Families Institute](/graduate-school-of-social-service/centers-and-institutes/children-and-families-institute/)supports agency-based practitioners to promote practice innovation in agencies and organizations serving children and families.[Institute for Women and Girls](/graduate-school-of-social-service/centers-and-institutes/institute-for-women-and-girls/)works to promote gender equality by addressing all forms of discrimination against women and girls locally and globally.[Henry C. Ravazzin Center](/graduate-school-of-social-service/centers-and-institutes/ravazzin-center/)on Aging and Intergenerational Studies empowers students, professionals, and communities to address the needs of older adults and their families through practice, policy, and research. The Center focuses on practices and policies across the lifespan with an emphasis towards promoting social justice.
