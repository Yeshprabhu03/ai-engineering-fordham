# Fordham Center for Nonprofit Leaders

![Students walking in LC building - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/RLozier_090814_4474.jpg)

Are you interested in a leadership position in the nonprofit field? Do you want to advance in your current role? The Fordham Center for Nonprofit Leaders, established in 2010, can help.

The Center for Nonprofit Leaders offers an 18-hour [Executive Education Certificate in Nonprofit Leadership](/graduate-school-of-social-service/academics/executive-education-certificate-in-nonprofit-leadership/) program.
