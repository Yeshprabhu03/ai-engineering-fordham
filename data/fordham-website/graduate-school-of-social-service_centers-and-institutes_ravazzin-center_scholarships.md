# Scholarships

![Scholarships, Ravazzin](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/pasted_image_0__1__2.png)

The Ravazzin Center offers scholarships for the study of gerontology and palliative care to further strengthen the role of geriatric social work and our understanding of aging.

**The Palliative Care Fellowship**

This scholarship is open to students committed to a career in palliative care social work in the Clinical Concentration who will be starting their Advanced Year field placement in September.

For more information or to apply for the Palliative Care Fellowship, contact Dr. Cathy Berkman, Associate Professor, and Director, Palliative Care Fellowship, at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#1b797e6970767a755b7d74697f737a76357e7f6e).

**The Aging Scholarship**

This scholarship is open to students who are committed to a career in aging and who will be starting their Advanced Year Field Placement in September. The student must complete a field placement with an agency serving older adults as well as a project in the field of aging.

Advanced Standing students must have been accepted into the MSW program, and confirmed their acceptance into the program by May 1 in order for their application to be reviewed.
