Dr. Goldkind is a professor at Fordham’s Graduate School of Social Service and the Editor in Chief of the Journal of Technology in Human Services. She is the founder of the

[Participatory AI Research, Education and Development (PAIRED) Lab](https://www.non-profits.ai/). Goldkind’s current research has two threads: data justice and artificial intelligence in human services and nonprofits, and artificial intelligence in social work practice. She has a robust network of community partners in New York City and internationally, including the International Federation of Settlement Houses, United Neighborhood Houses, and Caritas Macau. She holds an MSW from SUNY Stony Brook with a concentration in planning, administration, and research, and a PhD from the Wurzweiler School of Social Work at Yeshiva University. -
