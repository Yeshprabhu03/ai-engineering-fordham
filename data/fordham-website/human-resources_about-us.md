# About Human Resources

We understand that choosing where to dedicate your talents is a significant decision. At Fordham, you'll find a workplace where your skills are valued, your contributions make a real difference, and you are part of a community that is deeply committed to its mission and its people. We believe you'll discover that Fordham is not just a place to work, but a place to grow, to connect, and to contribute to a caring community.

## Our Vision

To create an environment for the Fordham family of employees to be and do their best all day, every day for the benefit of themselves, each other, and the University.

## Our Mission

To inspire the Fordham community to be their best every day by providing innovative HR services and solutions that nurture a spirit of *cura personalis*—care for the whole person—and *cura apostolica*—care for the work we do together.

## Our Values

- Agile, Innovative & Equitable Solutions
- Compassionate, Respectful & Inclusive Engagement
- Courageous Leadership
- Individual & Organizational Development
- Expertise & Advisement
- Timely, Clear & Consistent Communication
