The Learning Experience

**A Commitment to Cura Personalis **

Fordham's benefits package is designed to address the multifaceted needs of its employees, reflecting a deep understanding of the Jesuit **cura personalis**. The University's approach goes beyond compensation, instead creating a comprehensive support system that ensures employees and their families are cared for in all aspects of their lives.

**Health & Wellness Programs**

Fordham offers a full range of benefits to support your health and well-being, including:

-
**Medical, Dental, Vision Plans & Mental Health:**These plans provide access to essential healthcare services, from routine check-ups to more treatments, ensuring employees and their families can maintain their well-being. -
**Wellness Programs:**The University's wellness initiatives, such as yoga, nutrition, and fitness programs, encourage a proactive approach to health. -
**Discounts on Products & Services:**The availability of discounts on various products, services, and entertainment further supports a balanced lifestyle.

**Work-Life Integration & Financial Security**

A key component of **cura personalis** is the recognition that a life extends beyond work. Fordham's benefits package is structured to promote a healthy **work-life balance** and provide **financial security**.

-
**Generous Paid Time Off:**This includes paid holidays, a full week off between Christmas and New Year's, and summer Fridays in addition to vacation time. -
**Tuition Programs:**Fordham offers three (3) types of tuition programs for employees and their dependents, which is a testament to the University's commitment to lifelong learning and family support. -
**Robust Retirement Plans:**The University's partnerships with Fidelity and TIAA offer employees robust investment planning that provides a sense of financial security for their retirement.
