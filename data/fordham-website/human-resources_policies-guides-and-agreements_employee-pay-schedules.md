# Employee Pay Schedules

Fordham University uses a bi-weekly pay schedule. Pay day falls on a Friday.

If you are submitting a payment request, please refer to the [FY26 Processing Schedule](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/human-resources/pdfs/FY26-Processing-Schedule---Community.pdf) for deadline information.

A fiscal year is a period used for calculating annual financial statements in businesses and other organizations. Fordham University's Fiscal Year is from July 1 - June 30.
