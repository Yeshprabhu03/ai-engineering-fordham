# Veteran Students at PCS

Fordham is the only New York City university to be among those ranked Best For Vets by *Military Times*

Fordham has been ranked as **a top Military-Friendly Yellow Ribbon University by Military Times for five consecutive years**.

An approved Yellow Ribbon Program university, Fordham guarantees that **all tuition and fees are fully covered** for admitted veterans, active military, and eligible family members who fall in the VA's maximum benefit category.

## Guaranteed Admission

Fordham is a proud participant in the Yellow Ribbon program, which covers 100% of all tuition and fees for eligible students under the Post 9/11 GI Bill. Fordham's School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS) has unlimited seat and tuition caps, and is one of only three New York colleges to offer the **Veterans Promise Program**.

**The Veterans Promise Program**

- Guaranteed undergraduate admission into Fordham's School of Professional and Continuing Studies
- Available to student veterans who have graduated high school in New York with a minimum GPA of 3.0, who received an honorable discharge, and have no disciplinary or criminal history
- Open to anyone enlisting in active duty, National Guard, or Reserve Forces of the U.S. military
