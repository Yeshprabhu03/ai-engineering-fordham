# Rose Hill Integrated Learning Community: Manresa Program

**Integrated Learning Community**

[Loyola Hall](/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-rose-hill/residence-halls/first-year-student-housing/loyola-hall-residential-college/) is home to approximately 125 first-year Fordham College at Rose Hill and Gabelli School of Business students in the Manresa Scholars Program. Loyola Hall houses classrooms that are equipped with SMART technology, a kitchen, study nooks, event space, and the St. Ignatius Loyola Chapel.

![Loyola Hall](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/loyolahall.jpg)

Manresa students are enrolled in a course taught by one of Fordham's most talented professor-scholars. Each professor also serves as the academic advisor and intellectual mentor to each student.

The Residence Hall Staff in Loyola Hall consists of one full-time resident director, four resident assistants, four academic live-in tutors, and one Jesuit priest-in-residence. **Loyola Hall Residential College and the Manresa Program Pre-Application**

Students are able to pre-apply to the Loyola Hall Residential College and the Manresa Scholars Program via the Rose Hill Residential Life Housing Portal. Please check your Fordham Gmail account for information. In order to fill out your application, please prepare the following essay ahead of time:

*Not exceeding 300 words, please articulate your understanding of a year-long commitment to the expectations and spirit of the Manresa Program and the community standards of the Loyola Hall Residential College.*

Although you fill out the pre-application, you will still need to fill out your housing application through [MyPages](https://www.fordham.edu/my-pages/). Please be sure to select Loyola Hall as one of your top 3 choices. If you indicate Loyola Hall as a choice on the online Housing Application, you will be directed to the Manresa Program Course Selection page to indicate which seminars you are interested in. Additionally, you will receive a follow-up letter from the Advising Center. The selection process is first come, first served.
