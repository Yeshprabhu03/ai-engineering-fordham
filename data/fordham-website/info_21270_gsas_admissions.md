# GSAS Admissions

Fordham University's Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS) is a selective school with a population of approximately 800 students enrolled in our master's and doctoral programs.


## The admissions process

Our Admissions Team is committed to advancing the GSAS mission, which fosters the cultivation of knowledge, wisdom, and a deeper understanding of the human condition. We prepare our students for teaching and leadership in a global society, and welcome learners from diverse religious, economic, and cultural backgrounds into full participation in a scholarly endeavor.

We consider your application to be complete and ready for review only when it includes a submitted [online application](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/portal/gsas_application) and all required supplemental materials. Please consult the [How to Apply](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/admissions/how-to-apply/) and [Application Deadlines](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/admissions/how-to-apply/application-deadlines/) pages on the GSAS website for more information on the various elements that constitute a completed application, and be sure to review the admissions requirements of your [program of interest](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/academics/programs-of-study/).

### Application fee

The application fee is **$85**. Select applicants may qualify for an application fee waiver. For more information and eligibility requirements, please visit the [Tuition and Fees](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/admissions/tuition-and-fees/) page.
