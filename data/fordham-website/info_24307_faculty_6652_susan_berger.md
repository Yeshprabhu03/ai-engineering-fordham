# Susan Berger

Professor of Political Science Emerita

Email: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#6604031401031426000914020e070b48030213)

-
Dr. Berger received her BA from the University of Michigan and her MA and PhD from Columbia University.

-
Dr. Berger specializes in Comparative Politics, with a focus on Latin America. She has written on Guatemalan politics, the women's movement in Guatemala, and immigration, gender, and the law in the United States.

-
Guatemaltecas: The Women's Movement, 1986-2003 (University of Texas Press, 2006)
