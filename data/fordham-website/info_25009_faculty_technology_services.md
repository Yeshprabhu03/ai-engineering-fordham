# Academic Technology

**For Faculty**

## Overview

Educational Technologies and Research Computing assists faculty with the integration of technology in teaching and research.

## We Offer

- Support for the Fordham Learning Management System (
[Blackboard](/information-technology/it-services/blackboard-learn/)). - Workshops on Blackboard, and other applications for teaching and learning. RSVP for all workshops at the
[IT Events Calendar](https://itnews.blog.fordham.edu/?post_type=tribe_events&tribe_event_display=month). [Faculty EdTech Bootcamp](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/educational-technologies/faculty-edtech-bootcamp/)series offered several times throughout the year complete with recorded sessions and a resource library.[Research computing support](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/research-computing/)and resources including a high performance computing system.- Guidance and resources on tools and approaches to enhance classroom and online learning.
- Learning space design, labs, and training. A
[learning commons environment](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/emerging-ed-tech/); LITE, for innovating with technology innovation. [Computer Teaching Labs](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/computer-teaching-labs/)available on all three campuses.
