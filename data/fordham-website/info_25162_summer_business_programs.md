# Summer Business Programs

![Summer Business Programs in NYC at Fordham University](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/4O4A4734__1_.jpg)


Join us this summer! Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business offers three unique summer programs designed specifically for high school students. In just a few days, you can get a firsthand idea of what it’s like to study business in college.

[Exploring Entrepreneurship](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/specialty-programs/summer-business-programs/exploring-entrepreneurship/)

High school juniors and seniors can explore entrepreneurship in New York City at the Foundry, Fordham's hub for innovation and entrepreneurship.[Finance Institute: Wall Street in the Classroom](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/specialty-programs/summer-business-programs/finance-institute-wall-street-in-the-classroom/)

The Finance Institute is geared towards students who are considering studying finance in college or who want to learn to manage their own finances.[Global Finance](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/specialty-programs/summer-business-programs/global-finance/)

This program is designed for students interested in learning more about global financial markets and global investment opportunities.[Invest in Her: Financial Literacy and Leadership for Women](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/specialty-programs/summer-business-programs/invest-in-her-financial-literacy-and-leadership-for-women/)

This program geared towards female high school students curious about the possibility of studying finance and holding leadership positions in their future
