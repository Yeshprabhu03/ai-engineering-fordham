# Microsoft OneDrive

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

Fordham’s Office 365 subscription includes OneDrive. Sync, share, and save your files. Access them across all your devices.

## Connect

For seamless access to your files, go to [onedrive.microsoft.com](https://onedrive.microsoft.com/) or install the OneDrive client on your computer.

Fordham University currently supports the OneDrive client on:

- Windows 10
- Mac OSx

Fordham does not support the client for:

- Windows 7
- MyDesktop VDI
- Public lab or podium PCs

### Restoring files

-
Files deleted from OneDrive are retained in the trash for 93 days and then permanently deleted.

-
Files manually deleted from the trash can be recovered within the 93 day window; contact the

[IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/)to request assistance.

See also [Microsoft training for OneDrive](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/OneDrive-training-1f608184-b7e6-43ca-8753-2ff679203132?ui=en-US&rs=en-US&ad=US#ID0EABAAA=OneDrive_for_Business)

## Acceptable Use Policy

If you use OneDrive to save Fordham data, please consult the [data classification guidelines](/information-technology/it-security--assurance/it-policies-procedures-and-guidelines/data-classification-guidelines/) and Fordham University’s [Data Classification Policy](/information-technology/it-security--assurance/it-policies-procedures-and-guidelines/data-classification-and-protection-policy/). The policy applies to all data produced, collected, stored, or used by University employees, student workers, consultants and agents during their relationship with the University.
