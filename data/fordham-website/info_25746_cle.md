# Continuing Legal Education

![CAAL event](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/CLE-Banner.jpg)


Fordham Law, a leader in American legal education, presents an array of continuing legal education events and public programs to further attorneys' professional development and engage the community in exploration of important legal topics.

Most offerings are available for CLE credit and encompass a wide variety of legal fields. Speakers and participants in our programs represent diverse perspectives and provide valuable insights on national and international legal issues.

Cancellations and Substitutions

Cancellation notices must be received via email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#b0dcd1c7f3fcf5f0dcd1c79ed6dfc2d4d8d1dd9ed5d4c5) at least ten (10) business days before a program to qualify for a full refund, less a $50 administrative fee. There are no refunds after this time. Registration fees cannot be transferred to another Fordham program. Substitutions are accepted via email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#dbb7baac98979e9bb7baacf5bdb4a9bfb3bab6f5bebfae) until two (2) business days before the program. Programs canceled due to force majeure will receive a full refund.
