# Center for Judicial Events & Clerkships

[Launched in 2019](https://news.law.fordham.edu/blog/2019/09/04/fordham-law-launches-center-for-judicial-engagement-clerkships-led-by-professor-capra-and-assistant-dean-endrizzi/), the Center for Judicial Events & Clerkships has two intertwined mandates:

**Judicial Events**: To develop innovative ways to engage members of the judiciary in the life of the School; and**Clerkship Support**: To strengthen and build upon our clerkship successes by enhancing the existing clerkship support for our J.D. students and alumni.

Center Leadership

Learn more about the CJEC’s Leadership and Affiliated Faculty: [Meet the CJEC](/school-of-law/centers-and-institutes/center-for-judicial-events--clerkships/center-leadership-and-affiliated-faculty/)
