# Information Security and Assurance Resources

## Overview

Information Security and Assurance has curated this content to help the Fordham community with incident reporting, data loss information, ransomware awareness, risk management, security training, and other resources.

Subscribe to our [SecureIT ](https://itsecurity.blog.fordham.edu/)or [Privacy blogs](https://privacy.blog.fordham.edu/), follow us on Twitter ([@FordhamSecureIT](https://twitter.com/FordhamSecureIT)), and like our [Facebook Page](https://www.facebook.com/FordhamSecureIT) for updates on recent phishing activity and other security issues affecting the Fordham community.

Contact Information Security and Assurance directly at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#9bf2f5fdf4e8fef8dbfdf4e9fff3faf6b5feffee).

Endpoint protection is essential for securing your computer against cyber threats. Fordham deploys [CrowdStrike Falcon](https://www.crowdstrike.com/platform/endpoint-security/) on all University-owned and managed devices, offering advanced security features such as antivirus and malware protection.

For personal devices, we recommend the following free endpoint protection applications:

-
[Microsoft Defender](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/windows/stay-protected-with-windows-security-2ae0363d-0ada-c064-8b56-6a39afb6a963), which already comes with Windows -
Mac OS X:

[Bitdefender Virus Scanner](https://www.bitdefender.com/solutions/virus-scanner-for-mac.html),[AVG](https://www.avg.com/),[Avast](https://www.avast.com/en-us/free-mac-security)

### Educate Yourself About Security Awareness

Practicing good cyber hygiene is crucial in defending against cyber threats. Enhance your knowledge by taking our free, self-paced online information security awareness training for employees.

To access the course:

- Visit
[fordham.edu](https://www.fordham.edu) - Click
"Log in" on the portal to authenticate using your Fordham credentials - Navigate to "My Apps" and select
[Proofpoint Security Awareness Training](https://cybersecuritytraining.it.fordham.edu/).

If you have any questions, please contact the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).
