# IT Security Guides and Tips

## Overview

Cybersecurity is everyone’s responsibility. Students, staff, and faculty can help contribute to an environment of safe online practices. Information Security and Assurance gathered cybersecurity safety tips for prevention and risk mitigation. Security tips, best practices for email security, and ransomware awareness are included in this section.
