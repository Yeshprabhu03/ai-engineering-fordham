## Workshops

Throughout the year, the Educational Technologies offers faculty and graduate students online and in person workshops on Blackboard and other applications used for teaching, learning, and research. View and register for current EdTech Workshops on [IT Events Calendar](https://itnews.blog.fordham.edu/events/category/academic-technology/list/). We send regular emails to faculty, staff, and graduate students with a list of upcoming workshops and events.

See also the recordings and resources from the [Faculty EdTech Bootcamp](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/educational-technologies/faculty-edtech-bootcamp/), a week of workshops that provide faculty with immediately applicable strategies to save time, boost productivity, and energize classes. The bootcamp is held several times a year and offers sessions on the following topics: Fordham Essentials, Digital Citizenship, AI, Boosting Productivity, Blackboard, and Content Creation and Tools.
