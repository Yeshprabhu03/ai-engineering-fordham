# Faculty Technology Workshops

**For Faculty**

## Overview

[Educational Technologies](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/educational-technologies/) offers a variety of workshops throughout the academic year. All workshops are open to faculty and offered remotely.

The [IT Events Calendar](https://itnews.blog.fordham.edu/?post_type=tribe_events&tribe_event_display=month) contains a complete list of events and workshops sponsored by Info Tech. In addition, an email goes out once a month to all faculty and staff with a list of upcoming events and workshops.
