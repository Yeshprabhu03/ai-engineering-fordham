# Papercut Client (Personal Device)

### Overview

All Fordham managed devices have the Paper Cut client installed. You may choose to install the client on a personal device as well.

### Install the PaperCut Print Deploy Client

- Go to
[https://print-server.fordham.edu:9164/setup](https://print-server.fordham.edu:9164/setup) - Select the appropriate installer for your device
- Click on
**Download** - Start the installer
- Click
**Next** - Click
**Finish** - Click on the Papercut icon in the taskbar (lower right corner for Windows, Upper right corner for Mac)
- Click
**View my printers** - When prompted, log in with your Fordham username and Password
- The client will show you a printer called FindME[Printer](Mobility) with the status installed


### Print to Papercut

- Print a document from any application
- Select the printer destination FindME[Printer](Mobility)
- Note: you may need to select ‘see more’

- Enter the appropriate settings and click print
