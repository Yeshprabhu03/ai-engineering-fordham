# Scan & Fax

## Overview

Faculty, Staff, and students can use the Canon devices to scan to their email, Google drive, OneDrive, or send as a FAX.

## Scan to your email

- Place documents face-up in the feeder or facedown on the glass.
- Tap your Fordham ID over the card reader or log in with your Fordham username and password
- Tap the
**Scan**icon - From the main scan screen, touch
**Scan to My Email** - You can modify the file name and subject line here, or, if necessary, apply different scan settings.
- Touch
**Start** - To add pages to your current document, touch
**Scan new page**. - To start scanning a new document, touch
**Scan new document**. - Touch
**Finish**to send the document. - Touch the logout button on the top/right to end your session

## Scan Settings

You can modify any of the following scan settings prior to sending the document to your selected destination such as OneDrive, Google Drive, or your email:

- Duplex Mode: Choose whether your original document is 1-sided or 2-sided.
- File type: Specify the document type you are scanning (e.g., PDF, TIFF, JPEG).
- Orientation: Select the orientation for displaying your scanned document: portrait or landscape.
- DPI: Select the resolution for your scanned document. The default setting is 300 dpi. NOTE: A document scanned at 600 dpi will take longer to process.
- Paper size: Select your document size.
- Color mode: Change the color settings of your document.
