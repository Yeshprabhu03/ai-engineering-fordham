# Advising Notes

**For Advisors**

## Overview

The Student’s Case Record offers the ability to add Notes that you may have after appointments with students. After posting a note, it is also easy to edit or delete it.

## Viewing Notes

- On the center portion of the Advising Case, open the Notes tab by clicking on
**Notes**. - Scroll down to see list of all advising notes
- Click on the
**note name (N-number)**to view the note

## Add a New Advising Note (ad-hoc)

To add a note on a student that is NOT part of an appointment:

- On the
**Advising Case**, open the**Notes**tab - Enter S
**ubject**, select**Sharing Policy**, and enter the body of the note - Click
**Submit**

**Note Sharing Policy**

**Private**- Only you will have access to view the note.

**Shared**- Shared notes automatically share it with everyone who has access to the notes for the primary school of the advisee. For example, if the student’s primary program is in the Gabelli School of Business, every note on the student’s advisee case record will be shared with all advisors and staff with access to notes for the Gabelli School of Business.

**Public**- This will make the Advising Note visible to all users with access to Advising Notes.


## Add a Note to an Appointment

If you want to add notes for a specific appointment to the Advisee’s record, you can add an Advising Note to the appointment record and it will automatically link to the student’s advising notes.

- From the calendar view of the SSH Appointment Manager, click into a scheduled appointment to bring up the Appointment Details and Advisee Snapshot.
- In the Appointment Details, select
**View Appointment.** - Once in the appointment record, the right panel should prompt you with a screen to add a new note. Fill in the Subject and Body of Note and click
**Submit**.
