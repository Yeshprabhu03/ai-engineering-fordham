# Back to School Information

**For Students**

## Overview

Fordham Information Technology is dedicated to assisting all students with their transition into the new semester.

Need help in your residence hall, for school, or off campus? The IT Team assists students at the beginning of the semester. All semester long, you can count on the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/) to provide high-quality technology support.


## Student Guide To Technology
