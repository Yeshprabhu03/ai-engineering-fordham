# Academic Computing Intern

**Department:** Cloud Agile Systems Management and Instructional Technology/Academic Computing (These groups support the Academic Computing Environment (ACE). ACE is a digital space for faculty to conduct research and experiment with innovative technology projects.)

**Position:** Academic Computing Intern

**Supervisor:** Head of Cloud and Agile Systems Management and Technical Manager-Academic Computing

**Qualifications**

- Must have 15-19 hours of the week available
- Must be a full-time Fordham undergraduate student in good academic standing
- Self-starter who can work with minimal supervision

**Responsibilities**

- Testing on various web, digital humanities and software systems
- Research and implement new technology
- Communicate with university personnel regarding ACE environment
- Develop scripts to manager ACE environment

**Technical Knowledge**

- Competent with Windows Office and Google Applications
- Basic knowledge of Windows and Linux based operating systems
- Knowledge of Java, Google Script, Bash, Powershell
- Knowledge of WordPress frontend

**Compensation:** $18.00 per hour
