# Acquisitions Assistant | RH

**DIVISION:** Student Technology Services

**POSITION:** Acquisitions Assistant

**SUPERVISOR:** Joe Amador, IT Acquisitions & Software Assets Manager

**SUMMARY**

The Acquisitions Assistant will help in the collection of data about new equipment that is delivered across campus and enter the information into the IT database. Data collected includes when the equipment is delivered, the campus, building location, and end user name. Will also assist in configuring new acquired equipment for deployment and the retirement of replaced equipment.

**QUALIFICATIONS**

- Must be a registered Fordham University student in good standing
- Excellent communication skills
- Must be detail oriented

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

- The individual will help maintain the ServiceNow database
- Enter and update tickets as needed

**TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE** (Training provided for ServiceNow)

- Proficient with Windows and Mac OS, MS Word, Excel

**COMPENSATION:** $17.00 per hour
