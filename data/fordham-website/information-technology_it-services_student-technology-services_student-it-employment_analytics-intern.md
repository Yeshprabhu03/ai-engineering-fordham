# Fordham Analytics Intern

**For Students**

**DIVISION:** Fordham Analytics

**POSITION:** Fordham Analytics Intern

**SUPERVISOR:** Director of Fordham Analytics

**SUMMARY**

Are you...

Passionate: About swimming in data? Do you want to help convert mountains of data into meaningful information? Do you have a passion for data and information design?

Highly Technical: Have you taken a statistical analysis class? Do you know how to query a relational database?

A Team Player: Would you like to be part of a team with diverse industry experience and 20+ combined years of experience in the most strategic technology on CIO radars?

**QUALIFICATIONS**

- Have 15-19 hours a week available
- Be a full-time Fordham student in good academic standing
- Reliable, quick learner, who can work with minimal supervision
- Uphold, enforce and abide by all university policies

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

- Research and consolidate findings for analysis
- Solid communication and writing skills needed
- Other duties, as assigned

**TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE**

- Familiarity with Microsoft Power BI

- We are happy to provide up to two weeks for training and ramp-up - Strong sense of design and user interface best practices

**COMPENSATION:** $18.00 per hour

**APPLY**[Apply to this job](http://fordham.edu/itjobsapp). You will be asked to attach your resume to the job application.
