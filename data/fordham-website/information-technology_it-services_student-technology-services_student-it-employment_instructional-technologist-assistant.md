# Instructional Technologist Assistant | LC

**DIVISION:** Faculty Technology Services

**POSITION:** Instructional Technologist Assistant

**SUPERVISOR:** Senior Instructional Technologist

**SUMMARY**

We are looking for a part-time student worker to help in the Faculty Technology Center at Lincoln Center, with the main responsibility of creating and editing content in the areas of faculty development, emerging technology, and digital citizenship. The candidate needs to have a genuine interest in learning and technology, have excellent written and oral communication skills, and have some experience with design and media creation. Training will be provided.

**QUALIFICATIONS**

- Must be a registered Fordham University student in good standing
- Graphic Design and Media Creation background and/or experience
- Excellent communication and organizational skills
- Strong interpersonal skills
- Strong presentation and training skills
- Experience with graduate-level coursework or academic support
- Genuine interest in technology; stays informed of the latest tech advances

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

- Assist with Blackboard day-to-day work.
- Create and update online course materials, ensuring accessibility and user-friendly design.
- Support analytics and website maintenance.
- Create/format PowerPoint presentations with visuals/animations.
- Create/edit images, videos, flyers, infographics, and emails.
- Assist in managing faculty workshops and events.
- Research academic websites for ideas and best practices.
- Interact and communicate with peers for collaborative ideas.
- Assist in the development and execution of surveys to gather feedback on workshops and events.
- Participate in webinars or training sessions to enhance skills related to Blackboard and analytics.
- Provide technical support to faculty and students using Blackboard and other online tools

**DESIRED CHARACTERISTICS**

- Excellent customer service and written/oral communication skills.
- Willingness to take on tasks without constant supervision and proactive in seeking additional responsibilities.
- Honesty, integrity, and a professional work ethic.
- Maintains a positive attitude towards colleagues and tasks.
- Willingness to learn and seize development opportunities presented.
- Initiative and proactive thinking toward program improvement.
- Ability to finish projects and tasks on strict deadlines.
- Effective time management: Efficient use of time and prioritization to meet deadlines.
- Team player with a collaborative attitude.
- Critical thinking ability to find solutions to challenges.
- Relevant skills and proficiency in tools/software related to the job.
- Customer service (if applicable): A friendly and helpful demeanor when interacting with faculty.
- Demonstrates a professional attitude and behavior in the workplace.
- Punctuality: Consistently arrives on time for work and meetings.
- Respectful: Treats colleagues, supervisors, and others with respect.

**TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE** (Training Provided)

- Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, PowerPoint)
- Proficient in Google Suite (Google Docs, Sheets, and Slides)
- Web development skills (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP)
- Graphic design proficiency (Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign)
- IT troubleshooting and technical support expertise
- Familiarity with audio/image/video formats and compression settings
- Basic knowledge of Photoshop/Illustrator (or similar software) required

**COMPENSATION:** $17.00 per hour
