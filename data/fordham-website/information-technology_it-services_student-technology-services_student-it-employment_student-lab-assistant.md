# Student Lab Assistant | Westchester

**DIVISION:** Instructional Technology Academic Computing (ITAC). ITAC is a division of IT and manages the Computer Teaching Labs and the Public Computer Labs on all three campuses.

**POSITION:** Student Lab Assistant (OPEN FOR **WESTCHESTER** ONLY)

**SUPERVISOR:** Robert Blanchette

**QUALIFICATIONS**

- All students assigned to work at the Computer Teaching Labs are required to have knowledge of Windows and OSX
- Computer Lab Assistants must be registered Fordham students in good academic standing during the semester of their appointment
- Students are required to use Fordham University email system for all work related communication

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

- Daily checks to ensure that all computing equipment is fully operational at all times
- Ensure that there is NO eating or drinking in the Labs
- Ensure that all software programs and applications (in particular, the University's standardized software programs and applications) are available and operational
- Assist public computing users who request help in using either hardware or software installed on the Center's computers
- Provide informational assistance to queries about other IT services, especially ITAC's
- Distribute users' print jobs
- Ensure printer supplies (paper, toner, etc.) are in stock
- Other tasks as assigned by the Lab supervisor

**TECHNICAL KNOWLEDGE**

- Must have basic working knowledge of the Pharos print system
- Must have basic working knowledge of the products such as Blackboard and Office 2010

**COMPENSATION:** $17.00 per hour
