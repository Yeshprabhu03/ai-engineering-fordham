# Tech Kiosks

**For Students**

## Overview

Our Tech kiosks at the Rose Hill and Lincoln Center campuses provide students the ability to borrow a laptop for up to four hours for free. To borrow a laptop, visit a kiosk and follow the on-screen instructions.

Rose Hill Campus

- Kiosk location: McShane Student Center
- Devices: 24 laptops (Mac & Dell) and 12 Portable 110 V Chargers

Lincoln Center Campus

- Kiosk location: Lowenstein Hall, SL Southwest Corridor near the bookstore and ATM
- Available Devices: 12 Laptops (Mac & Dell)

## Borrowing Guidelines

Once checked out, students are responsible for laptops.

- Users are responsible for the laptop and all its components and accessories while on loan.
- Never leave the laptop unattended.
- Users should not save any work on the laptop. Don't save data directly to the laptops, they are erased periodically. Instead, you may upload your work to cloud storage (e.g. Google Drive, OneDrive) or external storage (e.g. USB flash drive).
- Laptops are routinely wiped clean.
- Anti-virus software is installed on all laptops; the university is not responsible for any computer viruses that may be transferred during use.
- Do not rely on the laptop clock for the correct time.
- Keep all food and drink away from the laptop computer.
- Laptops must be returned to the same kiosk that it was checked out from. It can be returned to any slot on the kiosk from which they were checked out.
- There is no charge for borrowing a Laptop if the Laptop is returned within 4 hours. If you return the laptop after 4 hours you will be charged a late fee.
- You can only borrow one laptop at a time.
- You can only borrow 2 laptops maximum within a 24-hour period. If you borrow and return a laptop, you must wait 2 hours until you are allowed to borrow another.

If you return the laptop after the 4-hour rental period, you will have a 10-minute grace period for which you will only be charged a $5 late fee. If the laptop is not returned within the grace period, you will be charged $40 per hour. If the laptop is not returned within 4 days, you will be charged the full replacement cost of the laptop, and this will be billed to your student account.

Summary of fees:

- Grace period (up to 10 minutes): $5 late fee
- After 10 minutes late: $40 per hour
- After 4 days: Full replacement cost of the laptop

Return the laptop as soon as you realize there is a problem with it. Please choose the displayed option that most closely describes the problem you had with the laptop. Then check out another laptop if available.

-
You are responsible for the loss or damage up to the total cost of the laptop per the Loaner Laptop Terms of Use you accepted when borrowing a laptop. If the loss is due to a theft, please report it to IT and Public Safety.

A laptop cannot be borrowed while it is in charging mode, if it was recently returned, or if it is damaged.

- Charging laptops will show up as yellow on the kiosk screen.
- To have a good experience with the device, and to provide you with a clean device, the kiosk performs functions such as deleting the previous user’s data and recharging the battery once a laptop is returned. This takes less than 15 minutes and during that time the kiosk device cannot be borrowed.
- Damaged devices will show up as yellow with a wrench icon. Damaged devices will be repaired and replaced as soon as possible.

-
Laptops are wiped clean of data to protect user’s privacy after each use, and any data on the laptops will no longer be available. You must copy and save your data on an external device, or cloud account before returning the laptop.

-
We recommend that you use an external storage device and save your work frequently that way. If the laptop reboots or runs out of power anytime during the allotted borrowing period all data is lost.

-
Laptop checkouts cannot be renewed. After you return the laptop to the kiosk, the kiosk performs functions such as deleting the previous user’s data and recharging the battery. This takes at least 15 minutes.
