# Technology Support

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

**The IT Service Desk serves as a central point of contact for all technology-related questions and concerns. With a focus on prompt and effective solutions, the service desk team offers expertise in troubleshooting, technical guidance, and problem resolution. The team provides comprehensive support and assistance encompassing a wide range of technology resources and services, contributing to a seamless and efficient technology experience for users across the university.**

The service desk support team is dedicated to ensuring the smooth operation of technology resources and service. Their expertise extends to resolving technical issues, offering guidance on software and hardware, assisting with account management, and providing a knowledgeable response to a diverse array of technology-related inquiries. In addition to their technical skills, the service desk support team maintains a customer-centric approach. They prioritize effective communication, attentively listening to user concerns and inquiries. By offering personalized solutions and step-by-step guidance, they empower users to navigate technology challenges with confidence. Let us know how we can be of assistance to you!

## Tech Help Service Portal

**Explore the Tech Help portal to utilize the self-service option, where you can search our knowledge base for solutions to your inquiry, or alternatively submit a support ticket for more personalized assistance. The Tech Help portal can be accessed at any time via fordham.edu > My Pages > Tech Help.**
