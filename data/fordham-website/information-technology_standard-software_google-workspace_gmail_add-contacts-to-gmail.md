# Add Contacts to Gmail

**For Students, Faculty, Staff, Guests, Alumni**

## Overview

Add contacts to your Gmail address book to ensure that critical messages from the University do not end up in your Spam folder.

### Desktop: Add Contacts

- In your browser, navigate to
[Google Contacts](https://contacts.google.com/)([contacts.google.com/](https://contacts.google.com/)) - Click Create contact on the upper left
- Enter the contact's information. Important Fordham email addresses:
- Click Save

### Android: Add Contacts

- Open the Settings app on your Android phone.
- Find and tap Accounts or Users & Accounts.
- Tap Google or Add account, then select Google.
- Sign in to your Google account using your Gmail address and password.
- Ensure the Contacts sync option is turned on.
- Your contacts will automatically sync from your Google account to your phone.

### iPhone: Add Contacts

- Open the Settings app on your iPhone.
- Scroll down and tap Contacts.
- Tap Accounts, then Add Account.
- Tap Google and sign in to your Google account.
- Once signed in, ensure the Contacts toggle is switched on.
- Tap Save to finish syncing. Your Google contacts will now appear in your iPhone's Contacts app.

### Unmark an email as Spam

Refer to Google's help page to [unmark an email as Spam on your computer, Android, or iPhone](https://support.google.com/mail/answer/1366858?co=GENIE.Platform%3DAndroid&hl=en).
