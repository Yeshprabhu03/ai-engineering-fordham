# Microsoft Office

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

All active Fordham students and employees are eligible to receive a free full version of Microsoft Office 365, which includes Word, Excel, PowerPoint and other Office apps.

## Connect

To initiate access or to login, go to fordham.edu > My Apps > Applications > Microsoft Office 365. To sign up, follow the prompts.

For installs on mobile devices, follow the links for [Apple devices](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Install-and-set-up-Office-on-an-iPhone-or-iPad-9df6d10c-7281-4671-8666-6ca8e339b628) and [Android devices](https://support.office.com/en-us/article/Install-and-set-up-Office-on-an-Android-cafe9d6f-8b0c-4b03-b20a-12438a82a22d).

**Do not download Microsoft Office resources to any University-owned device.**

If you have questions, contact the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).
