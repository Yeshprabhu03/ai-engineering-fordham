# Plagiarism Prevention/Academic Integrity

**For Students, Faculty**

## Overview

Blackboard allows instructors to insert plagiarism detection software directly into assignments given to students. By designing assignments with plagiarism detection built-in, students are often deterred from purchasing papers or copying unattributed material into their submissions. Our experience has been that creative assignments that are centered around studentsâ€™ unique perspective, analysis, and critical thinking, encourage original (and properly attributed) student submissions.

## SafeAssign

A Blackboard-owned plugin that is available whenever a new Blackboard assignment is created. SafeAssign identifies whether a paper contains a high percentage of unoriginal material. It also helps identify the original source material. [Learn about creating SafeAssign assignments](https://help.blackboard.com/SafeAssign/Instructor/In_Your_Course#ultra_use_safeassign).
