# Speechify

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

Speechify transforms your digital content into high-quality audio, offering a powerful tool that makes information more accessible and learning more flexible. Whether you're a student reviewing course materials on the go, a professor preparing lectures, or a staff member managing multiple documents, Speechify converts Word documents, PDFs, webpages, and even scanned materials into clear, natural-sounding audio.

Key features:

- Converts various formats (PDFs, websites, ebooks, and printed materials) into clear audio using OCR technology
- Enhances focus with real-time text highlighting and synchronized audio playback
- Offers comprehensive study tools including annotations, bookmarks, and highlight extraction for note-taking
- Provides customizable voices, languages, and reading speeds to suit individual preferences
- Works seamlessly across devices through mobile apps, browser extensions, and desktop applications

## Availability

Available to all current students, faculty, and staff through our university site license, Speechify is provided by the [Office of Information Technology](https://www.fordham.edu/information-technology/) as part of our commitment to creating an inclusive and technologically advanced learning environment. This initiative was implemented based on the recommendation of the [Fordham Accessibility Committee](https://www.fordham.edu/resources/accessibility-at-fordham/) and joins other leading institutions like USC, Stanford, Barnard, and Columbia in providing this essential learning tool.

## Accessibility and Benefits

While Speechify serves as an essential accessibility tool for individuals with reading difficulties, visual impairments, or learning differences, its versatility benefits everyone in our academic community. Listen to academic papers while commuting, multitask during document review, or enhance reading comprehension through multi-sensory engagement.

Speechify particularly supports:

- Students with print disabilities such as dyslexia
- Individuals with visual impairments
- Those with physical disabilities that affect reading
- Anyone who benefits from audio-learning formats

## Notes

- Using your Fordham email address allows you to use Speechify for free via this license as long as your Fordham email address is valid and you are a currently active student.
