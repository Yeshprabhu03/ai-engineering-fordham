# Survey Software

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

[The Qualtrics Research Suite](https://www.qualtrics.com/), a web-based survey tool, is a robust and professional survey system, designed for academic and business research. Easily design and implement surveys, evaluate the results.

## Get connected

Log in at fordham.qualtrics.com with your Fordham username and password. Or, log into fordham.edu > My Apps > Applications > [Qualtrics](https://fordham.co1.qualtrics.com/homepage/ui).

### New users

First time users must create an account. When you log in to [fordham.qualtrics.com](https://fordham.qualtrics.com/) with your Fordham username and password, select “I don’t have a Qualtrics account,” and new account will be created for you. For an overview of the software, please review the [Getting Started](https://www.qualtrics.com/university/researchsuite/basic-building/getting-started/) tutorials.

### Returning users

If you have not logged into your Fordham Qualtrics account since the application was linked the University's single sign-on system, you'll need to create a new account that merges with your former one in order to retain your old files. Log in to [fordham.qualtrics.com](https://fordham.qualtrics.com/) with your Fordham username and password and click “I already have a Qualtrics account.” At the prompt, enter your old username and password and click “Migrate Account.” A new account will then be created. If you do not remember your old username and password for the migration, please contact the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).

## Support

[View a full list of Qualtrics support topics](https://www.qualtrics.com/support/survey-platform/getting-started/qualtrics-topics-a-z/).

Qualtrics is an internet-based software that works on PCs & Macs and is supported on Firefox, Safari, and Chrome.

Qualtrics accepts data from other survey services, such as Survey Monkey. [The instructions here](https://www.qualtrics.com/support/survey-platform/data-and-analysis-module/data/import-responses/) detail how to download the data as a .csv file, edit the format, and import.

Tutorials, webinars and help documentation are available from Qualtrics as part of [Qualtrics University](https://www.qualtrics.com/university/researchsuite/).

Fordham’s Qualtrics license includes both email and phone support. If you have questions about how to use Qualtrics or experience technical difficulties, please contact Qualtrics directly by calling 1(800) 340-9194 (recommended) or emailing [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#52212722223d2026122327333e26203b31217c313d3f). Help is available 24/7 via Qualtrics.

If you need assistance logging in, sharing surveys, creating and managing groups, or changing survey ownership, please contact the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).
