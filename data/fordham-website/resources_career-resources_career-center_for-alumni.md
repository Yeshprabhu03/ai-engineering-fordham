# Career Center Info for Alumni

The Fordham Career Center is committed to meeting the needs of our Fordham alumni. We strive to support alumni seeking or considering a new job, looking to hire Fordham talent, and giving back to current Fordham students.

### Career support

Whether you are a recent graduate looking for your first job or an experienced alumni ready to make a career change, the Fordham Career Center can work with you to achieve your continuing career goals.[Join RamConnect to schedule an appointment today](https://ramconnect.fordham.edu/hub/fordham/advising/people) with Jerry Goldstein or John Moriarty.

### Be a mentor

Provide mentorship to Fordham students and alumni!

Register with RamConnect for networking, one-off questions, virtual meetings, and more.

Learn more about our organized mentorship programs, including the [Fordham Mentoring Program](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=457) and [Rams Helping Rams](/resources/career-resources/career-center/for-alumni/rams-helping-rams/).

### Stay involved

Attend an event to boost your professional development, or share your insight by participating in panels and presentations. Get involved digitally through blog and video alumni spotlights.

To learn more about upcoming workshops and events hosted by the Career Center, please visit the [Career Center Blog events calendar](https://careercenter.blog.fordham.edu/professional-development-events/). If you're interested in participating in Career Center programming or contributing to initiatives and resources, please [submit this form](https://forms.gle/dPi3X1zgHJEvT6KW7).
