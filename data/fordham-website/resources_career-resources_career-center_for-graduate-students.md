# Career Center Information for Graduate Students

The Career Center is happy to support graduate students during their career exploration and job application process.

We serve students in the Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, the Graduate School of Education, and the School of Professional and Continuing Studies - Graduate Programs.

More information about internships, job opportunities, or to make an appointment with a career counselor can be found on [Handshake](http://app.joinhandshake.com/).
