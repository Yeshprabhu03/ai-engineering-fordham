## Career Center services for GSE students

-
Handshake is the platform the Career Center uses where students can make career counseling appointments, view job and internship opportunities, see our upcoming events, and have access to a variety of resources.

-
Appointments are available in person, virtually, or by phone to help you with anything from career guidance and mock interviews to resume and cover letter review.

**Employer information sessions**

Information Sessions are a great way to learn more about schools and educational organizations, and network one on one with employers in a small group setting.**On-campus recruiting**

OCR takes place throughout the year. View the schedule, and apply for interesting jobs via[H](https://app.joinhandshake.com/)[andshake](http://app.joinhandshake.com/). Before participating in OCR, you are required to have your resume reviewed, either in person or via email.**Special events**

During the year, the Career Center and GSE also host special events with employers, students, and alumni. Events can include employer panels, alumni networking events, and roundtables.
