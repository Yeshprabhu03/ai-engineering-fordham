Prefer to chat? Get help from a Fordham or AJCU librarian. Service is available 24/7.

*Chat and text are not available on Thanksgiving Day, Christmas Day, New Year's Day, and Independence Day.*

### Text a Librarian

Use your cell phone to text a message to a Fordham librarian! Just text 71-TXTX-1284. Use Text a Librarian for simple, quick questions of up to 160 characters that can be answered in 320 characters or less. Use Live Online Reference Chat 24/7 for questions requiring lengthy or detailed answers.
