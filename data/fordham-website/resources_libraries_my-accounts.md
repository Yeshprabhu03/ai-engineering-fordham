# My Accounts

[Library Account](https://fdhm.ent.sirsi.net/client/en_US/default/search/patronlogin/http:$002f$002ffdhm.ent.sirsi.net$002fclient$002fen_US$002fdefault$002fsearch$002faccount$003f/)

Log into your library account to reserve books, renew current borrowed items, and view any outstanding balances

[ARES](https://fordham.ares.atlas-sys.com/ares/)

ARES is an electronic reserves platform that provides access to academic collections.

[ILLiad](https://fordham.illiad.oclc.org/illiad)

ILLiad is an Interlibrary loan service use to obtain items from non-Fordham libraries. To request a book or article through Interlibrary Loan, you must provide complete and accurate bibliographical information.

[EndNote](https://fordham.libguides.com/BibliographicManagement)

Fordham IT provides access to EndNote, a web and desktop client for bibliographic management. The Library staff has produced a LibGuide with details on installing and using EndNote, with links to training materials and videos, as well as information on alternative citation management tools.
