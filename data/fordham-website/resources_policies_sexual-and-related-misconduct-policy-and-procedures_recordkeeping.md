# Recordkeeping: Sexual and Related Misconduct Policy

The University will maintain the following materials for a period of seven years:

- Records of any actions, including any supportive measures, taken in response to a report or Formal Complaint under Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972.
- Investigative Records under Title IX of the Education Amendments of 1972, including any determination regarding responsibility;
- Any audio or audiovisual recording or transcript of any hearing;
- Any appeal and the outcome of the appeal;
- Any information regarding the Informal Resolution Process, including the outcome; and
- All materials used to train any Title IX Coordinators, investigators, decision-makers, and Informal Resolution facilitators.
