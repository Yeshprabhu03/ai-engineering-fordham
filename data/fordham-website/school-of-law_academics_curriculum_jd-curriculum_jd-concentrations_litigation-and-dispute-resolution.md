# Litigation and Dispute Resolution

## In the 21st-century legal marketplace, employers demand skilled litigators and creative problem solvers.

This Concentration helps you develop the skills to expertly resolve disputes both in and outside the courtroom.

Courses and faculty

Foundational and specialized courses compose the litigation and dispute resolution Concentration. You are also encouraged to take courses that strengthen your writing and research skills and to participate in relevant student-led extracurricular programs.

Please note that not all courses are offered every semester. Current students can log in to LawNET ([https://my.lawnet.fordham.edu/sites/jdconcentrations/SitePages/Litigation-and-Dispute-Resolution.aspx](https://my.lawnet.fordham.edu/sites/jdconcentrations/SitePages/Litigation-and-Dispute-Resolution.aspx)) for the most up-to-date course listing.
