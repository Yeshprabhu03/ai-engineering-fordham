# Fees

All students are required register for one 3-credit course. Course tuition is $4305. Students are also required to submit a $100 Application Fee. In addition, students are responsible for the cost of their airfare to and from Seoul; housing; health insurance and immunizations; meals and all other living expenses for the duration of the program. We estimate the total of these additional expenses to be in the range of $3000 to $4000.

For students participating in the optional second course, the tuition is $2870.

For students participating in the internship program, the 3-credit internship tuition is $2900 ($500 due on application; $2400 due May 1, 2026); the 2-credit internship tuition is $2100 ($500 due on application; $1600 due May 1, 2026); and non-credit internships require a $500 placement fee on application. Interns will also want to budget for extended housing, meals and other living expenses.

Please also note that the tuition and housing fees are subject to change. The final numbers, which will likely not vary significantly from what is listed here, will be announced by late January.

All payments should be made in USD. Program fees are non-refundable in the event that a student withdraws from the program; however, all fees will be refunded to students who are not admitted into the program, or in the unlikely event that the program is canceled.

## Late Fees

Full tuition and all optional fee payments are due by May 1, 2026. Those students who submit payment up to one week late (between May 2 and May 7, 2026) will be assessed a $100 late fee; up to two weeks late (between May 8 and May 14, 2026) a $200 late fee, and so on.

## Refunds

Students who withdraw from the program prior to May 1, 2026 will receive a full tuition and fees refund (minus the $100 non-refundable application fee) except as noted below. Students withdrawing between May 2 and May 15, 2026 will be charged an additional $150 withdrawal fee.

The internship placement fee from an applicant will be refunded only if the program fails to place the student in an internship by April 30, 2026. After May 15, 2026, no tuition or fees will be refunded.
