Take-home exams are administered exclusively through **exam4.com**. Exams will be available on the website beginning at the date and time listed above, unless a professor specifies a different opening time. Students will download a PDF version of the exam from **exam4.com**, complete their answers in a Word document, and upload the completed file directly to **exam4.com**.

All take-home exam answers must be uploaded to **exam4.com** by the closing date and time listed above or by the professor’s deadline — whichever comes first.


The Exam4 software should never be used for take-home exams. DO NOT use the Exam4 software under any circumstances for take-home exams.
