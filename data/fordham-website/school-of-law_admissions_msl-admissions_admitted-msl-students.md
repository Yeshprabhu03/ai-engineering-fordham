# Admitted M.S.L. Students

**Before accessing the portal, you must claim your account at claim.fordham.edu. Keep in mind that this portal is for in-person M.S.L. students only. Online M.S.L. admitted students should review this resource. **

### What can you do in the admitted student portal?

- Review important dates
- Connect with our student ambassadors
- Pay deposit
- Download enrollment forms
- Review our enrollment checklist
- Register for classes
- Review orientation details
- Learn housing options
- Check out our community, student news, and much more!
