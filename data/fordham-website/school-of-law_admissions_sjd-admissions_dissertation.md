# Dissertation

The S.J.D. program culminates in a candidate's dissertation. This final project is research-based and will consist of at least a 50,000-word book of publishable quality or three publishable articles of the same aggregate length. In consultation with their supervisors, candidates will determine which format to pursue.

### Committee

In consultation with his or her Dissertation Adviser, the candidate should form the Dissertation Committee. The Chair of the Committee is the Dissertation Adviser, and the Committee should consist of two additional members, at least one of whom must be a member of the full-time faculty of Fordham Law.

### Oral Defense

The Dissertation Defense will be open to the members of the Law School community and will consist of a 20-30 minute presentation of the central thesis by the candidate, followed by a period of questions by the members of the Dissertation Committee.

At the discretion of the Committee Chair, questions may also be raised by others in attendance.
