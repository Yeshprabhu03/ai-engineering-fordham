# Worldwide Chapters

Alumni chapters are a great way to network and connect with Fordham Law alumni in your area, as well as professors who visit through our Faculty on the Road program and CLE credit-qualifying lectures.

Is there a chapter in your area? If not, contact the [Alumni Relations Office](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#2c404d5b4d40594142456c404d5b024a435e48444d4102494859) to learn how you can start a new FLAA chapter.
