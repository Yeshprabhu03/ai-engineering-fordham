# Careers

## Careers

Our coordinated career support can help you define your professional path and help you get there.

**Supporting Your Career Journey**

Your Fordham Law degree is a gateway to a meaningful career, a life of service, and ennobled lawyering. Whether you want to lead in the law, business, politics, finance, government, public interest, academia, or another career field, your Fordham Law education will prepare you for success. The Law School’s coordinated career support can help you define your professional path, develop faculty connections, and tap into Fordham Law’s alumni network.
