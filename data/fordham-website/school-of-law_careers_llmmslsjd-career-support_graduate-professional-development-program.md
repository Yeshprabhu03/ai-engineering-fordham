# Graduate Professional Development Program

**Services include assistance with:**

- Building a U.S.-style resume and cover letter
- Networking techniques
- Interview preparation
- Online resources to help you get where you want to go

The GPDP also hosts a series of conversations with LL.M. alumni who share their insights on practice, networking, and making the most of your LL.M. and time in New York City.

Check out highlights from our [LL.M. Alumni Professionalism Series](https://www.fordham.edu/school-of-law/careers/llmmslsjd-career-support/graduate-professional-development-program/llm-alumni-and-friends-professionalism-series/). See also our [LL.M. Alumni Spotlights](https://web.archive.org/web/20221205002903/https://www.fordham.edu/info/20643/llm_admissions/11266/llm_alumni_spotlights).

Fordham Law is one of a select number of U.S. law schools that take part in the [International Student Interview Program (ISIP)](https://www.law.nyu.edu/isip), a major job fair for LL.M. students that takes place each year in New York City.
