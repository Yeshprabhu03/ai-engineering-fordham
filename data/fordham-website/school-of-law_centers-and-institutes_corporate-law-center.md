# Corporate Law Center

Supporting learning

The Center allows students to learn the fundamental principles of business law from leading scholars and prominent practitioners. Our business law faculty includes professionals who have been involved in high-level business transactions. Our adjunct professors work in prominent positions for large companies throughout the city and are as dedicated to teaching as they are passionate in their business career.

The 25th Annual A.A. Sommer, Jr. Lecture on Corporate, Securities and Financial Law featuring SEC Chairman Paul Atkins

October 7, 2025

On October 7, 2025, we celebrated the 25th anniversary of the Sommer Lecture by welcoming SEC Chairman Paul Atkins to deliver remarks. After a welcome by Dean Landau and Morgan Lewis partner Ben Indek, Chairman Atkins delivered a lecture regarding Wells submissions and the ways in which the Wells process could benefit from certain reforms. After the lecture, Chairman Atkins joined guests for a reception.
