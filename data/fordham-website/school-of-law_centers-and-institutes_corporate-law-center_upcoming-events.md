# Corporate Law Center Upcoming Events

View from Chambers: Texas Business Court

Join us for a timely discussion on the newly established Texas Business Court system and its potential impact on the national landscape of corporate litigation. Meet Judges Melissa Davis Andrews, Grant Dorfman, and Bill Whitehill and hear about their experience from the new frontier for business disputes.

3rd Annual M&A Workshop

Fireside Chat with Tipper X, Tom Hardin

Hear Tom Hardin's story as he discusses his new book, "Wired on Wall Street: The Rise and Fall of Tipper X, One of the FBI's Most Prolific Informants." Part financial crime thriller, part personal transformation story, hear how a young hedge fund analyst turned into one of the FBI's most prolific informants for the FBI's largest insider trading investigation in a generation.
