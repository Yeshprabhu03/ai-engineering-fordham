## Stein Alumni Networking Directory

We are excited to introduce you to the **Stein Alumni Networking Directory**, which includes over 200 Stein graduates organized by 23 public interest areas of practice. (**The Stein Scholars Directory** includes all students and graduates of the Stein Scholars Program, while the Networking Directory is a subset.) We encourage you to use the Networking Directory to contact Stein alums who are practicing in an area of interest to you or because you want to learn more about what they do or what steps they followed to get where they are today.

The nearly 500 Steins that constitute the alumni community are engaged in countless types of practice around the country and abroad, and the alumni community grows by 20 attorneys every year. It is a unique and robust resource and we look forward to doing our part to help you make the most of it!
