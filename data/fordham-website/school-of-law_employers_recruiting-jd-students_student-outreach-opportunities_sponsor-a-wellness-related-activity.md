# Sponsor a Wellness-Related Activity

The Law School's Office of Professionalism is organizing a number of wellness-related events during the Spring 2026 semester that include an opportunity for employer sponsorships:

**Developing Healthy Habits Workshop***Ensuring Respectful Treatment of Transgender Clients***De-Stress Fest**

The details of each are listed below, including relevant dates.

Developing Healthy Habits Workshop

In this workshop, participants will learn strategies for integrating healthy habits into their busy schedules, and they will be provided with tools to support mindfulness and well-being while managing the rigors of law school. This workshop will take place on March 5, 2026.

The fee to sponsor this programis $500. Employers interested in sponsoring this program should email Maya FitzGerald Gobes, Director of Professionalism ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)) no later than 1 p.m. on Monday, February 9, 2026, indicating their interest. In the event that multiple employers express an interest in sponsoring the activity, the Office of Professionalism will conduct a lottery to determine which employer will garner the sponsorship. All employers who expressed interest will receive an email by 5 p.m. on Friday, February 13, 2026, informing them whether they’ve been selected for this sponsorship.

Ensuring Respectful Treatment of Transgender Clients

In this training, students will learn proper terminology and best practices for working with transgender clients. Through a series of interactive case studies, students will have the opportunity to develop their interpersonal skills and cultivate the cultural sensitivity necessary for effective and respectful client engagement. This workshop will take place on March 31, 2026.

The fee to sponsor this program prizes is $500. Employers interested in sponsoring it should email Maya FitzGerald Gobes, Director of Professionalism ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)) no later than 1 p.m. on Monday, February 23, 2026, indicating their interest. In the event that multiple employers express an interest in sponsoring the activity, the Office of Professionalism will conduct a lottery to determine which employer will garner the sponsorship. All employers who expressed interest will receive an email by 5 p.m. on Friday, February 27, 2026, informing them whether they’ve been selected for this sponsorship.

De-Stress Fest

In advance of finals week this spring, the Office of Professionalism will be offering a variety of activities for students to participate in as an opportunity to “de-stress.” Activities will include meditation, art therapy, board games, and outdoor games (i.e. cornhole, kickball, etc.). Healthy, energy-boosting snacks will be provided to students. The De-Stress Fest will take place on April 22, 2026.

The fee to sponsor this event is $500. Employers interested in sponsoring the De-Stress Fest should email Maya FitzGerald Gobes, Director of Professionalism ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)) no later than 1 p.m. on Monday, March 23, 2026, indicating their interest. In the event that multiple employers express an interest in sponsoring the activity, the Office of Professionalism will conduct a lottery to determine which employer will garner the sponsorship. All employers who expressed interest will receive an email by 5 p.m. on Friday, March 27, 2026 , informing them whether they’ve been selected for this sponsorship.
