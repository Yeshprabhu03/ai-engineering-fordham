Experience

Danielle joined Fordham Law Library in November 2022 as the Head of Instructional Services. Before coming to Fordham, she spent more than 15 years as a public high school teacher and administrator. Danielle was a Sparer Fellow at Brooklyn Law School, and earned master’s degrees in library science and teaching at Queens College, as well as a certificate in educational leadership from Baruch College. She is admitted to practice law in New York. Danielle’s interests include special education and public interest law.
