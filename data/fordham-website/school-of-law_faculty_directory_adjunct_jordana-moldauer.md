# Jordana Moldauer

## Adjunct Professor of Law

-
Jordana Moldauer is Assistant General Counsel at IPG Mediabrands, a division of The Interpublic Group of Companies (IPG), a publicly traded advertising company. In her role, Jordana advises multiple advertising agencies on campaigns involving original, custom and branded content, influencers, sponsorships, and experiential components. She also negotiates agreements with all relevant parties to execute these campaigns. Prior to joining IPG, Jordana held various positions in the in-house legal departments of media and entertainment companies, including VICE Media Group and Sony Music Entertainment, where she advised on marketing, music, and other intellectual property issues, with a particular focus on digital media and content production matters. A native of Geneva, Switzerland, Jordana worked for two years for a Geneva-based law firm before moving to New York to pursue an LL.M. degree at Fordham Law School.
