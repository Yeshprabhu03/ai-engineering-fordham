# Kathleen Thompson

![Fordham Law School Adjunct Faculty Kathleen Thompson](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Kathleen-Thompson.jpg)

Adjunct Professor of Law

Kathleen is a Reference Librarian at the Fordham Law Library. She joined the library in May 2022. Kathleen received a J.D. from Fordham University School of Law. Immediately after graduation, she practiced law at Dewey & LeBoeuf LLP. She then received her MLS from Queens College. She previously served as an adjunct instructor at St. John’s University’s Tobin College of Business, where she taught Law in a Business Environment. Additionally, she practiced law at a regional litigation firm, Vigorito, Barker, Patterson, Nichols and Porter, LLP.
