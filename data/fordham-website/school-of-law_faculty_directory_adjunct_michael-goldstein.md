# Michael Goldstein, Esq.

![Adjunct Faculty](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Michael-H.-Goldstein_300px.jpg)

Adjunct Professor of Law

Biography

Michael H. Goldstein has more than forty years of experience in reinsurance and insurance coverage dispute resolution, including more than fifty arbitration hearings to award, trials, and appeals throughout the United States representing insurers, cedants, and reinsurers. Mr. Goldstein will begin teaching at his alma mater, Fordham Law School, in the Spring of 2025. He teaches Risky Business: Insurance Law and Practice.
