Ronald Blum, a partner at Manatt, Phelps & Phillips, LLP, represents clients in complex criminal and civil matters. He has special expertise in parallel proceedings in which clients face simultaneous criminal penalties and civil liability, and has extensive experience handling the issues that arise at the intersection of criminal and civil law, including questions concerning privileges and waiver of privileges, discovery and voluntary disclosure.

Mr. Blum regularly conducts internal corporate investigations, has tried numerous cases before juries, judges and arbitration panels, and has argued appeals before many appellate courts.
