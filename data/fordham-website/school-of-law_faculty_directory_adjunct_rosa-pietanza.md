# Rosa Pietanza

![Adjunct Faculty Fordham Law Rosa Pietanza](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Rosa-Pietanza.jpg)

Adjunct Professor of Law

Biography

Rosa Pietanza is an Adjunct Professor at Fordham Law and has taught Commercial Drafting and Negotiating for LLM students over several semesters. Rosa is Senior Director and Counsel, Energy Markets and Regulatory, at Brookfield Renewable. Prior to joining Brookfield in 2019, Rosa worked at several financial institutions focusing on commodities physical and financial transactions. Rosa began her legal career in an Energy boutique law firm after receiving her J.D. degree from Fordham University School of Law.
