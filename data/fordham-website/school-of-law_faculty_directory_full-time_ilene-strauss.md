# Ilene Strauss

![Ilene Strauss Faculty Image.](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Ilene-Strauss.jpg)

## Director of Writing and Lawyering Programs; Clinical Associate Professor of Law

-
Ilene Strauss is a Clinical Associate Professor of Law and the faculty director of the Law School's Legal Writing and Lawyering Skills programs. Ilene teaches introductory and advanced writing and lawyering classes to a broad range of audiences. She is also a founding and supervising faculty member in the Paralegal Pathways Initiative, a program that aims to amplify the voices of formerly incarcerated people by providing them with skill-based legal training.
