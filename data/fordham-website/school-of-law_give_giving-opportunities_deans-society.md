# Dean's Society at Fordham Law

You are invited to demonstrate your commitment to Fordham Law and the Dean’s priorities by joining the Dean’s Society. The Dean’s Society recognizes the School’s most loyal supporters—alumni and friends who make annual gifts at leadership levels.

This distinguished group of donors provides the Dean with critical resources to address immediate needs such as funding for new programs, support for comprehensive learning experiences, and financial aid for exceptional students.

When you ** join the Dean’s Society**, you display your pride in the past, present, and future of Fordham Law School. Your gift of $2500 or more ($1000 for those graduated 10 years ago or fewer) provides you

- Access to exclusive Dean’s Society events
- Special updates from the Dean
- The opportunity to network with fellow Dean’s Society members
