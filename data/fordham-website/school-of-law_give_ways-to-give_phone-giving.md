# Phone Giving

**The quickest method to make a gift is online through our secure website.**

**Contact us at 212-636-6529** **to make your gift by phone.**

Please have the following info ready:

- Donor information (name, class year, address, phone, email, etc.)
- Gift or pledge information (amount and gift designation)
- Credit card type (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover)
- Credit card information (name as it appears on card, expiration date, CVV)
- Billing address


Our team is available Monday through Friday, from 9:00 am to 5:00 pm Eastern Time.
