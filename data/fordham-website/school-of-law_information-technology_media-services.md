# Fordham Law Media Services

Fordham Law Media Services team is responsible for the planning, upkeep, and support for all audio-visual spaces in the Law School, including classrooms, conference rooms, and event spaces. The Media Services Team coordinates with the Office of Public Programs and CLE to support customers hosting virtual events and utilizing event spaces throughout the building on a first-come, first-served basis.

## Fees

Any additional fees are assessed on a case-by-case basis depending on the request, circumstances, and availability of audiovisual equipment services.

### Late Requests

Media Services audiovisual assistance **must** **be requested at least two weeks before the day of the event**; a month before is **preferred**.

- Requests made less than two weeks before the event are not guaranteed, but if approved and provided, the sponsor should expect to pay an additional 25% surcharge of the total costs.
- Requests made on the day of or the day before the event are not guaranteed, but if approved and provided, the sponsor should expect to pay an additional 50% surcharge of the total costs.
- The Fordham Law Media Services Team reserves the right to deny last-minute audiovisual requests, add-ons, or changes.

### Off-hours

Audiovisual support requests outside working hours may result in a charge per hour per technician for setups or breakdowns. If the request causes the team to pay overtime, the customer will be assessed an additional fee to support the event.

### Damage, Lost, Missing Items or Equipment

Additional fees will be assessed for replacing or repairing damaged, lost, or misplaced equipment.
