# Law Diversity Faculty Directory

## Fordham Law professors represent the diverse citizenry that exists in New York City and throughout the United States.

This directory highlights faculty members from underrepresented racial, ethnic, and LGBTQ groups in the legal profession and academia who help to create the intellectually stimulating environment students enjoy at Fordham Law.
