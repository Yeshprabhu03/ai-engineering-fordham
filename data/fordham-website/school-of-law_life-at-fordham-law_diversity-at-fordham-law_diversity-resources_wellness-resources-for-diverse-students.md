# Wellness Resources for Diverse Students

![dei collaboration hands](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/DEI_Wellness_Page_Photo_1800x865.jpg)


The Office of Student Affairs is committed to the well-being and emotional health of our community of diverse students. Our goal is to support the growth of our student's intellectual, emotional and social development. Through the resources listed below, we hope that students feel free to explore their identities, personal experiences, beliefs, and more.
