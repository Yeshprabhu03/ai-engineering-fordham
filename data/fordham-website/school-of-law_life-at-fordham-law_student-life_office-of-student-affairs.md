# School of Law Office of Student Affairs

The Office of Student Affairs is located in Suite 4-101 on the Fourth Floor of the Law School. The office is open Monday to Friday from 9:30am to 5:30pm. It is staffed by a team of attorneys, all of whom have practiced and who support the development of students as attorneys-in-training.

We are responsible for enhancing the quality of life of students and accomplishes this goal by supporting student groups, providing academic advisement, academic assistance and personal counseling. Student Affairs also provides faculty, staff and administrators and alumni with necessary services and data to fulfill the academic mission of the Law School.
