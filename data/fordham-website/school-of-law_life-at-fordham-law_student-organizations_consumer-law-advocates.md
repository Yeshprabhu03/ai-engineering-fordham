# Consumer Law Advocates

## Consumer Law Advocates (CLA)

Consumer Law Advocates (CLA) is chapter of the [Consumer Law Advocates, Scholars & Students (CLASS) Network](https://consumerlaw.berkeley.edu/projects-and-programs/consumer-law-advocates-scholars-students-class-network)--an innovative, nationwide initiative that seeks to foster the next generation of lawyers working for consumer protection and economic justice. It raises awareness about the legal issues surrounding the field of economic justice and works with the [Feerick Center for Social Justice](https://www.fordham.edu/school-of-law/centers-and-institutes/feerick-center-for-social-justice/) to recruit, train, and support student volunteers and researchers to get involved in economic justice projects.
