# Environmental Law Advocates

## ELA

Environmental Law Advocates (ELA) is a community of Fordham Law students interested in learning about environmental law, advocating for environmental protection, and promoting environmental justice. ELA seeks to connect students and practitioners in the field of environmental law and provide educational, recreational, and advocacy opportunities both on and off campus.
