# Fordham International Law Association

About FILA

Fordham International Law Association (FILA) is a student-led organization of international law enthusiasts at Fordham Law School. Its mission is to unite students, alumni, faculty, and legal practitioners who share a passion for international law. Through a variety of educational initiatives, engaging events, and networking opportunities, FILA fosters a deep understanding and active involvement in pressing international legal issues.
