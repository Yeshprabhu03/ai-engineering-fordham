# Muslim Law Students Association

## MLSA

The Muslim Law Students Association is dedicated to exploring Islamic legal issues and bringing a greater understanding of Islam and the legal profession to all members of Fordham Law School and the community. The MLSA seeks to provide for the cultural and religious needs of Muslim law students and promotes the participation of all Fordham students through a diverse program of activities, including lectures, panel discussions, and halaqas, informal forums open to all students and faculty to discuss issues of relevance to Islam and Muslims, including religion, current events, politics, and the law.
