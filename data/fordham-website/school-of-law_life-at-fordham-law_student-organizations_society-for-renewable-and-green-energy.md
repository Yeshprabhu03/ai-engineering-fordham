# Society for Renewable and Green Energy

## SRGE

The purpose of this organization is to expose Fordham Law students to career opportunities and insights into the renewable and clean energy industries, as well as to expand Fordham Lawâ€™s presence within the renewable energy professional community.

The objectives of SRGE shall be:

- Identify growing trends in the renewable energy industry that students can build professional knowledge of;
- To facilitate interactions between students and legal professionals in the renewable energy industry;
- To demonstrate the increasing importance of involving legal professionals in an energy transition, in the context of climate change and broader environmental issues;
