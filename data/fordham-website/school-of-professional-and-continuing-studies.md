# School of Professional and Continuing Studies

## Why Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies?

### Our wide-ranging professional and continuing studies programs won’t just shape your career – they’ll offer a transformative experience.

![PCS Veteran in Keating Hall](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/PCS_Veteran_in_Keating_Hall___MD.jpg)

Fordham University’s School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS) is dedicated to meeting the academic interests and practical needs of working adults. By delivering a prestigious Fordham University education at a competitive, affordable price, we bring college graduation and professional dreams to life.

We offer [Bachelor’s degrees](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/undergraduate-degree-programs/), [master’s degrees](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/graduate-programs/), [professional certificates](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/continuing-education/), and a [pre-med program](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/post-baccalaureate-pre-medicalpre-health-program/), designed for the working professional.

- Evening, weekend, and online classes are available at
[three campus locations](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/about/), the Bronx, Lincoln Center, and Westchester. - Ranked as a top Military-Friendly Yellow Ribbon University by
*Military Times.*We offer unlimited Yellow Ribbon seats to veterans. - Classes have 10 to 20 students, on average, so you’ll know your teachers and classmates and receive the support you need.
- Flexible transfer credit opportunities for our undergraduate programs: We accept up to 75 credits and offer multiple ways to receive credits including life experience.
