# Contact PCS

Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS) provides unmatched opportunities for adult learners. Achieve your academic and career goals while balancing work and family responsibilities.

Whether completing a bachelor’s degree or just getting started, students find both intellectual breadth and rigor at Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies .

**The Fordham Experience Means:**

- Exciting and challenging opportunities. More than 30 majors to choose from.
- Convenience. Courses are available at all three campuses—Lincoln Center in Manhattan, Rose Hill in the Bronx and Westchester in West Harrison; on days, evenings and weekends—and some courses can be taken online.
- A unique standard of intellectual inquiry, ethical decision-making and academic excellence.
- Full participation in the Yellow Ribbon Program. Fordham received "Best for Vets" 2016 recognition from Military Times' EDGE magazine.

Since 1841, the Fordham degree has been valued by many of the world’s most prestigious companies, at which thousands of our graduates pursue successful careers.
