# College at 60 Tuition and Refund Policy

**Tuition**

The tuition in the College at 60 program is $480 for 12 week courses and $240 for six week courses. The tuition charge includes all student fees. Some courses that include field trips may require an addition nominal fee (eg. theater tickets or museum entrance fees).

**Refund Policy**

12-week courses

No tuition refunds will be granted after **February 13**, regardless of class attendance. Students must contact the College at 60 office by close of business **Friday, February 13**, via email, to request to withdraw from the course and request a refund.

Six-week courses

No tuition refunds for the class Spanish and Cultures for Beginners, will be granted after **February 11**, regardless of class attendance. Students must contact the College at 60 office by close of business **Wednesday, February 11** via email, to request to withdraw from the course and request a refund. No tuition refunds for the class *Head, Heart, Hands: Public Speaking, Jesuit Education & Eloquentia Perfecta,* or for T*he Nature of Language: An Introduction* will be granted after **April 1.**
