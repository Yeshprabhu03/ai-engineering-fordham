# Human Resource Management Certification Instructor Bio

Eileen Hackett

MS, SHRM-SCP

HR Director

Artimus LLC and K&R Realty Management Inc.

Eileen Hackett. is an accomplished senior HR professional and facilitator currently serving as HR Director for Artimus LLC, a well-known and successful construction company located in NYC and K&R Realty Management Inc. a trusted residential Property Management company also headquartered in NYC.

Prior to joining Artimus, Eileen was the HR Director at ACA Financial Guaranty, a Bond Insurance company, and AVP of Organizational Development at Assured Guaranty, also a Bond Insurance company. Eileen has also held HR manager positions at HSBC and Long-Term Credit Bank of Japan and performed outplacement services as a Certified Associate at Lee Hecht Harrison on a per diem basis.

The variety of human resource roles has given Eileen experience in all the relevant HR competencies with an expertise in organizational development, employee relations, employee development, and strategic initiatives.

Eileen has instructed employees at all her firms in several areas including Anti-Harassment training, Performance Management, Interviewing, and New Manager training to name a few. She is a member of SHRM, HRNY, and has mentored HR professionals as part of HRNY as well as in her current firm.

Eileen has her SHRM-SCP and is Certified in both the MBTI Step I & II and 360-Degree Assessments. She also has a certificate in Career Counseling from NYU and is enrolled in the Executive Coaching Certification program at Coach U. She currently performs both career counseling and executive coaching. services in her private practice. Eileen has a MS in Counseling from Queens College and obtained a BS in Psychology with a minor in English from Marywood University.

Eileen likes to spend her time taking horse riding lessons, hiking, biking the BRP, cooking healthy recipes, entertaining, and playing with her rescue dog Molly!
