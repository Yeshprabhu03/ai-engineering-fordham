# Fordham Post-Baccalaureate Pre-Med/Pre-Health Admission Requirements

![](/media/home/schools/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/Post-Bacc.png)

Below are the admissions requirements for the Post-baccalaureate Program:

- candidates submit an online application for PCS admission as non-matriculated students
- a personal statement
- official transcripts for all post-secondary school coursework to be sent electronically to
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#fc8c9f8f95929a93bc9a938e98949d91d2999889). - an admission interview teleconference with the Director

The general requirement for consideration is a minimum 3.00 GPA.

Official transcripts can also be sent directly from the academic institution to:
