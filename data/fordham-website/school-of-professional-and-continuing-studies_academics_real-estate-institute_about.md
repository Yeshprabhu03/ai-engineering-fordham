# About the Real Estate Institute

The Fordham University Real Estate Institute (REI) offers a [Master of Science in Real Estate (MSRE)](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/master-of-science-in-real-estate/), a Master of Science in Real Estate Development, a Master of Science in Construction Management, a Master of Science in Hospitality, advanced graduate certificates in [real estate finance](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/advanced-certificate-in-real-estate-finance/), [real estate development](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/advanced-certificate-in-real-estate-development/), [real estate management](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/advanced-certificate-in-real-estate-management/), [construction management,](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/advanced-certificate-in-construction-management/) hotel operations, and hospitality investment and development, a [Bachelor of Science in Real Estate (BSRE)](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/bachelor-of-science-in-real-estate/) and a comprehensive array of [professional certificates](https://beta.fordham.edu/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/professional-certificates-and-continuing-education/) in real estate and construction online and at its Lincoln Center campus in Midtown Manhattan, NYC. The programs are developed and taught by leading industry practitioners and are centered on imparting real-world professional skill sets. Flexibility and convenience are program hallmarks as classes can be taken in-person, online, and at various paces.

## Our Mission and Vision

Fordham REI’s mission is to advance the professional development of the real estate and construction industry by offering a comprehensive array of educational programs, training, events, research and scholarly activities.

Fordham REI’s vision is to be a globally recognized leader in real estate and construction education known for the intellectual and professional accomplishments of its faculty and students, the quality and relevancy of its programs and its outreach and service to the real estate and construction industry and the community it serves.
