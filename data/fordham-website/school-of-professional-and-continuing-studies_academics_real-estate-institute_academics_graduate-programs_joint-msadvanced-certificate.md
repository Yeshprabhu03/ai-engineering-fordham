# Joint MS/Advanced Certificate

![Skyscraper](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Skyscraper___SM.jpg)

**Add an area of focus to your master's degree that can help you stand out to future employers.**

Further your academic and career objectives by **pursuing a master's degree and an advanced (graduate) certificate concentration simultaneously** from Fordham’s Real Estate Institute. Advanced Certificates in Real Estate Finance, Real Estate Development, Real Estate Management and Construction Management provide added specialized course work in their respective areas.

## Program Advantages

- Add a specialization to your master’s degree to showcase career-specific skills that meet your professional goals
- Save time and money due to credit sharing between an MS and an advanced certificate program
- Take courses in-person or online

**Joint Status Requirement**

- MS students must declare their intention to complete the additional advanced certificate before they are conferred the MSRE, the MSRED or the MSCM degree

**MSRE/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Finance**

Students who wish to receive both the Master of Science in Real Estate (MSRE) and the Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Finance may apply up to 9 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.

**MSRE/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Development**

Students who wish to receive both the Master of Science in Real Estate (MSRE) and the Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Development may apply up to 9 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.

**MSRE/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Management**

Students who wish to receive both the current Master of Science in Real Estate (MSRE) and the Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Management may apply up to 6 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.

**MSRED/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Finance**

Students who wish to receive both the Master of Science in Real Estate Development and the Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Finance may apply up to 9 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.

**MSRED/Advanced Certificate in Construction Management**

Students who wish to receive both the Master of Science in Real Estate Development and the Advanced Certificate in Construction Management may apply up to 9 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.

**MSCM/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Management**

Students who wish to receive both the Master of Science in Construction Management and the Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Management may apply up to 6 credits of successfully completed coursework with a grade of B or better towards each program.
