# Summer Leaders Academy

**Fordham's Summer Leaders Academy** is an immersive pre-college program that combines innovative academic courses and college application preparation on our beautiful 85-acre campus with activities in the most exciting classroom of all, New York City!

With on-campus housing available, this pre-college program is crafted to redefine your summer and set the stage for your future success.

![Students enjoy the sun on the large lawn in front of Keating Hall](/media/home/commonly-used-images/campus-beauty/Keating-Summer.jpg)


## Program Highlights

-
Choose from a wide range of courses including STEM, humanities, social sciences, business, and the arts.

-
Whether you live on campus or commute, participate in scavenger hunts and game nights, and access state-of-the-art facilities like the new RamFit Center.

-
Enjoy organized excursions, both in and outside of class, taking you to iconic museums, theaters, and historical sites.


-
Learn about the college application process, including admission strategies and essay writing techniques.

-
Get insights into careers such as law, medicine, business, and more.

-
Develop your leadership, communication, and problem-solving skills in the Jesuit tradition of educating people for others.
