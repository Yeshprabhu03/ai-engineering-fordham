# REI Refund Policy for Graduate Students

**14-Session/Week Graduate REI Courses Refund Policy**

| Withdrawal Date | Due Fordham | Refund to Student |
|---|---|---|
| Prior to second week of term | 0% | 100% |
| Prior to third week of term | 30% | 70% |
| Prior to fourth week of term | 45% | 55% |
| Prior to fifth week of term | 60% | 40% |
| Prior to sixth week of term | 75% | 25% |
| Thereafter | 100% | 0% |


**Fewer than 14-Session/Week Graduate REI Courses Refund Policy**

| Withdrawal Date | Due Fordham | Refund to Student |
|---|---|---|
| Prior to commencement course | 0% | 100% |
| Prior to second session of course | 40% | 60% |
| Prior to third session of course | 75% | 25% |
| Thereafter | 100% | 0% |
