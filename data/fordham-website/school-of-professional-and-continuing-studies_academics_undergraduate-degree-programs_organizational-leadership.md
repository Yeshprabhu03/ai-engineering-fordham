# PCS Organizational Leadership

The Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies' Organizational Leadership degree program offers you the universal skills required to be an effective manager in any field. It provides an alternative to business programs that focus heavily on finance.

**The organizational leadership program will familiarize you with essential organizational functions. Students who complete the major in this program will learn**

- Effective communications with your employees and the public
- Strategic planning, including budgeting and legal issues
- Human resource management
- Critical thinking skills that allow you to adapt and change

**Careers**

A degree in organizational management prepares you to assume a leadership position in many sectors and organizations, including business, government, cultural institutions, and non-profit organizations.

This bachelor's degree program is offered at the Lincoln Center, Rose Hill, and Westchester Campuses.
