# PCS Graduate Admissions

Explore the graduate programs offered by the Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies. Learn how to apply to Fordham’s M.S. in Real Estate and M.S. in Hospitality programs as well as the Advanced Graduate Certificates in Real Estate Finance, Construction Management, Real Estate Development, Property Management, Hotel Management, and Hospitality Investment & Development. These resources also apply to Fordham University’s Master of Science in Applied Health Informatics, launched Fall 2021.


Fordham PCS takes a holistic view of its applicants, considering work and life experience as part of the applicant’s knowledge base and area of expertise. These experiences may allow applicants to waive the GMAT or GRE requirement for some of the programs.
