# Dancer Tuition Discount

![](/media/home/schools/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/Group-of-Students-Dance-in-Front-of-Building.jpg)

Any full-time professional dancer (New York City Ballet, and other companies, i.e., American Ballet Theater) is eligible for the Dancer Discount, 33% off tuition!

Apprentices do not qualify.
