# PCS Information Sessions

## Real Answers from Real People

Making time in your life to complete your degree or advance your career comes with a lot of questions, and we are here to help you put your plan into action. Whether you are interested in adult undergraduate classes or a professional certificate, your story at Fordham begins with an information session.

We hold frequent information sessions, across all three of our New York campuses- Lincoln Center, Rose Hill, Westchester and online, where our advisors will introduce you to the program and answer any questions you might have.
