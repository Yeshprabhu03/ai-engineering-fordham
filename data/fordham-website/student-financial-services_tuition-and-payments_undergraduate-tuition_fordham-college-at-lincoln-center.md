# Fordham College at Lincoln Center Tuition and Fees

## 2025-2026 Academic Year Tuition and Fees

**New and Continuing Students**

**Full-time**: *$65,920 per year ($32,960 per term)**Part-time**: $2,197 per credit

**Full-time assumes 12-18 credits per term. Students will incur additional tuition charges above 18 credits.*
