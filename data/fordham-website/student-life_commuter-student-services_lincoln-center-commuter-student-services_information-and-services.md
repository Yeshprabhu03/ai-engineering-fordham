# Information and Services for Lincoln Center Commuters

![Male Student at LC](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Orientation.jpg)


Stop into
[Commuter Student Services](/student-life/commuter-student-services/lincoln-center-commuter-student-services/), located in the Office for Student Involvement in 140 W 62nd St. Room G33.

We provide services specifically for commuters throughout the year. If you are looking for a way to get involved, curious about how student organizations work, interested in attending a program, have a question about a University policy or need to locate a resource on campus—this is your place to turn.

We’re glad you’re part of our Fordham community, and
[Commuter Student Services](/student-life/commuter-student-services/lincoln-center-commuter-student-services/) is here to help make campus your home away from home!
