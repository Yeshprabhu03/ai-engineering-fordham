# Rose Hill Commuter Assistants

![Fordham Students in Fordham Commutes Shirts.](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/student-life/Fordham-Students-in-Fordham-Commutes-Shirts.jpeg)

To support the incoming commuter community at Fordham University Rose Hill, Commuter Assistants (CAs) establish ongoing communication between themselves and their freshman student group throughout the year. The purpose of which is to aid in the integration of Fordham's first-year commuters into the Fordham campus as a whole, through the creation of community and active participation.

## Commuter Assistants

- Provide guidance and support you while transitioning into and succeeding at the university.
- Assist you with Fordhamâ€™s resources by responding to first-year commuter questions and concerns.
- Help you become effectively integrated into the campus community as a whole by building relationships and participating in campus events.
- Develop events, programs, and community meetings for commuter students.

### Commuter Assistants Stay in Contact by:

- Regular in-person meetings
- Telephone and text message check-ins
- Email correspondence
- Attending on and off-campus events

### Commuter Assistants are Responsible for:

- Programming commuter-related events
- Conducting workshops for first-year commuter students
- Keeping office hours in the Student Lounge so they are easily accessible
