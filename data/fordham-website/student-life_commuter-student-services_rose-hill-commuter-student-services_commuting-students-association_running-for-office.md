# Running for Office

Membership and Elections

Were you a part of student government in High School? Interested in creating change on campus?

The Commuting Students Association holds elections at the end of the spring semester for the upperclassman senate boards for the upcoming academic year. The Freshmen senate is elected in the beginning of the fall semester in the first few weeks of school. The positions available are Class President, Vice-President, Treasurer, Program Coordinator, and Marketing Coordinator.

To run for a position, students must do the following:

-
Fill out a election packet (can be found at our election tablings or obtained after emailing us)

-
Attend a CSA Info Session

-
Collect 50 signatures from constituents

-
Campaign on campus through posters, social media, etc
