# Transportation and Parking for Rose Hill Commuters

General Parking and Parking Permits

Driving to campus? Register for a parking pass or part-time coupon book by:

- Logging into my.fordham.edu
- Click the "My Apps" Tab
- Select: Login to Student Parking

For more information, see [Rose Hill Parking Facilities](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/public-safety/services/parking-facilities/rose-hill-campus-parking/) and [University Regulations on Automobiles](/student-life/deans-of-students-and-student-life/student-handbook/university-regulations/a---z-listing/automobiles/).

Public Transportation and Public Safety Shuttle

A van will not leave the subway station until the other van arrives back from campus. Another van will leave the Bathgate entrance every half hour and make stops throughout the Belmont community.

Vans are operated by a Public Safety guard and students, faculty, and staff boarding the van must present a valid Fordham identification card.
