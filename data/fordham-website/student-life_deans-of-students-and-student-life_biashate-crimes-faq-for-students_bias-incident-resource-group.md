# Bias Incident Resource Group

Fordham's Bias Incident Resource Group (BIRG) was created to bring together the multiple departments involved in responding to bias incidents within the Division of Student Affairs, including multicultural affairs, counseling and psychological services, residential life, student involvement/leadership and the deans of students for each campus.
