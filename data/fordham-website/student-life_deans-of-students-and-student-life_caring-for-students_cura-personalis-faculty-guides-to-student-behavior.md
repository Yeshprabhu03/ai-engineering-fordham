# Cura Personalis: Faculty Guides to Student Behavior

[Responding to Mental Health Emergencies](/student-life/deans-of-students-and-student-life/caring-for-students/responding-to-mental-health-emergencies/)

This guide is meant to assist faculty when they observe that a student may need assistance with a mental or emotional crisis.

[Responding to a Student's Threatening Behavior](/student-life/deans-of-students-and-student-life/caring-for-students/responding-to-a-students-threatening-behavior/)

This guide is meant to assist faculty concerned about a student who appears threatening or potentially violent.

[Responding to Students in Emotional Distress](/student-life/deans-of-students-and-student-life/caring-for-students/responding-to-students-in-emotional-distress/)

This guide is meant to assist faculty when one of their students exhibits signs of emotional distress or a mental health problem.

[Responding to Students' Alcohol and Drug Abuse](/student-life/deans-of-students-and-student-life/caring-for-students/responding-to-students-alcohol-and-drug-abuse/)

This guide is meant to assist faculty when one of their students exhibits signs of alcohol or drug abuse.
