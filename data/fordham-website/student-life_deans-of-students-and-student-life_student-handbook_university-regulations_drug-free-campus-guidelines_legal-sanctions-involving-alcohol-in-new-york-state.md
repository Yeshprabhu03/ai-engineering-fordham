# Drug-Free Campus Guidelines: Legal Sanctions Involving Alcohol in New York State

All students, as well as employees, are expected to have knowledge of the applicable provisions of the New York State Alcoholic Beverage Control Law (ABC Law) and the New York State General Obligations Law (GOL) which appear in the Student Handbook.
