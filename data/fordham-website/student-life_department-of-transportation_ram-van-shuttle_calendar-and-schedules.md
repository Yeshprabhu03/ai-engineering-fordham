# Ram Van Shuttle Calendar and Schedules

Ram Van's schedule varies depending on the day of the week and time of year. Use this calendar to determine which schedule we will run on any day. Then use the accordion below the calendar to determine the exact service schedule for that type of day.

-
**Monday - Thursday****Departing Rose Hill**6:15 AM | 7 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Rose Hill bound for Lincoln Center at 10:30 PM | 11 PM.

**Departing Lincoln Center**7:15 AM | 8 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Lincoln Center bound for Rose Hill at 11:30 PM | 12 AM.

**Office Hours**The office opens at 6 AM and closes shortly after 12 AM. The administrative staff is typically available from 8 AM to 7 PM.

**Friday****Departing Rose Hill**6:15 AM | 7 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Rose Hill bound for Lincoln Center at 2:30 AM | 3 AM.

**Departing Lincoln Center**7:15 AM | 8 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Lincoln Center bound for Rose Hill at 3:30 AM | 4 AM.

**Office Hours**The office opens at 6 AM and closes shortly after 4 AM. The administrative staff is typically available from 8 AM to 7 PM.

**Saturday****Departing Rose Hill**7:30 AM | 8 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Rose Hill bound for Lincoln Center at 2:30 AM | 3 AM.

**Departing Lincoln Center**8:30 AM | 9 AM, then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Lincoln Center bound for Rose Hill at 3:30 AM | 4 AM.

**Office Hours**The office opens at 7 AM and closes shortly after 4 AM. The administrative staff is typically available from 10 AM to 6 PM.

**Sunday****Departing Rose Hill**8 AM | 9 AM then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Rose Hill bound for Lincoln Center at 10 PM | 11 PM.

**Departing Lincoln Center**9 AM | 10 AM then every half hour on the half-hour with the last two scheduled departures from Lincoln Center bound for Rose Hill at 11 PM | 12 AM.

**Office Hours**The office opens at 8 AM and closes shortly after 12 AM.
