# Ram Van Early Arrival Program

## Monday, August 18th to Friday, August 22nd

If you're interested in a student job at Ram Van, there's a tremendous end-of-summer opportunity! Every year, the Ram Van office holds a **paid** week-long training program that leads right into a job at the Ram Van office for the fall semester.

-
**Move-in early!**If you live in Fordham housing, you will be approved to move in Sunday, August 18th.**Money!**Get paid for a week's training and begin the school year with a well-paying and convenient job.**Hands-on instruction!**Our administrators are all experienced driving safety trainers. All "early arrivers" get one-on-one behind-the-wheel instruction. By the week's end, you'll be a pro at driving a 15-passenger van all over New York City. -
**Get behind the wheel:**You'll receive hours of hands-on training. Participants receive one-on-one instruction on how to drive with the highest standard of safety & passenger comfort.**Certified Instruction:**New drivers will take a National Safety Council Defensive Driving Course and attend lectures by Fordham's experts on sleep hygiene, time management, and conflict resolution.**Navigate NYC:**By the end of the week, all participants will be able to drive to Lincoln Center, Fordham's Calder Center for Biological Research, and both New York City airports. -
All participants are required to meet the essential pre-requisites for

[University driver certification](/student-life/department-of-transportation/university-driver-policy-and-certification/who-can-drive/).All participants must be fully available from 9 AM-5 PM Monday through Friday (lunch breaks will be provided each day).

All participants must commit to a minimum of one semester working in the Ram Van office, working 12 hours per week.

All participants must be approved by the Office of Student Employment for a work-study position in our office.
