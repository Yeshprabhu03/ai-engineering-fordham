## Guide to Submitting an Exam Request

**Are you registered with our office?**

**No -**If you want to register with our office, please[complete our application](https://hood.accessiblelearning.com/Fordham/)and upload documentation of your diagnosis to your application. You will then receive an email to schedule your intake.**Yes -**Do you receive alternative testing accommodations? (see below)

**Do you receive alternative testing accommodations?**

**No -**No action required.**Yes -**Did you sign up to take your test at ODS? (see below)

**Did you sign up to take your test at ODS?**

**No -**If you want to take your exam at ODS with accommodations, please submit an exam request.**Yes -**Is it a make-up exam? (see below)

**Is it a make-up exam?**

**No -**Please come to ODS for the time/day the exam is scheduled.**Yes -**Please send us ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#f6929f8597949f9a9f828f859384809f959385b6909984929e979bd8939283)) an approved excused absence from your dean/advisor and permission from your instructor to take your exam on a different day.

- Look in your left hand side tool bar under “My Accommodations” to find “Alternative Testing”
- Click “Alternative Testing”
- In the select class drop down menu, select the class for which you would like to schedule an exam
- Click “Schedule and Exam”
- Complete the “Exam Detail” form with the details of your request
- Select the time you wish to take the exam, not the time for which the exam is scheduled (if the two are different)
**Do not use**the “exam length (specified by student)” box, unless the professor has indicated that this exam is of a different length than he/she indicated on his/her testing contract- Select the boxes for the accommodations you will need for this exam
- This form will only allow exams scheduled between 9am-5pm, though our offices do have extended hours on some days. If you wish to take an exam that will extend beyond these hours, please speak with a staff member before scheduling
- Click “Add Exam Request”
- If you are entering your request fewer than 5 business days before the exam, you will see a “Late Exam Notice” and be asked to enter a “Late Exam Request
- You
**must**select a reason. - Check the box that you have read and understand the late exam request policy.
- Click “Submit Late Exam Request”
- If you do not follow these late exam steps after the regular “Add Exam Request,” your exam request
**will not be submitted** - Your request will be reviewed and approved, or a rescheduling option sent, in approximately 24 hours (longer if submitted over a weekend or holiday)
- Exams can be submitted as far in advance as you would like. Final exams will not be approved until we make the final exam schedule during the last week of the semester.
