# Lincoln Center Campus Family Weekend

![LC Family Weekend Student with Parents - MD](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/LC_Family_Weekend_Student_with_Parents___MD.jpg)

##### Join us for Family Weekend 2025!

October 24- 26, 2025 | Lincoln Center Campus

Join us for the annual Family Weekend at the Lincoln Center campus. This weekend of events is open to the families of all undergraduate students from Fordham College at Lincoln Center and the Gabelli School of Business at the Lincoln Center Campus.

The weekend provides the chance for students' families to experience Fordham University firsthand and to interact with:

- Current undergraduate students
- Other Fordham families
- Deans and administrators

Information regarding the [hotel block](/student-life/events-and-activities/family-weekend/lincoln-center-campus/family-weekend-lincoln-center-hotel-information/) and [area hotels](/about/maps-and-directions/area-hotels/) is now available.
