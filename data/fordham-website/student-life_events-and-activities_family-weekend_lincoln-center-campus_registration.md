# Lincoln Center Family Weekend Registration

Families interested in attending Lincoln Center Family Weekend are encouraged to register in advance for the weekend's activities, as some events may have a limited capacity. In addition, all events, regardless if they are free of charge, require a ticket.

**Registration begins**: Friday, August 22. Follow this link to register for all events -> [fordham.universitytickets.com](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/).

**Registration ends:** Most tickets will close 2 weeks prior to the weekend or when events are at capacity. Please check individual tickets for specific early closing deadlines.

**Refund Policy**

Tickets to Lincoln Center Family Weekend are for the most part non-refundable. Outdoor events occur rain or shine. Refunds will not be provided based on weather.
