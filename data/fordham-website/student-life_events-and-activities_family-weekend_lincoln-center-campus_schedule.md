**Museum of the City of New York**** **| 1220 Fifth Ave., New York, NY 10029 | 1:00 p.m.

Tickets: $33.75

Join Fordham for an afternoon tour of the Museum of the City of New York: Museum Overview Tour. Ticket cost includes tour and admission to the museum exhibits.

The Museum of the City of New York is dedicated to showcasing the past, present and future of urban life in the Big Apple, and how it came to be one of the world's most influential metropolises.

Spread across four floors, the exhibitions include paintings, toys, photographs and many other items that illustrate the history of New York and New Yorkers throughout the generations, from the golden age of Broadway theatre to objects from the city's historic police and fire departments. Videos, interactive touch screens and other audiovisual material allows for an immersive experience of the City That Never Sleeps.

**Transportation**

Throughout your weekend, you can use public transportation for your family to see all the city has to offer. The subway station closest to Fordham LC is the 59th Street Columbus Circle station a block away from campus. The A, C, B, D and 1 trains stop there. The M11 bus stops right in front of campus. Here are links to the local public transportation:

[Metropolitan Transit Authority (MTA)](http://www.mta.info/)for the subway, bus, Long Island Railroad and Metro-North information- The
[PATH train](http://www.panynj.gov/path/)for access to Manhattan from New Jersey - New Jersey Transit for rail and bus transportation to Manhattan from New Jersey

The MTA subway system makes it easy to get around the city. Your family can purchase a[ MetroCard](http://web.mta.info/metrocard/) at any station. [OMNY](https://new.mta.info/system_modernization/omny) is the MTA’s new fare payment system. With OMNY, you can use your own contactless card or smart device to make fare payments and enter the system.
