# Senior Week

The 2026 Senior Week Committees and the Offices for Student Involvement at Rose Hill and Lincoln Center are proud to sponsor the Senior Week 2026 events for eligible graduating Seniors at each campus.

**Senior Leadership Awards**

Each year, Student Affairs is proud to present awards to members of the Senior Class who have made outstanding contributions to our community. Class of 2026 graduates who were nominated will be recognized and winners will be announced.

**Transitions: Senior Year Experience Program**

Transitions is a series of programs developed by the Senior Week Committee and the Offices for Student Involvement to ease the transition from college to the real world. Last year, the Transitions Series covered everything from apartment hunting and surviving the company cocktail party to paying bills, navigating student loans, and financial management after graduation.
