# About Us

## Meet the Staff

![Several orientation leaders pose with moving boxes](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/student-life/LC-Orientation-Leaders.webp)

A dedicated staff of full-time Fordham employees and an army of student orientation coordinators, captains, leaders, and assistants help guide new students through the orientation experience, leading small groups, and helping them get to know the campus.

-
**Christina Frankovic-Sepsi**

Director for Student InvolvementA native of Bergen County, New Jersey, Christina became the Director for Student Involvement in October 2022. Christina received her undergraduate degree from Fordham University and completed her Master of the Arts in Higher Education/Student Personnel Administration from New York University. As a proud Alumnus of Fordham College at Lincoln Center, Christina served as an Orientation Leader and an Orientation Captain during her undergraduate years. She is so excited to welcome all of the new students to our Fordham family.
