# January New Student Orientation at Lincoln Center

## Join us for our Winter Orientation Programs!

The Orientation Team is excited to welcome all of the incoming students for the Spring Semester! Winter Orientation will takes place on Friday, January 9, 2026 on campus at Fordham Lincoln Center. Please note that Winter Orientation is designed for incoming students and not open to guests.

If you have any questions or concerns, please contact New Student Orientation at 212-636-6260 or email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#cfa0bda6aaa1bba3ac8fa9a0bdaba7aea2e1aaabba).
