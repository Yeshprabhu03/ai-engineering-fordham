# Lincoln Center Orientation App

## Get the App

### 2025 Fall Undergraduate Orientation Program Guide is Live!

Access your orientation schedule, campus map, and university resources in the Fordham Orientation App. The free app is available for all smartphones including iPhone and Android devices.

### Download Before You Go

We recommend downloading the app before you arrive so that you can make the most of your time on campus.

### Instructions

Apple Devices:

- Search "
**Fordham Orientation**" in the[Apple App Store](https://www.apple.com/app-store/) - Open the application
- Select "Find Guides" and search for and select "
**Lincoln Center 2025 Orientation**" - Select "Download Guide"
- Click the three lines in the left-hand corner to access the guide menu

Android Devices:

- Search "
**Fordham Orientation**" in the[Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.guidebook.apps.RamGuides.android) - Open the application
- Select "Find Guides" and search for and select "
**Lincoln Center 2025 Orientation**" - Select "Download Guide"
- Click the three lines in the left-hand corner to access the guide menu
