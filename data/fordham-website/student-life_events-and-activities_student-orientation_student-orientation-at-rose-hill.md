# Student Orientation at Rose Hill

## Ready? Set. College!

![Ramses dancing with Orientation leaders](/media/home/commonly-used-images/students-faculty-and-alumni-headshots/Orientation-Leaders-Ramses.webp)


Get ready for an unforgettable experience that will set the stage for your next four years.

Think of student orientation as a toolkit for your academic and social success. We’ll show you all the resources available to you—from tutoring centers to career services–and make sure that you’re meeting a lot of great people at the same time.
