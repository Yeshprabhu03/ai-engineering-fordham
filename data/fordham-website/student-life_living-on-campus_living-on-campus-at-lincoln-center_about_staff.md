**Vickki Massy**

She has also served as an Assistant Director of Residential Life at Lincoln Center for five years before becoming the Associate Director for Housing Operations.

**Joe Corcoran**

Assistant Director of Residential Life

212-636-7100[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)

As the Assistant Director of Residential Life, Joe oversees residential education initiatives, manages assessment and evaluation, acts as a liaison to campus partners, supervises four Resident Directors, and serves as Administrator On-Call for emergency and crisis situations. Joe holds a Master of Arts degree in Student Affairs in Higher Education from Indiana University of Pennsylvania and a Bachelor of Arts degree in Journalism, with a minor in International Studies, from American University. Prior to serving as Assistant Director, Joe was Resident Director for First-Year Students in McKeon Hall for four years and Resident Director for first-year, upperclass, and law students in McMahon Hall for three and a half years.

Before joining the Res Life Ramily, Joe worked in Residence Life, International Student Services, and Career Services at universities in the Pittsburgh metropolitan area. Joe's professional interests include student leadership and empowerment within underrepresented communities, international and transfer student engagement, and LGBTQIA+ issues in higher education.
