# McMahon Hall at Lincoln Center

**Home to Undergraduate Students**

![Male Student on Bed in Dorm Room](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/092214_Lincoln_Campus_129.jpg)


McMahon Hall is a 20-story residence building with 24-hour security, study lounges, social lounges, three laundry rooms, and a fitness center. Undergraduate students live in single, double, and triple bedrooms in either two-bedroom or three-bedroom apartments. Each apartment is fully furnished, air-conditioned and includes one or two bathrooms, a living/dining room area, and a kitchen.

**Fast Facts**

**Population:**- Upperclass and First-Year students
- Approximately 830 residential students

**Room Types:**- Double and Triples, Converted Triples for first-year students, Upperclassmen Singles
- Apartments of 2 or 3 bedroom apartments, with either one or two bathrooms per apartment

- Typical Room Dimensions
- Storage space underneath a bed
**Mattress size:**- Twin XL

- 5 Elevators, 2 stairwells
- Security Guards: 24/7
- The gym and lockers are located on the second floor
- 1 Resident Assistant per floor
- Floor 2-20 Residential Living
- Staff serving in a 24/7 crisis rotation

**McMahon Features**

**Lounges**- 5th floor - Quiet Lounge
- 8th floor - Social Lounge

- 17th floor - Quiet Lounge


**Kitchen**- One standard refrigerator/freezer, sink, cabinets

**McMahon Hall Housing Rates for Undergraduate Students**

![Old dorm at LC - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/KGamble_090814_8491__SM.jpg)

First-year students assigned to McMahon Hall are not required to take a [meal plan](/student-life/living-on-campus/meal-plans/). First-year residents are encouraged to purchase an [optional meal plan](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans) or DCB (declining balance dollars) for the convenience of getting at least some meals in the Undergraduate Dining Hall.

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and use of the fitness center located on the second floor of McMahon Hall. (Note: All rates are subject to change.)

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for McMahon Hall. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.
