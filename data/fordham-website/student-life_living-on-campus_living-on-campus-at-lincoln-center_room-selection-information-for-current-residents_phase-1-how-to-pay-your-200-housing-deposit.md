# Phase 1: How to Pay your $200 Housing Deposit

## Phase 1: How to Pay your $200 Housing Deposit

### Due Tuesday, February 17 by 5pm.

- Log onto My.Fordham.edu
- Click the Students Tab under My Pages.
- My Accounts
- Student housing/Housing Deposit Payment
- Select the Term (Fall 2026).
- Pay by credit card or electronic check. The deposit amount is $200.
- Print out your payment authorization confirmation for your own proof of deposit.

**Please note:** If you are having difficulty paying your deposit online, you can pay in person at Financial Services on the 2nd floor of Lowenstein. If you have questions, please contact The Office of Residential Life at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#a4d6c1d7c8cdc2c1c8c7e4c2cbd6c0ccc5c98ac1c0d1).

### Housing Deposit Waivers

Residents who are eligible for deposit waivers due to special scholarship programs **must email the Office of Residential Life by Wednesday February 11.** stating the office and administrator that is responsible for paying your housing deposit. Please keep in mind that this is the responsibility of the student to inform Residential Life about any housing deposit waivers.

### Things to remember

- Students must make a $200 housing deposit by Tuesday, February 17 by 5p.m.
**A charge will NOT be added**to your student account. - Late deposit submissions: If you do not submit your $200 Online Housing Deposit by the February 17 deadline, your housing is not guaranteed. Students who deposit late will be placed in post-room selection and assigned over the summer months if a space becomes available. If you withdraw before May 15 you are able to get a refund for your housing deposit.
- First-Year Core Programming: If you did not complete your First-Year Core Programming requirement, you will be entered into post-room selection. You will not be allowed to participate in the regular room selection process with your friend(s). In addition, no space can be held for you in a particular room or suite. If you wish to return to housing for next year, you still must submit your $200 online housing deposit by the February 17 deadline.
- Residential Life Probation: Students on Residential Life probation are not able to participate in Room Selection and will be placed in housing over the summer months as long as they pay their Fall 2026-Spring 2027 housing deposit.


### Housing Deposit Refund Schedule

Students can receive their housing deposit refund once they complete the housing withdrawal form by the following deadline to cancel University housing for the academic year.

May 15 - $200 Housing Deposit Refund

June 15 - $100 Housing Deposit Refund

June 16 - July 31 $0 Housing Deposit Refund

Aug 1 - Aug 22 $1,000 Late Fee
