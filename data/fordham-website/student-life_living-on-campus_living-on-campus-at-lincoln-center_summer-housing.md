# Summer Housing at Lincoln Center

## Summer 2026 Housing Information

![Male Student with Laptop](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/KGamble_090814_6006_1.jpg)

**non-graduating**Fordham undergraduate students and current resident Fordham law students are eligible to apply to summer housing as long as they are taking classes or participating in an internship.

### Summer Housing Deposit

- $50 nonrefundable deposit
- Deposits will be accepted between Tuesday, April 7 - Friday, April 17 at 5pm
**The deadline for Summer housing deposits is April 17 at 5pm**

### How to Make Your Deposit:

- In order to deposit, students need to go to
[My.Fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/)and select Housing Deposit Payment in the housing channel.**Please be sure to deposit $50 for SUMMER 2026 and not another year or amount.** - Summer Housing Applications will be available on Monday, April 20 at 12pm and will need to be submitted by Wednesday, April 29, at 5pm
- Summer Housing will ONLY be guaranteed to students who deposit for Summer 2026.

### Other Important Information

**Summer Housing Applications must be completed between Monday, April 20 at 12pm to Wednesday, April 29 at 5pm**- First Come - First Served: Lincoln Center Summer Housing as well as Rose Hill summer housing are first come-first served; however, priority will be given to students taking classes.
**Current resident students at Lincoln Center will need to be available from Wednesday, 5/13 through Sunday, 5/24 to move into their summer assignment.**If you cannot move during this time frame, you will need to move out all of your belongings and return to campus on Sunday, May 24 for Summer Session 1 move in.- Commuters need to email the Office of Residential Life at
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#d1a3b4a2bdb8b7b4bdb291b7bea3b5b9b0bcffb4b5a4)in order to fill out the application. - Any student who submits a summer housing application and withdraws will lose their $50 deposit.
