# Become a Student Staff Member at Rose Hill

Thank you for your interest and inquiry into the Resident Assistant Recruitment and Selection process for the 2026-2027 academic year. We have prepared some basic information for potential applicants in order to plan ahead and be aware of the upcoming process.

If you have any specific questions about this process, please contact the **Resident Assistant Recruitment and Selection Committee** in the **Office of Residential Life** at **718-817-3080** or via email at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#26746774434554534f524b43485266404954424e474b08434253).

### How can I learn more about the position?

Anyone interested in applying for the [Resident Assistant position](https://www.fordham.edu/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-rose-hill/about/become-a-student-staff-member/resident-assistant-position/) can see a detailed position description by following the link provided. They can also learn more by attending one of the information sessions being held across campus.

### How do I apply?

All eligible candidates **MUST** complete the online application form that can be found on the Resident Assistant Position page. A resume must be submitted along with the application form in order to be considered for the Resident Assistant position.

### Who is eligible?

All applicants for the Resident Assistant position must be either an upcoming (as of Fall 2026) sophomore with at least a 3.0 GPA, or an upcoming (as of Fall 2026) junior, senior, or graduate student with at least a 2.5 GPA. Residential, commuter, and graduate students are eligible to apply.

### How will my financial aid be affected?

Applicants with questions are encouraged to address these [inquiries with a financial aid](https://www.fordham.edu/undergraduate-financial-aid/) officer.
