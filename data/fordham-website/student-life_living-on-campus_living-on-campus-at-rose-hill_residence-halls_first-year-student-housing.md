# Rose Hill Campus: First-Year Student Housing

**It All Starts Here**

![Students sitting on stairs in front of Rodriques coffeehouse - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/KGamble_041612_2314.jpg)


As a first-year student living on-campus, you will have greater proximity to your classes, [student activities](/student-life/events-and-activities/), and the beautiful green spaces that the Rose Hill campus has to offer.

First-year students who live on-campus:

- Have an easier time transitioning into college life
- Often meet their core group of friends
- Become a part of a supportive community
- Earn a higher grade point average
- Meet students with a diversity of life experiences
- Enjoy a more convenient and less expensive living style
- Learn principles of civility among roommates and neighbors
