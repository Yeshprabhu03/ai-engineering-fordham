# Rose Hill Campus: Alumni Court South

![Alumni Court South](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Alumni_Court_South.jpg)

Opened in 1987, [Alumni Court South](http://youvis.it/MXD1z6) residence hall accommodates mostly first-year students in five stories of corridor living.

## Fast Facts

- Population: 250 to 350 First Years and Limited Upperclassmen
- Room Types: Double and Triples, Converted Triples, Upperclassmen Singles
- Co-ed with wings being either all male or female
- Common bathrooms
- Typical Room Dimensions: 12 x 17 feet
- Storage space underneath a bed: 26 inches maximum
- Mattress size: Twin XL
- Elevator

## Features

- Game room with a pool table and ping pong table
- Social lounges with a TV/DVD player
- Two study lounges
- Presentation lounge
- Laundry and kitchen facilities

## Housing Rates

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center.](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/) Meal plan options and rates are available. These rates do not include the residence hall activity fee. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for Alumni Court South. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

All residents in this dormitory are required to have a [meal plan](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans/Category?cat=Rose_Hill_Fall_Mandatory_Meal_Plans_9254&lid=9254).

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.
