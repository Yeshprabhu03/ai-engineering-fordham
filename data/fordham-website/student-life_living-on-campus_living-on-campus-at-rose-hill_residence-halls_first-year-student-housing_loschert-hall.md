# Rose Hill Campus: Loschert Hall

![Building exterior - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_042010_0098.jpg)

Opened in 1987, [Loschert Hall](http://youvis.it/uUw9Lp) accommodates 240 students in four stories of corridor living.

## Fast Facts

- Population: 240 First Years and Limited Upperclassmen
- Room Types: Double and Triples, Converted Triples, Upperclassmen Singles
- Co-ed with wings being either all male or female
- Common bathrooms
- Typical Room Dimensions: 12 x 17 feet
- Storage space underneath a bed: 26 inches maximum
- Mattress size: Twin XL
- Elevator

## Features

- Social lounges with a TV/DVD player
- Game room with pool and ping pong table
- Two study lounges
- Presentation lounge and kitchen
- Laundry facilities

## Room and Board Rates

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/). Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for Loschert Hall. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

All residents in this dormitory are required to have a [meal plan](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans/Category?cat=Rose_Hill_Fall_Mandatory_Meal_Plans_9254&lid=9254).

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.
