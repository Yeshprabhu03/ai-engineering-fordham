# Upper Classmen Student Housing

![Students walking on campus - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_100908_0246.jpg)


Upperclassmen who live on-campus:

- Continue to be apart of a supportive community
- Earn a higher grade point average
- Enjoy a more convenient and less expensive living style
- Remain close in proximity to classes, student activities, and libraries
