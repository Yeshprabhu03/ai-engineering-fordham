# Walsh Hall

![Two female students sitting at dining table - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/092414_RoseHill_Campus_91.jpg)

Once known as “555” for its original address on 191st Street, [Walsh Hall](http://www.youvisit.com/tour/fordham/79970?loc=trail39:27:1:1&pl=v) is a 13-story residence hall for upper-class students, primarily juniors and seniors. Named after Fordham President Father Michael P. Walsh, SJ, who oversaw University operations from 1969 to 1972, this elevator-equipped residence hall is the tallest on campus. 492 upperclassmen live in two- and three-bedroom apartments. Each apartment includes a living/dining area, bathroom, full kitchen, and air conditioning. Walsh also offers a community lounge and laundry facilities.

**Students are asked to bring their own lighting as they prepare to occupy their room.**

## Fast Facts

- 4 or 6 person apartments
- Featuring a living space, kitchen and one or two bathrooms
- Storage space underneath a bed: 26 inches maximum
- Mattress size: 36 x 76 inches

## Features

- Large community lounge
- Multiple laundry rooms

## Housing Rates

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/). These rates do not include the residence hall activity fee.

[Meal plan](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans/Category?cat=Rose_Hill_Fall_Mandatory_Meal_Plans_9254&lid=9254) is **not** required.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for Walsh Hall. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.
