# Spring Break and Easter Break

**Spring Break Stay Request Form**

Students wishing to stay on campus during Spring Break will need to complete the stay request form. Filling out the form does not guarantee that your request will be granted. Contact the office of Residential Life at Rose Hill if you have any questions at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#413324322d282724332901272e332529202c6f242534) or 718-817-3080.

Only students who live in Campbell Hall, Salice Conley Hall, Walsh Hall or in Belmont Community Housing may stay in their assigned space. All other halls will be closed and students must find a host within an open hall to remain on campus. The forms will be emailed to students' Fordham accounts.

**Easter Break Stay Request Form**

Students wishing to stay on campus during Easter Break will need to complete the break stay request form. Filling out the form does not guarantee that your request will be granted. Contact the office of Residential Life at Rose Hill if you have any questions at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#beccdbcdd2d7d8dbccd6fed8d1ccdad6dfd390dbdacb) or 718-817-3080.

Students should plan accordingly.

The forms will be emailed to students' Fordham accounts in the Spring.
