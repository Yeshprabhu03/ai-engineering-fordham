# LGBTQ Resources

The Office of Multicultural Affairs coordinates, co-sponsors, and supports a number of LGBTQ and Ally Network of Support focused events and programs on campus that educates the campus community and raises awareness about the experiences of Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Questioning, and Ally members of the Fordham community. These events are planned by our office in collaboration with student organizations and other departments on campus. All of these programs are grounded in the Jesuit tenet of Cura Personalis, or care of the whole person.
