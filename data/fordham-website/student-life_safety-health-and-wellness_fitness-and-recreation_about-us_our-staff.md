# Ram Fit Center Staff

The Ram Fit Center staff is led by Sarah Longley Bickford, administrator of fitness and recreation.

Bickford, who has over 25 years of experience in the fitness industry, was named administrator of fitness and recreation for the new Ram Fit Center at Fordham University in September 2012. She is responsible for managing the newest fitness facility on campus, as well as organizing group exercise classes.

As a locally recognized fitness professional, Bickford has a broad set of fitness expertise including leading group exercise classes, personal training, lecturing on anatomy and physiology, and management consulting to major fitness companies. Her knowledge of nutrition and fitness leadership has added value to innovative fitness marketing, program development, and increased revenue and membership retention.

Bickford holds a degree in Exercise Science and Nutrition along with fitness certifications include NASM certified personal trainer; AFAA Group Fitness Instructor; kickboxing; NASM Certified Nutrition Coach; TRX- Group Suspension Training; Mad Dogg Spinning; IM=X Pilates Reformer & Mat; Muscular Strength & Range of Movement (Chair based class for adults 65+) She is also certified in CPR/AED.

The front desk staff includes Fordham student workers and campus security guards. In addition, our fitness professionals provide group classes.
