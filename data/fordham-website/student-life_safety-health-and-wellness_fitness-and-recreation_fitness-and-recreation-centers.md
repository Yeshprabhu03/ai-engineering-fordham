# Fitness and Recreation Centers

Fordham University has three fitness centers located across Rose Hill and Lincoln Center.

The [Ram Fit Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/ram-fit-center/) and the [Vincent T. Lombardi Memorial Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/) are located at the Rose Hill campus. The [Lincoln Fit Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/lincoln-center-fitness/) is located at the Lincoln Center campus.
