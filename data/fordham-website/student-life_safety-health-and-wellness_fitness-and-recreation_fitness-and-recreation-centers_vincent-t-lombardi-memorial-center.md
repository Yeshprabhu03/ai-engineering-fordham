# Vincent T. Lombardi Memorial Center

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/fitness-center/JOboyle-20151014-0041-1000x480.jpg)


The [Lombardi Center](https://youvis.it/s0XHaO) opened in 1976 and is situated behind the McGinley Center and Rose Hill Gymnasium on the Rose Hill campus in the Bronx.

The Lombardi Center Features:

- 200-meter indoor running track
- Tennis and squash courts
[Swimming pool](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/aquatics-center/)- Competition diving facilities
