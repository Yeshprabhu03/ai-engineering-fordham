# The Fieldhouse

Students are encouraged to take full advantage of the facilities and equipment offered at the McGinley Center. Courts may be used recreationally or by athletic and intramural teams for training.

Basketball: four full courts

Tennis: two indoor courts

Volleyball: two courts

Track: four lane, 220-meter indoor running track, long jump pit and pole vault pit, high-jump apparatus
