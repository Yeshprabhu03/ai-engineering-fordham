**CrossFit SPOT**

The CrossFit SPOT located at [55 Amsterdam Ave. (62nd St. and Amsterdam Ave.)](http://www.cfspotnyc.com/) offers a progressive and dynamic workout regime that pushes you to challenge your limits further in all CrossFit movements. Skill work, recovery, and injury prevention is built into all of our programming to prevent plateaus and regression. Fordham students are eligible for a 10% discount on membership.
