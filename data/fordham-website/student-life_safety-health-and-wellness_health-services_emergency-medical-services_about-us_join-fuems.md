# Join FUEMS

Come Volunteer with Fordham University EMS

**Reasons to Join**:

- The people you work with are really cool.
- It's a great way to jump start your career goals.
- You get to help out your fellow student.
- You learn a lot about Fordham, the surrounding communities and the medical profession.
- It's a great way to meet people.
- You can become a New York State Certified EMT for free.
- It's fun to volunteer.
- Impress your friends by casually throwing around terms like OPA, PCR, and sphygmomanometer.
