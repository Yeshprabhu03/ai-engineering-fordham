# Physical Therapy at Rose Hill Campus

We offer Physical Therapy at University Health Services on the Rose Hill Campus. We have an experienced Physical Therapist that provides one-to-one care for your orthopedic, neurological, or post-surgical needs. We have a variety of equipment on site and appointments to meet your personal needs. You can reach Physical Therapist, Susan Greenburg, at 914-450-3833 to schedule your appointment.

Please note: UHS does not participate in insurance billing however, you can request a receipt and submit it to your insurance for reimbursement if eligible.
