# Virtual Urgent Care

**Note: **Students are encouraged to call their insurance company for a list of participating providers. The Fordham University Health Center does not endorse any specific physicians for referrals, however, listed below is information about Virtual Urgent Care provided by Montefiore Hospital.

**With Virtual Urgent Care...a doctor is as close as your cell phone, computer, or tablet.**

When you suspect a common illness or you have a minor injury, you don't have to leave your home. Montefiore's Virtual Urgent Care can connect you with a Montefiore Emergency Medicine physician or Montefiore Medical Group physician quickly.

Stay home and see the doctor. Request a Virtual Care Visit on MyChart.

Visit [www.montefiore.org/virtual-urgent-care](https://www.montefiore.org/virtual-urgent-care)
