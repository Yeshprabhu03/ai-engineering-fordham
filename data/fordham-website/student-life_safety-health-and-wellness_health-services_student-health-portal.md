# Student Health Portal

## Portal Login

Your Student Health Portal may be found on your [fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/) login under the **My Apps** link. You can access all of your medical forms at [https://fordham.studenthealthportal.com/Forms](https://fordham.studenthealthportal.com/Forms).

Within the Portal, you'll have access to:

- Upload your medical information, immunization records, and insurance cards
- Complete your medical forms
- Secure message your provider
- View your discharge encounter summary, laboratory results, and educational materials regarding your illness.
