# Rose Hill EV Charging Stations

There are eight (8) chargers available on a first-come first-served basis in the garage. You can find them on the ramp between the first and second floor in **Section 2A** of the parking facility. No reservations are needed.

Please follow these guidelines when using the charging stations:

- Faculty, staff and students
**must**have a current academic year parking pass, be the holder of a parking coupon book, or pay the daily parking fee in order to use the EV charging stations. - Users
**must**have a ChargePoint account and use their access card or mobile device app to operate the charging stations. - To create a ChargePoint acccount, please visit their website,
[chargepoint.com](chargepoint.com)create an account and a free access card will be mailed to you within 7-10 business days or simply download the Chagepoint app from the Google Play store or the Apple store and follow the instructions to sign up. - There will be a $0.39 per kWh user fee.
- There is a 4 hour time limit after which users are required to relocate their vehicle to a regular parking space. ChargePoint will notify users via text message or email when their EV is fully charged.
- Please note that users will be charged an additional station time rate of $5.00 per hour after the 4th hour of charging.
- Parking in an EV charging space is
**restricted**to vehicles plugged in and charging**ONLY**. Any vehicle not actively charging will be immobilized with a booting device and users will be charged a $50 removal fee. - EV charging spaces may be closed for maintenance, construction and special event parking without prior notice.


The University bears no responsibilty or liability for damage caused to vehicles while using the EV charging stations.

Thank you for your cooperation in reducing Fordham's carbon footprint.
