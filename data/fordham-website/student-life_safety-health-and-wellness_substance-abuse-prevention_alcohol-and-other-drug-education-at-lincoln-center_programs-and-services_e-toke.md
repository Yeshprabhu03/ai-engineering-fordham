# e-TOKE

The electronic THC Online Knowledge Experience (e-TOKE) is a brief, self-assessment that provides you with accurate, detailed and personalized feedback on your use of marijuana, and specific information and helpful resources in the Fordham University community.

**Program description:**

- Takes about 15 minutes
- Provides quick, confidential feedback
- Allows for a personal check-up on multiple occasions to track changes in marijuana use and risk (just keep your program identification number for comparisons)
