# Schedule an Appointment with AODE

![Typing on Mobile Phone](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Stock_Photo_TypingOnMobilePhone.jpg)

If you are interested in scheduling your first BASICS or E-Toke appointment, please contact us directly at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#b5d4dad1d0d9d6d6f5d3dac7d1ddd4d89bd0d1c0). Please provide both your schedule and your contact information in the email. AODE Program staff will schedule your session using the schedule that you provide.

Students who are assigned a BASICS or E-Toke sanction are responsible for scheduling their appointment prior to the deadline. If you do not attend a scheduled appointment, you may be fined for failure to comply.

To learn more about the AODE Program, please contact us directly at 212-636-6250 or [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#34555b5051585774525b46505c55591a515041).

If you are concerned about a friend's substance or alcohol use or have general questions, please feel free to reach out directly or to schedule an appointment.
