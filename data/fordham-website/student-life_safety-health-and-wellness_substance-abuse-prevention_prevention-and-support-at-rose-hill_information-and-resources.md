# Substance Abuse Prevention and Support at Rose Hill: Information and Resources

Prevention and Support offers various information and resources to assist in the prevention and reduction of alcohol and other drug abuse. We are responsible for campus awareness activities and programming with a distinct focus on healthy decision making regarding alcohol and other drugs. Any student who seeks additional substance abuse services is encouraged to reach out to Prevention and Support to schedule an appointment.
