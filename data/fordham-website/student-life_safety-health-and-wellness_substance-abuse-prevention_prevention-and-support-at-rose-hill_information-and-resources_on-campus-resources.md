# Substance Abuse Prevention and Support at Rose Hill: On-Campus Resources

**SAFE (Substance Abuse Fordham Educators)**

This student-run organization focuses on educating the Fordham Rose Hill community in the areas of alcohol and other drug alternatives, and its members serve as active change agents and leaders in services striving to continuously improve the quality of student life. It promotes social responsibility within Fordham and helps to advance the social, moral, and intellectual development of the members of the Rose Hill campus. SAFE plans events and develops campaigns to increase positive behaviors and decrease the harmful effects associated with alcohol and other drug use.

[Counseling and Psychological Services](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/counseling-and-psychological-services/)

The mission of Counseling and Psychological Services is to assist students in maintaining their optimum level of mental health and wellness so that they are best able to achieve their goals. They are located in the O'Hare Hall Lower Level and can be reached at 718-817-3725.

[University Health Services](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/)

Health Services provides accessible, high-quality care to Fordham students. Services include primary care, physical examinations, allergy clinic, immunizations, laboratory x-rays, and more. Please stop by O'Hare Hall Lower Level or call 718-817-4160 for more information.
