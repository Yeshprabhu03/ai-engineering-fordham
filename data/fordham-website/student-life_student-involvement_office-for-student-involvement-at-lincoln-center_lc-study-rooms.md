# LC Study Rooms

**Spring 2021**

For Spring 2021, Fordham University has additional spaces on campus that are available for students to study or participate in online classes if you are on campus. These spaces are open to commuters and residents. Please see the below list of spaces that are reserved for studying. If additional spaces become available or schedules of spaces change, this page will be updated too.

Since many of these spaces will be available for use by more than one student at a time, please bring headphones with a microphone component if possible when participating in Zoom classes in some of these spaces.
