# Winter Ball

![Winterball Decorative Candles](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Winterball_Decorative_Candles.png)

The annual semiformal dance for Fordham Lincoln Center undergraduates is Winter Ball hosted by United Student Government and Campus Activities Board.

**Date:** Friday, March 1st

**Time:** 7 p.m. - 12 a.m.

**Location:** [The Current at Pier 59](https://piersixty.com/contact/?utm_source=google&utm_medium=ppc&utm_campaign=General_-_Venue&utm_content=General_-_Venue_-_Current_-_Phrase&utm_term=pier%2059%20current&gad_source=1&gclid=Cj0KCQiAwvKtBhDrARIsAJj-kThdAgUsSpc7H2ydJHbMKcNI267L7O3eM7R2-ygYuhEkfASFOZMPHPwaArMOEALw_wcB) | Pier 59, Chelsea Piers, New York, NY 10011

**Cost:** $90 price for LC Fordham undergraduate students | $120 for guests

**Additional Fees:** Online credit card transactions do incur an additional fee. LC undergrad student tickets have a $4.60 surcharge. Guest tickets have a $5.60 surcharge.

**Ticket Sales:** Tickets are now on sale [online](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/w/event.aspx?id=2422&p=1). Tickets can be purchased in person on the LL Indoor Plaza or in the 140W Garden Lounge.
