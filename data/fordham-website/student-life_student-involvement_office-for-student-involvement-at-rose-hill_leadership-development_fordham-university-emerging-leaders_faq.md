# FUEL FAQ

-
Guided by the theme

*Leadership In Our Midst*, the Leadership Initiatives Committee will design the upcoming FUEL schedule to offer sessions that will develop essential skills for success at Fordham and beyond, while highlighting the leadership, resources, and opportunities available across the Fordham community. -
Graduates of the program receive a certificate of completion and acquire a full skill set ready to showcase in their future endeavors. They also gain access to a vibrant network of peers, faculty, and staff, providing valuable connections to strengthen and expand their Fordham community.

-
Faculty, staff, and administrators lead sessions designed to help participants strengthen their personal leadership skills, amplify student voices within the community, and inspire and empower the next generation of student leaders.

-
Students who have demonstrated leadership skills and want to further develop their potential are encouraged to apply. Applicants from all class years are welcome.
