# Rose Hill Leadership Initiatives Committee

![Leadership Initiatives Committee](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/LIC_LOGO.png)



Join the Leadership Initiatives Committee and help bring Leadership Weekend and the Fordham University Emerging Leaders (FUEL) Program to life! Collaborate with the Assistant Director for Student Leadership and Engagement in the Office for Student Involvement to build your skills in event planning, marketing, and mentorship while making a lasting impact on our campus community. To be eligible, applicants must be in good academic and disciplinary standing.


There are seven positions to choose from:

**Marketing & Brand Chair**

Bring our programs to life! The Marketing & Brand Chair leads creative outreach for Leadership Weekend and the FUEL Program—designing visuals, managing social media, and promoting opportunities that inspire campus involvement.

**Recruitment & Assessment Chair**

Help us find the next generation of leaders! The Recruitment & Assessment Chair drives participant outreach, oversees applications, and gathers feedback to strengthen and grow our leadership programs.

**Logistics & Operations Chair**

Be the organizer behind the magic! The Logistics & Operations Chair manages supplies, schedules, and setup for all events and programs, making sure everything happens seamlessly behind the scenes.

**Mentorship Chair**

Empower students through connection! The Mentorship Chair leads mentorship initiatives by pairing mentors and mentees, organizing networking opportunities, and fostering meaningful personal and professional growth.

**Special Events Chair**

Plan experiences that make an impact! The Special Events Chair coordinates Leadership Weekend and other signature events—managing logistics, brainstorming ideas, and ensuring every detail runs smoothly.

**FUEL Co-Chair: Programs & Planning**

Shape the future of the FUEL Program! This Co-Chair focuses on developing session content, collaborating with facilitators, and ensuring the program delivers engaging, high-impact leadership experiences.

**FUEL Co-Chair: Engagement & Operations**

Keep FUEL running strong! This Co-Chair focuses on participant communication, logistics, and engagement—ensuring every session runs smoothly and participants feel supported throughout their leadership journey.
