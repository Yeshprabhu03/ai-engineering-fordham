# Rose Hill Student Organizations

There are 170+ student organizations at Fordham Rose Hill. We provide students with meaningful interactions outside of the classroom that connect them with their peers and with other members of the University community.

All students are welcome to join any club here at Fordham. Learn more about [our mission](https://www.fordham.edu/about/mission-statement/) and [our strong commitment to access and non-discrimination](https://www.fordham.edu/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/title-ix-office/non-discrimination-policy/).

Check out the list of organizations to see the many ways you can get involved today! Current FCRH & GSB RH undergraduate students can also log into our online Rams Involved platform to join student organizations here: [communities.fordham.edu](http://communities.fordham.edu/)

Want to start a club that isn't listed? Learn more about the New Club Process at the link [here](https://usgrh.fordham.edu/committees/operations/).
