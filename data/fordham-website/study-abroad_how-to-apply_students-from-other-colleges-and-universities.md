# Visiting Students

## Students from Other Colleges and Universities

![A student wearing a Fordham London sweatshirt stands in front of the ocean](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/study-abroad/Fordham-London.png)

Applicants should have sophomore standing, a GPA of 3.0 or higher and a clear disciplinary record.

Check List

- Visit the program pages of Fordham's Study Abroad programs to learn more about courses, dates, and program locations:

[Fordham in London Gabelli School of Business](https://www.fordham.edu/study-abroad/programs/fordham-in-london/programs/gabelli-school-of-business/)

- Have a conversation with the appropriate person at your home institution about your plans to study abroad. This may be your major advisor, Dean, and/or study abroad office.
- Start your application with your home institution and contact Fordham's Office of Study Abroad (
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e2919697869b8380908d8386a2848d90868a838fcc878697)) about opening an application.
