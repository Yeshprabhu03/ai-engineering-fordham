# Gregory Minson, Chair

![Gregory Minson - London Centre Advisory Board](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/study-abroad/Minson-image.png)

Gregory Minson is the global chief operations officer of the Merchant Banking Division-Real Estate for Goldman Sachs, a position he has held since July 2019. Prior to his current position, he served as head of Europe, the Middle East, Africa and Asia-Pacific and global COO of the Realty Management Division at Goldman Sachs.

He began his career at Goldman Sachs in 1998 as a financial analyst, shortly after graduating from Fordham. In 2009, he was made a managing director of the firm.
