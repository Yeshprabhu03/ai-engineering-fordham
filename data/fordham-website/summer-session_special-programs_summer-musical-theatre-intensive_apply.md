# Apply to Summer Musical Theatre Intensive

Who is Eligible

College students and recent graduates are eligible to apply. Rising high school seniors with a G.P.A. of 3.0 or better are also welcome to apply (you will be asked to provide an unofficial high school transcript). Experienced actors and beginners are both welcome!

To Apply

**Click**. As part of the application, you will be asked to submit a 1-page cover letter and/or resume outlining your theatre experience and aspirations. Please also upload a video audition of a song.

[Here](https://pcsadmissions.fordham.edu/portal/summer_application)to apply2026 tuition rate is $4536. Once you are accepted to the program, a $300 deposit is required to secure your spot.

If housing is desired, an additional $50 housing deposit will be charged (housing is charged separately). The rate for 2025 was $1508 for five weeks. 2026 will be available soon.

Please note that high school students are not eligible for housing through this program. However, there is a two-week [Musical Theatre Workshop](https://www.fordham.edu/summer-session/pre-college-programs/summer-leaders-academy/program-and-activities/immersion-2/musical-theatre-workshop-/) that is residential.

When your application is received, you will receive an email notification. You will then be contacted by the director of the program to schedule an informal interview phone call.

Students not accepted to the program will be fully refunded.
