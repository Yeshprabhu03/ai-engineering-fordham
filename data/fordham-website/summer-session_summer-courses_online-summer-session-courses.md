Fordham's high-quality online courses allow students to earn credits while taking advantage of a variety of benefits for students, including:

- Opportunity: Online programs enable students who cannot attend in person to become part of Fordham's learning community, which in a Jesuit tradition, is dedicated to educating the whole person.
- Flexibility: With online courses, students can access course materials and complete assignments at a distance. This is particularly beneficial for students who have work or family commitments that may make it difficult to attend on-campus classes.
- Support: Online and hybrid programs are supported by a team of dedicated faculty and staff who are committed to helping students succeed. Students have access to a range of
[support services]( Flexibility: With online courses, students can access course materials and complete assignments at a distance. This is particularly beneficial for students who have work or family commitments that may make it difficult to attend on-campus classes.), including academic advising, tutoring, and technical assistance. We will connect you to the resources you need to complete your course successfully.

Most Summer Session courses are five weeks long and meet regularly during the designated class time on Zoom or another online meeting platform. Please note that the times listed are EST, and attendance is mandatory.

To find our online courses, please use the [Course Search](https://myapps.web.fordham.edu/pcs_summer/?_ga=2.254855048.186335420.1679409644-561621549.1644613242) tool. Under **Campus**, select **Virtual**. You can add additional criteria like Department or Session to narrow your results.
