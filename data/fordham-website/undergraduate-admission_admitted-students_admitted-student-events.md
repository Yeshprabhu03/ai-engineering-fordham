# Admitted Student Events

### Admitted to Fordham? Congratulations!

### Have questions? We're here for you.

Whether you've officially declared your college choice or you're still trying to decide which place is right for you, **our team will work to get you the information you need**.

We want to once again extend our hearty congratulations to you and your family, and we can't wait to see you - whether virtually or on campus!

## Weekly Admitted Student Visit Options:

**To join us for an on-campus event,** please go to our [on-campus visitation calendar](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit?_ga=2.217035030.1010799906.1670854689-539978480.1666098408) and select the date you would like to attend and register for your desired event. **To join us for a virtual event,** please go to our [virtual events calendar](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/virtualevents?_ga=2.175092130.1010799906.1670854689-539978480.1666098408) and register for your desired event.

[Admitted Student Presentation & Tour: Two Hours](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit)

**March 16-May 1**

- Offered 3-4 times weekly
- Admitted Student presentation led by an admission counselor

[Guided Tour: One Hour](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit)

[Guided Tour: One Hour](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit)

**January 20-May 1**

- Student-led campus tour
- Opportunity to speak with an admission counselor at the conclusion of your tour
*Please note:**Guided tours are offered Monday-Friday (excluding University closures). Self-guided tours are available Monday-Sunday. You can register for your desired option through our visitation calendar.*

[Self-Guided Tour: One Hour](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit)

[Self-Guided Tour: One Hour](https://admission.fordham.edu/portal/campusvisit)

- Offered weekdays and most weekends, year-round
- Arrive anytime within your chosen window
- Times vary, though a self-guided tour may take anywhere from 45-90 minutes
- This tour has no guide, though you will receive a tour booklet
- Opportunity to speak with an admission counselor at the conclusion of your tour
*Please note:**Residence Halls are***not**part of a self-guided tour.
