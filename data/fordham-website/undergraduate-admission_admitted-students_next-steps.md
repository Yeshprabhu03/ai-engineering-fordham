# Next Steps

We hope you’re proud of your accomplishments and excited to join our community. Now, what do you have to do between yesterday and when classes begin?

Newly admitted students typically have a lot of questions. On this site, we've included important deadlines and upcoming events that will help you plan your next steps in the enrollment process. You can also take a look at [a calendar of important upcoming dates](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/other-important-info/save-the-dates/). We look forward to welcoming you.

**Celebrate Your Offer of Admission!**[Check out important details about your offer of admission](http://admission.fordham.edu/status).**Visit Us**

Learn more about our campuses, programs, and student community at one of our[in-person or virtual events](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/admitted-student-events/)exclusively for admitted students.**Explore Your College**[Find out even more about your campus and college of choice](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/academics-and-advising/)—from academic offerings and research opportunities to advising support.**Join Fordham's Social Community**

Meet other admitted students and connect with current students and University staff! Check your email for an invitation to join Fordham's online social community for the Class of 2030.**Check out The University's Social Accounts**

You can follow the official[University Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/fordhamuniversity/)account or follow any of the[University's official channels](https://www.fordham.edu/about/social-media/).**Connect with Your Admission Counselor**

Still have questions? That’s why we’re here.[Look for your assigned admission counselor](https://admission.fordham.edu/register/admissioncounselors)and ask them whatever’s on your mind.**Pay Your Enrollment Deposit**

Ready to commit?[Make it official by submitting your deposit](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/make-your-deposit/). You’ll need your student ID from your admission and aid packet or digital admit letter.**Accept Your Financial Aid Package**

To[accept your financial aid package](https://fass.fordham.edu/NetPartnerStudent/PgHome.aspx), you’ll need your student ID from your admission and aid packet or digital admit letter.[Explore your financing options](/student-financial-services/undergraduate-financial-aid/first-year-students/).**Get Ready for Orientation**

Get excited for what’s next![Explore more info on orientation, residential life, our commuter community, and everything else](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/student-life/)that awaits you at Fordham.
