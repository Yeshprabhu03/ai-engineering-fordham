# Accelerated Degree Programs

**Did you know that as a Fordham undergraduate, you can earn a master’s degree with as little as one additional year of study?**

## Workforce-Ready Advanced Degrees

Whether you want to excel in STEM fields like **cybersecurity** and **data science**; learn with experts and scholars at the forefront of **psychology**, **economics**, **public media**, and other areas; jump-start your career as a **teacher** or **social worker**; or optimize your expertise with programs in **business analytics**, **marketing intelligence**, **quantitative finance**, and **more**, our accelerated programs will help you maximize your impact in the workplace.

Take the Next Step

If you’re interested in pursuing an accelerated advanced degree, get in touch with your advisor and one of the program contacts listed below to learn how to apply.
