## Where you will learn

At Fordham, you’ll choose more than just a major. You’ll select a field of study that will help you develop solutions and explore possibilities. Even if you’re not sure what you will study yet, we’re here to help you discover [what drives you and what matters most to you](/undergraduate-admission/majors-and-minors/).
