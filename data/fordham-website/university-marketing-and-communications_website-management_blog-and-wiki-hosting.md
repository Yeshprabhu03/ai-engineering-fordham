# Blog and Wiki Hosting

Blog and wiki hosting is available for schools and departments, as well as individual [faculty members](#faculty). It is the **responsibility of the department or individual** to build and maintain their blog site. View relevant hosting information and [WordPress resources](#blogsupport).

## For Schools and Departments

![Down Shot of Students Walking](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Down_shot_of_students_walking_.jpg)

You can now fulfill your need for a blog or wiki that exists outside of the Terminalfour content management system. Fordham University has procured cloud-based servers for you to host your blog or wiki. Through this platform, you can create and maintain a blog or wiki for your department or school.

The process to request this type of hosting:

**Discuss**your intention and commitment with the[Senior Director of Online Communications](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#bfd2c9d6d1dcdad1cb8e8fffd9d0cddbd7ded291dadbca).- Then you must
**choose a subdomain name**for your site

A subdomain is the URL that users will go to when they type your URL in a browser.

This must be in the form of .blog.fordham.edu or .wiki.fordham.edu

For example, in the subdomain*https://mysite.blog.fordham.edu*, the subdomain name is "mysite".

The chosen subdomain must conform to the established policy for subdomain. - Follow instructions on how to fill out and submit a
**Blog Request Form**- Login to
[My-Pages](/my-pages/) - Under My Pages, select "Tech Help"
- The
**Service Portal**will pop up in a separate browser tab* - Click on 'Service Catalog'
- Click 'Communication & Collaboration'
- Click 'ACE Omeka/Wordpress Request'
- Complete the Form details
- Click 'Request' to submit your blog request
-
The Web Committee will review and approve your subdomain name.


- Login to
- The Web Committee will inform you that your subdomain name has been approved. Please
**allow 1 - 2 weeks**for your blog to be created. You will then receive an email from WordPress, be sure to keep an eye on your inbox and your spam folder.

Each division is responsible for designing their WordPress blog. View samples of blogs:[IT](https://itnews.blog.fordham.edu/),[Theatre](https://theatre.blog.fordham.edu/),[Communication](https://cms.blog.fordham.edu/)[and](https://cms.blog.fordham.edu/)[Media Studies](https://cms.blog.fordham.edu/)
