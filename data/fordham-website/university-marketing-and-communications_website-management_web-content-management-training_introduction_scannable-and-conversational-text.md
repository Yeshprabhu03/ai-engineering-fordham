# Scannable and Conversational Text

## Scannable Text

- Short sentences. Short paragraphs.
- Descriptive headings and subheadings
- Lists, bullets, tables
- Descriptive link names (that can stand alone, outside of context;
[maps and directions](/about/maps-and-directions/),[edit web content](/university-marketing-and-communications/website-management/web-content-management-training/terminalfour-training/terminalfour-tutorials/edit-content/) - Consider what content type will be most relevant and useful to the user; be sure to draw attention to it
