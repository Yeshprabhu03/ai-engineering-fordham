# Deleting Sections

Do Not Trash an Item in Terminalfour if You Are Unsure of Its Usage!

**Prevent 404s (broken links):** Before you delete content or a page, follow these steps:

## Temporarily Delete or Take a Page Offline

- When viewing the Site Structure, navigate to the Page you would like to delete
- Click on the name of the page
**(General Tab)** - Select ‘Inactive’ from dropdown and click ‘Save’.
- On the next publish, this content will be taken offline.

![Take Web Page Offline](/media/home/commonly-used-images/training-screenshots/Take-Web-Page-Offline.jpg)


## Permanently Delete or Take a Pages and Content Offline

- When viewing the Site Structure, navigate to the Page with the designated content type
- Click the name of the Page and then the
**"Content" tab** - For each content type marked for permanent deletion, click the blue
**"Actions"**but then select**"Delete"** - Click the red ‘Delete Section’ button at the bottom of the page
- If the page is linked in other areas, a prompt will come up listing the linked pages.
- If the listed pages are outside of your area, you will not have access to make edits.
- Instead, cancel out of this section/do not delete the page. Make a note and
**contact the Web Team****. We will assist with taking this content offline and putting a redirect in place.**- If a prompt does not pop up, proceed with deleting this content.
- You should then click the ‘Actions’ button, select ‘Move’ and move the content type to the ‘Trash-Delete From System’ folder.

The "Purge" - The Web Team will start conducting monthly clean up sessions and
**purge/permanently delete**content in trash folders that is no longer needed.


![Take Individual Content Types Offline](/media/home/commonly-used-images/training-screenshots/Take-Individual-Content-Types-Offline.jpg)
