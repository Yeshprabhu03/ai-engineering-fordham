# Image Variants and Aspect Ratio

### Creating a Variant

This video tutorial will show the process for creating a variant for image(s). Creating variants allow the same image to be resized and cropped to accomodate various content types without changing the original image.

View the video tutorial on [How to Create an Image Variant](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1aaW79f9IUQZZmKTdAmtUt_0IrSCQA4tS/view?usp=sharing) (sign in to your University email account).
