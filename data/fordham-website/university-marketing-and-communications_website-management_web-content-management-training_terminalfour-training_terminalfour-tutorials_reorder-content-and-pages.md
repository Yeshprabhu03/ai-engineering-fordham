# Reorder Content and Pages

## Reorder Content and the Navigation

### Reorder Content Types

To reorder content types on a page, click on the icon to the left of the page name

- You can drag and drop in order of preference.
- Click 'Save changes'

Note: only 10 items are displayed at a time. Be sure to set the page count to 50 or more so you can see all content types.

### Reorder the Page Navigation

To reorder the page navigation, once you click on the page from the Site Structure:

- Click on 'More' > 'Child section' and reorder the navigation using the icon under the 'Order' column.
- Click Save changes
