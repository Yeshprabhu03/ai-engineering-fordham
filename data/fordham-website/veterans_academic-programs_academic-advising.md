# Academic Advising for Veterans

Fordham University has an unsurpassed reputation for quality in Undergraduate, Graduate, Masters, Certificate, and Professional Degree programs.

- Fordham provides the best, most convenient, and flexible course schedules for you and your family members.
- Fordham has dedicated academic advisors on all three campuses to help you take the next step and assist in choosing the best campus for the program you want.
- Your academic advisor can provide quick answers to your questions about admission requirements, with full and part-time schedules, including options for taking day, evening, weekend, and online classes.
- Your academic advisor will work with you to make sure all the necessary paperwork, including retrieving transcripts from any previous school you have attended, is completed in a timely fashion.

**For Undergraduate Academic Advising, please contact the advisor from your school:**
