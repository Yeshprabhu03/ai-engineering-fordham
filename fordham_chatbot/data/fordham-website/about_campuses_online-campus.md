# Online Campus

-
## Why study online?

- Receive a Fordham graduate degree or advanced certificate while living anywhere in the world
- Benefit from online study that incorporates live synchronous teaching and learning, and asynchronous study
- Earn your graduate degree faster through online modules that allow for the completion of multiple courses during each semester.
**Some degrees can be completed in as little as 12 months.** - Interact and network online with students from across the country and around the world, who share extensive industry and agency experience

-
## Advanced Graduate Certificates

Not ready for a full master’s degree?

**Take a short 12 or 15-credit advanced graduate certificate**- one of the fastest growing post-secondary credentials. With academic advisor approval, these graduate courses are applicable towards our master’s degrees.
