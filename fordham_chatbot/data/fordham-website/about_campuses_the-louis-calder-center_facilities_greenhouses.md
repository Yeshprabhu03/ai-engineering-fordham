# Calder Center Greenhouses

![Greenhouse at Calder Center - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/New_Greenhouse_pic_2.jpg)

Two large, temperature-controlled greenhouses are used for plant and aquatic ecology experiments. Our most recently constructed greenhouse has an automatic irrigation and lighting system. Two 1000-gallon cisterns collect rainwater for use in the greenhouse.
