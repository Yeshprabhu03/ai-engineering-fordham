# Calder Center Library

The Library at the Louis Calder Center has both journals and books (currently about 1250 volumes) available to faculty, staff and students at the LCC, as well as visiting scholars, with permission. Access is also available to students and researchers from other institutions with permission by sending an email to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#a4c7c5c8c0c1d6c7c1cad0c1d6e4c2cbd6c0ccc5c98ac1c0d1) or by phoning the Administrative Office: 914-273-3078, ext. 10.

We accept donations of recent books in natural history, ecology and conservation.
