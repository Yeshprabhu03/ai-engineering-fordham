# Summer Workshop Housing at the Calder Center

On-site housing is offered on a limited basis. Spaces will be filled in the order we receive registrations. For the Freshwater Algae Identification Workshop, rooms are offered as either double occupancy or single occupancy.

Each of our housing options offers free wifi access, along with a washer and dryer. There is a phone available for local calls only.

## 47 House

3 bedroom, 2 bathroom, 2 story building. Common areas: kitchen, dining area, living room.

## Calder Cabin

12 bedroom, 4 bathroom, 2-story building.

The common room on the second floor is furnished with a couch, chairs, tables and a flat screen T.V. An upper balcony is accessible from this room. The common area kitchen includes 2 sinks, 2 dishwashers, 2 refrigerators, 2 ovens w/stovetops and 2 microwaves. Adjacent to the kitchen is a dining area that can seat up to 12 people.
