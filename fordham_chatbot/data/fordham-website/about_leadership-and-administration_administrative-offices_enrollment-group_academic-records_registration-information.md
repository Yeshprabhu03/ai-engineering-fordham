# Registration Information

To register for courses, use the [Fordham.edu portal](https://my.fordham.edu/).

**Registration dates for Spring 2026**

Please note that your **opening date** for registration is based on **earned credits**, * not *including credits in progress from the courses

**you are currently taking**this semester.

However, your eligibility to register for courses that have a **restriction **based on **class year** (i.e., first year, sophomore, junior, and senior, for undergraduates) is based on **applied credits**, which includes **earned **credits * and *credits that are

**currently in progress**.
