# Bronx Climate Change Town Hall 2022

**Bronx Climate Justice Town Hall**

*Sept. 26, 2022, All Hallow's High School. Bronx, NY*

![](/media/home/Screen-Shot-2022-12-13-at-10.25.01-AM.png)


The Center for Community Engaged Learning sponsored "The Bronx Climate Justice Town Hall", a panel discussion on policies and actions needed to address the impact of climate change on the borough of the Bronx. The event, held at All Hallow's High School in the Bronx, aired live on BronxNet, the local public access television chanel.
