# Urban Agriculture Roundtable

**Urban Agriculture and Food Security Roundtable**

*Feb. 6, 2024*

*McShane Campus Center, Fordham University, Bronx, NY*

On February 2nd, CCEL hosted ![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/Screen-Shot-2024-02-02-at-1.33.55-PM.png)


*Urban Agriculture and Food Security*, a roundtable discussion, at the McShane Campus Center, which brought together so many of Fordham's local grassroots partners here in the Bronx.

With a focus on organizations and initiatives doing related urban farming, farmers’ markets, and sustainable food systems, the event centered on discussion and envisioning, building connections between non-profit organizations and government agencies at different levels that can provide support and expand on existing related programming.

Co-sponsors included

[Congressman Ritchie Torres](https://ritchietorres.house.gov/), the

[United States Department of Agriculture](http://usda.gov/)and the

[Mayor’s Office of Urban Agriculture](https://www.nyc.gov/site/agriculture/index.page)for their presentations examining strategies for enhancing opportunities for urban green space and food security.
