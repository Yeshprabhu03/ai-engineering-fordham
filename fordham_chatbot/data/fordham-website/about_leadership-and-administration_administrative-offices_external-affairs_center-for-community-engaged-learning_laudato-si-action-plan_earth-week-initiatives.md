# Earth Week Initiatives

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/Trees-NY-volunteer-planting.png)



### April 15th, 2023: Fordham Volunteer Activation

In an exciting collaboration, The Center for Community Engaged Learning at Fordham University partners with [Partnerships for Parks](https://cityparksfoundation.org/about-partnerships-for-parks/), a public-private partnership between City Parks Foundation and NYC Parks that supports neighborhood volunteers by providing the tools needed to advocate and care for neighborhood parks and green spaces.

**On Saturday, April 15th,** in anticipation of Earth Week 2023, we are activating the Fordham Community to engage at ** Poe Park (<- click for Google map), **located in the immediate vicinity on Grand Concourse between E. 192 St. and, E Kingsbridge Rd, Bronx, NY 10458 (

**ten minutes walking distance from Fordham University).**

** Sign-up to volunteer.** Different shifts and activities are available. Be prepared to start or finish your volunteering activities with dance lessons by the local community organization

[ORISHA](https://www.orishawholeness.org/about-us). You will be provided with all the necessary materials. The meeting location is the visitor center (follow the

[link](https://goo.gl/maps/9LxJruvn8Gays9Gg7)for the exact location).

**All participants must register and sign online waivers with NYC Parks through this**

[link](https://www.nycgovparks.org/reg/pfp-private/13671)to volunteer.

### April 19th, 2023: Caring for Community Trees and Greenspaces NYC ReLeaf Urban Forestry Event

Community organizations around the Bronx are seeking volunteers to help with tree plantings, tree care, community gardens, and other urban green spaces. Come and learn about projects going on in our area and the organizations coordinating them, then [sign up](https://www.signupgenius.com/go/10c0949aaae29aaf8c52-fordhams#/) to get involved.

Community organizations highlighting their volunteer opportunities include:

- NYC Parks - Natural Area Volunteers
- NYC Compost Project
- New York Botanical Garden
- Trees NY
- Bronx GreenUp
- Van Cortlandt Park Alliance
- Bronx is Blooming
- Woodlawn Cemetary

**Additional Details:**

**Parking**Parking is available for attendees who preregister. Walk-ins are welcome; a check-in table will be available at the event for last-minute guests.**COVID-19 requirements:**Fordham University requires visitors to be fully vaccinated for COVID-19, including booster shots. Proof of vaccination may be required to be shown upon entering campus. Masks are encouraged but not required.
