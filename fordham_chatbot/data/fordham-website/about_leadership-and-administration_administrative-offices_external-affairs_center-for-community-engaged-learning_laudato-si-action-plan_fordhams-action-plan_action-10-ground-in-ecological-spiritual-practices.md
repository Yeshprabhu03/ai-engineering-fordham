# Action 10: Ground-In Ecological Spiritual Practices

“Help us to rescue the abandoned and forgotten of this earth, so precious in Your eyes.”

- Pope Francis, from “A Prayer for Our Earth”![Library on Rose Hill campus](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/Copy-of-Fordham_102022_1639.jpg)

- Partner with Campus Ministry to offer a series of
**sustainability-focused retreat experiences**for students, faculty, administrators, and staff. **Expand Loyola Hall’s Ecological Examen of Consciousness program**to all residence halls and to the Commuting Students Association at Lincoln Center and Rose Hill.**Conduct annual reading groups**for faculty, students, administrators, and staff to engage with the text of*Laudato Si’*alongside other texts on climate change.
