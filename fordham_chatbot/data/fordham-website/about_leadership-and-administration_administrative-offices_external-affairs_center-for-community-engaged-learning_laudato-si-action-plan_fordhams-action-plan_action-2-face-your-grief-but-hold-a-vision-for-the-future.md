# Action 2: Face Your Grief but Hold a Vision for the Future

“We have to feel the full force of this new reality in our bones. There is a power to consciously bearing witness to all that is unfolding without turning away, and counter-intuitively, you may actually feel better about the situation when you deeply accept the reality of it.”

- Christiana Figueres and Tom Rivett-Carnac![Magi statue at Lincoln Center](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/KGamble-20111003-1572.jpg)

- Appoint a
**Sustainability Coordinator**who is responsible for implementing, sustaining, and evaluating the original Laudato Si’ Action Plan, and for updating the plan as necessary. - Convene an
**annual Laudato Si’ Sustainability Council**to lead action planning and implementation of the University’s sustainability plan and provide interdisciplinary, interdepartmental, and interschool perspectives, and to ensure work progresses appropriately. **Conduct****annual assessments of campus progress**toward established goals.
