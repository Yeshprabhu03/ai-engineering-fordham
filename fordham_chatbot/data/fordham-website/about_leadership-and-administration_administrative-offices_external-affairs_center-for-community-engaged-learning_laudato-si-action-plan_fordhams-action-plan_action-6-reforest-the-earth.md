# Action 6: Reforest the Earth

“Achieving balance on a global scale is still possible. We can be the generation to achieve it.”

- Christiana Figueres and Tom Rivett-Carnac![Rose Hill campus in the spring](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/fu-100908-0796-nef.jpg)

- Partner with community organizations (Youth Ministries for Peace and Justice, and Hester Street) to undertake a
**Bronxwide biodiversity and reforestation initiative**in Bronx green spaces. **Stop overwatering the campuses**and, where possible, work to promote indigenous and biodiverse planting on campus.**Reduce meat and grain consumption**in the cafeteria and campus dining locations by labeling food options with “environmentally friendly” or “sustainable” stickers to promote strong food choices by individuals; assess results.- Host and promote
**food awareness campaigns**, such as meatless Fridays and fasting days alongside events such as a community iftar, vegetarian challenges, etc. **Partner with Hunts Point Produce Market on a food-distribution program**and dashboard to deliver excess produce to small grassroots organizations in the Bronx and Upper Manhattan.
