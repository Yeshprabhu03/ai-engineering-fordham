# Action 7: Use Technology Responsibly

“As we grow more comfortable with machines being part of our lives, we will need to use technology responsibly, mindful of its power and influence, and ensure that proper governance systems are in place.”

- Christiana Figueres and Tom Rivett-Carnac![Buildings surrounding the plaza at Lincoln Center](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/center-for-community-engaged-learning/CShinn-20121001-0183.jpg)

**Update technologies**in classrooms, offices, and other campus buildings to be energy efficient.- Work toward
**LEED certification**for each building, tripling current certification by 2040. **Incorporate a sustainability panel into the International Conference on Cyber Security**, held every 18 months at Fordham, to explore the connections between advances in cybersecurity and sustainability goals.**Create a concentration or pathway that****connects artificial intelligence and machine learning with sustainability and ecology goals**.
