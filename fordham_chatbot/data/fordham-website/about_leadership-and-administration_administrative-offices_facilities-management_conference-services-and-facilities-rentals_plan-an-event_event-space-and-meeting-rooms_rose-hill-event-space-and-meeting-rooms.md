# Rose Hill Event Space and Meeting Rooms

Requests for space to be used for events or programs should be submitted through the Office for Student Involvement at Rose Hill. Requests should be made at least 2 weeks in advance of the requested date. Requestors will receive an email confirmation within 5 business days. The Office for Student Involvement will arrange for any audio/visual equipment needed in the McGinley Center rooms.

Spaces offered include:
