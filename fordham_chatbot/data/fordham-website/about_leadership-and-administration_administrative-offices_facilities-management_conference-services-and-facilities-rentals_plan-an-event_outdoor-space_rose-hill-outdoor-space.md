# Rose Hill Outdoor Space

Rose Hill located in the Bronx has multiple outdoor spaces that can be used for multiple purposes such as athletic events, outdoor picnics, concerts and gathering.

![Side View of Murphy Field for Soccer](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Murphy_Field_Soccer___SM.jpg)


**Murphy Field**

- Is one of our athletic fields where the Fordham’s lacrosse and soccer teams play.

**Jack Coffey Field**

- Is another athletic field were Fordham’s Football team plays and occasionally the baseball team.

![Students sitting on Edwards Parade](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/RH_Students_at_Edwards_Parade__MD.jpg)


**Edwards Parade**

- Is one of Fordham’s lawns were you can often see students laying out, playing frisbee, reading a book, hanging out with friends and more. Large events are also hosted here throughout the academic year.

**Martyrs Lawn**

- Is the other lawn at Fordham Rose Hill Campus. During nice days you can also find student hanging out with friends, reading a book, laying out and playing outdoor games. This locations is frequently used for outdoor events throughout the year such as spring weekend event, senior week events, large outdoor picnics and dinners.

**Bahoshy Field**

- Is Fordham’s baseball and football field.

- Telephone number
- Email address
- Date of event
- Time of event
- Proposed activity
- Equipment to be used
- Number of people expected

Student groups must first be approved by the Office for Student Involvement at Rose Hill. Requests should be made at least two weeks in advance.

All catering must be arranged through [Aramark](https://fordham.campusdish.com/Catering.aspx). Deborah Russelli will confirm the request by e-mail or telephone.
