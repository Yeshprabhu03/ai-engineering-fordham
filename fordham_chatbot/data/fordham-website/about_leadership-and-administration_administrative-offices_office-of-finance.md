# Office of Finance

The Office of Finance is responsible for the stewardship of Fordham's financial resources. We oversee the financial management and financial operations of the University including Rose Hill, Lincoln Center, and Westchester campuses, and Fordham London. Our groups include financial planning and analysis, accounting services, post-award grant management, strategic sourcing, accounts payable, payroll, travel and expense, treasury and risk, office services, internal audit, and the Fordham Investment Office.
