### Purchasing Method

Individual reservations for car rentals should be made through CTP either online through FINTREX via Concur Travel, or directly with a CTP agent. Reservations may also be made directly with a designated car rental agency using one of the discount codes above.

Refer to Fordham's [ Travel and Expense Policy](/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/office-of-finance/about-us/finance-groups/treasury-and-risk/travel-and-expense-reporting/travel-and-expense-policy/) for travel guidelines.

**Employee Purchasing Program**

There is an “Employee Purchasing Program” available through National Car Rental. You may utilize the discount codes listed above when renting a car for personal use.
