# Tent Rental

Stamford Tent & Event Services serves as the **official university preferred supplier** for all tenting requirements. They are dedicated to providing and designing **bespoke tent layouts** of varying dimensions and materials, meticulously tailored to accommodate the diverse needs of university-sponsored events. This includes, but is not limited to, prominent annual events such as **Jubilee and Homecoming**, as well as a range of student-focused activities like **Orientations and Spring Weekend**.


**Purchasing Method **

- Contact Vendor for a quote
- Submit a non-catalog purchase order in FINBUY
