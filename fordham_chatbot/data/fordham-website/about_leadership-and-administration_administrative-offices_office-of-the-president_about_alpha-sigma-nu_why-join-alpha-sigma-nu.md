# Why Join Alpha Sigma Nu?

ΑΣΝ is unique among honor societies. Your induction not only rewards your academic achievements, *but equally importantly*, your commitment to the values of your Jesuit education and service to others. Membership is a marker of your past accomplishments, as well as your future potential. Alpha Sigma Nu members represent the embodiment of what Jesuit education hopes to impart on its students.

Chapters provide opportunities for members to develop leadership skills, create positive change in their communities, more deeply commit to the mission of Jesuit education, and to have a unique connection with the Jesuit community.

Network through [Alumni Club](https://www.alphasigmanu.org/alumni/find-a-club/) events across the country, exclusive social media groups, and our online [Member Directory](https://www.alphasigmanu.org/member-login).

Stay connected to your values as a lifelong leader through events and online resources.

Take advantage of scholarships, ΑΣΝ Grants, internship scholarships, and advanced clearance in Federal Government jobs.
