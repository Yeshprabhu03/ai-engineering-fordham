# Brooke Thomas

## Executive Assistant to the President

![Brooke Thomas](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/office-of-the-president/brook_thomas.jpg)

Brooke Thomas serves as the Executive Assistant to President Tetlow. Prior to this role, Brooke was the Assistant and Project Lead for the Senior Vice President of Global Operations at fresh beauty, an LVMH Maison. She also has over a decade of experience as an educator and campus minister in Catholic schools.

Brooke originally hails from Ohio and received a B.A. in Communication Management from the University of Dayton. She settled in New York City and earned her M.A. from the Fordham Graduate School of Religion with a concentration in Justice and Peace Ministries and a focus on gender and religion in America.
