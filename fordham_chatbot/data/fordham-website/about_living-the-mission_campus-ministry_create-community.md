# Create Community

## Come create community with CM!

![3 students standing next to each other in front of trees, smiling](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/campus-ministry/Untitled-design.png)

Grow your community with new, valuable friendships! Through a variety of opportunities offered by Campus Ministry, you can meet and connect with other Fordham students seeking meaningful relationships. Learn about a variety of spiritualities, engage in relevant topics of our time, and discover what you believe in a supportive community of others on a similar journey!

Check out our various ministries and community groups by exploring this section's subpages!
