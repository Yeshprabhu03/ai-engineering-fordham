Imam Ammar brings over a decade of expertise in Islamic counseling and pastoral care to his role as Director of Muslim Life at Fordham University. His journey in Islamic studies has been enriched by teachings from esteemed scholars across the globe, shaping his profound understanding of Islamic principles and practices. Driven by a passion for nurturing inclusive and engaging Muslim communities, Imam Ammar is dedicated to creating a supportive environment where students can explore and strengthen their faith. He believes in the power of education, dialogue, and service to foster spiritual growth and meaningful connections among individuals of diverse backgrounds. Imam Ammar is excited to collaborate with the Fordham community to promote mutual respect, understanding, and social responsibility while empowering students to integrate their Islamic values into their academic and personal lives. His vision is to build bridges of interfaith and intercultural understanding, contributing positively to the vibrant tapestry of university life at Fordham.
