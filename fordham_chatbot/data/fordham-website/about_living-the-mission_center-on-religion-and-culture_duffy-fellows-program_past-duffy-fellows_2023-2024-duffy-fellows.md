# 2023-2024 Duffy Fellows

**Patrick Cullinan (FCRH 2024)**

*Discerning the Call: Change in the American Priesthood** *(documentary)

**Major: **Latin and Economics

**Concentration:** American Catholic Studies

Patrick Cullinan is deeply interested in Catholicism and its interaction with American culture, having rediscovered the faith during his time at Fordham and later received the sacrament of confirmation at the University Church. Cullinan and his research partner Jay Doherty built an oral history which explored the discernment process across several generations of American Jesuits.

**Amelia Medved (FCRH 2023)**

*A Breathing Place: Sanctified Burial Sites in New York City Public Spaces *(book publication)

**Major: **Environmental Studies and Visual Arts

Amelia Medved is passionate about environmental advocacy, specifically with a focus on parks equity and community-based ecological stewardship. Her research explored the use of public spaces as burial sites by religious communities throughout New York City.

**Madison Morris (FCRH 2024)**

*Every Creeping Thing** *(graphic novel)

Major: Theology and English

Concentration: Creative Writing

Madison Morris strives to create characters, situations, images that make readers ponder a question instead of rushing toward an answer. For her Duffy Fellow project, Morris authored and illustrated an original graphic novel revisiting the Biblical story of the Garden of Eden.
