# Initiative on Migrants, Migration and Human Dignity

![A group of Fordham professors standing and sitting together in front of a building with a colorful mural.](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/living-the-mission/Immigration-1280-final.jpg)


Fordham University’s **Initiative on Migrants, Migration and Human Dignity** believes that the dignity and uniqueness of all persons is core to the Fordham University mission. As such, we stand in solidarity with migrants and members of our community who are affected by migration policies.

Stay up to date with Fordham’s Initiative on Migrants, Migration, and Human Dignity.
