# Directions to Lincoln Center

Fordham University's Lincoln Center campus is located at the northwest corner of Columbus Avenue at 60th Street (113 West 60th Street, New York, NY 10023). The main entrance to the School of Law is located between Columbus Avenue and Amsterdam on 62nd Street (150 West 62nd Street, New York, NY 10023).

The main phone number for the Lincoln Center campus is 212-636-6000.

The Lincoln Center campus is home to Fordham College at Lincoln Center, the Gabelli School of Business, the School of Professional and Continuing Studies, the Graduate School of Education, the Graduate School of Social Service, and the School of Law.

## A Note on Congestion Pricing

Fordham’s Lincoln Center campus is located just outside of Manhattan’s Congestion Relief Zone. If you are driving to campus from the north, you can avoid a [congestion pricing toll](https://portal.311.nyc.gov/article/?kanumber=KA-03612) by staying and parking above 60th Street. If you’re driving to campus from other directions, it’s possible to avoid the toll, but it may involve taking an indirect route. For more information, see the section on reaching the Lincoln Center campus by car.

## GPS and Navigation Information

If you are planning to visit the Lincoln Center campus, please enter the following address into your GPS or other navigation system:

113 West 60th Street

New York, NY 10023

The directions will lead you directly to the main entrance of the campus. If you wish to avoid paying a toll in Manhattan, look for an option that does not include congestion pricing.

### To Reach the Lincoln Center Campus by Subway

The A, B, C, D and 1 subway trains all stop at 59th Street/Columbus Circle.

The campus is located one block west.

You may wish to view a subway map or a bus map available online through The MTA website.

### To Reach the Lincoln Center Campus by Bus

For local bus service take the M5, M7, M10, M11, M31 M57, or M104.

All stop close to Columbus Circle or Lincoln Center.

You may wish to view a subway map or a bus map available online through The MTA website.

### To Reach the Lincoln Center Campus by Ram Van

To get you back and forth between our Rose Hill and Lincoln Center campuses, Fordham University offers intercampus transportation via the Ram Van.

Call 212-636-6250 for schedule information.

### To Reach the Lincoln Center Campus by Plane

Fordham University is easily accessible from all the major airports in the New York metropolitan area: Kennedy International, LaGuardia, and Newark. The Riverdale/Jitney Limo service will provide transportation to the Rose Hill campus (718-884-9400). Taxi service to both the Rose Hill and Lincoln Center campuses is available from all airports.

You may wish to view additional information about airport transportation and other forms of public transportation available online through The Port Authority of New York and New Jersey website.

### To Reach the Lincoln Center Campus by Car

**From Points North, via the George Washington Bridge/95:** Join the Henry Hudson Parkway (West Side Highway/Route 9A) South. Exit at 79th Street (Boat Basin). At the second traffic light, turn right onto West End Avenue. Continue south to 65th Street and turn left. Turn right onto Columbus Avenue. Keep to the left on Columbus Avenue and turn left onto 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

**From Points West, via the Lincoln Tunnel*:** Take I-78 East to the New Jersey Turnpike North. Exit at the Lincoln Tunnel, #16E. Follow signs uptown. At 41st Street turn left. Turn right onto 10th Avenue and continue to 62nd Street. Turn right onto 62nd Street. A public parking garage is located on your left, across from the School of Law. To reach the main entrance of the Lincoln Center campus, proceed on 62nd Street to Columbus Avenue and turn right. Keep to the left side on Columbus Avenue and turn left onto 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

*This route includes a congestion pricing toll in Manhattan.

**From Points South, via the Lincoln Tunnel*:** Take Parkway North to Exit 142 (Union Tollbooth). Go to far right toll booths of Exit 142 for I-78 East. Follow I-78 East to the New Jersey Turnpike North. Exit at the Lincoln Tunnel, #16E. Follow signs uptown. At 41st Street turn left. Turn right onto 10th Avenue and continue to 62nd Street. Turn right onto 62nd Street. A public parking garage is located on your left, across from the School of Law.

To reach the main entrance of the Lincoln Center campus, proceed on 62nd Street to Columbus Avenue and turn right. Keep to the left side on Columbus Avenue and turn left onto 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

*This route includes a congestion pricing toll in Manhattan.

**From the East Side (Queensboro Bridge, Midtown Tunnel)*:** Drive uptown to 66th Street and turn left. Drive through Central Park. Continue on 66th Street to Columbus Avenue. At Columbus Avenue, turn left. Keep to the left on Columbus Avenue and turn left at 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

*This route includes a congestion pricing toll in Manhattan.

**From Brooklyn:** Brooklyn Queens Expressway to Brooklyn Battery Tunnel. Out of the tunnel, bear left, then turn right onto West Street. Follow West Street to the Henry Hudson Parkway North (West Side Highway). Exit at 54th Street*. Take 10th Avenue uptown to 62nd Street. Turn right onto 62nd Street. A public parking garage is located on your left, across from the School of Law. To reach the main entrance to the rest of the Lincoln Center campus, proceed on 62nd Street to Columbus Avenue and turn right. Keep to the left side on Columbus Avenue and turn left onto 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

*This route includes a congestion pricing toll in Manhattan if you exit at 54th Street. You can avoid this toll if you exit at 79th Street, then follow the directions above from Points North.

**From Long Island*:** Long Island Expressway to the Midtown Tunnel, to 34th Street West. Turn right onto 10th Avenue. Take 10th Avenue uptown to 62nd Street. Turn right onto 62nd Street. A public parking garage is located on your left, across from the School of Law. To reach the main entrance to the rest of the Lincoln Center campus, proceed on 62nd Street to Columbus Avenue and turn right. Keep to the left side on Columbus Avenue and turn left onto 61st Street. A public parking garage is on the right. The main entrance to the University is across Columbus Avenue at the northwest corner of 60th Street.

*This route includes a congestion pricing toll in Manhattan.
