## To Reach Fordham Westchester by Train

There are two Metro-North stations that are close to Fordham Westchester - they are the "**White Plains**" (on the Harlem Line) or "**Port Chester**" (on the New Haven Line) stations.

For train schedules, please visit the [MTA](http://new.mta.info/mnr) website.

From either train station, it is a short taxi or bus ride to the Fordham Westchester Campus.

## To reach Fordham Westchester by Bus

The Westchester County Bee Line Bus system has three routes to get to the Fordham Westchester campus. The routes serving Fordham Westchester are the Local 12, Local 13 and Shuttle Loop A. For Bee Line Bus schedules, please visit the [Westchester Bee Line Bus](http://transportation.westchestergov.com/timetables-and-maps) website.
