# Office of Sustainability

![Office of Sustainability logo](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/sustainability-at-fordham/Fordham-Sustainability-Logo.png)

## Mission

The Office of Sustainability at Fordham University is dedicated to advancing green initiatives, aligning with the university's commitment to sustainability outlined in AASHE STARS. Our mission is to optimize energy usage in a campus-like setting guided by environmental stewardship while nurturing a culture of sustainability among students, faculty, and staff.

## Guiding Principles

As a participant in AASHE STARS, Fordham University adheres to rigorous sustainability standards. Our office integrates these benchmarks into our initiatives, ensuring that our projects contribute meaningfully to the university's sustainability goals.

## Internship Program and Project Goals

** **Each project within our internship program is aligned with the United Nations' 2030 Sustainability Goals, providing students with a comprehensive understanding of their role in addressing global challenges.
