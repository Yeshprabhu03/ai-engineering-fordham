# Office of Sustainability Projects

The Office of Sustainability advocates for CO2 reduction projects utilizing cost savings from the utility budget, subsidies, and PPAs. We purchase utility commodities and address NYC CO2 reduction regulatory requirements.

**Upcoming Projects**

- Solar Rainwater Capture System at Lombardi Center and McShane Center
- LED lights at Rose Hill Sports Fields

**Past Projects**

- Solar Installations
- Staten Island Community Project
- Rose Hill Parking Garage
