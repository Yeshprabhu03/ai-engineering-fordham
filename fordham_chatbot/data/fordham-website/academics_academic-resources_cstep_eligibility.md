# CSTEP Eligibility

## Eligibility Requirements

![CSTEP Scholar Award Ceremony](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/CSTEP_Eligibility_Page.jpg)

**To be an eligible applicant for the CSTEP program, you must:**

- Students must meet the minimum 12-month New York State residency requirement.
- Be a currently enrolled Fordham University student.
- Meet the
[income guidelines](https://www.nysed.gov/sites/default/files/income-eligibility-criteria-21-26.pdf)set by the New York State Education Department.- Your parent/guardian’s

Your family receives
