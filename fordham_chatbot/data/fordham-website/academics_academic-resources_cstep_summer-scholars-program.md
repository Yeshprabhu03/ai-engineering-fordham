# Summer Scholars Program

![Male Student in Labcoat](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Male_Student_in_Labcoat.jpg)

Want to get a head start on your first year?

The CSTEP Summer Scholars program is a free residential program for incoming Fordham first-years interested in pursuing careers in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM), as well as health and other licensed professions. This full-time program is designed to help you transition from high school to the rigors of college.

## Program Highlights

- Dates: July 2026
- A 3-credit Fordham course that satisfies a university core requirement. Students can select one of two tracks:
**Liberal Arts Track**- THEO 1000: Faith & Critical Reason**Pre-Health Track**- BISC 1000: Foundations of Biology

- College Skills seminar
- Guest speakers in CSTEP-targeted fields
- Individualized academic counseling and career planning
