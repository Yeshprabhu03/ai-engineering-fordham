**Culture Club**

Guided field trips give IALC students an insider's view of New York.

**Learning Technology**

Fordham's [digital learning facilities](/academics/academic-resources/language-learning-center/) offer the most up-to-date multimedia learning technology.

**Fordham Libraries**

Hundreds of thousands of [scholarly resources](/resources/libraries/) in every subject are a click away.

**Fitness Centers**

Discounted access to Fordham's [fitness centers](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/), including the Olympic-sized pool at Rose Hill.

**Location**

Our central location is an easy commute from every neighborhood, making [housing options](/academics/academic-resources/esl/tuition/housing-alternatives/) plentiful.

**Academic Workshops**

Weekly grammar, pronunciation, conversation, and TOEFL workshops are free for all IALC students.

**Language Exchange**

Make friends with Fordham students who want to learn your first language and improve proficiency fast.
