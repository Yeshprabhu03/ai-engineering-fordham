# Via Fordham Fees and Housing

## 2025 - 2026 Academic Year

**Total Tuition:** the total tuition is approximately $10,800 per semester. Program applicants are not eligible for financial aid.

University Fees

The following 2025-2026 academic year fees are listed for reference. Prices are subject to change.

| Estimated Total Tuition | $10,800 per semester |
| Housing |
$10,382 per Fall/Spring semester for double occupancy room. $1,500 summer 7-week term for double occupancy room. |
| Meal Plan | $8,060-$9,330 depending on the meal plan. Joining the campus meal plan is not required. |
| Medical Insurance | $4,650 per academic year |
| General Fee | $648 per year |
| Technology Fee | $680 per year |
| Late Payment Fee (Monthly) | $15 or 15% on the outstanding balance, whichever is greater. |
