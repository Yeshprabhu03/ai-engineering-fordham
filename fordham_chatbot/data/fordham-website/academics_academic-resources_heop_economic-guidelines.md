# Economic Guidelines

Economic guidelines mandated by the New York State Education Department are set up, as indicated below, according to the number of people in the household. Money earned by a student is not included in this guideline unless the student supports or helps support the household.

Please consult the [Financial Aid Office](/student-financial-services/) or [HEOP](/academics/academic-resources/heop/contact-us/) concerning any clarifications needed on the guidelines.

| Household Size | 2024 Income |
|---|---|
| 1 Person in Family/Household | $28,953 |
| 2 Persons in Family/Household | $39,128 |
| 3 Persons in Family/Household | $49,303 |
| 4 Persons in Family/Household | $59,478 |
| 5 Persons in Family/Household | $69,653 |
| 6 Persons in Family/Household | $79,828 |
| 7 Persons in Family/Household | $90,003 |
| 8 Persons in Family/Household | $100,178 |

**For 2026-27, add $10,178 for each family member in excess of 8.*

Learn more about the [Economic Eligibility Criteria for Opportunity Program](http://www.highered.nysed.gov/kiap/colldev/HEOP/).
