# International Faculty, Staff, and Scholars

The Office for International Services (OIS) assists with the immigration needs of all visitors (non-U.S. citizens). This includes visiting scholars/researchers and instructional staff.

If you have any questions, please contact the office at 718-817-3145 or at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#8ee1e7fde4fdede6e1e2effccee8e1fceae6efe3a0ebeafb).

## Green Card Process

Fordham University's Office of Legal Counsel can assist eligible employees with the Green Card process. If you believe you might qualify, please have your Department Chair contact the Office of Faculty Affairs for further guidance.

You can learn more about faculty employment policies by visiting the Office of Faculty Affairs Policies webpage.

## O-1 Filing

Fordham University's Office of Legal Counsel can assist eligible employees with O-1 Visa filings. If you believe you might qualify, please have your Department Chair contact the Office of Faculty Affairs for further guidance.
