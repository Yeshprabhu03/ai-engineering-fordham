# Navy ROTC Career Opportunities

U.S. Navy and Marine Corps Officers are some of the most well-respected men and women who serve our country.


Leadership opportunities, world-wide travel, superior benefits and excellent credentials are just a few enticements for becoming an Officer.


The Navy and Marine Corps offer careers that match the diverse talents and interests of NROTC students.


As long as you are serving as an officer in the Navy or Marine Corps, you will receive world-class training, exceptional benefits, and the experience of a lifetime.

Navy Careers:

[Surface Warfare Officer](https://www.navy.com/careers/surface-warfare-officer-swo)[Surface Nuclear Officer](https://www.navy.com/careers/nuclear-officer)[Nuclear Submarine Officer](https://www.navy.com/careers/nuclear-officer)[Naval Aviator (Pilot)](https://www.navy.com/flynavy)[Naval Flight Officer](https://www.navy.com/flynavy)[Special Warfare Officer (Navy SEAL)](https://www.navy.com/careers/navy-seal)[Special Operations Officer (Explosive Ordnance Disposal)](https://www.navy.com/careers/explosive-ordnance-disposal-technician)

Visit [U.S. Marine Corps Careers](http://www.marines.com/officer) for more information.
