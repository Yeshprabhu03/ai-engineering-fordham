# SRCD Comment to Proposed Rule Making Changes

Read the [Society for Research in Child Development's (SRCD)](http://www.srcd.org/) comments on the implications of the [Advanced Notice of Rulemaking (ANPRM)](https://www.hhs.gov/ohrp/humansubjects/anprm2011page.html) to the responsible conduct of biomedical and social-behavioral research involving infants, children and adolescents below. The task force responsible for drafting these comments was chaired by Ethics Center director
[Dr. Celia Fisher](/academics/departments/psychology/faculty-and-staff/celia-b-fisher/).
