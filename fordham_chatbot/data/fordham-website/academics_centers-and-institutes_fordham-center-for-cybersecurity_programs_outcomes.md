# M.S. Cybersecurity Program Outcomes

This list is representative of the main programmatic learning outcomes from the [M.S. in Cybersecurity program](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/academic-programs/graduate-programs/master-of-science-in-cybersecurity/). These outcomes are vital for our students to experience before entering the cybersecurity field and workforce.

### Learning Outcomes:

- Essentials of securing and sustaining the complex cyberspace ecosystem
- Process of turning data into actionable knowledge
- Technological expertise and practical training
- Exposure to the ten Common Bodies of Knowledge for CISSP certification
