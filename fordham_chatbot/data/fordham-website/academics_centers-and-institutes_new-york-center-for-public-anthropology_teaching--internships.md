# New York Center for Public Anthropology & Sociology Teaching and Internships

## Teaching

Our department offers Community Engaged Learning courses that offer opportunities for our students to create meaningful relationships with our neighborhood partners. Examples include: Introduction to Cultural Anthropology: Community Engaged Learning (ANTH 1100), Language and Culture: Community Engaged Learning (ANTH 1413), The Anthropology of Food: Community Engaged Learning. (ANTH 2800).

## Internships

The Demystifying Language Project offers competitive internships to our Fordham students and our partner school, UMass-Amherst.
