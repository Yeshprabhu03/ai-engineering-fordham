# Orthodox Christian Studies Affiliated Faculty

The following Fordham Faculty have developed courses specifically for the undergraduate, interdisciplinary minor in Orthodox Christian Studies. They also participate in the intellectual life of the Center by mentoring students, organizing public events, and evaluating applications for various Center-sponsored fellowships and awards.
