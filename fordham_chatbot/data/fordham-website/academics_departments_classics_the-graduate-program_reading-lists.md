# Classics Graduate Program Reading Lists

The works on the reading list are the basis of the translation exam. The reading list will vary depending on the student's degree track and academic interests. For example, a student more inclined towards Greek will have a longer Greek reading list and a shorter Latin reading list, and vice-versa. A Ph.D. student will also have a larger reading list than an M.A. student. Please select the appropriate list from the menu below.
