# James Jennewein

-
BFA from the University of Notre Dame

MFA from the UNIVERSITY OF CALIFORNIA, RIVERSIDE (Palm Desert campus)

Creative Writing and Writing For the Performing Arts Low-Residency MFA Program

-
Jim Jennewein has written extensively for film and television. His film credits include: “The Flintstones,” “Richie Rich,” “Major League II,” “Getting Even With Dad,” and “Stay Tuned.” His novels include: “Runewarriors: Shield of Odin,” (2008) “Runewarriors: Sword of Doom” (2009) and “Runewarriors: Ship of the Dead,” (2010) published by HarperCollins. He lives in NYC and teaches at Fordham University.
