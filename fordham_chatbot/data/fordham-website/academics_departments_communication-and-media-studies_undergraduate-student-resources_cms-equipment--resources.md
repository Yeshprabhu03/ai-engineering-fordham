# CMS Equipment & Resources

The CMS Department provides access to professional-grade production equipment to support your coursework and projects. This page outlines everything you need to know about accessing, checking out, and returning departmental equipment.

Equipment is offered by the CMS Department and not Fordham University or the Visual Arts department. All checkout policies and procedures are managed by the CMS Department.

### Eligibility

Equipment is available to CMS majors, CMS minors, or students currently enrolled in a CMS course. You **must** meet at least one of these criteria to access departmental equipment.

**Before requesting equipment, please review the checkout policies, request procedures, and available equipment listed below.**

**Questions?** Contact Chris Vicari at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#4a293c23292b3823780a2c25382e222b27642f2e3f) (Lincoln Center) or Hopeton Campbell at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e18982808c9183848d8da1878e938589808ccf848594) (Rose Hill).

**Checkout Period**- 3-day checkout period for all equipment
- All equipment must be returned to the designated location within this timeframe
- No exceptions without prior approval

**Responsibilities**- You are responsible for the equipment from checkout to return
- You are responsible for covering the cost of damages and missing equipment
- Equipment cannot be shared with other students
- Equipment must be returned in the same condition it was checked out

**Consequences for Late Returns****Grade penalties may be issued**by your professors if equipment is not returned within the requested time period- Repeated late returns may result in loss of equipment checkout privileges
