# Communication and Media Studies Department Study Abroad Program

Communication and Media Studies students are encouraged to enhance their educational experience by studying in other countries and cultures.

Many CMS majors take advantage of study abroad opportunities around the world after their sophomore year -- for a semester, a year, or a summer -- through [Fordham University's own programs](/study-abroad/programs/) or [approved exchange programs](http://internationalprograms.fordham.edu/index.cfm?FuseAction=Programs.SearchResults&Program_Name=&Program_Type_ID=1&pi=%7F&pc=%7F&pr=%7F&pt=%7F&p_10002=%7F&p_10002_t=MULTI&p_10005=%7F&p_10005_t=MULTI&p_10006=%7F&p_10006_t=MULTI&p_10007=%7F&p_10007_t=SELCT&p_10008=%7F&p_10008_t=MULTI&p_10009=%7F&p_10009_t=MULTI&p_10011=%7F&p_10011_t=MULTI&p_10012=%7F&p_10012_t=MULTI&p_10030=Communications%7F&p_10030_t=MULTI&Sort=Program_Name&Order=asc&pp=10002%2C10005%2C10006%2C10007%2C10008%2C10009%2C10011%2C10012%2C10030) with international partners specifically designed for CMS majors. For detailed application instructions, visit our [Applying for Study Abroad page](/academics/departments/communication-and-media-studies/undergraduate-student-resources/cms-study-abroad/applying-for-study-abroad-with-cms/).
