# 3-2 Engineering Program

Special Track In Computer Science Major For Students in the Combined Plan in Engineering

### Short Outline

CS majors in the 3-2 program at Fordham take six courses offered by other departments at Fordham in order to satisfy the equivalents of the Columbia courses required of all majors and seven courses in the computer science department at Fordham.

### Course Requirements

- Department of Mathematics
- MATH 1206 - Calculus I (4 credits)
- MATH 1207 - Calculus II (4 credits)
- MATH 2001 - Discrete Mathematics (4 credits)
- MATH 2004 - Multivariate Calculus I (4 credits)

- Department of Physics
- PHYS 1701 - Physics I (3 credits)
- PHYS 1702 - Physics II (3 credits)

- Department of Computer Science
- CS 1600 - Computer Science I (3 credits)
- CS 1610 - Computer Science I LAB (1 credit)
- CS 2000 - Computer Science II (3 credits)
- CS 2010 - Computer Science II Lab (1 credit)
- CS 2200 - Data Structures (4 credits)
- CS 3400 - Java Programming (4 credits)
- CS 3593 - Computer Organization (4 credits)
- CS 4080 - Computer Algorithms (4 credits)
- CS 4090 - Theory of Computation (4 credits)
- CS 4615 - Data Communication and Networks (4 credits)


It is important for freshmen entering the 3-2 program to speak with [Dr. David Swinarski ](http://faculty.fordham.edu/dswinarski/)(LC) or [Dr. Damian Lyons](http://storm.cis.fordham.edu/~lyons/) (RH) first.
