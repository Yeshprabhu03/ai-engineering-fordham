# Electronic Mail

Your account on the Departmental server includes an electronic mail account. Details about how to use it are given
[in a following section](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/educational-resources/cis-systems/electronic-mail/reading-and-sending-unix-mail/). However, many people prefer to continue using their Fordham email or a personal email account with a commercial provider. If so, you should
[set up forwarding](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/educational-resources/cis-systems/electronic-mail/forwarding-your-unix-mail-to-another-address/) of your Unix mail to that account. By default, your incoming Unix mail is
[checked for spam.](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/educational-resources/cis-systems/electronic-mail/spam-filtering/).
