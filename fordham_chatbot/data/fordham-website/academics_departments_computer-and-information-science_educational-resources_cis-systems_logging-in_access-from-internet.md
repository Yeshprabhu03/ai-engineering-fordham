# Access from Internet

You can log in to the Department's servers from your home computer, if it is connected to the Internet. If you have your own Internet Service Provider (ISP) at home, follow their directions to connect to the Internet. Then follow the directions below for connecting to the Department's servers using the
[remote desktop capability](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/educational-resources/cis-systems/logging-in/access-from-internet/remote-desktop-connection/) or using
[Secure Shell (SSH)](/academics/departments/computer-and-information-science/educational-resources/cis-systems/logging-in/access-from-internet/connection-using-secure-shell/).
