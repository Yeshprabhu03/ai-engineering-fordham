# Design and Technology Facilities

**Collaborative Space**In Martino Hall (45 Columbus Ave), 7th floor, you will find the collaborative space for faculty and our students. There are tables and lounge spaces for working and hanging out.

**Computer Labs**

At Lincoln Center, you will find the mac lab in Lowenstein LL310. Additional macs are in LL308. At Rose Hill, you will find the Walsh mac lab in FMH 317. The Rose Hill lab is not open on the weekend, but you can find other computer labs on campus

[here](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/computer-teaching-labs/).

**Equipment**

To check out equipment at Lincoln Center, please go to

[CMS Undergraduate Student Resources](/academics/departments/communication-and-media-studies/undergraduate-student-resources/cms-equipment--resources/)to see information on checkout policies and available equipment.

**LITE Center**

At Rose Hill, students have free access to cutting-edge technologies and spaces at the LITE Center: collaboration areas (lounge, TVs, and Jamboards), recording rooms, XR, and makerspace including 3D printers, engraver, large format plotter, and other equipment. Location: Walsh Library, Lower Level, Room 014.

At Lincoln Center, students can find the LITE annex: VR and Jamboard and a makerspace with 3D scanning/printing. Location: Lowenstein building, Room 416 [located within the Faculty Technology Center].

Both locations are open Monday – Thursday 9 a.m. – 5 p.m. You can find more information

[here](/information-technology/it-services/academic-technology/emerging-ed-tech/).
