# Design and Technology Newsletter

**Stay up to date with events, news, and opportunities. **

The New Media and Digital Design program sends out a newsletter at least once a month with events, updates, and opportunities for NMDD faculty and students.

Majors and minors are automatically added to the newsletter email list, but if you are not receiving this email you can sign up below.
