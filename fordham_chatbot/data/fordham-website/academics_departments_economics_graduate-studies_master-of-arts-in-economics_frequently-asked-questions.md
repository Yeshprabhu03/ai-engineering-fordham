# M.A. in Economics Frequently Asked Questions

**Can I apply for an M.A. degree without an Economics major.**

Yes. To be successful in our program, however, you should complete the minimum requirements listed on the [Course and Degree Requirements page](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gsas/economics/ma/?_ga=2.23885562.1990384956.1556542992-501728368.1539882202).

**Can I apply for the M.A. degree with a three year B.A. or B.S. degree?**

Yes. So long as you have a diploma (in-hand or forthcoming) from an accredited university for your B.A. or B.S., you are eligible to apply.

**Do you offer funding or graduate assistantships?**

At this time, unfortunately, we are unable to offer funding or graduate assistantships for M.A. students.

**How fast can I complete the degree?**

If you take four courses each semester and two courses in the summer, you can complete the M.A. degree in one calendar year (12 months).

**Is there a time limit on completing the degree?**

You can complete the degree on a part time basis with one or two courses per semester and summer. There is a time limit of 5 years from start to finish of the M.A. degree.

**Do you offer job placement services?**

We have a career placement office that organizes twice yearly career fairs and we have a network of Fordham M.A. Economics alumni in NYC, across the US, and abroad, who can help find placements and provide advice.

**Do you have a cut-off for GRE scores or GPA?**

No. We consider each application as a whole and read letters of recommendation and statements of purpose carefully.

**Do I have to take your required courses at Fordham?**

In general, yes. Please see the [GSAS policy handbook](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gsas/resources/handbook/) for more details.

**Is your CIPS code STEM compatible?**

Our M.A. degree in economics is designated as a STEM program.. Currently our CIPS code allows two years of OPT extension for international students.
