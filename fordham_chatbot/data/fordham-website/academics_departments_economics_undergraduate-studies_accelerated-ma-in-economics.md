# Accelerated MA in Economics

The Accelerated M.A. program in Economics allows selected economics majors to obtain both their B.A. and M.A. degrees in five years. This advanced training in economics prepares students for careers as economists in both the private and government sectors.

For more information, please consult the [GSAS Accelerated Master's Programs page.](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/academics/accelerated-masters-programs/)
