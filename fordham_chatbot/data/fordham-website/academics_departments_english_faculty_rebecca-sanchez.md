# Rebecca Sanchez

### Professor

BA, DePaul University; PhD, University at Buffalo

Research and Teaching Interests: Transatlantic modernism; disability studies; poetics

-
[Professor Sanchez](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#2755544649444f425d151f67414855434f464a09424352)'s research interests include transatlantic modernism, disability studies, and poetics. She is the recipient of a 2015-16 AAUW American Fellowship, and her work has appeared in journals including M*odern Language Studies, American Literary Realism,*, and the[M/C: A Journal of Media and Culture](http://journal.media-culture.org.au/index.php/mcjournal/article/viewArticle/258)*CEA Critic*. Her first book,, is now available from New York University Press.[Deafening Modernism: Embodied Language and Visual Poetics in American Literature](http://nyupress.org/books/9781479805556/)
