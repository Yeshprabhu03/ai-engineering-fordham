# Shubhangi Mehrotra

![Shubhangi Mehrotra](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/english/Shubhangi-Garg-Mehrotra.jpg)

### Advanced Lecturer

BA, English Honors - University of Delhi; Bachelor of Education- GGSIP University; MA, Annamalai University; PhD, University at Buffalo (SUNY)

Areas of Interest: Disability Studies, Social and Environmental Justice, Climate Fiction, Human Rights, Decolonization, Global South, Composition and Rhetoric, Digital Rhetoric, Creative Writing



-
Shubhi is a Lecturer in the English Department specializing in Disability Studies. She teaches courses on rhetoric and composition, multimodal literacy, social and environmental justice, and disability studies. Her teaching has been recognized by the

[Alpha Chi Omicron Teaching Society](/academics/departments/english/undergraduate/alpha-chi-omicron/teaching-awards/). Shubhi's pedagogical research examines the decolonization of the English language, and advocates for disability justice and accessibility in composition classrooms.
