# Vlasta Vranjes

![English Professor Vlasta Vranjes](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/english/Vlasta-Vranjes.jpg)

### Assistant Professor

BA, Marquette University; PhD, University of California, Berkeley

Research and Teaching Interests: Victorian literature and culture; nineteenth-century British novel; theories of nationalism, cosmopolitanism, and empire; gender studies; law and literature

-
[Vlasta Vranjes](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#8dfbffece3e7e8fecdebe2ffe9e5ece0a3e8e9f8)'s current research examines literary responses to the role marriage legislation played in shaping nineteenth-century Englishness. She is particularly interested in complex forms of English nationalism and cosmopolitanism as revealed in both literary renditions of and cultural debates on English marriage. Her current book project is titled*English Vows: Marriage Law and National Identity in the Nineteenth-Century Novel,*and her work has been published in such journals asand[Journal of British Studies](https://www.cambridge.org/core/journals/journal-of-british-studies/article/abs/english-cosmopolitanism-andas-nationalism-the-great-exhibition-the-midvictorian-divorce-law-reform-and-brontes-villette/5561C2C39C7264A80C3AEFDB0B7DFC8D)*Clio: A Journal of Literature, History, and the Philosophy of History*.
