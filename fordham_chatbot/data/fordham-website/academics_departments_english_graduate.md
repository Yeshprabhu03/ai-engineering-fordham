# English Graduate Department

At Fordham’s Department of English, we offer more than just advanced degrees—we provide a transformative educational experience that blends rigorous academic training with personalized mentorship.**Elevate Your Expertise with Our Graduate Program**

Our programs are designed to cater to your unique academic and professional aspirations. The English Department offers the following graduate programs:

Join a doctoral program tailored to foster scholarly excellence, teaching expertise, and professional formation in a collaborative and supportive community environment. Our renowned faculty are as dedicated to teaching and mentorship as to developing and fostering groundbreaking research.[Ph.D. in English](/academics/departments/english/graduate/doctorate-phd/):Immerse yourself in a dynamic curriculum that culminates in a capstone writing project. Work closely with our esteemed faculty to explore your fields of interest and develop your research and analytical skills. This program is ideal for those aiming to deepen their literary knowledge and prepare for various professional paths.[M.A. in English](/academics/departments/english/graduate/master-of-arts-ma/):Complement your graduate work within English with this credentialized certificate program in writing pedagogy. The program is ideal for students whose future goals include scholarship, teaching, administration, and/or consulting related across a range of academic, professional, and community settings.[Advanced Certificate in Rhetoric and Writing](/academics/departments/english/graduate/certificate-in-rhetoric-and-writing/):

Our students benefit from:

**Intimate Seminar-Style Courses:**Small,[seminar-style courses](/academics/departments/english/graduate/current-courses/)facilitate student-centered exploration and meaningful interaction with our esteemed faculty. These resources are designed to help you navigate diverse professional worlds with confidence.**Job Placement Assistance:**Benefit from the expertise of our dedicated Director of Placement and Professional Development, who provides tailored support to help you transition smoothly into the[professional arena](/academics/departments/english/graduate/doctorate-phd/placement/).
