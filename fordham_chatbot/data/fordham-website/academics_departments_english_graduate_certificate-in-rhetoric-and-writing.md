# Advanced Certificate in Rhetoric and Writing

Fordham University's Advanced Certificate in Rhetoric and Writing offers an interdisciplinary approach to the study of discourse and its impact on culture, politics, and everyday life. This program is designed to complement graduate work in English, providing specialized training that enhances your academic and professional credentials.

Our certificate program is designed to strengthen your ability to connect, communicate, and lead in a wide range of contexts. You'll encounter diverse theories and approaches to the field of rhetoric and writing studies and gain a deeper understanding of how symbolic communication shapes human interaction. Whether you're pursuing a career in scholarship, teaching, administration, or consulting, this certificate equips you with the skills needed to excel in various professional and community settings.
