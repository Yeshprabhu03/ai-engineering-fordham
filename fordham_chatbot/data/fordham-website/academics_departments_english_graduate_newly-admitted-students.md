# Newly Admitted Students

![Building - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_100908_0232.nef__SM.jpg)

Newly Admitted Students

Welcome to Fordham's English Graduate Program!

We encourage you to visit our [English Graduate Program](/academics/departments/english/graduate/) and the [Graduate School of Arts and Sciences (GSAS)](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/) web pages and to [ask us questions](/academics/departments/english/contact-us/).

Some highlights of our program include:

- A New Student Orientation Day before classes begin in the fall.
- One-on-one guidance with the Director of Graduate Studies to help you keep you on track.
[GSAS Distinguished Fellowships](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/financial-support/fellowships/distinguished-fellowships/)and[Graduate Student Association (GSA) grants](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/financial-support/graduate-student-support-grant/)that can help with funding your projects and research.- Professional development and job placement assistance through our
[Director of Placement and Professional Development.](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/departments/english/graduate/resources-and-opportunities/) - Auxiliary academic and social activities with the
[Graduate Student Council](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/student-resources/graduate-student-council/)and the[Graduate English Association (GEA)](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/departments/english/graduate/graduate-english-association-gea/).
