# Environmental Science Program

![Student looking at beaker - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fordham_042508_0508__SM.jpg)

In the environmental science major at Fordham, you will

- investigate the complex intersection of physical, chemical, and biological sciences that together forms the field of environmental science;
- rely on science to understand the anthropogenic (human-made) causes and effects of environmental problems;
- study in New York City, one of the most vibrant centers of environmental education and urban ecology in the world.
