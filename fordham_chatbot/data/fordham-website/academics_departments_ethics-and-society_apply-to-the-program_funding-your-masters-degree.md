The Fordham/Santander Universities International Scholarship in Ethics Education provides financial support for international students interested in masters-level study in Ethics and Society at Fordham University, New York City, USA.

Graduate Assistantships:

Full and Part-time Assistantships in the Center for Ethics Education and Fordham’s Institutional Review Board are open to students in the Ethics and Society program. These competitive funding opportunities offer students a stipend, tuition support, and the chance to engage in ethics education programming or work for the protection of human subjects in research. Contact Steve Swartzer, Ph.D., Associate Director for Academic Programs and Strategic, at [email protected] to apply.

Helen & Norman Burg Prize

In memory of the parents of the Center’s Director, Celia Fisher, PhD, this prize is open to current graduate students and awarded for the best student writing on an ethics-related topic.
