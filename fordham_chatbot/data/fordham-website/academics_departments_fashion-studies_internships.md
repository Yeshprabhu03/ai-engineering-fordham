# Fashion Studies Internships and Mentorships

## Internships

Students minoring in Fashion Studies are eligible to enroll in a three-credit Fashion Internship course (FASH 3800) that will count towards their elective credits. Students looking to find an internship should consult the Fashion Studies advisor or the Office of Career Services.

## Fashion Mentorship Program

The Fordham Fashion Family is a network of Fordham alumni that work in the fashion industry. Each year, members of the Fordham Fashion Family and Fashion Studies minors are encouraged to register for participation in the mentorship program.
