# Fashion Studies Minor

Fashion Studies is an interdisciplinary minor. This means that Fashion Studies courses are hosted by departments across the university including Anthropology, Art History, Communications, Marketing, and Theatre. Students take six courses to complete the minor: one from each of the **Commercial**, **Cultural**, and **Visual** categories, and three electives which can be selected from a wide variety of courses that have a Fashion Studies (FASH) attribute. Please click on the link below for a complete list and course descpriptions.

Students enrolled in any of Fordham's undergraduate colleges are welcome to minor in Fashion Studies. Please contact the [Fashion Studies Advisor](/academics/departments/fashion-studies/faculty-and-staff/) if you are interested in enrolling in the minor.
