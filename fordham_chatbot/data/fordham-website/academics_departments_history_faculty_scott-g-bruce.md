Scott G. Bruce is a historian of religion and culture in the early and central Middle Ages (c. 400-1200 CE). His research interests include monasticism, hagiography, and the reception of classical and patristic traditions in medieval Europe. He has published several books about the abbey of Cluny, most recently

(Cornell University Press, forthcoming 01/2026).
