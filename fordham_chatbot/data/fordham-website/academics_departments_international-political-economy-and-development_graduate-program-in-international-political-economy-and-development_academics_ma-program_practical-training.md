# Graduate IPED Practical Training

![IPED Catholic Relief Service Practical Training](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/international-political-economy-and-development/Practical_Training_1_MD.jpeg)

The MA program provides important practical training useful for job placement. You will be required to obtain practical training if you lack previous relevant professional experience. This requirement can be fulfilled by completing one of the following practical training programs:

[Internship](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/academics/ma-program/practical-training/internships/)with an approved international organization in the business, government, or non-profit sector[Overseas Study Tours](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/academics/ma-program/practical-training/study-tours/)[Language Immersions](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/academics/ma-program/practical-training/language-immersions/)[International Peace and Development Travel Program](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/academics/ma-program/practical-training/international-peace-and-development-travel-program/)

Generous financial aid is available for each of these practical training programs on a competitive basis.

The Program requires all its students who are participating in overseas international practical training programs to have adequate medical insurance and [proper immunizations](/academics/departments/international-political-economy-and-development/graduate-program-in-international-political-economy-and-development/academics/ma-program/practical-training/health-insurance-and-immunization-information/).
