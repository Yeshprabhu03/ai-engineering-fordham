# Graduate IPED Admission Deadlines

![Fordham students standing along a seaside cliff](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/international-political-economy-and-development/Admission_Deadlines_MD.jpeg)

To guarantee full consideration of your application, the Graduate Admissions Office must receive your complete application by the following deadlines:

**For Fall Admission**

With Aid Deadline: January 9, 2026*

Without Aid Deadline: April 1, 2026

Rolling Admissions Begins April 2, 2026

**Merit-Based Financial Aid Announcements**

Merit-Based Financial Aid offers are normally made by mid-March. Recipients must accept their offers by April 15. It there are any declined offers, additional merit-based financial aid offers may be made soon after April 15.

**For Spring Admission**

IPED does not offer mid-academic year admissions.

*Late applications for financial aid and fellowship decisions are unlikely to be considered
