# Major Requirements for International Studies

The International Studies Major requires 11 courses in the following 3 areas:

- The Major Core (5 courses)
- Elective Track (6 courses)
[Language Requirement](/academics/departments/international-studies/major-requirements/language-requirement/)(2 courses)

[International Studies Major Requirement](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/undergraduate/international-studies/major/#requirementstext)

**The Major Core - 5 courses**

There are 5 categories that make up, which are:

- Introduction to the Major
- International Politics and Law
- International Economics and Political Economy
- Contemporary World History and Culture
[Senior Thesis Project](/academics/departments/international-studies/senior-thesis/)(Senior Year, Fall Semester)

**Elective Track - 6 courses**

In addition, you will choose one of 2 specializations for further study: International or Regional Tracks.

Learn more about the [Elective Tracks](/academics/departments/international-studies/major-requirements/elective-track/).

**Language Requirement - 2 courses**

Students must take 2 additional language courses beyond the Fordham Core Language Requirement.

Learn more about the [Language Requirement.](/academics/departments/international-studies/major-requirements/language-requirement/)

Please [contact us](/academics/departments/international-studies/faculty/) if you have any questions about the major requirements.
