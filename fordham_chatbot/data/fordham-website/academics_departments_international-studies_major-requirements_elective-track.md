# Elective Track for International Studies

Choose 1 track of 2 elective tracks:

1. **The Regional Track.** Take 6 courses in one of the following regions:

- Africa
- Asia
- Europe
- Latin America
- Middle East and North Africa

2. **The International Track.** Take 6 courses with a significant international component. Most courses that address relations across borders and across regions (and/or address thematic issues in the international community) through the contemporary period can count towards the International Track.
