# Math and Computer & Information Sciences Program

This major, offered jointly by the Department of
[Computer and Information Sciences](/undergraduate-admission/majors-and-minors/computer-science/) and the Department of
[Mathematics](/academics/departments/mathematics/), is designed to give students the solid mathematical foundation necessary for a full understanding of computer and information sciences. The program requires careful reasoning and fosters a facility with ever-evolving technology, enhancing a studentâ€™s marketability.

The high degree of difficulty makes this joint major attractive to recruiters from various industries and prepares students for graduate studies in computer science and other applied quantitative fields.

### Program Activities

Math and computer & information sciences majors are eligible for honors in both fields. Students majoring in mathematics and computer & information sciences are encouraged to pursue internships, but please note that such internships are not required and do not count toward the two electives in the major.
