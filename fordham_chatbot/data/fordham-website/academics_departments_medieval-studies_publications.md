# Medieval Studies Publications

Before Publication: The Typescript of *Vernacular Literary Theory from the French of Medieval England: Texts and Translations from 1120-1450*, Ed. Jocelyn Wogan-Browne, Thelma Fenster, and Delbert Russell.

The Center for Medieval Studies is active in a variety of publication outreach efforts, including two book series at [Fordham University Press](https://www.fordhampress.com/series/fordham-series-medieval-studies/), brochures on our various digital projects, an [annual newsletter](/academics/departments/medieval-studies/publications/center-newsletter/), and the well-respected scholarly journal, [Traditio](/academics/research/libraries-and-collections/journals-published-at-fordham/traditio/).

The Center also maintains the [Venerable Blog](https://thevenerableblog.ace.fordham.edu/), which offers updates on the [digital humanities projects](/academics/research/libraries-and-collections/digital-humanities-at-fordham/) housed in the Center, as well as faculty, student, and departmental news.
