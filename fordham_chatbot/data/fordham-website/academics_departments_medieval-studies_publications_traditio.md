# Traditio

[Traditio](/academics/research/libraries-and-collections/journals-published-at-fordham/traditio/) is an international journal, published annually and dedicated to the study of ancient and medieval history, thought, and religion.

The Journal Publishes:

- Monographic essays
- Critical editions of texts
- Research tools such as catalogues of unpublished manuscripts

Submissions are accepted in the following languages:

- English
- French
- German
- Italian
- Spanish

Articles have focused on:

- History
- History of art
- Literature
- Philosophy
- Patristics or patrology, the study of early Christian writers from the end of the New Testament
- Theology in the period of late antiquity
- Early Christianity through the Middle Ages up to A.D. 1500
