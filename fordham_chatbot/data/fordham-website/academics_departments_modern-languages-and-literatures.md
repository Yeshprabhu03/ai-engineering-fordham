# Languages and Cultures

## We live in a multilingual, multicultural world.

![Group of students in front of Parthenon - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_rome_070910_545__SM.jpg)

Drawing on a long Jesuit tradition of scholarship on and respect for the world's diverse languages and cultures, we offer a wide range of courses and degree programs aimed at developing your linguistic proficiency and cultural competence in [Arabic](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/arabic/), [French](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/french/), [German](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/german/), [Hebrew](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/hebrew/), [Italian](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/italian/), [Japanese](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/japanese/), [Mandarin Chinese](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/mandarin-chinese/), [Russian](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/russian/), and [Spanish](/academics/departments/languages-and-cultures/curriculum/spanish/), and providing you with solid foundations in [Linguistics](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/undergraduate/languages-cultures/linguistics-minor/).
