## Program Benefits

![Visions of the Good in the Bronx Students Working on Journals on Keating Lawn.](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/philosophy/Visions_of_the_Good_in_the_Bronx_Students_Working_on_Journals_on_Keating_Lawn.jpg)

For the **two-week summer** program, every student receives:

- Free tuition and free room and board
- A significant stipend
- Books
- T-shirt
- Programming (guest speakers, extracurricular events)
- Mentorship by college faculty and college students

Following the summer, there will be regular meetings during the school year:

- Help applying to colleges
- Help finding scholarships to pay for college
- Strategies for succeeding in college

**Applications are due by no later than March 25, 2026. **
