# Resources for Philosophy Graduate Students

To aid current graduate students in their search for fellowships, the department has provided a list of external funding sources.

Students in search of forms, guidelines, and requirements should consult the [Forms and Guidelines](/academics/departments/philosophy/graduate/resources-for-students/forms-and-guidelines/) page and the [Graduate Philosophy Handbook](/academics/departments/philosophy/graduate/graduate-student-handbook/).

For a list of external funding sources please refer to [Fellowship and Postdoctoral Opportunities](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/philosophy/pdfs/Fellowship-and-Postdoctoral-Opportunities.pdf).

For a list of potential certificates please refer to [Fordham Philosophy, Certificates and Credentials](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/philosophy/pdfs/Fordham-Philosophy,-Certificates-and-Credentials.pdf).
