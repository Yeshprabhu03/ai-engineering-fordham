# Fellowship and Postdoctoral Opportunities

NB:

(1) The following lists are only for recurring opportunities and are thus by no means exhaustive.

(2) Deadline dates vary by year and are thus not listed below. Use the links provided to verify specific

application deadline dates.

(3) In addition to the external sources below, Fordham GSAS of ers several distinguished fellowships

([Research Fellowship](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/financial-support/fellowships/distinguished-fellowships/research-fellowship/), [Senior Teaching Fellowship](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/financial-support/fellowships/distinguished-fellowships/senior-teaching-fellowship/), and [Alumni Dissertation Fellowship](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/financial-support/fellowships/distinguished-fellowships/alumni-dissertation-fellowship/)).

(4) For additional help in finding and applying to fellowships, consult Fordham’s [Office of Prestigious](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/student-fellowships/office-of-fellowship-advising/)[Fellowships](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/student-fellowships/office-of-fellowship-advising/).

**General Fellowships**

[Mellon/ACLS Dissertation ](https://www.acls.org/competitions/mellon-acls-dissertation-innovation-fellowship/)[Innovation Fellowship](https://www.acls.org/competitions/mellon-acls-dissertation-innovation-fellowship/)

Dissertation research only. Intended for early stage dissertation writing. 9 month to

1 year. Must have completed all required coursework by the start of the fellowship.

Must have not advanced to PhD candidacy (ABD) prior to the starting year of the

fellowship.

Dissertation research only. International. 4 to 9 months of funding at a French

university. No knowledge of French is required. Intended for graduate students in the

dissertation writing phase of their degree.

[Berlin Program Dissertation Fellowship](https://www.fu-berlin.de/en/sites/bprogram/application/Fellowships/index.html)

Dissertation research only. International. 10 months to 1 year of funding to

research at the Freie Universität Berlin. Must be ABD by the beginning of the

fellowship. Must have some level of German language competence.

[University Center for Human Values](https://uchv.princeton.edu/fellowships-grants-prizes/graduate-prize-fellowships)

Dissertation research only. Located in Princeton University. 12 months of funding.

Must be ABD, and research must involve “the critical study of human values.”

International. 8 months of funding. Must be a graduate student interested in researching or teaching at a university in Canada.

International. Database of awards/fellowships. More often than not, it is expected that research takes place in a different country. Consult the [Award Search](https://fulbrightscholars.org/awards/search?query=&field_award_app_deadline=2&field_award_multi_country=All&field_award_type_list%5B8323%5D=8323&field_award_degree_reqs=All&field_invitation_req=All&field_do_you_offer_additional_de=All) to narrow search results.

International. 2 to 12 months of support for research at a German university. Must

be working in German philosophy or must be interested in conducting research at a

German university. One’s research project does not necessarily need to be a

dissertation. Knowledge of German is often, but not always, required. DAAD offers

an array of more specific fellowships/awards on their [scholarship database](https://www2.daad.de/deutschland/stipendium/datenbank/en/21148-scholarship-database/).

**AOS-Specific Fellowships**

[Charlotte Newcombe Dissertation Completion Fellowship](https://newcombefoundation.org/fellowships/)

Dissertation research only. 1 year. Must be ABD by the application deadline. Must

expect to complete the dissertation in the same year as the fellowship. Must be

working in something related to “religion, ethics, values, or morals.”

[American Society of Aesthetics Dissertation Completion Fellowship](https://aesthetics-online.org/page/dissfellowships)

Dissertation research only. Must be working in aesthetics (construed as “the

philosophical study of art, criticism, each of the arts, and related phenomena”). Must

expect to complete the dissertation by the end of the fellowship.

[Harry Frank Guggenheim Emerging Scholar Awards](https://www.hfg.org/emerging-scholars/)

Dissertation research only. 1 year of funding. Must expect to complete dissertation

by the end of the fellowship. Intended for those working on something pertaining to

“the causes, manifestations, and control of violence” broadly construed.

[AAUW American Dissertation Fellowship](https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/current-opportunities/american-dissertation-fellowship-program/)

Dissertation research only. 1 year of funding. Must identify as a woman and be

ABD. It is preferred that one’s dissertation relate to gender issues.

[The Robert H. N. Ho Family Foundation Dissertation Fellowships in Buddhist Studies](https://www.acls.org/competitions/the-robert-h-n-ho-family-foundation-dissertation-fellowships-in-buddhist-studies/)

Dissertation research only. 10 months of funding. Must be ABD. Research must

pertain to Buddhism or Buddhist Philosophy.

[Center for Ethics at the University of Toronto](https://ethics.utoronto.ca/doctoral-fellowships/)

International. Dissertation research only. 1 academic year of funding at the

University of Toronto Center for Ethics. Must have a dissertation related to ethics,

broadly construed.

[The Carter G. Woodson Institute Predoctoral](https://woodson.as.virginia.edu/fellowship-program)

Dissertation research only. 2 year fellowship for those whose work focuses on

African-American and African Studies, as well as related fields. Must be ABD.

Located at the University of Virginia.

[U.S. Holocaust Museum Annual Fellowship](https://www.ushmm.org/research/opportunities-for-academics/fellowships/annual)

Dissertation research only. 2, 4, or 8 month fellowship. Must be ABD, and must

have a dissertation related to the Holocaust or related topics.

[Mitchem Dissertation Fellowship Program](https://www.marquette.edu/provost/mitchem-dissertation-program.php)

Dissertation research only. 1 year of an in-residence fellowship at Marquette

University. Intended for those who specialize in philosophy of race.

[American Scandinavian Foundation](https://www.amscan.org/fellowships-and-grants/fellowshipsgrants-to-study-in-scandinavia/)

Short term fellowship (1-3 months of funding). It is preferred, but not required, that

the applicant is in the process of writing their dissertation. Must be working on

philosophy/philosophers from the Nordic region (Denmark, Greenland, Faroe

Islands, Finland, Iceland, Norway, Sápmi, and Sweden).

For non-recurring post-doc offerings, consult [PhilJobs](https://philjobs.org/jobQuery/fixedTerm).

[Michigan Society of Fellows Post-Doc](https://societyoffellows.umich.edu/)

3 year fellowship. Must have received PhD prior to the start date of the postdoc.

3 year fellowship. Must have received PhD prior to the start date of the postdoc.

[Princeton Society of Fellows Post-Doc](https://sf.princeton.edu/application/fellowships)

the fellowship.

[The Society of Fellows and Heyman Center for the Humanities at Columbia University](https://sofheyman.org/fellowships/society-of-fellows)

1 year fellowship at Columbia. Renewable for 2-3 years. PhD received in the last 4

years.

[University of Chicago Society of Fellows Post-Doc](https://fellows.uchicago.edu/)

4 year fellowship. Must have received PhD prior to the start date of the postdoc.

[Wolf Humanities Center at the University of Pennsylvania](https://wolfhumanities.upenn.edu/fellowships/andrew-w-mellon-postdoctoral-fellowship-humanities)

1 year postdoctoral fellowship. Must have received PhD at most 5 years prior to the

start of the fellowship. Theme varies by year.

[Cornell University Society of Fellows Post-Doc](https://societyhumanities.as.cornell.edu/society-fellowships)

1 year fellowship. Theme varies by year.

[Berlin Program Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.fu-berlin.de/en/sites/bprogram)

International. 10-12 months. Must have received doctorate within two years

before the start of the postdoc. Also see the more specialized [Max Kade Berlin](https://www.fu-berlin.de/en/sites/bprogram/application/Fellowships/brosch_maxkade_a4_2024.pdf)[Fellowships](https://www.fu-berlin.de/en/sites/bprogram/application/Fellowships/brosch_maxkade_a4_2024.pdf).

[Fulbright Post-Doctoral Awards](https://fulbrightscholars.org/awards/search?query=&field_award_app_deadline=2&field_award_multi_country=All&field_award_type_list%5B8303%5D=8303&field_award_degree_reqs=All&field_invitation_req=All&field_do_you_offer_additional_de=All)

International. Database of postdocs. More often than not, it is expected that research takes place in a different country. Details vary.

International. 6-24 month fellowship in Germany. Must have received PhD within 4 years prior to the start of the postdoc.

1 year academic fellowship. Academic themes vary by year.

1 academic year postdoc. Must have received PhD within the last 4 years.

**AOS-Specific Fellowships**

[AAUW Postdoctoral Leave Research Fellowship](https://www.aauw.org/resources/programs/fellowships-grants/current-opportunities/american-postdoctoral-research-leave-fellowship-program/)

1 year postdoc. Must identify as a woman. It is preferred that one’s dissertation

relate to gender issues.

[The Pembroke Center for Teaching and Research on Women](https://pembroke.brown.edu/funding-opportunities/postdoctoral-research-fellowships)

1 year postdoc at Brown University. Theme varies by year. Must specialize in work

related to women and/or gender.

[NAEd/Spencer Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://naeducation.org/naed-spencer-postdoctoral-fellowship/)

Must have received doctorate at most 4 years before the start of the postdoc. One’s

research must pertain to education (philosophy of education, pedagogical theory,

etc.).

[The Center for Philosophy of Science Postdoctoral Fellowship](https://www.centerphilsci.pitt.edu/programs/postdoc-fellowships/program-overview/)

Located within the University of Pittsburgh. Must have had doctorate awarded at

most 5 years prior to the start of the fellowship. Must be working on the philosophy

of science.

[Rutgers Center for the Philosophy of Religion](https://philosophy.rutgers.edu/rcpr/about-us)

Offers two postdoctoral fellowships on a semi-regular basis. Consult Fordham

Prof. Stephen Grimm or Prof. Brian Davies (both External Affiliates of the Center)

for more details.

[Center for Philosophy of Religion at Notre Dame](https://philreligion.nd.edu/fellowships/research/)

1 year postdoc. Must be working in philosophy of religion. NB: the Center has a

“theme” that varies by year.

[Center for Philosophy, Politics, and Economics at Brown](https://ppe.brown.edu/postdoctoral-fellowships/application-information)

1 year, renewable for a second. PhD must have been received at most 3 years prior

to the fellowship start date.

[University Center for Human Values at Princeton](https://uchv.princeton.edu/postdoctoral-researcher-moral-philosophy)

1 year postdoc Must be working in moral philosophy, broadly construed.

[Centre for Ethics, Philosophy and Public Affairs](https://ceppa.wp.st-andrews.ac.uk/)

International. Affiliated with University of St. Andrews. 1 or 2 semester long

fellowship. Must work in philosophy related to ethics or public affairs, broadly

construed.

[Center for Ethics at the University of Toronto](https://ethics.utoronto.ca/contact/post-doctoral-fellowships/)

International. 1 academic year of funding at the University of Toronto Center for

Ethics. Must specialize in work related to ethics, broadly construed. Must have

received PhD at most 5 years prior to the start of the fellowship.

2 year postdoctoral fellowship. Must work in ethics, broadly construed.

[McCoy Family Center for Ethics in Society](https://ethicsinsociety.stanford.edu/postdoctoral/fellowship-programs)

Affiliated with Stanford University. Must work in ethics, broadly construed. Must

have received PhD at most 3 years prior to fellowship start date.

[The Center for Hellenistic Studies](https://chs.harvard.edu/fellowships/fellowships-hellenic-studies/)

Affiliated with Harvard University. Must have received PhD in “Classics or related

field.” Must specialize in work related to ancient Greek civilization, broadly

construed.

[The Carter G. Woodson Institute Postdoc](https://woodson.as.virginia.edu/fellowship-program)

2 year postdoc fellowship for those whose work focuses on African-American and

African Studies, as well as related fields. Located at the University of Virginia.

1 year fellowship at St. Olaf College. Can be ABD or postdoc. Must specialize in Kierkegaard.

1 year postdoc, affiliated with Harvard University. Must have received doctorate at

most 3 years prior to the start of the fellowship. Must work in the environmental

humanities, broadly construed.

[Medieval Institute at the University of Notre Dame](https://medieval.nd.edu/research/grants-fellowships/)

Multiple postdoctoral fellowships offered. 1 or 2 year appointment length. Must

have work in Medieval Studies, broadly construed.

[The Institute for Advanced Studies in the Humanities](https://www.iash.ed.ac.uk/early-career-researchers)

International. Located in the University of Edinburgh. Theme varies by year.
