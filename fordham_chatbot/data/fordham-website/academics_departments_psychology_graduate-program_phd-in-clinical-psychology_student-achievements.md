# Ph.D. Student Achievements in Clinical Psychology

The Clinical Psychology Doctorate Program tracks research activities of our doctoral students during each academic year. The links below will take you to our impressive list that includes publications, presentations and awards and honors throughout the academic year.

Please see our list of [student publications](/academics/departments/psychology/graduate-program/phd-in-clinical-psychology/student-achievements/clinical-publications/) that appeared or will appear in edited books, journals and websites.

Please see our [conference presentation list](/academics/departments/psychology/graduate-program/phd-in-clinical-psychology/student-achievements/clinical-poster-presentations/) from our students that presented at regional, national, and international conferences.
