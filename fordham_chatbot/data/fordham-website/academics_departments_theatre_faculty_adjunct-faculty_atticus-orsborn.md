Classes Taught

Actor’s Vocal Technique

Atticus Orsborn is a voice and accent/dialect coach from the UK but based in New York, holding a distinction MA from the Royal Central School of Speech and Drama as well as being a licensed practitioner of Lessac’s Kinesensics voice-body work. He has worked with high profile individual and corporate clients and taught at world class institutions in London and NYC. He has also coached extensively for stage and screen. He is also a graduate of the three-year professional conservatory program at the Stella Adler Studio and is engaged professionally in the film and theater industries as a writer, director and actor.
