Acting for Non-Majors, Invitation to Theatre

Deonna is an Actor based in NYC. She’s appeared Off-Broadway at The Public Theatre, The Playwrights Realm, and ARS NOVA. She’s worked regionally at The Guthrie, Arena Stage, Actors Theatre of Louisville-Humana Festival, Cincinnati Playhouse in the Park, Two River Theater, and The Kennedy Center, and others. She’s had the pleasure of originating several roles and has spent significant time in new play development workshops at The O’Neill, Page 73, NYTW, The Lark, Ensemble Studio Theatre, and more. She’s been a performance collaborator in short films, video game production, commercials, and voice-overs. Training: MFA Acting from The University of Iowa.
