### Biography

Hillary is a first year student in the theological and social ethics program, interested in artistic contributions to conversations in theological ethics and bioethics.

Originally from Oregon, she completed a BA in Bible and Theology at Moody Bible Institute in Chicago, an MLitt in Theology, Imagination and the Arts at the University of St Andrews in Scotland, an MDiv at Yale Divinity School, and worked as a trauma chaplain in New Orleans and Oregon prior to moving to the Bronx.
