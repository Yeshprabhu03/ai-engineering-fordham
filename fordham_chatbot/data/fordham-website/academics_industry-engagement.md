# Industry Engagement

## Seamless Connection to the Jesuit University of New York

Fordham University is the university partner of choice for industry leaders seeking innovative research, staff upskilling, and collaboration to solve the world's most pressing problems. By partnering with us, companies gain access to premier educational programs, top talent, and streamlined engagement.

## The Fordham Difference

Fordham's Jesuit values of integrity, social justice, and care for the whole person drive our approach to collaboration. Partnering with us means not only gaining access to top faculty, talent, and research but also a shared commitment to ethical leadership and impactful, socially responsible solutions that benefits your business and the wider world.
