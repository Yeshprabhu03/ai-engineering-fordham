# August Yu

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research-consortium-on-disability/IMG_5611.png)


**Bio:**

Luyuan (August) Yu is a Ph.D. candidate and teaching fellow at Fordham University. His research centers on the autonomy and well-being of individuals with intellectual disabilities, with a particular emphasis on the intersection of Daoist philosophy, disability studies and bioethics.
