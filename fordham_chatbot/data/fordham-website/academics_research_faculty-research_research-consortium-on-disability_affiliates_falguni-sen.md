# Falguni Sen

Professor

**Bio:**

[Falguni Sen](/gabelli-school-of-business/faculty/full-time-faculty/falguni-sen/) is a professor and area chair of strategy and statistics. He is the head of Global Healthcare Innovation Management Center and co-director of the Research Consortium on Disability. His research interests relate to outsourcing, pharmaceutical industry, the role of public trust in competitive environments, and culture and public trust in healthcare.
