# Monika L. McDermott

Professor

*Bio:
*

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/research-consortium-on-disability/Mcdermott-fordham-leaning-on-desk.jpg)

Professor [Monika L. McDermott](/academics/departments/political-science/faculty/monika-l-mcdermott/) researches and teaches political psychology at Fordham, including how voters use stereotypes and information shortcuts to make voting decisions. She is the author of two books--most recently *Masculinity, Femininity, and American Political Behavior *(Oxford University Press 2016) and editor of the forthcoming volume M*asculinity in American Politics* (NYU Press 2025). Her most recent work involves voters' impressions of candidates with mental illness and whether and how this affects their vote.
