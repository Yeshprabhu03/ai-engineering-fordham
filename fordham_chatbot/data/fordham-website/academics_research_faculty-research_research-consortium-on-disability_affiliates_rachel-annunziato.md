# Rachel Annunziato

Professor

*Bio:*

[Rachel A. Annunziato](/academics/departments/psychology/faculty-and-staff/rachel-annunziato/) is a Professor of Psychology. She also directs a Pediatric Psychology externship at Mount Sinai Hospital that provides free consultation, intervention, and testing services to children with developmental and learning disabilities. In addition, her research program focuses on children and adults with special health care needs.
