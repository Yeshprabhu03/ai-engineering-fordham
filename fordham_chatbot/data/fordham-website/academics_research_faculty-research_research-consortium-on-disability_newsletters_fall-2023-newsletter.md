# Fall 2023 Newsletter

### Giving Tuesday

The Research Consortium on Disability conducts and coordinates research at Fordham to help guide the world on a path towards inclusion. The Research Consortium on Disability organizes events and supports research through seed grants and community building. Please consider donating to the Research Consortium on Disability using [this link](https://www.givecampus.com/campaigns/41304/donations/new?designation=researchconsortiumondisability&).

**launched the website**

__Elisabeth Frost__[A Picture of Health: Jo Spence, a Politics of Disability and Illness](https://pictureofhealth-jospence.com/). Funded by the Canada Council for the Arts and Fordham University, and co-curated with Kenny Fries, the website places the work of the radical artist and activist Jo Spence in the context of the lived experience of chronic illness and of contemporary disability arts. Also, as part of the project, a three-part event series based in the course "Introduction to Disability Studies" featured artists and writers exploring chronic illness and disability arts.
