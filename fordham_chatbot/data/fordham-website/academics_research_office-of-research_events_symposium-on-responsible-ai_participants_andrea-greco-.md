**Abstract:**

The Fireside Chat will explore recent progress and future directions in Responsible AI systems and infrastructure with a distinguished group of researchers, practitioners, and leaders in the RAI landscape. Discussion topics will include trustworthy design (spanning development and deployment) and responsible implementation (covering infrastructure and governance). In particular, the session will examine and, where possible, address the following questions:

(a) What are the current achievements and challenges in the design and implementation of RAI systems and infrastructure?

(b) What actions can and should we take to advance RAI in the future?

(c) How can we enhance RAI by responsibly integrating artificial intelligence (AI) with human intelligence (HI) and natural intelligence (NI)?
