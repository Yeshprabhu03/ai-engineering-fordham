# How to Apply

## Apply for the 2023 McGannon Book Award

We are now accepting nominations for the 2025 book award. The focus of the award is book-length research published in 2025. **The deadline for consideration is now Dec 15th, 2025.**

Nominations should consist of a cover paragraph summarizing the book’s research focus and contribution, along with four copies of the book. Self-nominations are welcome, as are submissions from early career researchers and scholars from diverse backgrounds. Edited volumes are not eligible for consideration. We also welcome suggestions for nominations. If you read a great book this year that you’d like to informally nominate, please email us with the author and title and we’ll take it from there.
