# Luncheon: View from Europe

![Hilde Van den Bulck and Mathias Klang](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Screen_Shot_2020_06_15_at_5.31.55_PM.png)

In collaboration with Fordham University’s [Public Media](/academics/departments/communication-and-media-studies/master-of-arts-in-public-media/) program, the McGannon Center will host a special luncheon featuring Visiting Research Fellow **Hilde Van den Bulck **and **Mathias Klang**, professor of digital media in the Communication and Media Studies department.

Hilde Van den Bulck and Mathias Klang will discuss, “What can Americans learn from a European view on communications?”

Lunch will be catered by a TBD delicious place.

**Time: **11:15 a.m. to 12:45 p.m.

**Date: **Wednesday, October 18, 2017

**Place: **McGannon Center, 2532 Hughes Ave
