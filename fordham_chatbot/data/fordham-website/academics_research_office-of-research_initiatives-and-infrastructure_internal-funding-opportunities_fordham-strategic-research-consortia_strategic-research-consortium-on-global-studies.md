# Strategic Research Consortium on Global Studies

Launched in the Fall of 2019, the Strategic Research Consortium on Global Studies has the mission of providing an infrastructure to connect existing research initiatives and enable future research on global studies. It provides support to researchers and research initiatives to foster interdisciplinary and transnational collaborations on global studies
