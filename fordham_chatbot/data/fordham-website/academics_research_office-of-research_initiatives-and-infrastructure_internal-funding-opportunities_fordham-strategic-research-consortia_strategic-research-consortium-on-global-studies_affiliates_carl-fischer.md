# Carl Fischer

Professor and Chair, Modern Languages and Literatures Department, Arts and Sciences

Areas of Research:

Literary, cultural and cinema studies in the Southern Cone of Latin America (particularly Chile but also Argentina, Uruguay, and Paraguay). Gender Studies and Queer Studies in a Trans-American context.
