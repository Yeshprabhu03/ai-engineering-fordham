# Research Fall 2025 Newsletter Symposium on Responsible AI

Fordham University will host an AI Alliance symposium on October 16-17, 2025 at the Fordham Lincoln Center campus. Titled “[Symposium on Responsible AI](https://www.fordham.edu/academics/research/office-of-research/events/symposium-on-responsible-ai/)” and sponsored by Fordham, IBM, NYU, and the AI Alliance, this symposium will feature keynotes, selected papers, and workshops for both the Fordham community and external guests. [The AI Alliance](https://thealliance.ai/), initiated by IBM and Meta in 2023, fosters open, safe, and responsible AI. Fordham joined the Alliance's Skills & Education focus area, enabling collaboration on projects to develop AI educational content.

Register at this [ link](https://form.jotform.com/251273737776165) to participate as a potential speaker or as a member of the audience. The full schedule of events will be available in September.
