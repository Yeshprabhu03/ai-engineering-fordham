# Contact the Office of Sponsored Programs

The Office of Sponsored Programs is located in Canisius Hall (Building 2546), next door to Faculty Memorial Hall on the Rose Hill campus. We also have a satellite office at Lincoln Center (LL203C). **The best and fastest way to reach our team is via email!**

Important note to interdisciplinary programs: We encourage you to review the department/school lists below and choose the Office of Sponsored Programs staff you feel has the most relevant portfolio to your work.

These lists are meant as a guide and are not exhaustive.
