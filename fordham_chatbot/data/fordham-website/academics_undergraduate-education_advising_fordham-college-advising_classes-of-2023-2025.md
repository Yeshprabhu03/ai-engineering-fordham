## Advising for Classes of 2023-2025

During your first three semesters at Fordham, our academic advisers will prepare you for major declaration, [study abroad](https://www.fordham.edu/study-abroad/) programs, undergraduate research, [internships](https://www.fordham.edu/resources/career-resources/career-center/), and all other opportunities you will receive here. - The vital importance of committing to academic integrity while at Fordham.
- Skillful advising on strategies for achieving academic success and remedies to any academic difficulty.
- Thoughtful exploration of the right study choices, major selections, and professional options for you.

Once you declare a major, you are assigned a professor from that department to serve as your faculty adviser. You will meet at least once a semester with your faculty adviser to plan courses for the next semester, discuss issues in the current and past semesters, and to plan for the future, including jobs and graduate school. You will also be advised and supported by your class deans.
