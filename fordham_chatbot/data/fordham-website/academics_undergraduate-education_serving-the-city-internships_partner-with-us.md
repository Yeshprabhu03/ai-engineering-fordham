# Partner with Us

Fordham University works with a wide variety of agencies to offer Serving the City internships. Please note that this program partners with nonprofit organizations only.

Internships are designed in collaboration between Fordham and the partner agency to advance the organization's mission while providing students with meaningful, substantive professional experience. The organization takes the lead in shaping the internship and works with Fordham to ensure that the position provides undergraduate students with opportunities for growth.

We encourage you to contact us at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#91e2f4e3e7f8fff6e5f9f4f2f8e5e8d1f7fee3f5f9f0fcbff4f5e4) to learn more.
