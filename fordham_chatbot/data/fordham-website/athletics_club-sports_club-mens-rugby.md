# Club Men's Rugby

## The Bronx's Toughest and Strongest Brotherhood

Fordham Rugby Football Club (FURFC) is a brotherhood committed to teammates, coaches, alumni, and the greater Fordham community as a whole. We display our commitment through strength of effort embodied in physical perseverance and mental determination, the defining characteristics of our great sport. Fordham Rugby embodies the values of collaboration, dedication, and individual responsibility to the whole in all aspects of life.

![Men's Rugby championship](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/athletics/rugby-championship.jpg)


- Fordham Rugby competes in the Liberty Rugby Conference.
- Practices are held Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays from 7 - 9 pm on Coffey Field.
- Students interested in Fordham Rugby should contact a club officer. Contact information is available on the Officers page.
