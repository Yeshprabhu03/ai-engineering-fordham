# Club Women's Lacrosse

Fordham University Women’s Club Lacrosse aims to to provide a fun and competitive environment for lacrosse players of various levels. It’s a great way to stay in shape, meet new people, and continue playing the game you love! We hold practices and games against nearby schools throughout both the fall and spring semesters. As a club team, we do not recruit members. Students interested in learning more about the team should contact us.

Interested in Women's Lacrosse? Join our club!
