# Intramurals

![Group of female students playing soccer - SM](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_100908_0685_2.jpg)


Welcome to Fordham University Intramurals! We provide up-to-date schedules, results, rules and special announcements.

Who Can Play

Fordham University Athletics prides itself on a recreational sports program tailored to the needs of the student body, faculty, and staff. Any member of the Fordham University community may compete in Intramural events.

Sign Up or Join a Sport

Please follow the directions on [how to create a team or join an existing one](/athletics/intramurals/sign-up-or-join-a-sport/).

## Rules

The spirit of Intramural competition is fun. [Rules of conduct](/athletics/intramurals/intramurals-rules/) and fair play aid in that goal. You are responsible for knowing and understanding all the Rules of Intramurals and individual sports before beginning league play. Intramural supervisors and game officials adhere to and enforce **all** Intramural rules.
