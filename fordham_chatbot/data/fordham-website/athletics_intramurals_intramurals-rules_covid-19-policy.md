# Intramurals COVID-19 Policy

## COVID-19 Rules and Policies

The Intramural Sports COVID-19 policy is fluid and will be adapted as necessary to reflect the ongoing changes with the current pandemic. At this time, participants for indoor Intramural Sports are not required to wear a mask while participating, although it is strongly encouraged. All participants for outdoor Intramural Sports are not required to wear a mask, although it is strongly encouraged. All participants in the Intramural Sports program are required to have their COVID-19 vaccination seeries completed and have received the COVID-19 booster, in adherence with Fordham Universities COVID-19 policy.
