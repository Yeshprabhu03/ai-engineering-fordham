# Indoor Campus Viewing Locations

## Saturday, May 16, 2026

For your convenience, the 10 a.m. outdoor University Commencement ceremony will be live-streamed to the following indoor locations.

- Rose Hill Gymnasium
- Keating First Lecture Hall in Keating Hall
- McGinley Ballroom, 2nd floor of the McShane Campus Center

The outdoor diploma ceremonies immediately following the University Commencement will be live-streamed to the following locations:

- Fordham College at Rose Hill: McGinley Ballroom, McShane Campus Center
- Gabelli School of Business: Rose Hill Gymnasium
- Fordham College at Lincoln Center: Tognino Hall, Duane Library

All locations are climate controlled and accessible to people with disabilities.
