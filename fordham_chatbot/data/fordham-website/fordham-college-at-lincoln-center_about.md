# About Fordham College at Lincoln Center

![Students at Lowenstein Plaza](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Students_at_Lowenstein_Plaza___LG.jpg)

Fordham College at Lincoln Center is a close-knit, intellectual, and creative community of faculty and students located in the heart of Manhattan, and conducted in the Jesuit and Catholic traditions of education.
