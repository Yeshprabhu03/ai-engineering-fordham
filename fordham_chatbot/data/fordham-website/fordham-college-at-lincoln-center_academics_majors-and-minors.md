# Majors and Minors at Lincoln Center

Explore majors and minors offered at Fordham College at Lincoln Center.

Note: Some program options featured below may also be available at our other undergraduate schools.


![Image for Undecided](/media/home/commonly-used-images/campus-beauty/campus-beauty-hero-banners/RH-Ram-1.jpg)

Undecided

Did you know that each year, about 20% of our incoming class is undecided about their major? And we think that's great. Helping you find a life path is one of the goals of a Jesuit education, and Fordham's core curriculum will expose you to a wide range of subjects and fields.

**College or School: **Fordham College at Lincoln Center, Fordham College at Rose Hill, Gabelli School of Business (Undergraduate)

[Read More](/undergraduate-admission/majors-and-minors/undecided/)

![Image for Urban Studies](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/RLozier_090814_6321.jpg)

Urban Studies

Our urban studies major offers a broad introduction to global cities and urban environments through classes, lectures, and research—all in the biggest urban area in the world.

**College or School: **Fordham College at Lincoln Center, Fordham College at Rose Hill

**Choose Fordham because »** You’ll see your coursework come to life across New York City as you **conduct research and engage in fieldwork**—and pursue global opportunities through our study abroad program.

[Read More](/undergraduate-admission/majors-and-minors/urban-studies/)

![Image for Visual Arts](/media/home/commonly-used-images/program-finder/Visual-Arts.jpg)

Visual Arts

Visual arts majors develop technical skills and find their artistic voice, through concentrations in one of five areas of study: built environment, film and video, graphic design, painting and drawing, or photography.

**College or School: **Fordham College at Lincoln Center, Fordham College at Rose Hill

**Choose Fordham because »** New York City will be part of your coursework through **museum tours, studio visits, artist lectures**, and more—and your work will be featured alongside that of professionals in galleries on campus.

[Read More](/undergraduate-admission/majors-and-minors/visual-arts/)

![Image for Women, Gender, and Sexuality Studies](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fordham_102907_0773.jpg)

Women, Gender, and Sexuality Studies

Our women, gender, and sexuality studies major takes an expansive view of gender and sexuality studies as both a lens to examine and a vehicle to create social change.

**College or School: **Fordham College at Lincoln Center, Fordham College at Rose Hill

**Choose Fordham because »** You’ll be connected to **internship opportunities across New York City**, setting you up for careers in public policy, law, medicine, business, and other fields.
