# Declaring a Major at Lincoln Center

The college offers majors in the humanities, social sciences, and interdisciplinary natural sciences, as well as outstanding programs in theatre, dance, visual arts, music, education, and social work. A vast array of internship opportunities, including in our signature program for paid [Serving the City internships](/academics/undergraduate-education/serving-the-city-internships/), enable students to connect real-world experience to their academic learning and make the most of the unparalleled opportunities New York City offers. Through our affiliation with The Ailey School, our students study at one of the country's premier schools of dance.

Distinguished authors, actors, politicians, and intellectuals regularly enrich the life of the college with talks and seminars.

## Steps to declaring a major

We encourage you to discuss your interests with your advisor and schedule meetings with the department associate chair or program director to learn more. Please consult this [list of departmental/program contacts](/fordham-college-at-lincoln-center/academics/majors-and-minors/contacts-for-majors-and-minors/) for FCLC students.

When you are ready to declare, you will complete a "Major/Minor/Concentration Form" available under "[Student Electronic Forms](/my-pages/students/electronic-forms/)" on your Fordham portal.
