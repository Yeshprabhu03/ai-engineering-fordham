# ARS NOVA - Arts and Research Showcase

## ARS Nova Arts & Research Showcase

Each year, the ARS Nova Arts and Research Showcase features the work of talented students in all fields of study at Fordham College at Lincoln Center. Join us for poster presentations of student projects on Thursday, April 23 from 12:00-2:00 in the 12th Floor Lounge.

In addition, we invite you to join us for the 7th Annual Digital Showcase. This curated collection of student projects, featured on the ARS Nova website, will be available to view in April (specific date is TBD).
