# Manresa Program Alumni Spotlight

## Featured Manresa Alumni

**Benjamin Delikat**, **FCRH 2013**, was a Manresa Scholar in the 2009-2010 academic year. He completed majors in English and Urban Studies, ran track and cross-country, and studied Arabic. Ben exemplifies the intellectual curiosity, authentic motivation, and committed citizenship that Manresa Scholars develop and practice each day.

He presently works full-time in New York City as a Program Manager at Peer Health Exchange, a non-profit organization with which he began working as a first-semester freshman in the Manresa Program.
