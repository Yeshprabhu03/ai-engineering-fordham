# Manresa Research Spotlight

## Introduction to Behavioral Health Seminar

**Healthy Lifestyle Engagement and Well-Being in College**

In her Manresa seminar, Introduction to Behavioral Health, Rachel Annunziato, Ph.D., led a research project with her Manresa Scholars, titled "Healthy Lifestyle Engagement and Well-Being in College." Take a look at their poster for a summary of their fascinating findings.



We asked participants to rate a variety of aspects regarding both their health practices, like what they eat, and mood, like how often they feel sad. As predicted, we established a positive correlation between nutrition and happiness. Our study tied together what the course was all about:


the Mind-Body Connection.

- Julian Franks and Emilia Klapak, Manresa Scholars
