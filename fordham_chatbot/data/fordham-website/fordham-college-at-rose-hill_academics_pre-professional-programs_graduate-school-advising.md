# Graduate School Advising

## Graduate School Advising Program

![Female Faculty Member in Front of Slide](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fu_cba_101409_0164_1.jpg)


The Graduate School Advising Program offers sessions that provide undergraduate students with helpful information and advice related to applying to and pursuing degrees in graduate school. These sessions are open to all Fordham University undergraduate students and cover topics such as the Graduate Record Examination (GRE), Financial Aid & Fellowships, Personal Statements & Letters of Recommendation, as well as graduate programs and career pathways for particular fields.
