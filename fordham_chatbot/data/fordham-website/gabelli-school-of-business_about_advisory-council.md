# Advisory Council

Alumni and members of the worldwide corporate community with a vested interest in the success of business education at Fordham are invited to volunteer for the council that advises the Dean.

Council members serve three-year terms on councils focused on topics that are critical to the mission of the business school, such as career placement, globalization, curriculum, technology, and faculty.
