# Ali El Mahdy, GABELLI ‘17

![Ali El Mahdy](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Ali-El-Mahdy.jpg)


El Mahdy’s experience includes years of corporate leadership with full responsibility for profitability and growth in the MENA region. More than 15 years of managing restructuring and revitalization of companies, leading changes and growth strategy development, and efficient hands-on management of organizational, financial, business development, and operational activities.

El Mahdyhas a bachelor's degree in Civil Engineering from Cairo University and has obtained an M.B.A. from the Gabelli School of Business at Fordham University.
