# Edward D. FitzGerald, GABELLI ‘85

![Ed FitzGerald](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/ed_fitzgerald.jpg)


Ed FitzGerald is a vice chairman of Bank of America's Technology, Media, and Telecom Investment Banking Group. He serves as the firm’s head of technology-enabled and business services investment banking. He has over 30 years of investment banking and corporate finance experience. FitzGerald earned an M.B.A. from Columbia University and a B.S. *summa cum laude* from Fordham University. He also completed the professional examination and other requirements to become a CPA.
