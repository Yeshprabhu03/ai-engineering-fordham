# Greg Minson, FCRH ‘98

![Greg Minson](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Greg-Minson.jpg)

Gregory Minson has over 25 years of experience as a senior leader within the financial services industry. He recently joined the Intermediate Capital Group (ICG), the global alternative asset manager, as global head of asset management within its Real Estate business. Greg previously served as global COO for the Real Estate Group within the Asset and Wealth Management Division at Goldman Sachs. Prior to this position, he served as head of Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia-Pacific, as well as global COO of the Realty Management Division at Goldman Sachs. He now serves as chair of the Fordham London Advisory Council.

Greg began his career at Goldman Sachs in 1998 as a financial analyst, shortly after graduating from Fordham. He has served on the Fordham College at Rose Hill Board of Visitors. He earned his undergraduate degree in economics from Rose Hill in 1998 and was a Dean’s Scholar. He lives in London with his wife, Ros, and their two children.
