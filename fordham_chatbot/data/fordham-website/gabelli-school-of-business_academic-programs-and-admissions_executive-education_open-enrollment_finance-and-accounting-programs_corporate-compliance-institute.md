# Corporate Compliance Institute

Euromoney Learning (London) and Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business are partnering to offer two exceptional Compliance Programs during July 2024. Both will take place in the heart of New York City at Fordham's Lincoln Center campus in Manhattan.

**Choose from two unique options:**

## Compliance Risk Management

July 8 – July 10 (3-day program)

$ 2,000 USD

*Designed for those who seek to enhance their effectiveness in compliance and risk, and control roles.*

## Chief Compliance Officer Program

July 15 – July 19 (5-day program)

$ 2,500 USD

*Tailored for those aspiring to manage effective and efficient compliance functions.*

Discounts are available for Fordham alumni and for multiple participants from the same institution. Certificates issued upon completion.

- Immersive learning through lectures from top industry experts, live role play and computer-based simulations, and site visits to New York City financial institutions.
- Builds skills in
**compliance, control, risk, audit, operations**, and other front, middle, and back office functions that bring unique value to your organization from a compliance perspective. - Unprecedented networking opportunities with local, national, and global industry peers.
- The combined reputation and prestige of the Gabelli School of Business, a top U.S. Business School; and Euronews Learning, a driving force in global financial education.
