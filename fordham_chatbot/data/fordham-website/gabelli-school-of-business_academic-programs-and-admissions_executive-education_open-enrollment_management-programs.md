# Management Programs

![Management](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Management.jpg)


No matter what your specific field, executive education in management can make you a better, more effective businessperson and leader.

The Gabelli School’s management systems faculty, in collaboration with colleagues from other business disciplines, has developed open-enrollment programs that make the building blocks of great leadership accessible to all.

Current offerings

[Developing a Sustainable Business Strategy](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/executive-education/open-enrollment/management-programs/developing-a-sustainable-business-strategy/)

In eight weeks, participants will learn to manage for the planet, people, profit, and longevity simultaneously. Culminates in a personalized, actionable strategic plan for your organization big or small.

[Fordham Leadership Accelerator](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/executive-education/open-enrollment/management-programs/fordham-leadership-accelerator/)

Leadership is defined by an unrelenting commitment to improvement and change—which is why leaders in any organization or business field can benefit from the Fordham Leadership Accelerator.
