# Gabelli M.S. Programs

![Gabelli M.S. Programs](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/gsb_ms_programs.jpg)

Many jobs call for training in one particular business niche. To prepare students to land those positions, Fordham's Master of Science programs provide targeted knowledge and skills.

These M.S. programs can often be completed quickly to allow rapid re-entry into the professional world—some in as little as nine months. Students in all M.S. programs are also offered the [Responsible Business Leadership Certification™](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/ms-programs/responsible-business-leadership-certificate/), and can benefit from a number of [consecutive MS degree options](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/consecutive-ms-degree-pathways/). Click on each program to learn more. **Students who earn a degree in select U.S. government-designated STEM programs may be eligible to apply for a 24-month STEM OPT extension.**
