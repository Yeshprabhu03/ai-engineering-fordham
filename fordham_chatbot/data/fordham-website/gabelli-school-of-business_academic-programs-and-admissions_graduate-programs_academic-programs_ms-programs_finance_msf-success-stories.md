# M.S.F. Success Stories

![Adishya Venkateswaran](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Adishya_Venkateswaran.jpg)

Adishya Venkateswaran

**M.S.F. ’18**

*Where are you working now?*

I am working as a senior auditor at the New York City Economic Development Corporation.

*Why Fordham?*

The Gabelli School of Business is one of the top business schools in the country. Coupled with its excellent location situated in the heart of NYC, it allows you to make connections with the best in the industry.

*What was the best part of your program? *

The best part of the M.S.F. program was the opportunity to choose our electives and specialize in distinct topics. I chose subjects with a combination of risk management, investment management and investment banking, which allowed me to become more well-rounded and gain knowledge in different areas of finance.

Brendon Integlia

**M.S.F. ’15**

*Where are you working now?*

I’m a securities analyst at MIBIT Capital Holdings Company.

*What’s the biggest advantage you gained from the Gabelli School M.S.F. program?*

What I learned at the Gabelli School about financial modeling and valuation, among other disciplines, is directly applicable to my work. Not only has it allowed me to bring valuable and important skills to my company, but it also required less on-the-job training from my employer.

*What was your favorite class in the program?*

Emerging Markets with Professor Cheah. I really enjoyed his teaching style and his ability to draw on his vast work experience at the Singapore Monetary Authority. The subject matter is current, real world, and relevant to our lives and careers as global investors.
