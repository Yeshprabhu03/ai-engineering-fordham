# Information Technology Career Pathways & Partners

## Advances That Offer New Opportunities

Because of monumental changes occurring in business and technology, the field of information technology offers more career options than ever before, increasing your ability to excel in a discipline where IT professionals with managerial skills are in high demand. The M.S. in Information Technology prepares multifaceted professionals who secure positions in corporations and organizations across industries as well tech start-ups, in addition to those who choose to launch their own entrepreneurial ventures.

No matter where their professional path leads, they are ready and able to compete and succeed.
