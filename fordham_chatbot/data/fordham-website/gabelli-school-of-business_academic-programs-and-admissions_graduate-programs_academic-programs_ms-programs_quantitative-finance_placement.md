# Placement

The M.S.Q.F. program trains students in the areas of asset management, risk management and algorithmic training and data science. We have a set of highly trained professionals on the market this year. Students have excelled in courses, and done projects with hands-on data experience, in the areas of advanced derivatives pricing, bank risk management and CCAR, machine learning, risk management, textual analysis etc. along with C++, Python, R and SQL.
