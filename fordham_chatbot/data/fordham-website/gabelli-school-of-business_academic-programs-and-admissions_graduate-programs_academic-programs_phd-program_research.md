# Research

![Gabelli School of Business PhD program](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/gsb_phd_research.jpg)


The Gabelli School of Business Ph.D. program believes that fostering an active research environment at the highest level is crucial for doctoral education. As such, the Ph.D. program is an active participant and co-organizer in several research ventures, including journal publishing, academic conference initiatives, partnerships with scholars from around the world, and collaboration with other institutions here at Fordham.
