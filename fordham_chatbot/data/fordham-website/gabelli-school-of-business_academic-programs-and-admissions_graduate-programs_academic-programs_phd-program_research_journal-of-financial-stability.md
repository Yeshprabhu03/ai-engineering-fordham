# Journal of Financial Stability

The Gabelli School is home to a top-tier business publication, the Journal of Financial Stability. Founded in 2004 and published in collaboration with Elsevier, the JFS has joined the ranks of international business journals as a rising publication that specifically considers the questions and issues surrounding global financial crises. The JFS is primarily focused on applied research with public policy applications and has been particularly active in publishing special issues in collaboration with partner universities and institutions, including:
