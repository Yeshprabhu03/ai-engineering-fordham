# Participating Programs

IBB, students may apply to any of the [specialized master's programs](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/ms-programs/) offered at the Gabelli School of Business, for which you are qualified, without having to retake a language test. Please consult your candidacy with a member of the Gabelli Graduate Admissions team.

Please note:

- Suggested minimum language score for IBB: TOEFL iBT 80+/ IELTS 6.0+/ PTE Academics 53+
- The Gabelli School will determine how many semesters of the IBB program a student is required to do prior to enrollment in a Gabelli School MS program
