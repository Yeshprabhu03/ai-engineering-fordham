# Professional (Part time) M.B.A.

## Earn an M.B.A. Your Way

Pursuing an M.B.A. while balancing personal and professional obligations can be a challenge. The Gabelli School of Business **45-credit Professional (Part time) M.B.A. (PMBA) **provides the flexibility and support to make it happen.

The program offers a wealth of advantages for those who seek to enhance their knowledge and advance or change their career, but who need to do it on their own terms. Taught by world-renowned faculty, the Professional M.B.A. can be completed in as little as 21–24 months through **evening and weekend classes at Fordham’s Lincoln Center campus** and through **online courses**. A [variety of scholarships](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/graduate-admissions/financing-your-graduate-education/admissions-scholarships/) and employer tuition reimbursement programs, allow our students to earn their degree affordably.

Study in a supportive learning environment with professionals who strive to get ahead. Benefit from [comprehensive academic advising and career development services](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/professional-mba/pmba--academic-advising-career-development/) while growing your professional network. Gain deep international insights through our [one-week global study immersion program](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/academic-programs/professional-mba/pmba-one-week-global-immersion-program/).

### Frequently Asked Questions

-
Pursuing a part time M.B.A. remains a highly valuable option for many working professionals. It allows them to maintain their current employment and income while advancing their education. One of the key advantages is the ability to immediately apply newly acquired knowledge and skills to their existing roles, enhancing both learning and job performance. Increasingly, part time M.B.A. students are leveraging their education to earn promotions, transition to new employers, or even pivot to entirely different careers. Perhaps most significantly, these programs offer rich opportunities for networking and collaboration with fellow professionals, fostering connections that can benefit both personal and professional growth.

-
Although completion time may vary among students depending upon their job commitments and personal preferences, most part time MBA (PMBA) students are able to complete their degree within approximately 21 months.

-
Generous scholarships are available to help make your education more affordable. PMBA students also may be eligible for employer tuition reimbursement, which can offset the cost of the program. This reimbursement typically varies by employer, ranging from a fixed dollar amount to a specific percentage of the total tuition expenses. Such support can make pursuing a part time MBA more financially accessible for working professionals.

-
Part time MBA (PMBA) students have access to comprehensive academic advising and support throughout their program. At the start of their journey, they receive detailed information about academic requirements during orientation. To further assist with planning, webinars are offered before each registration period, providing guidance and updates. Additionally, weekly virtual walk-in hours are available for students who have questions regarding their academic progress or related concerns. For more personalized support, students can also schedule individual appointments at any time to discuss specific issues or seek tailored advice.

-
Yes, tutoring is available to PMBA students who seek additional support beyond the classroom experience. Students can request tutoring particularly for quantitatively focused courses such as Statistics, Economics, Accounting, and Finance. This resource ensures that students have the help they need to succeed academically, especially in more challenging subject areas.

-
Yes, the PMBA program is thoughtfully designed with working professionals in mind, incorporating flexible program structures, course scheduling, and supportive activities that accommodate their busy lives. With careful planning and disciplined effort, PMBA students are able to successfully navigate and complete the program while balancing their professional and personal commitments.

-
PMBA students benefit from a wide range of career services, including access to a dedicated career advisor who provides guidance through multiple stages of career planning and management. This support covers everything from exploring new job opportunities to navigating complete career transitions. In addition, PMBA students enjoy exclusive access to seasoned Executive Coaches—professionals with extensive experience advising Wall Street executives—who offer valuable insights and support on career-related topics, helping students make strategic decisions and achieve their professional goals.

[Please visit the Student Success Center academic career development site.](/gabelli-school-of-business/student-and-career-resources/graduate-student-success-center/graduate-career-development/) -
From the perspective of employers and the general public, there is no distinction in respect or reputation between part time and full time M.B.A. programs. Both are held in equally high regard. Furthermore, the diploma awarded to graduates simply states that they have earned an M.B.A. degree, with no indication of whether the program was completed on a part time or full time basis.
