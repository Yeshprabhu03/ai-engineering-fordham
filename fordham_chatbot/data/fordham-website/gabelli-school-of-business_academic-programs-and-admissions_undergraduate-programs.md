## What makes Fordham different?

Ask yourself this question: Do you want to shape the world through your career? Or do you just want a job? Fordham prepares you to transform society—to be the game-changer who comes up with innovative solutions that turn everyone's head.

Our education goes beyond business fundamentals. We give you [leadership training](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/core-curriculum/senior-year-leadership/). We teach you [problem-solving skills](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/core-curriculum/). We develop your ability to write and speak in ways that put CEOs, chief financial officers and chief marketing officers on a higher plane.

You will learn all of these things in the middle of the business capital of the world—New York City—with access to [global programs](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/global-business-education/) that introduce you to business on multiple continents and [scores of countries.](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/global-business-education/study-abroad/)

Fordham will prepare you for a career that excites you to get up in the morning and where you can see the meaningful difference your work makes.
