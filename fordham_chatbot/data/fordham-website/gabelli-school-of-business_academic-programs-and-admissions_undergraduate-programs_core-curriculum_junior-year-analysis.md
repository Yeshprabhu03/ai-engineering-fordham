# Junior Year: Analysis

When: Usually in junior year, fall or spring.

What You’ll Learn

This block is where you discover that business is driven by data. You will learn to take that data and analyze it to improve performance.

What courses You’ll Take

- Core business courses: Operations and Business Law
- Various core liberal arts courses: Fulfill any Fordham liberal arts requirements you did not complete earlier in your academic program
- Specific required and elective courses toward your major and/or concentration(s)

Analysis Real-World Project

Data is a crucial ingredient to informed decision-making, no matter your major. First, you’ll choose one of three analysis classes: financial modeling, marketing analytics, or business analytics. Then, you and your class will use big data to determine which move a company should make next.
