# Global Business Honors Program Travel

The Global Business Honors Program lives up to its name.

Students travel to the United Kingdom and either Asia or Latin America during their four years in the program, developing an understanding of what business looks like in other countries and other cultures.

Here is a sample itinerary of a recent trip to Santiago, Chile:

**University Lectures**

- The Chilean Economy
- Chilean History & Politics

**Company Visits**

- Banco Central de Chile (The Central Bank of Chile)
- Empresas Copec
- Casas del Bosque Vineyards

**Cultural Experiences**

- Bicycle tour of Santiago
- Visit to Jesuit Country House
