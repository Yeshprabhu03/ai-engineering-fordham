# Accounting Area

## #20 Best Undergraduate Accounting Programs

*U.S. News & World Report Best Colleges 2026*

Accounting has been part of the Gabelli School of Business since its founding in 1920. The proud tradition continues today. In an average graduating class, nearly one-fifth of all seniors go on to jobs in the Big Four.

Ways you can pursue accounting at Rose Hill:

- As a major (four major options - please see below)
- As a primary concentration
- As a secondary concentration
- As a minor (for Fordham College liberal arts majors)

Ways you can pursue accounting at Lincoln Center:

- As a secondary concentration
- As preparation for the CPA exam with a primary concentration in Global Finance and Business Economics

The four accounting major choices are:

- Public Accounting (120-credit, often a lead-in to a
[dual degree](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/undergraduate-to-graduate-pathways/)toward CPA licensure) - Public Accountancy (150-credit, 5-year program for CPA licensure)
- Applied Accounting and Finance (an accounting/finance hybrid, can be a lead-in to a
[dual degree](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/undergraduate-to-graduate-pathways/)toward CPA licensure) - Accounting Information Systems (for the growing field of accounting IT, can be a lead-in to a
[dual degree](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/undergraduate-to-graduate-pathways/)toward CPA licensure )

Accounting courses you might take:

- Assurance and Auditing
- Global Financial Statement Analysis
- Corporate and Partnership Taxation
- Ethics in Financial Reporting and Accounting
