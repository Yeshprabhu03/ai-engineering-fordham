# Business Analytics Track

This track prepares students for careers in one of today’s **fastest-growing areas in all areas of business**.

Data is crucial to business performance. **It’s what allows companies to make smart, well-reasoned, informed decisions.** Students who can spin management insights out of raw data will be beyond attractive to employers.

Courses in this track will teach students how to collect, clean, structure, integrate and analyze data to improve a company. Students who complete the Business Analytics sequence will be familiar with concepts, frameworks, trends, and software tools and techniques.
