# Digital Business Innovation

**Technology is an agent of transformation in business.** All business

Students in the Digital Business Innovation track will learn how to **harness technology to create major change for companies**.

With a command of cutting-edge trends such as ; e-commerce, mobile commerce, cloud computing, social technologies and tech startups, they will be able to understand and exploit disruptive digital innovation.
