A minimum of 30 credits must be taken at the graduate level. A 6-credit course waiver may not be available for MSA and MST due to CPA requirements.

There are more reasons than ever to complete a Gabelli School MS Degree:

- Up to six credits from undergraduate degree may be counted toward Gabelli School MS degree
- GMAT/GRE waivers may be available
- Expedited application process
- Application fee waived
- All 4+1 applicants will be considered for a maximum $15K scholarship; for students with a 3.5 GPA or higher, you may be eligible for the Early Leaders program.

**For Example:**

- FC Economics Major students can waive 6 credits of MS in Global Finance with two preapproved Economics courses, such as ECON 3347 International Finance and ECON 3636 Money and Banking.
