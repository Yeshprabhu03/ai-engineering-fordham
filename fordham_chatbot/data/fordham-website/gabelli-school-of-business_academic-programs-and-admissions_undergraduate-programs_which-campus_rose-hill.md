# Gabelli at Rose Hill

![Gabelli School at Rose Hill](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/change8.jpg)

Fordham's campus at Rose Hill is where most Gabelli School students pursue their business degrees. Rose Hill offers the best of two worlds: a [traditional college campus](http://www.youvisit.com/tour/fordham?pl=v) that feels like an oasis from the city, plus excellent proximity to Manhattan. It takes only 20 minutes to get from Rose Hill to midtown on the Metro-North train.

Students at Rose Hill pursue a Bachelor of Science degree, choosing from among the principal disciplines that drive the business world. The Rose Hill program offers a wide variety of [majors, concentrations, and minors.](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/)

What makes the Rose Hill program different?

- A peaceful, park-like
[college campus](http://www.youvisit.com/tour/fordham?pl=v)set within the city - Full bachelor of science degrees in core fields such as
[accounting](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/accounting/),[finance](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/finance/), and[marketing](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/marketing/). - Academic concentrations in
[entrepreneurship](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/entrepreneurship/),[sports business](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/sports-business/),[value investing](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/value-investing/),[alternative investments](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/alternative-investments/), and[sustainable business](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/majors-and-concentrations/social-innovation-and-sustainable-business/) [Hughes Hall](/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/undergraduate-programs/which-campus/hughes-hall/), a state-of-the-art building for business education- A larger program: About 450 students in each grade/class level
