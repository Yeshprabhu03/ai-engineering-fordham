# Alumni & Professional Events

## Meet, Mingle, and Network

Throughout the year, the Gabelli School of Business Office of Alumni Engagement and numerous academic departments and centers offer a range of opportunities for alumni to get together, network, learn, or just have fun! The Fordham University Alumni Association also holds events across the country and around the world.

Gabelli School Alumni & Professional Events

From large-scale industry conferences, such as the [International Cyber Security Conference](/gabelli-school-of-business/faculty/research-centers/center-for-professional-accounting-practices/news-and-events/future-events/the-international-conference-on-cyber-security/), [The Good Business That Comes From Good Business Conference](/gabelli-school-of-business/industry-collaborations/responsible-business-center/programming--events/good-business-is-good-business-conference/), and the [AI in Healthcare Conference](https://www.gabelliconnect.com/featured-events/gabelli-school-global-healthcare-innovation-management-center-in-collaboration-with-maynooth-university-hosts-conference-on-ai-in-healthcare/), to online webinars and social gatherings with top speakers, Gabelli School alumni have a wide range of ways they can interact in person and from afar. The School also hosts alumni events from NYC to LA and Miami to Beijing, bringing graduates together from across the disciplines and around the globe.

University Alumni Events

Home to more than 200,000 alumni, Fordham University offers a wide range of local, regional, national, and international events to keep alumni engaged and active. From large-scale events such as the [Jubilee Weekend](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=6999), [Block Party at Lincoln Center](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=9554), and [Homecoming](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=1095) to Regional Chapter Events.
