# Alumni Resources & Benefits

Whether you graduated this past May or several decades ago, the doors to our three campuses are always open to you - as long as you have your [digital Ram Pass](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=3102). Below are resources and benefits offered to Gabelli School of Business alumni.

Get started [by claiming and updating your alumni profile](http://forever.fordham.edu/myprofile) so you don’t miss any updates or important information.

**Coming Back to Campus**

-
[Apply for your Ram Pass so you can access on-campus resources](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=3102) -
[Read up on how to access the Fordham University libraries](https://fordham.libguides.com/AlumniLibraryServices/Home)

**Alumni Career Resources**

-
[Make an account on Fordham’s networking career platform, RamConnect](https://ramconnect.fordham.edu/hub/fordham/auth/create-account) -
[Request access to the Gabelli School Alumni LinkedIn group](https://www.linkedin.com/groups/57697/) -
[Peruse job listings or schedule an appointment with an alumni career advisor (graduate alumni only)](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2fdashboard) -
[Discuss your career plan with an alumni career coach](https://www.fordham.edu/resources/career-resources/career-center/for-alumni/) -
[Request a copy of your transcript](https://forever.fordham.edu/s/1362/18/interior.aspx?sid=1362&gid=1&pgid=8874)
