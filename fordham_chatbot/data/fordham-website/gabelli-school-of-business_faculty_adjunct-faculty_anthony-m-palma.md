# Anthony M. Palma, CAMS, CFE, CFSA

## Adjunct Professor of Finance

![Anthony M. Palma](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Anthony_Palma.jpg)

Anthony M. Palma is a member of the adjunct faculty of Fordham University’s Gabelli Business School with subject matter expertise in enterprise risk assessments, crisis management, business ethics, global corporate compliance, global corporate governance, fintech compliance, investor relations, corporate communications, and business writing.

Prior to joining Fordham University, Palma spent his career in corporate leadership positions at Morgan Stanley, Citigroup, JPMorgan Chase, Merrill Lynch, and Bankers Trust Company.

A frequent speaker at numerous financial services industry conferences, Palma is a senior advisor to a number of corporations and is a member of the board of directors of the Fiduciary and Investment Risk Management Association and the WH Hospitality Group.

He has contributed to and edited two books, *The Corporate Compliance Handbook* and the *Corporate Legal Department Handbook*. Tony is a Certified Anti-Money Laundering Specialist (CAMS), a Certified Financial Services Auditor (CFSA) and a Certified Fraud Examiner (CFE).

Palma earned a B.A. in Economics from Fordham University and an M.S. in Professional Accounting degree from Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business. He has participated in post-graduate programs at New York University, The New School, and Monmouth University.
