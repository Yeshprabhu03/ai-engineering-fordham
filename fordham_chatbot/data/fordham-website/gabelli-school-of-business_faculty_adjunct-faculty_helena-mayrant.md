# Helena Mayrant

## Adjunct Professor of Communications

![Helena Mayrant](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Helena_Mayrant.jpg)


Helena Mayrant is an experienced professional with a passion for communications, media strategy, and project management. Her success has been proven in social media, client relations, organizational structure, and systems integration projects. In her current role at Warner Bros. Discovery, she is crucial in building the bridges of communication and workflows amongst teams.

Mayrant is a proud alumna of Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business, where she completed a bachelor’s degree with a double concentration in marketing and communications and an M.S in Media Management.
