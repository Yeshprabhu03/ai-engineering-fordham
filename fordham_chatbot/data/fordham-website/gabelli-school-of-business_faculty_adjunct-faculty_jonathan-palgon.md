# Jonathan Palgon

## Adjunct Professor of Communications

![Jonathan Palgon](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Jonathan_Palgon.jpg)

Jonathan Palgon currently serves as senior director of product at Bleacher Report. His previous roles include ad ops manager at Condé Nast, media operations and analysis manager at TouchTunes, and senior director of ad ops and account management at Insticator. Palgon graduated from Duke University with an bachelor’s degree in economics and a minor in history. He earned an M.B.A. from Fordham in 2017, with a dual concentration in communications and media management, and finance.
