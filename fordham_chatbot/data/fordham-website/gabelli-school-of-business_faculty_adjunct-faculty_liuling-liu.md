# Liuling Liu

## Adjunct Professor of Finance

![Liuling Liu](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Liuling_Liu.jpg)

Liuling Liu holds a Ph.D. in finance from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI) and an M.S. in actuarial science from the University of Kent in the U.K. She has experience in teaching financial technology (fintech), cryptocurrency and blockchain (NFT), financial markets, financial institutions and risk management. Her research has been published in several core academic journals such as the *Journal of Financial Intermediation*, the *Journal of Corporate Finance*, the *Journal of Banking & Finance*, the *Journal of Business Ethics*, the *Journal of Financial Stability*, the *Journal of International Money and Finance*, and the *Journal of Financial Service Research*, among others.
