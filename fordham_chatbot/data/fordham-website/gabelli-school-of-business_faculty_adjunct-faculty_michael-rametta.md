# Michael Rametta

## Adjunct Professor of Business Law

![Michael Rametta](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Michael_Rametta.jpg)

Michael Rametta has been part of the Fordham community ever since he was a first-year student at Fordham College at Rose Hill. He has earned three degrees from Fordham: a B.A. in Philosophy and French Language and Literature; an M.A. in Ethics and Society; and an M.B.A. in Management Systems. His main interests in business ethics center on virtue theory.
