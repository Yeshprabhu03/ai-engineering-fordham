# Remy Blaire

## Adjunct Professor of Communications

![Remy Blaire](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Remy_Blaire.jpg)


Remy Blaire is a seasoned business journalist and communications professional with international experience in finance and management. She earned her executive M.B.A. from Fordham Universityâ€™s Gabelli School of Business, where she was a recipient of the Dean's Scholarship and the Academic Area Award in Strategy & Statistics.

Blaire is a distinguished member of Alpha Sigma Nu and the National Honor Society of the Financial Management Association. She graduated *magna cum laude* from NYU with a B.A. in International Studies.

Throughout her career, Blaire has covered myriad market events from the New York Stock Exchange, including the FTX collapse, the COVID-19 outbreak, the 2008 Financial Crisis, the Flash Crash, and the S&P downgrade of U.S. debt. Her professional journey has seen her working in traditional print and broadcast newsrooms, as well as in emerging streaming media startups in the U.S. and Japan. She honed her storytelling prowess during her tenure at legacy outlets such as CNN, France24, and Newsweek magazine, along with her work as a contributor to Cheddar News, TheStreet and Yahoo! Finance.

Dedicated to excellence in both academia and journalism, Blaire is committed to inspiring students and audiences alike through innovative teaching methodologies and insightful analysis.
