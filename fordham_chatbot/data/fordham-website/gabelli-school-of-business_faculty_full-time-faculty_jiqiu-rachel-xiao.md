Jiqiu “Rachel” Xiao joined the Gabelli School of Business in September 2023 as an assistant professor of Finance and Business Economics. She earned a Ph.D. in Finance from J. Mack Robinson College of Business at Georgia State University. Her research interests include empirical corporate finance, ESG, fintech, and household finance.
