Dr. Sheng joined the Gabelli School in 2017 as an associate clinical professor in finance, teaching in the quantitative finance area. Prior to joining Fordham, Dr. Sheng worked on Wall Street for 20 years and has a wide range of experience including derivatives modeling, running a multi-strategy hedge fund, automated market making in fixed-income securities, and high-speed options trading.

Dr. Sheng earned a bachelor’s degree in physics from Peking University and a Ph.D. in physics from Cornell University.
