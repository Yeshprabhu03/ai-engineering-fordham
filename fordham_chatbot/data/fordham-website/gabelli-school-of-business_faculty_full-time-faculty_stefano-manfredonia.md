**Stefano Manfredonia is an Assistant Professor of Finance at Fordham University. He obtained his Ph.D. in**[Economics and Finance at the University of Rome Tor Vergata](https://economia.uniroma2.it/phd/ef/board/)at end of 2021. During his Ph.D., he has been a visiting scholar at the[Einaudi Institute for Economics and Finance](http://www.eief.it/eief/index.php)(EIEF), a visiting researcher at[Fordham University](https://www.fordham.edu/info/20447/gabelli_school_of_business), and worked as a consultant for the[International Fund of Agricultural Development](https://www.ifad.org/en/)(IFAD). His main research interest is in empirical corporate finance and banking. -
