# Professionals in Practice

The** Professionals in Practice** video series, published by the Center for Professional Accounting Practices, showcases prominent members of the accounting profession and provides them with a platform to discuss the evolving and escalating challenges of today. The series contributes to the enrichment of Gabelli School students, alumni, and faculty by featuring interviews, round table discussions and other creative ways to illuminate theories, concepts, and useful practices in all areas of professional accounting practices.
