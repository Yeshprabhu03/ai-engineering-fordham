# Conferences

Thought leadership is crucially important to the Gabelli School of Business community, with faculty members playing a critical role through their research, projects and professional and academic connections. Academic conferences, seminars and symposiums provide the opportunity for faculty members to present and discuss research, as well as share and discover interesting and timely developments in their fields.
