# Journal of Accounting, Auditing and Finance (JAAF) Symposium 2025

**The Journal of Accounting, Auditing and Finance (JAAF) Symposium** will take place **October 3–4, 2025**, at the Gabelli School of Business, Fordham University Lincoln Center Campus in New York City. The Symposium is arranged in collaboration with Fordham University.
