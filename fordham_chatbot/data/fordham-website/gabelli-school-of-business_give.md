### The Gabelli School of Business Fund

This is the general fund for the Gabelli School of Business, which gives the dean the greatest flexibility to address the most pressing needs of the School. The Fund is able to provide unrestricted support for academic programs and extracurricular activities at the Gabelli School of Business.

Our students are our pride and the reason for our existence. Their success is our success. To ensure they have access to our unique brand of business education and the opportunities it provides, you can help fund scholarships for current academically outstanding, full-time undergraduate or graduate students who demonstrate financial need.

### Faculty Excellence Fund

At the Gabelli School, our students are taught and mentored by faculty members who are at the forefront of research in their academic areas, as well as practitioners who bring insights and first-hand experience from their field directly into the classroom. We are dedicating resources to equip them with the tools and support they need to achieve excellence in teaching, scholarship, and service.
