# Fordham AI Industry Roundtable

**SAVE THE DATE****Thursday, December 4, 2025**

**9:00 a.m. – 12:00 p.m.****Fordham University | Lincoln Center Campus****113 W 60th Street, New York, NY 10023****(Lowenstein Building, 12th Floor)**

About the Event

Artificial intelligence is transforming how organizations create value, manage risk, and compete in a rapidly evolving economy. Yet the path from innovation to implementation requires collaboration — across industries, disciplines, and communities.

Hosted by the Fordham AI Hub at the Gabelli School of Business, this invitation-only roundtable will convene senior business leaders across sectors for an interactive discussion on advancing responsible and inclusive AI adoption that drives both growth and public trust.

Together, participants will explore how to:

- Build an AI-ready workforce equipped for the future of business.
- Scale ethical and applied AI that delivers measurable impact.
- Strengthen cross-sector partnerships to ensure innovation benefits all.

Event Format

The Roundtable will feature:

- Opening insights from Fordham leadership and faculty experts.
- Moderated roundtable discussions led by Fordham’s new AI Industry Fellows.
- A networking reception to close the session and connect peers shaping the next era of responsible AI.

Participation

This is a no-cost, invitation-only event designed to foster candid, solutions-driven dialogue among senior decision-makers.
