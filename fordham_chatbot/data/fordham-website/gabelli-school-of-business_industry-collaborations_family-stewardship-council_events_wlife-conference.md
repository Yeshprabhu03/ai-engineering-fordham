# WLife Conference

## Women Leading in Family Enterprises

**SAVE THE DATE****March 12-13, 2026**

**Fordham University | New York, NYSchool of Law | 150 West 62 nd StreetCostantino and Bateman Rooms, 2nd Floor**

Purpose of the Conference

The *WLife Conference* is a groundbreaking, high-impact gathering designed to support, lift, and educate women across the family business ecosystem—from rising next-gen leaders to seasoned executives, owners, advisors, and key influencers.

This annual event is a beacon for elevating women’s voices, leadership, and presence in a space where personal identity, business legacy, and family values intersect. It is intentionally curated to:

- Celebrate the unique contributions of women in family-owned enterprises
- Build a vibrant and supportive network across generations, geographies, and industries
- Share practical tools, research, and insights to help women grow their leadership capabilities and confidence
- Spark real conversations about gender dynamics, ownership, succession, governance, and entrepreneurship
- Create visibility for women leaders who are shaping the future of family businesses locally and globally

Who Should Attend?

- Women business owners and family business leaders
- Rising generation successors and entrepreneurs
- Advisors (legal, wealth, management)
- Academics and students from leading business schools
- Corporate leaders committed to gender equity and family business sustainability
