# Collaborations for Recruiting Talent

![Young woman looking at laptop](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/gsb-talent-header.jpg)


The next generation of your company's leaders is studying at the Gabelli School today. We can work with you to identify current students, recent graduates, and experienced alumni who offer not only business skill and knowledge, but also a sense of purpose that comes with a values-oriented business education.

**For collaborators:**

Gain seamless access to our top-tier talent and showcase academic programs

**For students:**

Build direct connections to valuable career opportunities across a range of business fields

**For faculty:**

Provide students with distinct pathways that help them achieve career goals
