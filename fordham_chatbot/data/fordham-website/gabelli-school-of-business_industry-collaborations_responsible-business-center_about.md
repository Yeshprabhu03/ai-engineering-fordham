# About the Responsible Business Center

## Inspiring Business for Good

![Responsible Business Conference](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/responsible-business-conference.jpg)

**Responsible Business Center **at Fordham University’s Gabelli School of Business is a platform for interdisciplinary engagement in sustainability, ethical leadership, and business responsibility. Rooted in **Jesuit values**—*People for Others* and *cura personalis* (care for the whole person)—we advance a mission to **inspire, inform, and activate** business professionals, academics, and emerging leaders to collaboratively foster a more **just and sustainable business ecosystem** that serves all people and protects our planet.

Convenings & Engagement

Advance, convene, & inform cross-industry collaborations

Research & Consulting

Generate & support research

Education & Knowledge

Enrich education to activate responsible leadership

### Our Mission

To **inspire, inform, and activate** business professionals, academics, and emerging leaders to collaboratively foster a more **just and sustainable business ecosystem** that serves all people and sustains our planet.
