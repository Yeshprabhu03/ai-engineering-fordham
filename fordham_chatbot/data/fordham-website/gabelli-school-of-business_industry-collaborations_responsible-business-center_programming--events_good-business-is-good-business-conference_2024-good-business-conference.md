# 2024 Good Business Conference

## October 24, 2024 8:00 a.m. â€“ 6:00 p.m.

**Gabelli School of Business - McNally Amphitheater140 West 62nd Street, New York, NY, 10023 **

Join us at Fordham University's, Gabelli School of Business, Responsible Business Center for an enlightening and impactful event, **"The Good Business that comes from Good Business."** This dynamic gathering will convene corporate leaders, students, and academia to explore and discuss critical topics shaping the future of responsible leadership. Through insightful discussions, the exchange of innovative ideas, and collaborative networking, participants will delve into three main verticals: **Impacts for People and Planet, Resilient Food Systems**, and **Responsible Markets and Disclosures**. Our aim is to inspire and activate a new wave of responsible leadership that prioritizes the well-being of both society and the planet.
