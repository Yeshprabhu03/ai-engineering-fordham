# 2023 Graduate Diploma Ceremony Archive

The Class of 2023 enjoyed a sunny afternoon commencement ceremony with thousands in attendance to watch graduates receive their ceremonial diploma scrolls from Dean Lerzan Aksoy. Relive the memories and your time at the Gabelli School of Business by browsing through the archival content on this page.

## Relive Your Special Day – Tuesday, 23, 2023

**Graduate Diploma Ceremony Photo Gallery **

The Gabelli School of Business Graduate Diploma Ceremony, which occurred on Tuesday, May 23, 2023, was attended by nearly 650 graduates and thousands of family members and friends who cheered them on. Dean Lerzan Aksoy presided over the ceremony, with Fordham University President Tania Tetlow and Fordham Trustee Valerie Irick Rainford (FCRH ’86), delivering moving addresses. A celebratory champagne toast capped off the event.
