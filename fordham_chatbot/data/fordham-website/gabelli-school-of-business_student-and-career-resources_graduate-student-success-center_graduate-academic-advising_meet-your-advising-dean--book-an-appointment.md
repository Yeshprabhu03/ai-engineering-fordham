# Meet Your Advising Dean / Book An Appointment

## You’re in Great Hands!

Our advising deans have years of professional experience and are trained to ensure that you are receiving a level of guidance that is second to none. They are keen listeners who provide solid advice and invaluable support to ensure that your academic experience at the Gabelli School equips you for all that is to come.
