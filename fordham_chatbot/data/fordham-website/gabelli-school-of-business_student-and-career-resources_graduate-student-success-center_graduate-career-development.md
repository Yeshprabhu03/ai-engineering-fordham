# Graduate Career Development

**Charting a Course to Career Success**

Graduate Career Development services provided by the Gabelli School of Business focus on meeting you wherever you are in your career journey and finding a successful and satisfying path forward. Our professional staff of career advisors is innately attuned to the rapid changes occurring in today’s business environment due to paradigm shifts in the workplace, the globalization of the workforce, and the permeation of new technologies across industries.

We are here to help you explore your strengths, uncover your talents, discover your interests, and build upon the knowledge you have acquired at the Gabelli School. All of these become part of the framework to identify and secure the employment opportunities that best align with your current and future career goals and objectives.

![Students Reviewing Career Materials](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Students_Reviewing_Career_Materials.jpg)


## For Current Graduate Students

Students who are currently enrolled in any of the Gabelli School graduate programs can take advantage of the multitude of services we provide—from resume writing and review to mock interviews, career assessment, and job boards. It’s never too soon to explore all that we have to offer and to make an appointment with a Graduate Career Development advisor. We are here to help you make well-informed decisions about your career as you begin your studies and throughout your educational journey.

![Employer With Mentee](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Employer_With_Mentee.jpg)


## For Employers

The reputation of the Gabelli School of Business is built upon the cutting-edge curricula we develop and deliver to prepare our students for the future of business. It is our deeply rooted relationships with industry partners, however, that are critical in providing them with the internships and job placements, which will ultimately drive their career success. We look forward to working with you to develop long-standing connections that will benefit both our talented students and your business.

![Alumni Networking](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Alumni_Networking.jpg)


## For Alumni

Gabelli School of Business alumni number more than 45,000 strong. They live and work in countries around the world, utilizing the knowledge and connections they have gained here to define their professional paths and advance their careers. Because of the positive outcomes they have enjoyed in their own careers, many choose to give back by serving as mentors to our students, and/or providing networking, internship, and job opportunities.
