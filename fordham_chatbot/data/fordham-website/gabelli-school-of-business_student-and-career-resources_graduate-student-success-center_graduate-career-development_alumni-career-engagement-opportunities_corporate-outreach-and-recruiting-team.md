# Corporate Outreach and Recruiting Team

**Connecting the Boardroom & the Classroom**

At the Gabelli School of Business, we take tremendous pride in our deep industry connections and the pathways they provide for our students. If you are an employer or an alumna/nus who is interested in hiring our students, providing an internship or networking opportunity, affording them the chance to visit your company or organization, or participating in one our many professionally focused events as a panelist or guest speaker, we would love to connect. Please reach out through the contact information below and help the next generation of business leaders to gain the head start they need for a successful and satisfying career.

![Maricel Piriz](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Maricel-Piriz.jpg)


**Maricel Piriz**Director of Corporate Partnerships & Recruiting

**Maricel Piriz** develops and maintains the connections with employers who hire Gabelli School students. She provides a wide range of creative ways for companies, alumni, and industry leaders to connect with the Gabelli School and get to know our students. The impact of her work is felt in a great deal of the School’s career development programming. Before joining Fordham, she was assistant director of the Paul Milstein Center for Real Estate at Columbia Business School. She holds a B.S. in Business Administration from Georgetown University’s McDonough School of Business and an M.P.A. in Public and Nonprofit Management and Policy from New York University’s Robert F. Wagner Graduate School of Public Service.

![Yasmeen Hassan](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Yasmeen-Hassan-24.jpg)


**Yasmeen Hassan**Assistant Director, Campus Recruiting

**Yasmeen Hassan** is the Assistant Director of Campus Recruiting with the Career Development Center. She supports both the outreach and recruiting functions as well as managing aspects of the career portal. She holds a B.A. in Education from St. Francis College and an M.S. in Forensic Science from Syracuse University.
