# Career Job Boards

**Access to Opportunity**

As a student enrolled in any of our graduate degree programs, you’ll have access to job boards that can help you to match your skills with open opportunities across industries as they become available. From finance to technology, marketing to media, there are a wealth of options to explore. Use these resources directly by visiting the sites below. Don’t forget to set up alerts to ensure you are receiving information on positions that are posted in real time.


![Student Searching Career Job Boards](/media/home/schools/gabelli-school-of-business/Student_Searching_Career_Job_Boards.jpg)


-
[CareerPath](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/dashboard)is our Career Services Management portal where you can search and[apply for jobs](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2fjobPostings#/jobPostings/index),[schedule career advising appointments](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2fappointments),[register for events](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2fevents#/events),[report your jobs and internships](https://business-gabelli-fordham.12twenty.com/Account/Login?ReturnUrl=%2fProfile%3ftab%3djobs&tab=jobs), and more. -
[Careernomics.com](https://www.careernomics.com)is an integrated careers platform, that is focused on helping master’s degree students explore and identify the right career opportunities. It helps students to:- Broaden the job search and identify new employers and opportunities outside of the student radar
- Explore fast-track leadership development programs
- Deep-dive into specific opportunities and easily access the key information they need—from recruiting trends to culture and work permits
- Engage with employers live during online events

To set up a job alert in Careernomics so you’ll be notified when jobs matching your interests are posted,*You should regularly check Careernomics directly for opportunities.*[follow the instructions on the video here](https://www.careernomics.com/about-us/video.php). -
