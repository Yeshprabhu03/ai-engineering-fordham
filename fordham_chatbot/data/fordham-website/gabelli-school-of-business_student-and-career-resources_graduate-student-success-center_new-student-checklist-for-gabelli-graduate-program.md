# New Student Checklist for Gabelli Graduate Program

The below checklist is designed to prepare you for your studies at the Gabelli Graduate Program before you [start classes](https://go.activecalendar.com/FordhamUniversity/site/academic/?&ql=y&search=y). While there is a lot to cover, we are confident that by completing all the below tasks, you will be set up for success during your studies. We have also included some helpful links to various university offices.

The checklist is broken down into four (4) categories:

**Full-Time MBA, Professional MBA and Masters**
