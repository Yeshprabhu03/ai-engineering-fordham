# Gabelli Undergraduate Student Career Outcomes

Within six months of graduation, **95% **of the undergraduate Class of 2024 was placed in jobs, graduate education, the military, or service programs.

Their median starting salary was **$82k.**

**97%** of the members of the Class of 2024 reported holding at least one internship during their time at the Gabelli School, and many said that their internship led to a full-time offer.
