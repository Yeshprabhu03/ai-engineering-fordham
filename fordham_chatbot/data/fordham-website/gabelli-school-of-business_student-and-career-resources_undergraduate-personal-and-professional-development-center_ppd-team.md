# PPD Team

## Meet Your Career Advisors

Our team of industry-specific advisors covers the three largest majors at the Gabelli School of Business: accounting, finance, and marketing.

To make an appointment with one of the PPD team members, please make an appointment via [The Hub](https://fordham.my.site.com/success/s/my-appointments).

We look forward to working with you.

**Gina Hill LoBasso, PhD****Associate Director of Career Advising Industry Focus: Marketing**

[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#472f2e2b2b2b28252634342807212835232f262a69222332)

Gina Hill LoBasso has more than 25 years of experience across business and academia, specializing in marketing strategy, marketing communication, management, and leadership. As the Gabelli School’s undergraduate career advisor for marketing, she helps to make corporate connections for students and prepares them to compete successfully for internships and jobs.

Dr. Hill LoBasso's industry experience includes more than 11 years in business-to-business marketing and sales management. As director of direct marketing sales for the computer publisher Ziff Davis, she was responsible for new business and strategy development with industry leaders including Microsoft, Apple and IBM. She also was responsible for the training, development, and management of a highly successful sales team. Dr. Hill LoBasso has consulted for various for-profit and nonprofit organizations on domestic and international operations; her responsibilities have included developing competitive marketing and sales strategies and designing professional development and management skills workshops.

On the academic side, Dr. Hill LoBasso has taught marketing at Pace University and serves as an adjunct instructor at the Gabelli School. She also has been an assistant professor of mass communication at Iona University, where she earned her MBA. Dr. Hill LoBasso has completed her doctoral degree in management and organizational leadership with a dissertation titled “Identifying Opportunities for Innovation in Transformational Leadership: An Exploratory Qualitative Inquiry.”
