**Overview:** The BSSF is an outlet for the growing number of Fordham University students who seek a career in sports business. Sports has become a multi-billion dollar industry and is one of the fastest-growing and most stable markets in entertainment, yet it remains highly competitive. Building connections is critical, and the BSSF aims to open doors for its members.
