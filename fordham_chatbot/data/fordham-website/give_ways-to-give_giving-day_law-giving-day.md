# Law Giving Day 2025

Fordham Law Giving Day is an annual fundraising marathon that celebrates the power of collective generosity and the extraordinary strength of our alumni, students, faculty, and friends.

Your support fuels experiential learning, student support, academic excellence, and strategic initiatives that prepare Fordham Law students to lead with integrity, advocate for justice, and serve others throughout their careers.
