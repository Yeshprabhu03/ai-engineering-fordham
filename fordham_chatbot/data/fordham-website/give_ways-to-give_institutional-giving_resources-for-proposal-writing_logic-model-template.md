# Resources for Proposal Writing

## Logic Model Template

A logic model is an effective way to think about and summarize your project.

See the example, in table form, below. All project activities should have corresponding budget items.

All activities should be measurable and related to outputs and outcomes.

| Context | Project | IMPACT | |||
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Problem Analysis | Inputs | Activities | Outputs | Outcomes | Contribution to Change |
| What is the issue(s)? What is the resulting challenge? Why is it important? |
Expertise Funding Other Resources needed to make the project a success |
Activities clearly aligned with problem analysis | Outputs - specific and measurable (SMART) | Short term Medium term Long term |
