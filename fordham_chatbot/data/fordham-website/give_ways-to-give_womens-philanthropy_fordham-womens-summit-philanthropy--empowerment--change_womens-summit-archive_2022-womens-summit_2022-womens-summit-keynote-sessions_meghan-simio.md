# Meghan Simio


![Meghan Simio, GABELLI '13](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/daur/Meghan_Simio_400.jpg)

Meg Simio, director of the PVH Foundation at PVH Corp., earned a bachelor’s degree in marketing, with a minor in communications, from Fordham’s Gabelli School of Business. In her current role at PVH, she is responsible for managing the day-to-day operations of the foundation, including global philanthropic programming and strategic communications across PVH and its brands, which include Tommy Hilfiger and Calvin Klein.

Simio previously served as senior director at Changing Our World, a social impact consulting firm, where she worked with clients including Altria, ConocoPhillips, Make Up For Ever, Philips, and TD Ameritrade to develop or enhance their corporate philanthropic programs and foundations to align social impact goals with business goals.

Prior to joining Changing Our World’s marketing department in June 2013, Simio worked at Major League Baseball in the special events department, helping to plan, organize, and promote the 2012 MLB All-Star Fan Fest event in Kansas City, Missouri, as well as in the recruitment department, where she was responsible for staffing for the Australian Baseball League.
