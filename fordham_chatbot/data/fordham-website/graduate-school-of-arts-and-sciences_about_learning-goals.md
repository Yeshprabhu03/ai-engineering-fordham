# GSAS Learning Goals

![IPED Graduate students in class](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Graduate_Students_in_Class_SM.jpg)

Students will be able to:

- Conduct independent research, using tools and methods appropriate to their area of study
- Analyze large amounts of information, formulate supportable conclusions, and communicate their findings in a clear and persuasive manner
- Demonstrate in-depth knowledge of a discipline or area of study

Students graduating with a doctoral degree, will:

- Be equipped to make original contributions to advance knowledge in their fields of study
- Possess advanced skills for professional success in academic or compatible careers
