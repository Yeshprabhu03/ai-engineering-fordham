# M.S. in Biotechnology Enterprise Events and Opportunities

**Discover how Fordham’s M.S. in Biotechnology Enterprise can advance your career**

Choosing the right graduate program is a critical step in your professional journey, and we’re here to help you make an informed decision. Our M.S. in biotechnology enterprise information sessions offer a valuable opportunity to dive into our cutting-edge curriculum, explore the industry connections available to students, and discover the unique advantages of pursuing a biotech degree at Fordham.

During these sessions, you’ll hear directly about what makes Fordham’s program unique. Learn how our interdisciplinary approach, hands-on research opportunities, and prime location in New York City position you for success in the fast-growing biotech industry. Whether you’re just starting to explore graduate programs or are finalizing your application, these sessions will give you the insights you need to take the next step with confidence.
