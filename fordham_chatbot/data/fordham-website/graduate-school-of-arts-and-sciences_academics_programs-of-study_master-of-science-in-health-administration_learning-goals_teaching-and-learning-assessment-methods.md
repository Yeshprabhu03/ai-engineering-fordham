# Teaching and Learning Assessment Methods

The MSHA Program faculty collectively use the following teaching and learning assessment methods which have been added to the MSHA website:

- Cases
- Lectures (which also involve Interactive Case Discussions in class)
- Paper Production & Presentations (both individual and team-based)
- Participative (Team-Based) Collaborative Learning
- Real Projects for organizations
- Participative learning
- Team self-assessments

- Student summaries of what they have learned during the semester
- Role playing
- Interactive learning through discussion with guest lecturers and speakers
- Collaborating with peers
- Simulations
- Media Analysis
