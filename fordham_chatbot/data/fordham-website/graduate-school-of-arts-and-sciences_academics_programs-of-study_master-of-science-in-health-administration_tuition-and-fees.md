# M.S. in Health Administration Tuition and Fees

## 2025-2026 Academic Year

Tuition: **$39,029**

[Suggested Funding Opportunities](#fundingopport)

This [one year program consists of 3 terms](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/academics/programs-of-study/master-of-science-in-health-administration/2023-2024-class-schedule/) and students are charged in three installments:

***** The Enrollment Deposit will cover the General Fee and Technology Fee for the fall semester. ****** Health insurance is mandatory for all full-time students. For students who do not have health insurance coverage, Fordham University offers a Student Health Plan to all eligible students and the annual cost of $4,994.

Our [Office of Financial Services](/student-financial-services/graduate-financial-aid/) provides a variety of options that can make your education more affordable, including [loan programs](/student-financial-services/types-of-financial-aid/graduate-loan-resources/) and [payment plans](/student-financial-services/tuition-and-payments/paying-your-bill/).

Suggested Funding Opportunities

Many of our current students and alumni have received tuition reimbursement from their employer to assist with the cost of a graduate degree. We recommend students explore all options, this includes reviewing HR benefits with a current employer.

Current members of [National Association of Latino Healthcare Executives](https://www.nalhe.org/) (NALHE) and [National Association of Health Services Executives](https://www.nahse.org/) (NAHSE) are considered for additional scholarship opportunities at the point of acceptance.
