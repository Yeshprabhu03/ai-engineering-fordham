# GSAS Director of Graduate Studies Contact List

If you are a prospective student seeking more information about a GSAS program, feel free to contact the Director of Graduate Studies listed for your program of interest below. We also strongly encourage you to complete our [request for information](https://gradadmissions.fordham.edu/register/gsas) form. Submitting the inquiry form will ensure that you receive important information about GSAS, the application process, financing graduate education, and GSAS admissions events.
