# GSAS Graduate Housing

![Arthur II Graduate Housing](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Rose_Hill_Graduate_Housing_Arthur_II.jpg)

[Rose Hill Campus](/about/campuses/rose-hill-campus/)

The Office of Residential Life is pleased to offer graduate housing in University-operated apartments in [Arthur 2 House](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdDSyHBF1_3YhJ9zGkOboZwzUx6rl6gDjRJ2ht98YYYByjHeA/viewform), located at 2484-2488 Arthur Avenue, Bronx, NY. Due to the limited number of graduate housing spaces, only registered full-time students can be considered for housing. A graduate student pursuing a master’s degree may generally live in University Housing for no more than 2 years and doctoral students may live in University Housing for no more than 5 years.

Room Rates: Visit [Housing Rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) and scroll down to “Rose Hill Graduate Student Off-Campus Housing” to see current housing rates for Arthur House II. Room configurations and rates are subject to change. Rates are for the 9 month (or 12 month) academic year and include utilities and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/). These rates do not include the residence hall activity fee. A [Meal plan](/student-life/living-on-campus/meal-plans/) is not required, but they are available to off-campus students. Move-in dates vary; please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.

Applying for Housing at Rose Hill: Complete the online [Graduate Housing Application](https://docs.google.com/a/fordham.edu/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSd8vvvuJBRWs8hjGXo5kmyw23A9DMBbPCKauDNhmaGCdQ_pWw/viewform). A $200 housing deposit is required to reserve a room. You can direct inquiries regarding housing opportunities around Rose Hill to the Office of Residential Life at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#e39186908f8a8586918ba3858c91878b828ecd868796) or by phone at 718-817-3080.

![Wilshire Plaza Lincoln Center Graduate Housing](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Lincoln_Center_Graduate_Housing_Wilshire.jpg)

[Lincoln Center Campus](/about/campuses/lincoln-center-campus/)

Graduate student housing for the Lincoln Center campus in midtown Manhattan is offered at Wilshire Plaza, 134 W 58th St, New York, New York 10019. Visit the [Lincoln Center Graduate Housing website](/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-lincoln-center/graduate-housing/) for details.

Room Rates: Visit [Housing Rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) and scroll down to “Lincoln Center Graduate Off-Campus Housing” to see current housing rates for Wilshire Plaza.

Apply for Housing at Lincoln Center: Complete and submit the [Lincoln Center Housing Application](/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-lincoln-center/graduate-housing/application/). You can direct inquiries regarding housing at Wilshire Plaza to [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#93f4e1f2f7fbfce6e0fafdf4d3f5fce1f7fbf2febdf6f7e6) or by phone at 646-773-1936.
