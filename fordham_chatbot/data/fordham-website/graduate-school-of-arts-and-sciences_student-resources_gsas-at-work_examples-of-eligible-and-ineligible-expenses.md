# GSAS Examples of Eligible and Ineligible Expenses

Note: This is not an exhaustive list of eligible or ineligible expenses covered by the

fund. The University retains the right to determine a “compelling need.”

| Examples of Eligible Expenses | Examples of Ineligible Expenses |
|---|---|
| Unanticipated expenses related to visas | |
| Temporary housing due to displacement (fire, flood, unsafe living conditions, threat of personal violence or other harassment) |
Routine rent and utilities; moving expenses |
| Change of locks or replacement of essential items (e.g. clothing, books, computer) due to theft | Replacement of laptop or other technology due to damage or negligence |
| Travel costs relating to unexpected family crises or death | Travel home for non-emergency situations, conference attendance, or other personal travel |
| Immediate food assistance for sudden food-insecurity | Non-essential purchases or luxury items |
| Assistance with sudden loss of transportation due to auto theft or accident | Routine auto maintenance or repairs unrelated to theft or accident |
| University-related costs (e.g. tuition); parking tickets or other fines; veterinary bills; union dues |
