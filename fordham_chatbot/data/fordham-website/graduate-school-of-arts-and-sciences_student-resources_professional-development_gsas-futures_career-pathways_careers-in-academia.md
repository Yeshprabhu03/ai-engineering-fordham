# GSAS Related Careers in Academia

An academic career requires you to not only distinguish yourself as a researcher in your chosen field of study. Employers at prospective universities are also likely to value your skills and enthusiasm in the area of independent teaching. If you are a Ph.D. student interested in enhancing your teaching skills in the twenty-first century classroom, consider joining the
[GSAS Preparing Future Faculty](/graduate-school-of-arts-and-sciences/student-resources/professional-development/preparing-future-faculty-program/) initiative.
