# Fordham-GSAS Three Minute Thesis Competition Past Winners

![GSAS 3MT 2018 contestants](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/GSAS_3MT_2018_contestants.jpg)

Photo by Lauren Grizzaffi

Winners of the Three Minute Thesis competition showcased a valuable communication skill -- they distilled complex research topics into accessible principles that a varied audience could not only understand, but appreciate. The ability to pair substance with engagement plays a crucial role in a number of settings, from classroom teaching to job interviews.

You are welcome to view the winning presentations from the Fordham graduate students!
