# GSE Academics

Envision your future with the Graduate School of Education, where our master's, doctoral, and advanced certificate programs empower you to lead, innovate, and make a lasting impact in education.

[Teaching](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/)

- Develops skills for becoming reflective educators, with a focus on language, culture, and diversity in urban classrooms.
- Emphasizes collaboration with teachers, administrators, students, and the community.
- Offers paths to initial teacher certification and advanced/specialist certifications.
**Career Paths:**Classroom Teacher, Curriculum Coordinator, Educational Consultant, Instructional Coach, Special Education Teacher, Literacy Specialist, Educational Technology Specialist.

[Educational Leadership](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/)

- Prepares leaders with vision, knowledge of institutional politics/finances, and ethical leadership.
- Combines mentoring with theory and practice in leadership.
**Career Paths:**School Principal, District Administrator, Superintendent, Educational Policy Maker, Higher Education Administrator, Nonprofit Education Director, Educational Consultant.

[Spirituality, Ministry, and Formation](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/)

- Rooted in Catholic and Jesuit tradition, open to all.
- Prepares students for leadership, ministry, and spiritual formation in diverse
**faith-based**contexts. Blends theological study with ethical formation and practical application through online, hybrid, and in-person learning. **Career Paths:**Spiritual Director, Lay Minister, Retreat Leader, Chaplain, Theological Educator, Pastoral Counselor, Religious Community Leader, Ministry Program Director.

[Interdisciplinary Research](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/contemporary-learning-and-interdisciplinary-research-clair/) (CLAIR, Ph.D.)

- Focuses on innovative solutions to global educational challenges.
- Rooted in Jesuit traditions, promoting rigorous inquiry and reflection.
**Career Paths:**Educational Researcher, University Professor, Policy Analyst, Educational Consultant, Program Evaluator, Curriculum Developer, Data Scientist in Education.

[Counseling and Counseling Psychology](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-counselor-/)



![Image of a psychologist with a child](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-education/psychologist.jpg)

- Blends theory and practice using the scientist-practitioner model.
- Strong emphasis on research, intervention, and assessment.
**Career Paths:**School Counselor, Clinical Psychologist, Mental Health Consultant, Rehabilitation Counselor, Marriage and Family Therapist, Substance Abuse Counselor, Career Counselor.

[School Psychology](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-school-psychologist/)

- Trains psychologists to link psychological science with educational practices.
- Focuses on the development of children in diverse urban settings.
**Career Paths:**School Psychologist, Educational Diagnostician, Behavioral Specialist, Child Development Specialist, Learning Disabilities Specialist, Researcher in Educational Psychology, Crisis Intervention Specialist.
