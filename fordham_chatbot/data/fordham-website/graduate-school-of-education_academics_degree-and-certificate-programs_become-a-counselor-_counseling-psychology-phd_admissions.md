# GSE Counseling Psychology (Ph.D.) Admissions

![](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-education/CounselingPsychologyPh.D.Program_Faculty2.jpg)


**Start Semester:** Fall**Campus:** Lincoln Center**Full-time****Application deadline:** December 1

### Application Requirements

- Minimum 3.0 undergraduate GPA and 3.5 graduate GPA
- Completed online application
- Personal statement
- C.V. or resume
- Official transcripts from all schools/universities attended
- Two letters of recommendation
- GRE test scores (optional)

Applicants must possess a baccalaureate degree from an accredited college or university and have academic and professional goals consistent with the objectives and purposes of the program. As equity and inclusion are central to our program, the school, and the university’s mission, we welcome applicants who are committed and will actively contribute to our efforts to celebrate our cultural and intellectual diversity and richness and be steadfast in advancing social justice. We encourage applicants to discuss in their application essays how their previous personal, professional, and academic experiences might contribute to or strengthen the program’s goal of preparing culturally informed researchers and clinicians to work in the broad field of psychology with an increasingly diverse and global clientele.

*Please note*: All interviews will be conducted virtually.
