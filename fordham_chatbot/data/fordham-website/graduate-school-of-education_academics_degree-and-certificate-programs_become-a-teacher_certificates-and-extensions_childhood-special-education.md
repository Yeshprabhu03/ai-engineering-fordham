# GSE Childhood Special Ed Adv Cert

**Degree**: Advanced Certificate**Credits**: 15**Career Path**: Teacher of children with disabilities in childhood**Start Semester**: Summer or Fall **Campus**: Lincoln Center

Full-time/Part-time

### Program Prerequisites

Applicants must have a master's degree and an initial or professional certification in elementary or childhood education, or the equivalent.

We also offer a Childhood Special Education [M.S.T.](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/master-of-science-in-teaching/childhood-education-programs/childhood-special-education/)

Application Deadlines

- Summer: April 15
- Fall: June 1
