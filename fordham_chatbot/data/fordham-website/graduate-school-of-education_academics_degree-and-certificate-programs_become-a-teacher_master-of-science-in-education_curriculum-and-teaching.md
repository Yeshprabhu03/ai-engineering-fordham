# Curriculum and Teaching M.S.E.

![Female faculty member if front of blackboard - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/07_13_1DS_0940.jpg)

**Degree:** Master of Science in Education (M.S.E.)**Credits:** 30**Career Path: **Prepares graduates for positions as educational specialists, curriculum designers, and instructional leaders.**Start Semester:** Fall**Campus:** Lincoln Center**Full-time/Part-time**

The curriculum and teaching program provides a core of knowledge in professional studies and the opportunity to design, with an academic adviser, unique programs of study or specializations related to professional needs and interests. The program leads to a master of science in education degree, but does not lead to initial teacher certification.
