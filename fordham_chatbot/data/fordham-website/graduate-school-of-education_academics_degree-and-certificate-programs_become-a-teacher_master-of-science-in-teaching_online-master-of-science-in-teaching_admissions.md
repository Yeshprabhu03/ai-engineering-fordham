## Admissions Requirements

Fordham University Graduate School of Education (GSE) holds Education@Fordham applicants to the same high standards as our on-campus programs. We look for applications from those who are passionate about education’s role in improving the lives of children from all backgrounds. Applicants should demonstrate their commitment to the field of education and highlight their ability to work in collaborative learning environments.

Applicants Must:

- Hold a bachelor’s degree from a regionally accredited college or university.
- Have a strong academic record, typically with an undergraduate GPA of 3.0 or higher and a graduate GPA of 3.5 or higher, if applicable.
