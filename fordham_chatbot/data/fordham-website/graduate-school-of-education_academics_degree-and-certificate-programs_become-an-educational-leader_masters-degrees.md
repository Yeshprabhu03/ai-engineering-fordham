# GSE Educational Leadership Master's Degrees

These programs prepare students for leadership responsibilities and positions such as assistant principal, department chair, principal, school building leader, supervisor, etc.

[School Building Leader, Accelerated Master's (Accelerated M.S.E.)](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/masters-degrees/educational-leadership-accelerated-masters/)

Graduates are prepared for positions as school building leaders, such as principal, supervisor, department chair, assistant principal, etc.

[Administration and Supervision (M.S.E.)](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/masters-degrees/#d.en.35341) ([in-person](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/masters-degrees/administration-and-supervision/) or [online](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/masters-degrees/administration-and-supervision-online/))

Church and Non-Public Educational Leadership: This program is for students looking to develop their knowledge and skills for a leadership role in education on many levels.
