# Faith-Based Programs

## Rooted in Catholic and Jesuit tradition, open to all

**Spirituality, Ministry, and Formation**

**Advanced Certificate in Jesuit Studies**

Hybrid program focused on Ignatian spirituality, Jesuit history, and communal discernment. Includes a one-week residential experience in New York City.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/jesuit-studies/)

**Advanced Certificate in Christian Spirituality**

18-credit program exploring the Christian spiritual tradition, scripture, and discernment. Suitable for lay ministers, educators, and seekers.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/christian-spirituality-advanced-certificate/)

**Advanced Certificate in Spiritual Direction**

Prepares students to become spiritual directors through coursework, reflection, and supervised practice.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/spiritual-direction/)

**M.A. in Christian Spirituality**

Graduate degree offering general, research, or spiritual direction tracks. Integrates theology, justice, and spiritual practice.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/christian-spirituality/)

**Doctor of Ministry (D.Min.)**

Hybrid doctoral program for experienced religious professionals. Emphasizes social justice, global context, and theological reflection.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/faith-based-programs/doctor-of-ministry-dmin/)

**Faith-Based Educational Leadership**

**Ph.D. in Administration and Supervision (Church and Non-Public School Leadership)**

In-person doctoral program based at Lincoln Center. Combines Catholic social teaching with leadership theory in a cohort model.[Learn more](https://www.fordham.edu/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/doctoral-degrees/administration-and-supervision-phd/)
