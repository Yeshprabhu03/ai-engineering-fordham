# LPP Mission and Goals

## Mission

![students in a Fordham classroom](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-education/Law.jpg)

Established in 1989, Fordham University's Liberty Partnerships Program is dedicated to equipping our students and their families with knowledge and resources to create and achieve their academic, college, career and personal goals.


## Goals

![young students in a classroom with the word](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-education/SummerQuest.jpg)

- To support LPP students in completing middle-high school and prepare them for a successful transition to college and/or the workforce.
- To enhance LPP students’ development of leadership skills and self-discovery through academic support, social-emotional counseling, and cultural enrichment.
- To involve parents in supporting their children’s educational and personal development.
