# Educational Leadership Requirements

## School Building Leader

Degree: M.S.E.

- Minimum 3.0 GPA
- Online Application
- Personal Statement
- C.V. or Resume
- Official transcripts of all undergraduate and graduate coursework
- Two Letters of Recommendation (one must be from your current supervisor)
- $80 Application Fee

Applicants must have a provisional or permanent state certificate in teaching or another appropriate and acceptable educational specialty, and have a minimum of two years paid, full-time teaching/counseling experience

## Educational Leadership, Administration, and Policy (K-12)

Degree: Ed.D.

- Minimum 3.0 undergraduate GPA and 3.5 graduate GPA
- Online Application
- Personal Statement
- C.V. or Resume (one must be from your current supervisor)
- Official transcripts of all undergraduate and graduate coursework
- Two Letters of Recommendation
- $80 Application Fee

Applicants must also:

- Complete three years of appropriate leadership experience in a PreK-12 education setting (e.g., assistant principal, school administrator, district leader, superintendent, teacher leader, student support director, or department chair)
- possess appropriate professional administrative certification;
- possess a master's degree, including fundamental and intermediate courses equivalent to those required by the division; and
- show evidence of academic ability, effective communication skills, leadership potential, and seriousness of purpose.
