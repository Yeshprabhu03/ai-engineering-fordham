# GSE Billing and Payment

## Billing

You will receive a bill in the mail and payment is expected by the due date. If your payment is not received by this date you will incur a $15 or 1.5% late fee (whichever is the greater amount).

We encourage you to check your tuition balance regularly (as well as other pertinent student information) via your student page in the [portal](https://my.fordham.edu/mylogin).

## Payment

Acceptable forms of payment include cash, check, money order, automated clearing house (ACH) transactions, and credit card (with restrictions).

Tuition fees must be paid through PHONECHARGE 877-903-9328 or online at [fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/). Payment may be submitted in person, or via mail, the web, telephone, or wire transfer.

There are a number of financing alternatives available to you. The [Fordham Monthly Payment Plan](/student-financial-services/tuition-and-payments/paying-your-bill/#d.en.186486), Alternative Educational Loans, and the deferment payment plan.

Please visit the [Graduate Financial Aid website](/student-financial-services/graduate-financial-aid/) or contact the Enrollment Group at 718-817-3800 for more information on financing options.

## Applying for Student Loans

Students must be matriculated and enrolled at half-time status (6 credits) to be eligible for [Federal Stafford Loans](http://www.staffordloan.com/).

New Students must complete all of the following steps:

[File a FAFSA](https://fafsa.ed.gov/)(Federal School Code #002722)- Complete a Loan Request Form
- Complete an Entrance Interview
- Complete and sign your e-MPN with your guarantor (Master Promissory Note) (For NY, go to www.hesc.com)

Continuing Students must complete ALL of the following steps:

[File a FAFSA](https://fafsa.ed.gov/)(Federal School Code #002722)
