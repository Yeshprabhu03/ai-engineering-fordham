# Campuses

The Graduate School of Education offers **programs at three different locations.** Click each tab below for more information about opportunities available at our Lincoln Center, Westchester, and online campuses.

-
## Lincoln Center

![Lincoln Center Entrance with Taxis](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Lincoln_Center_Entrance_taxis_1.jpg)

With Broadway a block to the east and Central Park not much farther, nearly every great thing you can imagine about living in New York is right at your doorstep: museums, restaurants, music, all of it. The 8-acre campus spans two city blocks, with a landscaped plaza where you can find a respite from the City without actually leaving it.

At Lincoln Center, we offer programs in

[Teaching,](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/)[Educational Leadership](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/),[Counseling](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-counselor-/)and[School Psychology](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-school-psychologist/), and- and
[Interdisciplinary Research](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/contemporary-learning-and-interdisciplinary-research-clair/).


The Fordham University Lincoln Center campus is located at 113 W. 60th Street (at Columbus Avenue).

-
## Westchester

![Westchester Campus Plaza](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Westchester_63.jpg)

Located conveniently off I-287, I-684, and the Hutchinson River Parkway, our three-story, state-of-the-art building sits on 32 landscaped acres in West Harrison, NY.

Fordham Westchester offers class schedules designed for people with busy lives. Our programs help you earn the credentials to start a new career or strengthen an existing one.

[Read more about Fordham Westchester](/about/campuses/westchester-campus/).We offer select programs in

[Teaching](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/)and[Educational Leadership](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/)at the Westchester campus. Browse all programs.[Administration and Supervision](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/doctoral-degrees/educational-leadership-administration-and-policy-edd/), Ed.D.[Educational Leadership](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-an-educational-leader/masters-degrees/educational-leadership-accelerated-masters/), one-year accelerated M.S.E.[Early Childhood Education](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/master-of-science-in-teaching/early-childhood-education-programs/), M.S.T.[Early Childhood Special Education](/graduate-school-of-education/academics/degree-and-certificate-programs/become-a-teacher/master-of-science-in-teaching/early-childhood-education-programs/early-childhood-special-education/), M.S.T.

The Fordham Westchester campus is located at 400 Westchester Avenue, West Harrison, NY 10604.
