# Psychological Assessment Services

### Procedures

- Contact the center for information and an intake coordinator will assist you.
- If services can be rendered at the center, you will be contacted to schedule an appointment. A confirmation letter will be sent at this time.
- First session includes the initial consultation and intake interview.
- Typically there are two or three assessment sessions.
- Conference includes a discussion of results and recommendations.
- A comprehensive psycho-educational report is provided within two weeks after the conference.

### Psychological and Testing Services

Comprehensive psycho-educational evaluations, including:

- Assessment of cognitive, achievement, behavioral, personality, and neuropsychological (e.g., attention, executive functions) functioning
- Consultation with teachers, tutors, and other professionals
- Written report
- Psycho-educational consultation

Individual counseling services for children, adolescents, and adults, including:

- Intake process with feedback
- Ongoing individual counseling sessions using evidence-based practices
