# Contact RBERN

## Bilingual Team Members

The NYC RBERN delivers wide-ranging support to schools, districts and families across NYC. Our team has extensive experience in addressing the academic, linguistic, affective and cultural needs of English learners and their families. We are committed to providing comprehensive and quality services to NYC educators, parents and support staff.

Fordham University's Graduate School of Education and its Center for Educational Partnerships, under the leadership of Associate Dean Anita Vazquez Batisti, Ph.D., was awarded the contract for the NYC Regional Bilingual Education Resource Network by the New York State Education Department Office of Bilingual Education and World Language Studies.
