# Tom Beaudoin

![TB from GRE](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Beaudoin_DMP_5486_240x240_3.jpg)

**Professor Beaudoin studies how environments and practices considered religious/spiritual/sacred work in a world of diverse peoples, values, and ways of life. His research areas include theologies, philosophies, and religious studies of practice; religious affiliation/disaffiliation; spiritual exercises; popular music; sacred architecture in secular/postsecular contexts; Pantheon (Rome) studies; practical theology. **
