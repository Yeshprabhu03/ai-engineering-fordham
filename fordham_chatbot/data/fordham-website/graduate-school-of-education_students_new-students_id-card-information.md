# GSE ID Card Information

As a student of Fordham University, you are required to obtain a university photo identification card. This magnetically encoded photo ID card will be your official University ID for the length of your time at Fordham.

Students must have already registered for their courses in order to receive their ID cards.

To submit your photo for your ID card, log into the [fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/).
