# GSS Academics

![a student in a lincoln center campus classroom listening to a professor's lecture](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-social-service/Student-listening-in-classroom.png)


Fordham University’s **nationally recognized graduate programs** in social work take a bold, integrated approach to the future of the social work profession. Designed with your needs in mind, our graduate programs offer **flexible scheduling as well as on-campus and online options**. You’ll develop both professionally and personally from quality academics driven by a forward-thinking, skills-based curriculum.

By combining your rigorous courseload with the hands-on training of field education, Fordham M.S.W. students graduate prepared to meet the growing demand for social workers across the country.


## GSS Academic Programs

Fordham's social work programs are designed to combine the profession's historical values with cutting-edge theory and practice to produce graduates who advocate for others and meet society's needs. Our forward-thinking curriculum and experiential skills training will leave you a confident social worker throughout your career.
