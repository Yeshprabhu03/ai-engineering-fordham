# GSS Admissions

![a student sits in a classroom speaking to a professor.](/media/home/schools/graduate-school-of-social-service/student-in-classroom-explaining.png)


If you’re ready to transform your ideals into a meaningful career, we’re here to help. With a degree from one of our nationally ranked programs, you’ll graduate with the education and experience you need to foster positive change in yourself and others.

At Fordham University’s Graduate School of Social Service (GSS), our admissions counselors are here to help you through every step of the application process. Our team also can arrange for you to speak to a current graduate student about the Fordham GSS experience.

## Explore Our Programs

By choosing social work, you are earning a degree that matters. Whether you're just beginning your social work journey or looking to enhance your career, our programs will equip you with the skills you need to excel in the profession and turn your passion into action.
