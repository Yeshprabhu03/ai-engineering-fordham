# GSS M.S.W. Class Schedules

Next semester's MSW class schedules are posted prior to registration. (Check the [GSS Academic Calendar](https://go.activecalendar.com/FordhamUniversity/site/academic/?&ql=y&search=y) for the dates of registration.) The schedules are subject to change and some class sections may not be available at registration.

## Room Assignments

Room numbers are generally available a few days prior to the beginning of each semester. Room assignments are also posted at each campus during the first week of classes. (Check the [GSS Academic Calendar](https://go.activecalendar.com/FordhamUniversity/site/academic/?&ql=y&search=y) to learn when the semester begins.)

## Courses

Learn more about each class on our [course bulletin](https://bulletin.fordham.edu/gss/courses/).

## Instructors

Learn about the course instructors on our [Faculty](/graduate-school-of-social-service/faculty/) page.
