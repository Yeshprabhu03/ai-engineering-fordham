# Friday, October 14

Inauguration Day

*A series of events celebrating Fordham’s storied past, our new president, and our enduring influence as the Jesuit University of New York*

**10 a.m. Missioning Mass* | University Church***A Mass to confirm President Tetlow’s mission as director of the apostolic work of Fordham University*

**11:30 a.m. Lunch | Martyrs’ Court Lawn **

**2 p.m. Inauguration Ceremony* | Edwards Parade ***The official installation of Tania Tetlow as the 33rd president of Fordham University*

**4 p.m. PrezFest | Edwards Parade***Featuring FUV, fun, and NYC’s favorite food!***6 p.m. | Walsh Family Library Lawn ***Featuring the Preservation Hall Jazz Band and celebrating the 75th anniversary of Fordham’s public media station*
