Professor Holler joined the Fordham faculty in 2011. Prior to this he was as Senior Manager of Photonics R&D at Thermo Fisher Scientific, a position he obtained due to the acquisition of NovaWave Technologies, a small business focused on developing laser-based for environmental monitoring where he was Partner and Director of R&D. In addition, Professor Holler was a Senior Member of the Technical Staff at Sandia National Laboratories in Albuquerque, NM and a Senior Physicist, of Los Gatos Research in Mountain View, CA.
