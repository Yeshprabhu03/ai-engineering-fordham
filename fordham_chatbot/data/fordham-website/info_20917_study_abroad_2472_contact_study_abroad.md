# Contact Study Abroad

![Students stand in front of Granada alleyway](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/study-abroad/Hayley-Granada.png)

If you are interested in learning more about studying abroad and have questions about the process, please get in touch with us. You can schedule an appointment online, send us an email at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#0e7d7a7b6a776f6c7c616f6a4e68617c6a666f63206b6a7b) or call us. The [staff in the study abroad office](/study-abroad/staff/) look forward to hearing from you soon!

You can also contact a former or current study abroad student in our [Global Ambassador Program](https://www.fordham.edu/study-abroad/accepted-students/global-ambassador-program/) with questions about life abroad.

**The International & Study Abroad Programs Offices at RH and LC are open for in-person meetings by appointment only. Please book your appointment below. If you don't need an in-person appointment, you can book a zoom appointment online. You can also email or give us a call during the business hours listed below.*
