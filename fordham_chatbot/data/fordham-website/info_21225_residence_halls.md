# Rose Hill Campus Residence Halls

![Circle of students sitting on lawn - LG](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/DSC_2209.jpg)

Your college experience doesn't have to end after class each day. There are 14 residential halls and campus housing locations at Rose Hill. Many students choose to live in [Integrated Learning Communities](/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-rose-hill/living-and-learning-integrated-learning-communities/) - residence hall designed to help develop conscience, character, and intellect.

Beyond our commitment to high standards in academics and personal growth, our students also enjoy:

- Expansive green spaces
[Fitness Centers](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/)[On campus dining](http://fordham.campusdish.com/)- An array of
[clubs and student organizations](/student-life/student-organizations/) - Exposure to others who share your excitement of learning and who are seeking lifelong friendships.

We invite you to come [visit us](/student-life/living-on-campus/living-on-campus-at-rose-hill/about/contact-us/) at Rose Hill in the vibrant Bronx neighborhood, where we can answer all your questions in person.
