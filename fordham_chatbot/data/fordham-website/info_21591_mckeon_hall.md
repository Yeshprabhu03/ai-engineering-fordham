# McKeon Hall at Lincoln Center

**Home to First-Year Students**

![McKeon Hall](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/CShinn_100714_1165__1_.jpg)

The most recent addition to the Lincoln Center community houses approximately 415 first-year students in double and triple bedrooms on 12 floors.

**McKeon Features:**

- Free laundry
- 2 study lounges
- Movie lounge
- Game lounge
- Dance lounge
- 6 “living room” lounges
- 1 large kitchen
- 1 multi-purpose seminar room on the top floor with beautiful views of Manhattan

**Internet and Cable**

Hard-wired Internet access and cable television are available for each student in their bedroom, in addition to wireless connectivity throughout the hall.

**Personal Safety**

McKeon Hall offers 24-hour security for your personal safety. The building also has a comprehensive fire-safety system, including sprinklers and control panel with a public address system.

**Comfortably Furnished Spaces**

![Female student in Lincoln Center Dorm Room](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/092214_Lincoln_Campus_15_2.jpg)

We take pride in our ability to offer a variety of university housing options. All rooms are furnished with a bed, desk, dresser, chair, and wardrobe or closet.

**McKeon Hall First-Year Housing Rates for 2025-2026**

Rates include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and use of the fitness center located on the second floor of McMahon Hall.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for McKeon Hall. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

The required first-year dining plan for McKeon students is additional as indicated. (Note: All rates are subject to change)

Please visit [Fordham Dining](https://fordham.campusdish.com/) for more information.

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.
