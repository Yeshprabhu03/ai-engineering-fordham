# Joyce Studies Annual Journal

![James Joyce](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/James_Joyce.jpg)

Joyce Studies Annual gathers essays by recognized scholars and emerging voices in the field. Formerly published by the University of Texas, the journal was led for nearly two decades by Philip Sicker and Moshe Gold at Fordham University. **It is currently edited by Christopher GoGwilt (managing editor) and Keri Walsh (advisory editor).**
