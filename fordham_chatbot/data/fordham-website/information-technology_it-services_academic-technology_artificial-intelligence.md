## Read our AI Ready blog and Flipboard magazine

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/fordham-it/AI-stamp-blog-logo-copy-70x70.png)

Stay ahead in the evolving AI landscape with [ AI Ready](https://itnews.blog.fordham.edu/category/academic-technology/ai/), a weekly Fordham blog offering practical insights, tools, and strategies to effectively integrate artificial intelligence into teaching, learning, and research.

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/fordham-it/flipboard_logo_icon_170143-(1)-64x64.png)

[Faculty AI Interest Group Magazine on Flipboard](https://flipboard.com/@treglia/faculty-ai-interest-group-ge365ov9y) features a curated selection of articles from what faculty and the EdTech team are reading, showcasing the latest in AI, educational technology, and innovative teaching practices.
