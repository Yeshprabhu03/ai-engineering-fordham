# Rose Hill Computer Teaching Labs

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

Many Rose Hill computer labs are available by reservation for teaching applications and courses that require a computer. When classes are not in session, most labs permit members of the University community with a valid ID to drop in and use a computer.

## Reservations, locations, and hours

To schedule a lab for a semester-long course, contact your department administrator or the Registrar's Office. For a one-time reservation, schedule via 25Live, the University's room reservation system (login to [fordham.edu](/my-pages/) > My Apps > Common Tools > 25Live). Some labs may not available for reservation.
