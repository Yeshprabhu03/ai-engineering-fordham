# Employee Equipment Loaner Program

**For Faculty, Staff**

## Overview

The Equipment Loan Program offers faculty and staff short-term computer and peripheral loans for temporary needs, such as forgotten devices or repairs.

The Equipment Loan Program is intended for faculty and staff only. Students should use the [self-checkout student Tech Kiosks](/information-technology/it-services/tech-kiosks/) for laptop borrowing needs. If a student's device is being repaired however, students can inquire about a Chromebook loan which may be provided depending on availability.

## Getting Started

To make an equipment loan request, contact the IT Service Desk at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#337b565f437a6773755c41575b525e1d565746).

## Equipment Available

*Quantities of all equipment are limited. Availability is on a first-come, first-served basis. *

For faculty and staff:

- Windows and Mac laptops, Chromebooks, Microsoft Surface devices (3 weeks maximum)
- Document camera
- Lavalier wireless microphone
- Wireless presentation mouse

For students:

- Chromebooks may be available for loan on a limited basis (2 weeks maximum - please note that this time limit is separate from the self-checkout student tech kiosk)
