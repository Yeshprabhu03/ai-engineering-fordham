# Web Printing

### Overview

You can print on the go by uploading documents to Web Print.

Documents stay in your print queue for 10 hours.

The instructions below will guide you through the process.

### Install the PaperCut Print Deploy Client

- Go to
[print.fordham.edu](http://print.fordham.edu/) - Log in with your Fordham username and password
- Click on
**Web Print** - To upload your document to print, click on
**Submit a Job** - Select the printer
**PC-PRT06.DS.FORDHAM.EDU\FindME (virtual)** **Click 2. Print Options and Account Selection**- Enter the number of copies
- If prompted, select which account to charge
- Click
**3. Upload Documents** - Click on
**Upload from Computer**to upload your document(s) OR Drag and drop your files to upload - Click on
**Upload & Complete**- Your document(s) are now in the queue. Next ->
[Release your print job at a Canon print device](/information-technology/it-services/ram-print/release-print/)
