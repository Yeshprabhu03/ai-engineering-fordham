# RamCentral360

**For Advisors, Faculty, Staff**

## If you are a student...

The Fordham Hub for students, as of Fall 2024, has been rebranded as ** RamCentral,** complete with a fresh new logo and an enhanced focus on connecting you with everything our campus has to offer.

## Overview

As university staff, [ RamCentral360](https://fordham.my.salesforce.com/) empowers you to connect with students through

**the digital platform RamCentral, where undergraduate students—and soon graduate students—engage with academic advising, support requests, and other campus services.**

Whether you provide academic advising, manage help requests as an Enrollment or Career Center specialist, engage in community outreach, or offer spiritual guidance, the Fordham Hub is your tool to facilitate these important interactions.

[Click or tap here to log in to RamCentral360](https://fordham.my.salesforce.com/)**.** Alternatively, you can access it by logging into **Fordham.edu → My Apps → Common Tools → RamCentral360**
