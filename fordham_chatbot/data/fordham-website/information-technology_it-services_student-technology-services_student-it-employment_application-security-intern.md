# App Security Documentation Intern | RH

**DIVISION**: Fordham Office of Information Technology

**POSITION**: DevOps Application and System Security Intern

**SUPERVISOR**: Director, Application and System Security

**SUMMARY**: The DevOps division of Information Technology seeks a dynamic self-starter to assist in building training videos of security products, documentation, and data analytics.

**QUALIFICATIONS**:

- Must be a full-time Fordham student in good academic standing
- Computer Science or Information Systems majors are preferred, but not required
- Reliable quick learner who can work with minimal supervision
- Must uphold, enforce, and abide by all university policies
- Ability to work in collaboration with diverse teams
- Knowledge of Microsoft Office, video editing tools, and scripting languages

**RESPONSIBILITIES**:

- Work with internal teams to obtain understanding of software products and documentation requirements
- Assist with security applications/tools including but not limited to web application scanning, web application firewall, MFA, privilege access management etc.
- Help developers reproduce and debug issues from a student experience standpoint
- Produce documentation, user interface text, online help, and tutorials pertaining to security-related products
- Analyze existing and potential content for rewrite if necessary
- Other duties as assigned

**ESSENTIAL SKILLS:**

- An effective communicator with strong writing skills
- Self-Motivated, quick learner, good organizational skills, detail-oriented, ability to prioritize, multitask, and meet deadlines

**BENEFITS:**

- Flexible schedule
- Enhancement of your work portfolio
- Experience in working for an IT organization
- Employment references from an IT organization

**COMPENSATION**: $18.00 per hour

**APPLY**[Apply to this job](http://fordham.edu/itjobsapp). You will be asked to attach your resume to the job application.
