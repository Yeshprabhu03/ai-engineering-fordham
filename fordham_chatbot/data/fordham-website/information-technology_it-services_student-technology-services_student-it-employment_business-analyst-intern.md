# DevOps Business Analyst Intern

**DIVISION: **Fordham Office of Information Technology** **

**POSITION: **DevOps Business Analyst Intern

**SUPERVISOR: **Area Manager

**SUMMARY: **The DevOps division of Information Technology seeks a dynamic self-starter to assist in functional testing and documentation related to student experience applications.

**QUALIFICATIONS:**

- Must be a full-time Fordham student in good academic standing
- Computer Science or Information Systems majors preferred, but not required
- Reliable quick learner who can work with minimal supervision
- Must uphold, enforce and abide by all university policies
- Ability to work in collaboration with diverse teams
- Knowledge of Microsoft office and Google suite of tools

**RESPONSIBILITIES:**

- Work with internal teams to obtain understanding of software products and documentation requirements
- Perform functional testing of student experience features of software upgrades, including creating and executing student centric test cases, raising defects and recording results
- Help developers reproduce and debug issues from student experience standpoint
- Produce documentation, user interface text, online help, and tutorials pertaining to student experience applications
- Analyze existing and potential content for rewrite is necessary
- Other duties as assigned

**ESSENTIAL SKILLS:**

- An effective communicator with strong writing skills
- Self-Motivated, quick learner, good organizational skills, detail oriented, ability to prioritize, multi task and meet deadlines

**BENEFITS:**

- Flexible schedule
- Enhancement of your work portfolio
- Experience in working for an IT organization
- Employment references from an IT organization

**COMPENSATION:** $18.00 per hour

**APPLY**[Apply to this job](http://fordham.edu/itjobsapp). You will be asked to attach your resume to the job application.
