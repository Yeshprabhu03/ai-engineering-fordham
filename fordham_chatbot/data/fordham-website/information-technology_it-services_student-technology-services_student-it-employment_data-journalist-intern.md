# Data Journalist Intern

**DIVISION:** Fordham Analytics

**POSITION:** Fordham Analytics Intern

**SUPERVISOR:** Director of Fordham Analytics


**JOB SUMMARY: **Are you...

Passionate: About **writing **and **communication**? Do you want to help convert mountains of data into meaningful stories? Do you have a passion for data and information design?

A Team Player: Would you like to be part of a team with diverse industry experience and 20+ combined years of experience in the most strategic technology on CIO radars?


**QUALIFICATIONS:**

- Have 15-19 hours a week available
- Be a full-time Fordham student in good academic standing
- Reliable, quick learner, who can work with minimal supervision
- Uphold, enforce and abide by all university policies


**RESPONSIBILITIES:**

The Fordham Analytics Team within Info Tech seeks a student with a passion for Journalism or Communications (or similar) to help us **tell stories with data**.

You'll work alongside Data Engineers and help derive value from data by crafting meaningful stories.
