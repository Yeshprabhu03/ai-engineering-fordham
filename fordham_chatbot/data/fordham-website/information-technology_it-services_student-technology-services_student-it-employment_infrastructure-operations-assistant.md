**POSITION**: Infrastructure Operations Assistant

**SUPERVISOR**: Assistant Director, Infrastructure Operations

**SUMMARY**

The Infrastructure Operations group is dedicated to the maintenance, design, and research of the network at Fordham University. It is a highly paced and exciting environment that offers many opportunities for learning and growth. Must be registered Fordham student in good academic standing.

**QUALIFICATIONS**

Our group is looking for a motivated, eager-to-learn, and hands-on student with:

- Positive attitude
- Ability to work with people
- Good analytical and troubleshooting skills
- Good communication skills
- Respectful and courteous
- Ready to learn and work independently

**RESPONSIBILITIES**

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

- Assist customers at Fordham with network and unified communications issues that arise
- Monitor, review, update, escalate, and close all network-related incidents via our ServiceNow ticketing system on a daily basis
- Provide wireless coverage assessments
- Assist the Network Operations Technician with day-to-day issues
- Help maintain hardware, software, and IP inventory
- Deliver network equipment to and from our data closets

**COMPENSATION**: $17.00 per hour
