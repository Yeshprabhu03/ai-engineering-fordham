# Microsoft Product Support

**For Students, Faculty, Staff**

**Microsoft Office**

All current full-time faculty, staff, and students are eligible to download up to 5 copies of MS Word, Excel, PowerPoint, OneNote and Access to their personal Mac or PC. Copies may also be downloaded to mobile devices. The online version is available to all, including part-time and adjunct faculty. [Learn more](/information-technology/standard-software/microsoft-office/).

Faculty and staff who would like Microsoft Office 365 installed on a University-owned computer, call the [IT Service Desk](/information-technology/it-services/technology-support/).
