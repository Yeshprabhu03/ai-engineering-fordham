# SPSS for Home Use

**For Students, Faculty**

## Overview

IBM SPSS builds predictive models and conducts analytic tasks. Use SPSS to understand data, analyze trends, forecast and plan, validate assumptions, and drive accurate conclusions. SAS (Statistical Analysis System) is a programming language that comes with SPSS.

## Getting started

Licenses for home use of SPSS 29 are available to Fordham faculty and students. The Fordham community may also access this application on one of Fordham's campus computers.

**How to access 'SPSS for Home Use'**

- Log in to
[fordham.edu](http://my.fordham.edu/)portal > My Apps > Course Tools > SPSS Software. You will get a product key that will be required to activate the software.

This option is available to faculty and students. If you are a faculty member who would like a license for your Fordham-issued laptop or desktop, please use the same instructions or contact the IT Service Desk to request an installation. Licenses no longer limit one per user.
