# Mentoring in Higher Education Roundtable

## June 23 – 25, 2026 | New York, NY

Fordham University is proud to host the 2026 Mentoring in Higher Education Roundtable, a gathering for the top established mentorship programs from universities nationwide to share ideas, learnings, and best practices. This year, the Roundtable will focus on:

- Current Issues and Challenges Facing Mentoring in Higher Education
- The Future of Mentoring in an AI-Driven World
- How to Advocate for Mentoring in Uncertain Budget Times
- Intergenerational Mentoring
- and more!
