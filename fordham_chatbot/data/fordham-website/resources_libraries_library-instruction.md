# Library Instruction

[Library Instruction Request Form](https://fordham.libwizard.com/f/Library_Instruction_Request)

Use this form to request a synchronous group instruction session in person or online, or course specific asynchronous library instructional materials.

[Library Tutorial](https://fordham.libguides.com/librarytutorial)

This tutorial was created to give you a basic overview of how to conduct academic research and how to use the Fordham libraries.

[One-on-One Consultations](https://fordham.libguides.com/LiaisonContactInformation)

Fordham students, faculty, and staff may schedule a one-on-one consultation with a librarian. Select from the Liaison list to email the library subject specialist directly. Not sure who to email? Use our
[Ask-A-Librarian](/resources/libraries/ask-a-librarian/) service.

[Library Instructional Videos](https://www.library.fordham.edu/digital/browse/instruct)

Fordham University Libraries instructional videos that highlight library resources and show you how to use library resources to get your work done.

[Personal Librarian Project](https://fordham.libguides.com/librarytutorial/PersonalLibrarianRH)

When you sign up for a Personal Librarian, you will be matched with a librarian who will make it a priority to get to know you and your individual research needs. That librarian can serve as a friendly face for you when you need help in the library.
