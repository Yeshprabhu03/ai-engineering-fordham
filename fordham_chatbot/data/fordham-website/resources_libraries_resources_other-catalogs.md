# Other Catalogs

[Fordham University School of Law Library Catalog](https://library.law.fordham.edu/?search_focus=true)

Visit the School of Law library catalog for access to books, journals, articles, and more.

Includes over 46 million records from libraries in over 65 countries.

The New York Public Library consists of four research libraries and over 80 branches in the city boroughs of the Bronx, Manhattan, and Staten Island.

[The Brooklyn Public Library](http://www.brooklynpubliclibrary.org/) and [The Queens Borough Public Library](http://www.queenslibrary.org/)

The Brooklyn Public Library and the Queens Borough Public Library are separate systems. Those attending Fordham have access to and may apply for free borrowing privileges at any branch.
