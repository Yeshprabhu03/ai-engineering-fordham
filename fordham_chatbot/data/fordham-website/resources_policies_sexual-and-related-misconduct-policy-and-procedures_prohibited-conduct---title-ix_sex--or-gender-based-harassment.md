# Sex- or Gender-Based Harassment

**Sex- or Gender-Based Harassment:** Unwelcome conduct that a reasonable person would consider so severe, pervasive, and objectively offensive that it effectively denies a person equal access to a University education program or activity.

- Employees: Please refer to the
*Fordham Policy and Procedures Against Discrimination, Harassment and Retaliation in Employment*regarding rights and options granted under Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964 and/or other employment laws or policies addressing sexual and related misconduct.
