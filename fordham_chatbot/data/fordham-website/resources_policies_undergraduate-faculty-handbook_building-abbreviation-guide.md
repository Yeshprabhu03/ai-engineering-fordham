# Building Abbreviation Guide

## Key to Building Abbreviations

### Rose Hill

Telephone prefixes begin with 718-817-XXXX. When dialing from a campus phone, add a 1 before the four-digit extension.

- ACN – Alumni Court North
- ADN – Administration Building North
- CA – Canisius Hall
- CH – Cunniffe House
- CO – Collins Hall
- DE – Dealy Hall
- DU – Duane Library
- FA – Faber Hall
- FMH – Faculty Memorial Hall
- FR – Freeman Hall
- GYM – Rose Hill Gymnasium
- HU – Hughes Hall
- JMCC - Joseph M. McShane, S.J. Campus Center
- JMH – John Mulcahy Hall
- KE – Keating Hall
- LA – Larkin Hall
- LO – Loyola Hall
- MCG – McGinley Center
- OH – O’Hare Hall
- SA – Salice Hall
- TH – Thebaud Hall
- WL – Walsh Library

### Lincoln Center

Telephone prefixes begin with 212-636-XXXX. When dialing from a campus phone, add a 2 before the four-digit extension.

- LL – Leon Lowenstein Center
- McK – McKeon Hall
- MAR – Martino Hall
- MRH – McMahon Residence Hall
- 140W – 140 W. 62nd St.

### Westchester

Telephone prefixes begin with 914-367-XXXX. When dialing from a campus phone, add a 3 before the four-digit extension.

- WC – Westchester Campus
