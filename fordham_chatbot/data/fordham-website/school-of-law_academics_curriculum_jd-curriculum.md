# J.D. Curriculum

Our JD curriculum combines substantive coursework in traditional and cutting-edge fields of law with practical, skills-based experiential learning. For an overview of our curriculum and advice on how to navigate our upper-level electives, please see our
[Curriculum Guide](/school-of-law/academics/curriculum/jd-curriculum/jd-curriculum-guide/).

Students interested in tools to build depth into their course of study can opt for one or more
[Concentrations](/school-of-law/academics/curriculum/jd-curriculum/jd-concentrations/) in five different fields of law.
