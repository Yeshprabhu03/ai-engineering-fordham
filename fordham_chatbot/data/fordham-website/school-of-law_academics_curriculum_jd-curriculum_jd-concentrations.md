# J.D. Concentrations

Are you interested in focusing on a particular field of law? Do you want to signal to employers that you are prepared for serious legal work when you graduate?

With a Concentration, you hone your knowledge in a group of classes associated with a specialized area of legal practice.

Select one or more from among five concentrations.
