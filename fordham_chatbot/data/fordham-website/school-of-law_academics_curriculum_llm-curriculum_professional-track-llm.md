# Professional Track LL.M.

As an ambitious global legal professional, you have the drive to earn your master’s, but you don’t have the time.

We’ve made it possible.

## Introducing Fordham Law’s Professional Track LLM


A flexible executive master’s program for busy, foreign-trained lawyers/law graduates.

.

The Professional Track LLM program is perfect for working global practitioners whose professional demands prevent them from completing a traditional nine-month LLM residency.

Students in the program can become eligible to sit for the New York State bar examination by completing the degree in two years.

-
Begin your studies in August or January.

- Complete your first 4.5-month semester.
- Return home for up to 15 months.
- Come back to Fordham for your second 4.5-month semester.
- Receive your degree!

Sample Schedule #1

- Begin in August.
- Study through December.
- Return the following August.
- Complete the program in December.

Sample Schedule #2

- Begin in August.
- Study through December.
- Return one year later in January.
- Complete the program in June.

Sample Schedule #3

- Begin in January.
- Study through May.
- Return the following January.
- Complete the program in June.

Sample Schedule #4

- Begin in January.
- Study through May.
- Return in August of the following year.
- Complete the program in December.

Take advantage of the following special programs, events, and services:

- Exclusive networking opportunities with your Professional Track peers and members of the global Fordham Law alumni network.

Are you professionally committed to your employer and your country of residence?

**If you answered yes, then the Professional Track LLM program is for you.** -
• Complete at least 24 credits.

• Complete the degree requirements within two years (in order to become eligible to sit for the New York bar examination).

*Students in the Fordham Law Professional Track LL.M. program will not be eligible for Optional Practical Training when they graduate.*
