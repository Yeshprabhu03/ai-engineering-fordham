### Eligibility

- All Fordham University students and alumni who attended and completed
**all**of their coursework after 1990 are eligible to request an eTranscript. - Students who completed
**any**of their coursework prior to 1990 must use this[Transcript Request Form](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/pdfs/transcript-request-form-accessible.pdf)and submit it to the Office of the Registrar.

### Processing Time, Holds and Delays

- Typically, your eTranscript should be sent to your recipients
**within 30-90 minutes**. - If you have holds on your record that prevent transcript release, you will not be permitted to submit a request. Follow the instructions that accompany the notification of the hold to resolve the issue.
- If your request cannot be matched to your student record at the time of your order, further research will be needed by the Office of the Registrar to positively identify your student record. Such research will
**add approximately 2-3 business days**to your request, and is only conducted during normal business hours when the University is open. If such research determines that your student record has a hold, you will receive an email notification informing you of the needed action. - All eTranscript requests submitted with attachments must be reviewed by the Office of the Registrar staff, which will
**add approximately 2-3 business days**to the processing time of your request. Review of attachment documents is only conducted during normal business hours when the University is in session.

## Diploma Replacement

Diploma replacement policy for Fordham Law School’s Juris Doctor and Master of Laws alumni/ae, if you are a graduate of one of the other schools at Fordham University, you must contact that school directly.

In the event that your Fordham Law School diploma is permanently lost or destroyed, the Law School requires that you send us a notarized letter explaining the cause of its loss; we cannot order your replacement diploma without this letter. There is a charge of $75.00 per replacement diploma: please include a check or money order, made out to “Fordham University”, in this amount. Finally, graduates must complete and sign the "Replacement Diploma Order Form".

Please send your notarized letter, along with your $75 check or money order and signed "Replacement Diploma Order Form."
