# Application Status Checker

Thank you for applying to Fordham Law School. Once you've submitted your application, you can verify your application status via the [JD LSAC status checker](https://aso.lsac-unite.org/?guid=gsWj5A1mZaU%3D). You will be prompted to login using your LSAC credentials.

Should you have any difficulty logging in the status checker, please reach out directly the [LSAC technical support team](https://www.lsac.org/contact/technical-support).
