## Self-guided tours

You can now take a self-guided tour of the Law School building from Monday - Friday between the hours of 10am - 6pm. In order to do a self-guided tour of the Law School, you must register to gain access to the building. Your confirmation email will include a link to download the Self-Guided Tour Brochure. On arrival to campus, you must show the following documents to the security guard:

- Confirmation email
- Photo ID
