# Admitted LL.M. Students

## Congratulations!

We very much hope that you will decide to join our community. Each year we welcome about 150 LLM students from more than 40 countries around the world. Our alumni number in the thousands and live and work in some 90 countries.

### What can you do in the admitted student portal?

- Pay seat deposit
- Check student Visa information
- Review important dates
- Learn about orientation and registration
- Review course schedules
