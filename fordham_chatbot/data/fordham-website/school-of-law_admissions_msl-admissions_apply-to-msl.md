Anyone who has earned an undergraduate degree from an accredited university in the U.S. or another country may apply. Individuals who have earned a J.D., LL.B., or similar law degree are not eligible for M.S.L. admission. Instead, they should apply for admission to the LL.M. program.

The admissions committee will holistically evaluate qualified M.S.L. applicants. For students who come from countries in which English is not the official language, English-language ability is also an important consideration.
