# Current J.D. Students

The Career Planning Center of Fordham University School of Law offers comprehensive career development services and resources to JD students. For information about the specific services and resources available to current JD students please select the JD button.

For information about Fordham Law's career planning services for LL.M./M.S.L./S.J.D. students, please select the L.L.M. /M.S.L./S.J.D. button.

[The Public Interest Resource Center (PIRC)](/school-of-law/centers-and-institutes/public-interest-resource-center/) also provides career support to current students who are seeking internships, fellowships and entry-level positions with public sector employers. Visit PIRC Public Service Career Support.
