# Business Cards

We are pleased to announce that we are now able to offer Fordham Law School business cards to S.J.D., LL.M., and M.S.L. candidates.

Business card orders will be placed once per semester. Exact dates and deadlines for ordering will be advertised internally to students via LawNet. Requests that come in after the deadline cannot be processed and will be deferred to the following semester's cycle.


-
The cost is

**$200 for 250 cards or $300 for 500 cards**and must be paid online by credit card using the link below, or by cash in the Office of International and Non-J.D. Programs in Suite 8-131.**Please note that we do not process orders for JD students.**The following information will be included on the cards:

- Name
- Degree program (S.J.D., LL.M., and M.S.L.)
- Specialization (LL.M. and M.S.L.)
- Phone number
- Fordham Law School email address (no outside email addresses may be used)

Please note that the format of the cards is not flexible. We are not able to accept special requests for addition of titles (e.g., Dr., Mr., Ms.), alternate or additional email addresses or phone numbers, etc.

Business card orders will be placed once per semester. The dates for ordering will be advertised internally to students.
