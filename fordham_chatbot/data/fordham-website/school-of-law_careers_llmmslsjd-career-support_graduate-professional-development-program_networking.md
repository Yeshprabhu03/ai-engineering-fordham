# Networking

No matter where you are from or how much experience you have, networking is the most important aspect of any job search. But networking is not one-size-fits-all. Every person will have a different approach that works best for them. The tools on this page are designed to help you come up with your own personal networking strategy. Although the materials primarily refer to current LLM students, most of the advice is highly applicable to students and alumni alike.
