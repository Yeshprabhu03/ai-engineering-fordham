### About the Symposium

**Lunch (speakers and sponsors only)** (12:30 - 1:45 p.m.)

**Opening Keynote** (2 - 2:30 p.m.)

Caleb Barlow, Vice President, IBM Watson

**Panel 1: AI and Machine Learning for Regulatory Compliance** (2:30 - 3:45 p.m.)

This session will address how AI and ML technologies intersect with regulatory compliance and how accountability can be assured. The session will begin with a short kickstarter presentation to set the stage with some technological examples for the use of AI/ML in compliance. The panel will then discuss emerging AI/ML technologies, how they can be deployed for compliance, and address issues they raise.

**Coffee Break** (3:45 - 4:15 p.m.)

**Panel 2: AI and Machine Learning for Cybersecurity and Fraud Detection** (4:15 - 5:30 p.m.)

This session will address the use of AI/ML to protect against cyber attacks and to assist in fraud detection. The session will begin with a short kickstarter presentation to set the stage with some technological examples for the use of AI/ML and behavioral analytics in cybersecurity and fraud detection. The panel will then discuss emerging AI/ML technologies, how they can be deployed to protect against cyber attacks and address issues they raise.

**Wine and Cheese Reception** (5:30 p.m.)
