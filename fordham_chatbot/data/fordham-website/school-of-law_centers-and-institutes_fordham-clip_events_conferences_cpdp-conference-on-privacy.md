# CPDP Conference on Privacy

## January 22-24, 2014

CLIP was an organizing partner for the [Computers Privacy and Data Protection (CPDP) conference](http://www.cpdpconferences.org/archive.html), held in Brussels, Belgium January 22-24, 2014. Additional information on the annual CPDP conference, including a full conference program, can be found on [their website](http://www.cpdpconferences.org/).
