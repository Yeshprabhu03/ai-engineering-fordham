# Neuroscience and Law Center Faculty

## Leadership

![]() |
space |
Deborah Denno Arthur A. McGivney Professor of Law |

A national expert in integrating the law with social sciences, Professor Deborah W. Denno has directed for the past three years a large-scale interdisciplinary research project that systematically analyzes all criminal cases featuring neuroscience evidence in the United States over the past two decades. Professor Denno’s research has been published in top academic journals and has yielded groundbreaking results.
