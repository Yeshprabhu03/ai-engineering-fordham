# Public Service Loan Assistance

Fordham Law graduates have the ability to take advantage of important resources to enable them to pursue careers in public service work, regardless of educational debt.

[Public Service Loan Forgiveness (PSLF)](https://studentaid.gov/manage-loans/forgiveness-cancellation/public-service)is one example of the resources available to financially enable graduates to dedicate their career to public service.[Fordham Law’s Loan Repayment Assistance Program](/school-of-law/admissions/loans/repayment-programs/)is for graduates who are pursuing careers in the nonprofit sector.

The staff of the [Law School’s Financial Aid Office](/school-of-law/admissions/jd-admissions/jd-financial-aid/) include national experts and advocates in the area of loan repayment assistance. They are available for individual counseling sessions with students and alumni.
