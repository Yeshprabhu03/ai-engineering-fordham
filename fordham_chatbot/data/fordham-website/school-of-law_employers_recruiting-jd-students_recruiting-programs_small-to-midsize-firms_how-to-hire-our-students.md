# How to Hire Our Students

It is easy and free to hire a 1L, 2L or 3L or recruit a graduate.

Fordham Law’s Career Planning Center (“CPC”) is happy to post openings for summer, term-time, entry-level or lateral positions.

![Professionals networking at a reception by window](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Public-Interest-Networking-(PIN)-Reception-(3).jpg)


- Post a job in our
[online job bank](https://www.fordham.edu/school-of-law/employers/post-a-job---fordham-law-job-bank/) - Sign up for a simple Resume Collect
- Participate in a Virtual Interview Program (“VIP”) (Spring, Summer, Fall)

**These services are free for employers.**


To learn more about these no fee recruiting options, read more [here](https://www.fordham.edu/school-of-law/employers/recruiting-jd-students/recruiting-programs/the-2025-recruiting-program/2025-recruiting-key-dates/) or contact Victoria Behr, Assistant Director of Employer Outreach at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#5a2c383f32281a3c35283e323b37743f3e2f).
