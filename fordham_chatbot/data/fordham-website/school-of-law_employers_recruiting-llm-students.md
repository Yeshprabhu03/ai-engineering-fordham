# Recruiting LL.M. Students

Each year, there are over 100 students from 40 countries who arrive on our Lincoln Center campus to pursue their LLM degrees in one of the eight concentrations that Fordham Law offers: Banking, Corporate, and Finance Law; Corporate Compliance; Fashion Law; Intellectual Property and Information Technology Law; International Business and Trade Law; International Dispute Resolution; International Law and Justice; and, U.S. Law.

Members of the LLM class hold both undergraduate and graduate degrees in law from some of the world’s most distinguished universities. Moreover, the vast majority of Fordham Law’s LLM candidates enter our programs with professional experience which includes:

- International and local law firms
- Major accounting firms
- Global banks and insurance companies
- Intergovernmental organizations, such as the United Nations
- Courts and departments of justice, in national and regional governments
- Multinational companies, in various sectors
