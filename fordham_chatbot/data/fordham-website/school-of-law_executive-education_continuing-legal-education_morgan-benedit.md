# Morgan Benedit

![Morgan Benedit](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Morgan-Benedit.jpg)


Assistant Director, Conference Services

Office of Public Programs and CLE

Morgan Benedit is the Assistant Director of Conference Services at Fordham University School of Law, where she oversees all aspects of marketing, promotions, and logistics for various events. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for delivering exceptional experiences, Morgan began her journey at Fordham Law School in 2018 as a part-time reservations assistant. Recognized for her dedication and exceptional performance, she swiftly ascended to the role of full-time conference administrator later that year.

In 2022, Morgan's exemplary work ethic and leadership skills were once again acknowledged, leading to her promotion to Assistant Director of Conference Services. In this capacity, she plays a pivotal role in managing marketing initiatives and orchestrating Continuing Legal Education logistics for CLE events, ensuring seamless execution and maximum engagement.

Before her tenure at Fordham, Morgan cultivated valuable expertise in customer service through various roles spanning over five years. Her background includes providing exemplary service in both retail and event environments. As an event assistant for an event planner in Brooklyn, NY, and as an intern at a non-profit organization, Morgan adeptly handled event logistics and provided invaluable day-of support. Her meticulous attention to detail extended to post-event tasks, where she diligently collected data to provide for future event planning endeavors.

Morgan holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Marketing and Public Relations from the New York Institute of Technology in New York City, graduating in 2012. Her academic background, coupled with her hands-on experience and commitment to excellence, positions her as a valuable asset in the realm of event management and conference services.
