# Program on Corporate Ethics and Compliance

## Objective

In furtherance of its mission, PCEC is committed to creating an outstanding educational environment by:

- Admitting highly qualified and engaged students,
- Attracting expert faculty with a passion for teaching,
- Offering a broad and practical curriculum that covers key legal and regulatory frameworks and skills areas that are essential for ethics and compliance professionals, and
- Providing a range of extra-curricular events and programming that enables students to stay current on emerging topics and engage with leading professionals in the ethics and compliance field.

By achieving these objectives, Fordham Law School seeks to be, and to be recognized as, the leading provider of graduate studies in corporate compliance.

Dual Master of Business and Compliance

The Dual Master of Business Administration/Master of Studies in Law (M.B.A./M.S.L.) degree combines a comprehensive business education with a specialized concentration in legal compliance to **working professionals** **within as little as two years of part-time studies**.

Apply to this dual-degree program via a single Gabelli School of Business application from the beginning of their studies. The application requirements are the same as [those for the Professional M.B.A.](https://www.fordham.edu/gabelli-school-of-business/academic-programs-and-admissions/graduate-programs/graduate-admissions/graduate-admissions-deadlines-and-requirements/) (there are no additional application requirements for the M.S.L. degree).

Accepted students are part of the Professional M.B.A. **and** the M.S.L. in Corporate Compliance degree programs.
