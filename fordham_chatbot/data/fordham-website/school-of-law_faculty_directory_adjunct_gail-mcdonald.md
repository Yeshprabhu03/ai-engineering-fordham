# Gail McDonald

## Adjunct Professor of Law

Gail joined the Fordham Law Library in October 2016. She received a JD from Seton Hall University School of Law in 2011. While in law school, she interned for a state appellate judge as well as SCI’s Center for Seafarers’ Rights. After law school, Gail clerked for a judge in New Jersey Superior Court. She also served as general counsel to a non-profit land trust. Gail received her MLIS degree from Rutgers University in 2014. Gail is Head of Research and Instruction at the Fordham Law Library.
