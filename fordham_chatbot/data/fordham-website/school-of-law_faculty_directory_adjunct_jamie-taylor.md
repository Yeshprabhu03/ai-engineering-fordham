# Jamie Taylor

Jamie joined the Fordham Law Library in September 2023. She received a JD from Villanova Law School and her MLIS from Catholic University with a concentration in law librarianship. Prior to becoming a librarian, she worked as a Trusts and Estates attorney for Pitney, Hardin, Kipp & Szuch and as the Donor Services Officer of the Greater St. Louis Community Foundation. She is admitted to practice law in New York and New Jersey.
