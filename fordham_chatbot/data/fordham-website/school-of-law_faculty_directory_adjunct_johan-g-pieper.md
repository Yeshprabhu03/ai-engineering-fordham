J. Gardiner Pieper, the founder of Pieper New York-Multistate Bar Review, has been lecturing and tutoring students on all aspects of the New York State Bar Examination since 1972. Professor Pieper is admitted to practice law in over thirty states, after sitting for and successfully taking the local and Multistate Bar Examinations in each of those jurisdictions. He also serves as the Village Justice of Upper Brookville.

### Education
