# Michael C. Neus

![Michael C. Neus Profile Page Image.](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Michael-Neus.jpg)

## Adjunct Professor of Law

General Counsel

ExodusPoint Capital Management, LP

Michael C. Neus is General Counsel of ExodusPoint Capital Management, LP, and as an Adjunct Professor of Law, teaches a course entitled “Investment Management Regulation and Compliance” at Fordham Law School. Mike is a frequent author and lecturer on securities, compliance, hedge fund and private equity topics at industry events and for global regulators. Mike is the author with Terrance J. O’Malley, of Insiders’ Guide to Hedge Funds: Successfully Managing the Middle and Back Office, a book published by Wolters Kluwer, an imprint of CCH Incorporated, May 2018Mike was previously Senior Fellow with the Program on Corporate Compliance and Enforcement at New York University School of Law in 2017-2018.

From 2005 through 2016, Mike was responsible for all legal, compliance, human resource and administrative matters as Managing Partner and General Counsel of Perry Capital, LLC. Prior to joining Perry Capital in 2005, Mike was the Chief Operating Officer and General Counsel at RHG Capital, L.P., Chief General Counsel of Andor Capital Management, L.L.C., and General Counsel of Soros Private Funds Management LLC. Mike began his professional career as an associate at the law firm Coudert Brothers, in both the Singapore and New York offices. Mike is a Senior Fellow of the Regulatory Compliance Association and Vice Chair of the American Bar Association’s Business Law Sub-Committee on Institutional Investors. Mike is a former Director, Chairman of the Investment Advisory Subcommittee, and member of the Executive Committee of the Managed Funds Association. Mike received his J.D. from Columbia University School of Law, where he was a Harlan Fiske Stone Scholar, and his B.A. cum laude from the University of Notre Dame.
