Michael D. Maimin is an Assistant United States Attorney in the Southern District of New York, practicing in the Violent Crimes Unit. Prior to joining the United States Attorney's Office, he was a litigation associate at Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP.

Professor Maimin has spent a significant amount of time during his legal career working on federal appellate issues, scuba diving and playing squash (poorly).
