### Experience

- Retired Partner, Clifford Chance. Over thirty years as an attorney representing major United States and European corporations in domestic and international mergers and acquisitions, strategic alliances, corporate finance transactions, consensual debt restructurings, bankruptcy reorganizations and other significant corporate matters.
- Senior Advisor to the investment banking firm Old Post Management LLC.
- Teaches Corporate Transactions: Finance And Litigation.
- Has taught Legal Aspects of Corporate Finance for 23 years at Fordham Law School; previously he was an Adjunct Professor of Law at the New York University School of Law, for a number of years, teaching the same class.
- Adviser to the 2018 Regional Runner-up Fordham Law School Team in the

National Law Meets M&A Practice Moot Court Type Competition. - Lecturer at a number of Centro de Estudios Garrigues LL. M. Programs

in International Transactions. - Mediator and Arbitrator of corporate, complex commercial and securities law disputes. Special Master at the Appellate Division of the New York State Supreme Court, First Department.
- Expert Witness in various litigation matters involving various corporate

transactions. - Member of the TriBar Legal Opinion Committee; active in the preparation of its Reports on various aspects of legal opinion practice,
- Member of the Board of Directors of Metal Forms Corporation, Milwaukee, Wisconsin. Also on the Board of the Clifford Chance Foundation.
