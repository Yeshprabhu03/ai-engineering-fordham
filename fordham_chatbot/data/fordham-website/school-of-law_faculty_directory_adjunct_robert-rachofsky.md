# Robert Rachofsky

![fls](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/RachofskyEdited.jpg)

## Adjunct Professor of Law

“Bob Rachofsky is a senior corporate partner at Willkie Farr & Gallagher LLP. His practice includes mergers and acquisitions involving both public and private companies, private equity and other corporate transactions, as well as public and private offerings of securities, securities disclosure and corporate governance matters generally. A recognized authority on corporate transactions in the financial services industry, Bob has written and spoken frequently on corporate law topics. He has been recognized by both Chambers USA and Super Lawyers as a leading practitioner.”
