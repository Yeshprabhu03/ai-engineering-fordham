Dr. Scott Tenner serves as Director of Brooklyn Gastroenterology and Endoscopy Associates and Director, The Greater New York Endoscopy Surgical Center. He also serves as a Professor of Medicine at the State University of New York and an Adjunct Professor of Law at Fordham University. Dr. Tenner received his Doctorate in Medicine, Masters in Cell Biology, and Masters in Public Health at George Washington University, Washington DC. He completed his training in Gastroenterology and Endoscopy at Brigham and Womenâ€™s Hospital, Harvard Medical School, Boston. Board Certified in both Internal Medicine and Gastroenterology, he has served as Chief of the Division of Gastroenterology at Maimonides Medical Center, Program Director at the State University of New York, President of the New York Society for Gastrointestinal Endoscopy, and Governor for the American College of Gastroenterology.

Dr. Tenner served 7 years on the Board of Trustees for the American College of Gastroenterology assisting in the development of Clinical Guidelines. Chairing the National Affairs Committee for the College, Dr. Tenner worked closely with the NY State Department of Health and various Congressional Offices in developing healthcare policy. He has authored over 200 abstracts, papers, chapters, and books. Despite a busy clinical practice, Dr. Tenner continues his passion for teaching both medical students and law students.
