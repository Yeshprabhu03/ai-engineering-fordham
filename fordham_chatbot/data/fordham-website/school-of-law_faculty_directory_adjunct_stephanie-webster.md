# Stephanie Webster

## Adjunct Professor of Law

Stephanie Webster is a Director and Counsel in the General Counsel division of Credit Suisse. She is the head of US Bank Regulatory & ERISA group and advises on U.S. bank regulatory related matters, including advising on implementation of the Volcker Rule, Resolution and Recovery planning, and the Enhanced Prudential Standards.

She also leads a team of attorneys advising on ERISA matters including implementation of the Department of Laborâ€™s Fiduciary Rule. She is based in New York.

Professor Webster joined Credit Suisse in January *2011*. Prior to that, she was an associate at the law firm of Linklaters in the financial regulations group, advising primarily on U.S. bank regulatory matters, and at the law firm of Goodwin Procter LLP in the financial services group.

Professor Webster began her legal career at the law firm of Freshfields Bruckhaus Deringer LLP as an associate focused on international arbitration.

Professor Webster received her J.D. from New York University School of Law and her B.A., with honors, from Brandeis University.
