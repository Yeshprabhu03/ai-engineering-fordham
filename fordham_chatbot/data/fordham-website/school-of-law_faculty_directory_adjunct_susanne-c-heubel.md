# Susanne C. Heubel

##

Adjunct Professor of Law

![Fordham Law School Adjunct Faculty](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Susanne-Heubel-480px.jpg)

## Biography

Originally from Frankfurt am Main, Germany, Susanne C. Heubel is Senior Counsel with the law firm Harter Secrest & Emery LLP in the area of business and family immigration. She received her LL.M. in International Business and Trade Law from Fordham in 2000, after completing her full legal education in Germany, Switzerland and Argentina. She is an active member of the American Immigration Lawyers Association (AILA) and a frequent speaker on immigration topics.
