Wilson joined the Fordham Law Library in February 2024 as a Reference and Collection Development Librarian.

He was previously a Research Services Librarian at the Georgetown Law Library. He received his JD from New York Law School in 2014 and his MLIS from the University of Washington in 2021. Before becoming a librarian, he practiced law in and around New York City.
