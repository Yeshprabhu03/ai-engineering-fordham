## Professor of Law

### Bio

- Reference Librarian, Harvard College Library, 1964-66;
- Assistant Acquisitions Librarian, Harvard Law School Library, 1966-69;
- Attorney, 1969-71; Attorney in charge of data base definition and design, Mead Data Central, 1971-74;
- Director of Research, MELS (Ford Foundation and DC 37 grant), 1974-75;
- Associate Professor of Law, N.Y. Law School, 1976-82;
- Assistant Law Librarian, Columbia Law School, 1982-85;
- Contributor: Information High Tech;
- Principal subjects: Property, Criminal Law, Land Use, Constitutional History (at Fordham since 1986).

### Selected Publications

- Professional Staffing and Job Security in the Academic Law Library, Glanville Pub. 1988
- Various journal articles about Law Librarianship

### Education

- Harvard, JD, 1969
- University of Washington, ML, 1964
- University of Oregon, BA, 1963
