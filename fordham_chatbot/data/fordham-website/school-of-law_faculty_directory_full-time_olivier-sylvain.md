# Olivier Sylvain

![Fordham Law School Professor Olivier Sylvain](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/fls_faculty_sylvain_240x240.jpg)

## Professor of Law

-
Olivier is a Professor of Law at Fordham University and a

[Senior Policy Research](https://knightcolumbia.org/content/olivier-sylvain-joins-knight-institute-as-senior-policy-research-fellow-1)Fellow at Columbia University's Knight First Amendment Institute. His research is on information and communications law and policy. His most recent[writing](https://www.nytimes.com/2019/03/28/opinion/facebook-ad-discrimination-race.html),[scholarship](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/cf_dev/AbsByAuth.cfm?per_id=1084259),[commentary](https://www.npr.org/2025/01/23/nx-s1-5269693/is-trumps-order-to-allow-tiktok-to-continue-operating-in-the-u-s-even-legal), and[congressional testimony](https://www.commerce.senate.gov/2020/7/the-pact-act-and-section-230-the-impact-of-the-law-that-helped-create-the-internet-and-an-examination-of-proposed-reforms-for-today-s-online-world)are on[online](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3965463)[intermediary](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3157975)[liability](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=2997141),[commercial surveillance](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=2997141), and[artificial intelligence](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3499821). His forthcoming book,will come out in March 2026. In December 2025, the ANNALS of the American Academy of Political and Social Science published his book chapter, "*Reclaiming the Internet: How Big Tech Took Control—and How We Can Take It Back,*[Middleware and the Illusory Promise of End-User Control](https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=5923284)."The National Science Foundation and the John S. and James L. Knight Foundation have awarded him grants to support this work. He was a Senior Advisor to the Chair of the Federal Trade Commission from 2021 to 2023.

Olivier teaches Legislation & Regulation, Administrative Law, Information Law, U.S. Data Protection Law and Privacy, and information technology related courses.

Olivier is a member of the Council for Responsible Media and on the Academic Advisory Board of the Open Markets Institute. He has served on a range of boards, as a president and a member.

Before entering academia, Olivier was a Karpatkin Fellow in the National Legal Office of the American Civil Liberties Union in New York City and a litigation associate at Jenner & Block, LLC, in Washington, D.C.
