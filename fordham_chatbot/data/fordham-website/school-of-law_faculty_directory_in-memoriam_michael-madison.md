# Michael Madison (1941-2024)

## Professor of Law

![Michael Thomas Madison Fordham Law Faculty In Memoriam](/media/home/schools/school-of-law/Michael-Thomas-Madison-Fordham-Law-Faculty-In-Memoriam.jpeg)


Years of Service

1979- 2009

Michael Thomas Madison served as a Professor of Law at Fordham Law School from 1979-2009. He was proud of his published works including three casebooks and treatises used by thousands of law students.

-
Biography

- Associate, Valicenti Leighton Reid & Pine, 1967-68
- Private practice, 1969-71
- Senior Attorney, Office of General Counsel, New York Life Ins. Co., 1970-72
- Professor, William & Mary Law School, 1972-79
- Adjunct Professor, Columbia University Law School, 1984-85
- Taught for the NYU / IRS Continuing Professional Education Program, Wash. D.C.
- Principal subjects: Property, Real Estate Finance (at Fordham since 1979)
- Expert witness with respect to Real Estate malpractice litigation in New York and New Jersey

Michael is survived by his wife, Marcia Bernstein, and former wife, Terry Mizrahi, as well as two children, Eve Madison Rodsky and Joshua Madison.
