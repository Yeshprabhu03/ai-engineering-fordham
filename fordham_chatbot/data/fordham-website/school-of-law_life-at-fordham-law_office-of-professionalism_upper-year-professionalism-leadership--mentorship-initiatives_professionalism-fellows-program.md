# Professionalism Fellows Program

The Fordham Law Professionalism Fellows Program is a one-year fellowship program for six upper-year students with a demonstrated interest in and commitment to promoting professionalism, mentorship, and inclusion within the Law School. Each Professionalism Fellow receives a $2,000 stipend and is associated with one of the five Houses, playing a key role in:

- Organizing social and community-building initiatives within the
[House System](/school-of-law/life-at-fordham-law/office-of-professionalism/house-system/) - Coordinating the
