# School of Law 2024 Diploma Ceremony

Graduation Award Recipients


Teacher of the Year Award

**Daniel Capra***Reed Professor of Law*

This award is presented by the Student Bar Association to a professor who students identify as being a master teacher.

Dean’s Medal of Recognition

**James Kainen** *Brendan Moore Chair in Advocacy*

This award, the highest honor the Dean can confer, is presented to a person selected by the Dean who has shown outstanding commitment to the ideals of the Law School through their service to its community across their career.

Eugene J. Keefe Award

**Afrika Owes ’24***Director of Diversity, Equity, Inclusion & Belonging, Student Bar Association*

This award is presented to the person or organization that has made the most important contribution to the Fordham Law community. It is presented by the Student Bar Association in memory of a beloved faculty member who served at Fordham Law from 1926 to 1968.
