# Compliance and Technology

The purpose of the Compliance and Technology organization is to provide practical knowledge and skills necessary to navigate a career in compliance, prepare students for the technologies used, and network with compliance professionals.

### The organization aims to:

- Foster a deeper understanding of compliance issues and developments.
- Engage with practitioners, academics, and policymakers through seminars, panel discussions, and workshops.
- Provide career development opportunities through mentoring, networking events and professional interaction.
