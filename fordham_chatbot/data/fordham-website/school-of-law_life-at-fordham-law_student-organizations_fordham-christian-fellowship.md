# Fordham Christian Fellowship

## FCF

Fordham Christian Fellowship (FCF) exists to foster a community of believers in Jesus at Fordham Law School, to engage in open conversations about the Christian faith and the law, and develop lasting mentorship between students and practicing Christian attorneys. The Fellowship continually seeks to be open, inclusive, and a space of peace, relaxation, and community.
