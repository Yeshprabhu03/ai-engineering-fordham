# Fordham Federalist Society for Law and Public Policy Studies

The Fordham Law Federalist Society is a student chapter of The Federalist Society for Law and Public Policy Studies, a national organization comprised of over 40,000 lawyers, law students, scholars, and other individuals. The Fordham Chapter is committed to encouraging open and honest debate within the law school on the fundamental legal issues of our day. It will foster critical thought while placing a premium on individual liberty, limited government, and judicial restraint.

Current students should [complete this brief survey to become a member of Fordham's Chapter.](https://docs.google.com/a/fordham.edu/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSfBFjdMMVkjmYM13-J3W0AtHp5N1d6ISHKyfY5hOu5h1qh8uQ/viewform?c=0&w=1). Interested students are also strongly encouraged to join the national organization—at a discounted rate of $5/year for students—by visiting [www.fedsoc.org/join](http://www.fedsoc.org/join).

Members of the alumni community who would like to receive updates from or be involved with the Chapter should [fill out this brief survey](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1TAMce8pYSVr0fwrAiNXskPZ_VHWVFxEByqmABdIxl7g/viewform?edit_requested=true).
