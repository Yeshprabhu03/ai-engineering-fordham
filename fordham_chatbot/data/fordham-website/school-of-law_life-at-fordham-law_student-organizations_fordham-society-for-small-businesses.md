# Fordham Society for Small Businesses

## FSSB

Fordham Society for Small Businesses (FSSB) aims to bring together students interested in small businesses, especially the small-business ecosystem in New York City. By bringing interested students together, the Society hopes to ultimately assist small businesses or relevant infrastructure, thereby diversifying the economy and supporting local communities.
