# Irish Law Students Association

## ILSA

ILSA is an organization comprised of Fordham Law students sharing a common Irish heritage and tradition, or an interest in such. Membership is open to all students, regardless of heritage.

You can contact us or join our mailing list by emailing [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#f6b09984929e979bbfbaa5b7b6919b979f9ad895999b). Our weekly newsletter will keep you informed of Irish cultural events throughout New York City, as well as news throughout Ireland and the Irish diaspora.
