# About the School of Professional and Continuing Studies

![](/media/home/schools/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/Classroom-three-students.jpg)

The School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS) offers students a quality education in a small, personalized atmosphere that promotes individual growth and exploration. With campus locations in Manhattan, the Bronx and Westchester as well as a center in London, PCS is poised to meet students’ academic needs. Undergraduate classes are offered evenings, weekends as well as a Post-Baccalaureate / Pre-Medical / Pre-Health program. The Fordham Real Estate Institute, which is part of PCS, offers Master’s Degrees in Real Estate, Real Estate Development and Construction Management and multiple Certificate Programs various areas. A Master’s in Applied Health Informatics is also available in London, New York City or online.

### Mission

To help people change their lives by delivering quality educational opportunities that allow students to earn credentials they can be proud of in a format that fits their lives.

### Purpose

Our purpose is to help students open up opportunities for themselves by helping them earn a credential they can be proud of, which prepares them to understand, lead, and inspire others in a changing world.

### Locations

We draw students from across the tri-state region, and because geographic convenience is a key element in an adult learner’s success, you can take courses at any of Fordham’s three campuses.

[The 85-acre Rose Hill campus](/about/maps-and-directions/rose-hill-campus/), adjacent to the New York Botanical Garden in the Northern Bronx

[The Lincoln Center campus](/about/maps-and-directions/lincoln-center-campus/), in Manhattan, at 60th Street and Columbus Avenue

[The Westchester campus](/about/maps-and-directions/westchester-campus/), in West Harrison, just off Route 287, near the Hutchinson River Parkway and Route 684
