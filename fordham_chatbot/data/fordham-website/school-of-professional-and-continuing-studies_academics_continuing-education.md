# Continuing Education

## Take the next step professionally

The special programs offered by the Fordham School of Professional and Continuing Studies are designed to help individuals move to the next level in their careers. Courses include qualifying exam preparation as well as of-the-moment instruction on new technologies and industry policies. Short-term classes scheduled on nights and weekends make taking the next steps to advancement easy.

Most of these courses do not require an application; students may self-register using a valid credit card.

Choose a program below for more details regarding course availability and the registration process. If you have any questions or need assistance registering for courses, please call 718-817-2600 or email [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#522231213b3c343d12343d20363a333f7c373627).
