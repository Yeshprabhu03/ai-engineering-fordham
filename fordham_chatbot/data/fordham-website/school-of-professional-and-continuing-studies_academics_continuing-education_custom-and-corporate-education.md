# Custom and Corporate Education


## In a global professional environment that demands innovation, adaptation and ongoing skills acquisition, we provide training solutions for your team.

Fordham PCS offers a range of training, professional, and certificate programs distinguished by educational excellence, flexible formats, and relevance for corporate entities. Our goal is to help transform individuals into leaders in their workplaces, in their industries, and in their communities.

Fordham PCS is pleased to partner with organizations to create customized learning experiences that develop the talents of both new and experienced employees and that contribute to the overall health of your business.

- Corporate retreats and facilitated strategic planning sessions
