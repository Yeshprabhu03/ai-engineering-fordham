# Human Resource Management Certification Registration

The Human Resource Management Certification Program offered in partnership with SHRM is a 36-hour program. The program cost includes all official SHRM approved materials for the course.

**Registration is now open for Spring 2026**

| After 2nd class meetings | No refund |
