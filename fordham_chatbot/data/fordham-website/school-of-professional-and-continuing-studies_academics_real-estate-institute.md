# Fordham Real Estate Institute

Located in the heart of New York City, the Fordham University Real Estate Institute (REI) provides students with industry-specific experiential learning opportunities. Students learn from and network with leading real estate, construction, and hospitality industry professionals. ## Why Fordham Real Estate Institute?

As part of [Fordham’s School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS)](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/), the Fordham Real Estate Institute follows an interdisciplinary, practical approach to curriculum and instruction for an optimal learning experience.

As a result, REI presents a comprehensive range of in-person and online [graduate](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/graduate-programs/), [undergraduate](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/bachelor-of-science-in-real-estate/), and [noncredit professional development programs](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/academics/professional-certificates-and-continuing-education/) covering the spectrum of career tracks and disciplines within the commercial real estate industry.

The [student experience](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/student-experience/) includes an array of networking opportunities that focus on building lasting industry connections, with input from industry thought leaders on the [REI Executive Advisory Council](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/about/executive-advisory-council/).
