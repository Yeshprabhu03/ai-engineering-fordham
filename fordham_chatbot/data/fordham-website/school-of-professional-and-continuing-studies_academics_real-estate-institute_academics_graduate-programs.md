## Joint MS/Advanced Graduate Certificates

Further your academic and career objectives by pursuing a master’s degree and an advanced graduate certificate simultaneously. Save time and money with the benefit of credit sharing between an MS and an advanced certificate program.

**MSRE/Advanced Certificate in Real Estate Finance**
