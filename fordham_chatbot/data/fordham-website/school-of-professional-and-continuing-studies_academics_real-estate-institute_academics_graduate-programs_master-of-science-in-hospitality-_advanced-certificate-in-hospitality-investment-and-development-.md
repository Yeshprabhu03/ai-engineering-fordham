### Advanced Certificate in Hospitality Investment and Development Curriculum

**Curriculum Highlights **

- Core curriculum addresses the most important elements of the real estate development process, hospitality finance and investment, feasibility studies for hospitality, and development project leadership, management, and communications. (9 credits)
- Elective program allows students to immerse themselves in specialized topics of their choice (3 credits)
