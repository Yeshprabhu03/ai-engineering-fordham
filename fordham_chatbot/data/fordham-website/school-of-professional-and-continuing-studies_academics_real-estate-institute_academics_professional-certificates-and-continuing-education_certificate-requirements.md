# Real Estate Institute Certificate Requirements

- Students have up to three years from the date of their first course to complete a certificate
- All courses applied toward a certificate must have a passing grade
- NC (not complete) grades are not applicable toward a certificate
- Students may complete more than one certificate in a term
- Courses may not be applied to more than one certificate
- Certificate Request fee is $100, nonrefundable
