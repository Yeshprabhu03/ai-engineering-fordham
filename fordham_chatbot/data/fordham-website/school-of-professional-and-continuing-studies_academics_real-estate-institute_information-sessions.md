Drop in anytime during the event. You should complete your application before logging into the event. Make sure you upload all transcripts, supporting documents, and letters of reference (if needed for Pre-Med/Pre-Health and REI Master of Science degrees). Our admissions team will be on hand to answer questions, give you a placement test if needed, talk to you about your goals, and then let you know if you’ve been accepted for the term. We’ll even waive the application fee!

-
The quickest way to receive an admissions decision is to

[complete your application](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/real-estate-institute/admissions-and-aid/application/)before you log on. You must have copies of your high school and/or college transcripts in order for your application to be reviewed. While official transcripts are required by the time you start taking classes, we understand that they may be difficult to obtain at this time. We can make an admission decision based on the unofficial transcripts in this unique situation. Our system helps you keep track of what you’ve completed and what you’ll need. You can submit the application without paying the application fee—we’ll waive it when you attend the event.You may also schedule an appointment anytime for your own personal Express Admission Day. Call 718-817-2600 to make an appointment.
