**New Office Underwriting Standards: Can the Hospitality Model Save Office Values? **

The office space industry needs to have its own moment of creativity and innovation to sustain values and plot a course toward recovery. A possible step is adopting new underwriting standards and letting go of an old favorite: Weighted Average Lease Term (WALT). With rapidly changing business and workplace environments office asset managers should focus primarily on maximizing cash flows. Ultimately, a building that maximizes Revenue Per Available Square Foot (RevPAF) will likely generate more profits and thus be more valuable, all else being equal - a lesson that can be learned from the hospitality industry. [Read here.](https://www.naiop.org/research-and-publications/magazine/2023/winter-2023-2024/development-ownership/new-office-underwriting-standards/)
