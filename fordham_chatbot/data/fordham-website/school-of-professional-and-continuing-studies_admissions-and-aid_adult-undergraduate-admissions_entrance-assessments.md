# Entrance Assessments

A brief college skills assessment is part of the admission process and is intended to ensure that all prospective students have the skills required for success in a challenging university program. The Assessment consists of two 45-minute sections involving reading comprehension and the writing of one essay. There is no math component on the Assessment.
