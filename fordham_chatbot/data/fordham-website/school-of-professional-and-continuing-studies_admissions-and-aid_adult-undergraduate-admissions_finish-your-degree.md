# Finish Your Degree

## You’ve come this far. Earn a top-quality degree, with the support you deserve.

![PCS Students Working with Professor](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/PCS_Students_Working_with_Professor___SM.jpg)

With more than **50 undergraduate majors designed for adult learners** and **personal support** to help guide you through the process, Fordham’s School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS), can help you earn **a top-notch degree** that can open doors to your future.

[Request More Information](#RequestInfo)

### Thinking of how this fits into your busy schedule?

Our classes are usually **one evening a week, on weekends or online** and students can attend classes in our **Midtown, Westchester or Bronx campuses**.

### An esteemed Fordham degree designed for you.

![PCS Student Sitting on Bench Outside](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/PCS_Student_Sitting_on_Bench_Outside___SM.jpg)

-
**Flexible credit transfer and credit for life experience**. You may be closer than you think to the 124 credits required for a bachelor’s degree. We accept up to 75 transfer credits and up to 30 credits for professional, life, or military experience. Students may also sit for the College Level Examination Program (CLEP) and DANTES exams. -
**A valuable degree; a valuable network**. More than 100,000 Fordham alumni work in the greater New York area, in every sector. As part of this loyal network, you will have access to alumni chapters, events, and directories. -
**Small classes**. The average class size is 15, so you’ll know your teacher and classmates and receive whatever support you need. -
**Quality instruction**. All instructors have a master’s degree or doctorate in their subjects. Most are practitioners in their fields who have insights and contacts to share. -
**Affordability**. We will work with you to find scholarships, loans, and grants. Our goal is to make a Fordham education possible for every qualified student. We also cover 100 percent of tuition and fees for qualifying veterans through the Yellow Ribbon program.
