# Dean's Scholarship

An annual scholarship of $3,500 is awarded to newly-admitted students whose prior records and performance in the admissions skills assessment demonstrate outstanding promise, regardless of financial need. Scholarships are renewable for up to five years, assuming a B average in at least four courses per year.
