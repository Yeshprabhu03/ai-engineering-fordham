# PCS Returning Student Admission Requirements

We are happy to hear you would like to return to PCS! Please follow the readmission requirements below.

[Apply online.](https://www.fordham.edu/info/21211/undergraduate_admissions/2247/application)- Upload a Personal Statement (Include what you have been doing since you were last at Fordham, and why you plan to return).
- Provide any transcripts from accredited institutions that you have attended since you were last at Fordham. Order all
**official**transcripts to be sent to the preferred campus address below. If you have studied outside the United States you must also submit a third-party evaluation of your transcript, we use World Education Services -[www.wes.org](https://www.wes.org/)
