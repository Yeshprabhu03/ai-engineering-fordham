# Tuition Discount for Westchester County Employees

![Westchester Campus](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Westchester_Campus.jpg)


**Westchester County employees are eligible for a 25% discount on undergraduate tuition, and a 20% discount on graduate tuition**, when enrolled in Fordham University’s School of Professional and Continuing Studies at Fordham’s Westchester campus, located in West Harrison.

The discount is open to all current, qualifying employees of Westchester County government (including the Medical Center) or municipalities within the county who are in good standing.

[Fordham's School of Professional and Continuing Studies (PCS)](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/)offers:

[Undergraduate Degree Programs for Adults](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/undergraduate-degree-programs/): Evening, weekend, online, and accelerated formate classes with 20 majors to choose from; and a flexible transfer credit policy.[Post-baccalaureate Pre-Med/Pre-Health Program](/school-of-professional-and-continuing-studies/academics/post-baccalaureate-pre-medicalpre-health-program/): Take the science courses you need to prepare you for applying to medical, dental, physician assistant, or veterinary school.- Master of Science in Real Estate and graduate programs in real estate finance, construction, or development. Fordham's Real Estate Institute can help you expand your knowledge and professional credentials.
