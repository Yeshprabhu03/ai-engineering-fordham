# New Student Account Claiming

## How to Claim Your Account

**FORDHAM IDENTIFICATION NUMBER (FIDN): **Can be found on your admitted student portal

**ACCESSIT ID:** Your AccessIT ID is your electronic identity for logging on to services offered by Fordham University. You MUST claim your AccessIT ID to register for your classes and access the following services-mail, Blackboard and MyFiles. Tutorials on how to use these resources and services are available to you on the Home tab once you log in at My.fordham.edu. Detailed information on your AccessIT ID is available at: http://www.fordham.edu/accessIT.

**TO CLAIM YOUR ACCOUNT: **Click the link [here for step by step instructions](https://claim.fordham.edu/howto/).

**FORDHAM E-MAIL ACCOUNT: **Your Fordham E-mail Username is your: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection)

(As soon as you claim, your e-mail account is available for immediate use)

**FORDHAM ID CARD: **Instructions can be found here: [https://www.fordham.edu/information-technology/it-services/electronic-id/](/information-technology/it-services/electronic-id/)

**BLACKBOARD:** Fordham’s Web-based course-management system is designed to allow students and faculty to participate in classes delivered online or use online materials and activities to complement face-to-face teaching. Your courses will appear in Blackboard when classes begin.

**MYFILES:** Fordham’s file management system which provides 5GB of secure and private file storage for each student. Here you can backup pictures, photos, and coursework, minimizing the need for USB drives.
