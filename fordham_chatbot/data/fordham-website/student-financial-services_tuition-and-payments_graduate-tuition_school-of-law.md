# School of Law Tuition and Fees

## 2025-2026 Academic Year

**Doctor of Law (J.D.)**

Full-Time Day Students (1L to 3L): $78,078 per year / $39,039 per semester

Part-Time Evening Students (1LE to 4LE): $58,560 per year / $29,280 per semester

Part-Time Per-Credit Rate: $2,822 per credit

**Master of Laws (LL.M)**

Full-Time New & Continuing Students: $78,078 per year / $39,039 per semester

Part-Time Per-Credit Rate (On Ground): $3,254 per credit

Part-Time Per-Credit Rate (Online): $2,896 per credit

**Doctor of Juridical Science (S.J.D) **

Full-Time: $15,698 per year

**Master of Laws (MSL) - Corporate Compliance or Fashion Law**

Full-Time: $68,220 per year / $34,110 per semester

Part-Time: $2,274 per credit
