# ROTC Scholarship

Fordham values all of the dedicated students in the Reserve Officersâ€™ Training Corps (ROTC) program. Specific benefits for each ROTC branch are detailed below.

-
- Fordham covers tuition, mandatory fees, average room, average meal, and a stipend for the first year.
- After the first year, provided the candidates meet the criteria of the Naval Prep Program, they will be awarded a NAVY ROTC scholarship for up to four additional years to Fordham University, covering:
- tuition and fees
- a Room Incentive equal to the cost of an average double room or direct charges, whichever is lower
- meals are NOT included

- To receive the Room Incentive, students must:
- annually file the FAFSA
- maintain a minimum 2.0 cumulative GPA
- maintain Satisfactory Academic Progress
- continue to receive tuition and fees benefits from ROTC
- live in Fordham housing

- If the Air Force covers full tuition and fees, Fordham provides a Room Incentive equal to the cost of an average double room or direct charges, whichever is lower.
- Meals are not included.

*If the Air Force contribution is less than Fordham tuition, students are not eligible for the Room Incentive. They can apply for institutional aid through our normal financial aid process.*
