# New Applicant Forms

**You must log into your University Email Account before completing your Student Employment Placement Form. If you do not log in, you will not have access to the form.**

First Year with a Federal Work-Study Award in their Financial Aid Offer will be contacted in July.

Students interested in seeking employment and did not receive a federal work-study award will be contacted as employment opportunities become available.

Once contacted you must complete the following process:

- Complete your Student Employment Placement Form
- Consult with your parents and/or accountant regarding tax withholding forms since we are not able to provide tax advice.
- Complete the applicable forms listed below:

-
[Tax Worksheet for Student Employment](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/pdfs/jadu-single-folder-pdfs/Tax_Worksheet___Student_Employment.pdf)[Form W-4](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/fw4.pdf)- Choose one
[Allocation of Withholding Tax (Form IT-2104.1)](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/pdfs/jadu-single-folder-pdfs/IT_2104.1_Tax_Form_.pdf)(required if you live outside of the five boroughs of New York City or Yonkers)[NYS Paid Family Leave Waiver Form](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/pdfs/jadu-single-folder-pdfs/PFL_Waiver_Form.pdf)[I-9 Tax Form](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/financial-aid/pdfs/I-9-Form.pdf)(Please submit scanned or photographed I-9 documents electronically, using your[financial aid portal](https://my.fordham.edu/fasubmitdocs). Some examples include a passport or photo ID and either birth certificate or social security card)**Please be advised if you are unable to obtain original documents within the 3-day period, student worker employees will have to stop working and removed from the payroll.**[Direct Deposit Information](https://generalssb-prod.ec.fordham.edu/BannerGeneralSsb/ssb/directDeposit#/directDepositListing)(for students who want their paycheck sent directly to their checking or savings account)
