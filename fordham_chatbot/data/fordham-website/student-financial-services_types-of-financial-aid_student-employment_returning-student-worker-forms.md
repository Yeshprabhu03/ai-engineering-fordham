# Returning Student Worker Forms

If you are returning to your prior position:

- You must return a signed copy of your Placement Paperwork which will be sent to you prior to your start date.

Returning Student Workers must complete the appropriate placement form **ONLY** if they are requesting a change from their current position or department:

**You must log into your University Email Account before completing your Student Employment Placement Form. If you do not log in, you will not have access to the form.**

If you are seeking a new position, please contact the Office of Student Employment at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) to give notice of your request.
