# 511NY Rideshare Fordham Student Portal

**Learn About New Options to Improve Your Commute**

Fordham University’s Office of Student Involvement has partnered with 511NY Rideshare to bring you the [ Fordham Student Traveler Portal](https://urldefense.proofpoint.com/v2/url?u=https-3A__511nyrideshare.org_web_fordham-2Duniversity&d=DwMFAg&c=aqMfXOEvEJQh2iQMCb7Wy8l0sPnURkcqADc2guUW8IM&r=bFnSrM4VZ-SNNTCIXcybRky3w1qfsIzt6kibBnQyhkY&m=zr2NJdFyO9fe2CVhbHfBpsHSxFqHXN8j4Ogeuhn9g3cbB-cD3HUz-yQ78UPyMuy5&s=13fMztXQpOEWqMMNtMjlNvBtNEUchetlqGDPF3YT150&e=). Visit the portal for resources to get to and around our Fordham campuses, connect with other students to carpool, bike and take transit, and get Ram Van info and other transportation information.

Ridesharing can be a convenient option – allowing you to save money and connect with others. 511NY Rideshare offers ride matching for carpools and vanpools, and resources for taking transit, finding park and ride lots, bicycling, walking, telework, and other options.

** Fordham University is providing information about 511NY Rideshare as a resource and does not endorse the website or the services provided. ** If you use the website or the services, you do so on a voluntary basis, and not as a requirement. You, not Fordham University, will be bound by 511NY Rideshare's terms of service.

Rideshare users must proceed to the main gate and show the security officer their Vital Check confirmation. The electronic entrance cannot be used when passengers are in the vehicle.
