# Reserve a Locker at Rose Hill

The Office for Student Involvement reserves lockers for commuter students. Starting on the first day of classes, lockers are available for reservation online to all students on a semesterly basis. These lockers are located on the lower level of the McShane Student Center and are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis. Lockers can be used until the last day of finals during the Spring Semester.
