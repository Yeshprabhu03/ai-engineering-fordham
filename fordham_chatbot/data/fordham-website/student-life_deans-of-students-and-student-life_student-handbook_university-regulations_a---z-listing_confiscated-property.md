# Student Handbook: Confiscated Property

Items prohibited on campus may be confiscated by University staff and Department of Public Safety personnel to maintain a safe and orderly campus environment, and/or to adhere to the University Code of Conduct and/or University Regulations, relevant laws, and regulations. Reasonable efforts may be made to return this property, but the University reserves the right to discard or retain such items at its discretion. Please see the relevant section of University Regulations and/or the Office of Residential Life handbooks for additional specific information.
