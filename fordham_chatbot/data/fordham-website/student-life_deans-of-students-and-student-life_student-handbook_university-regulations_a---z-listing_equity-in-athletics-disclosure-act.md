# Student Handbook: Equity in Athletics Disclosure Act

In accordance with the "Equity in Athletics Disclosure Act," effective July 1, 1996, information regarding the intercollegiate athletics program is available for review upon request to the Director of Athletics, Fordham University, Rose Hill Gym, Bronx, New York 10458 (phone # 718-817-4300).
