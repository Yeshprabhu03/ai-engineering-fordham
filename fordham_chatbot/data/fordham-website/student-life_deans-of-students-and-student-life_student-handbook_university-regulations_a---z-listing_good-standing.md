# Student Handbook: Good Standing

Only matriculated students enrolled in degree programs who are not under academic or disciplinary probation will be eligible to run for or hold office in a student organization or apply for or hold certain leadership positions. This includes paid positions and positions to which students are appointed as well as elected offices.
