# Student Handbook: Windows

It is unacceptable to throw, move, or pass anything in or out of the window of a University building including persons without prior authorization from the Department of Public Safety and/or the Office of Residential Life for residence halls. Reflecting the Universityâ€™s concern about this activity, sanctions may be imposed upon any individual observed or discovered to be engaging in such behavior:

Sanctions for objects dropped or thrown from University buildings:

**Minimum sanction:**

- $100 fine or an equivalent educational sanction
- Probation for one semester

**Medium sanction:**

- Dismissal from residence (for resident students)
- $200 fine or an equivalent educational sanction and probation for one year

**Maximum sanctions:**

- Suspension or expulsion, depending on seriousness of injury, threat, or property damage
