# Ram Van Shuttle: Departmental Discount Books

Departments may purchase digital discount books via an interdepartmental budget transfer. A digital discount book costs $35 and includes 12 tickets, but for the price of 10 tickets. A certified Fordham budget administrator must submit requests.

- To request discount books,
[please use this form](https://podio.com/webforms/17314264/1172807). - A verification email with the total billable cost will be sent upon submission to the email address listed.
**The budget administrator must click the "Verify Charge" button to finalize the request**. - Each Discount Book purchased will result in 12 individual emails sent to your inbox.
**We highly recommend you set up an email filter or folder to organize your Ram Van discount book tickets.**[Please see our tutorial on keeping all of your tickets organized via Gmailâ€™s labeling feature](https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Qe1WevCPlYJPcLQkFyFzIqI9IS66g2mn/view?usp=sharing).

Electronic Discount Book tickets can be used in several ways:

- You may print the ticket with the QR code and physically distribute the ticket.
- A Ram Van driver may scan the QR code attached to each ticket at departure time.
- You may pre-pay for a seat using the pre-validation code included in your ticket information.
- You may email forward a ticket to the end-user, who can then choose one of the above options.
- If you are uncertain whether an electronic ticket has already been used, please contact our office at (718) 817-4346 for assistance before your trip.
