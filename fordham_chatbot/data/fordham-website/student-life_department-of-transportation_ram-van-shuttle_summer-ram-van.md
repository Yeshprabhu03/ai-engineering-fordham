# Summer Ram Van

**Office Hours and Schedule**

During the summer, Ram Van operates on weekdays only.

Our office is open Monday through Friday from 7 AM until 12 AM, with vans departing on the half-hour. Please see our [complete schedule](/student-life/department-of-transportation/ram-van-shuttle/calendar-and-schedules/#d.en.59408) for more details.

**Free Passes**

Students are eligible to receive free passes for credit-bearing classes and internships. Restrictions apply, so please review our [free passes policy](/student-life/department-of-transportation/ram-van-shuttle/free-passes-policy/) to see if you qualify.

**Fridays in July and August**

There will be **NO RAM VAN SERVICE** on Fridays in July or the first two weeks of August, as the University is closed.

**Off-Campus Shuttle**

The [off-campus shuttle,](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/public-safety/services/campus-shuttle/off-campus-shuttle-information/) operated by the Department of Public Safety, runs on a limited schedule during the summer. Contact Public Safety at 718-817-2222 for more information.
