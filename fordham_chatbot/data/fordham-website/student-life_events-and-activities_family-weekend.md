# Family Weekend

## Welcome to the Fordham Family. Let us share NYC with you.

![Family Weekend](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Family_Weekend_med_21412569339_f37c6c015e_o.jpg)

**Family Weekend: Friday, October 24 to Sunday, October 26, 2025!**

Join us for a weekend reunion packed full of events you can enjoy as a family. Let your undergraduate student show you campus through their eyes as they introduce you to the community they have built here.

You will have the opportunity to:

- Experience student performances
- Interact with campus administrators
- Cheer for our Rams at a football game
- Attend Mass on campus

Explore New York City together:

- Enjoy a meal on Arthur Avenue
- Explore Central Park
- Witness the magic of Broadway
- Capture Times Square at night
