# Senior Week Events at Lincoln Center

![Commencement Students in Cap and Gown](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/commencement_thanks_mom_and_dad_SM.jpg)

**Senior Week 2025: Tuesday, May 13-Friday, May 16**

If you are a 2025 graduating senior in Fordham College at Lincoln Center or Gabelli School of Business at the Lincoln Center campus, we hope you will join us for our Senior Week events. Ticket sales will begin on Monday, March 24, and will close on Sunday, May 4, or until tickets are sold out.

You may purchase tickets to Senior Week events at [University Tickets](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/) beginning Monday, March 24.

We are excited to announce our 2025 Senior Week at Lincoln Center Schedule!


**Tuesday, May 13th | Once A Ram BBQ**

LC Outdoor Plaza | 5:00 P.M. - 8:00 P.M. | Price: $15

*Join President Tetlow and all your senior classmates for a celebratory BBQ picnic on The Plaza. Thrilling backyard grad party-style with games, food, and most of all, fun! (Event co-sponsored by Senior Week, Office of the President, and Alumni Relations) (Valid proof of age is required to consume alcohol).*

**Wednesday, May 14th | Bowling Outing at Frames**

550 9th Ave. | 6:00pm | Price: $40 *Join us for a fun-filled Bowling Night at Frames! There will be strikes, spares, and an unforgettable bonding opportunity with fellow senior friends. Ticket price includes bowling lane, dinner buffet, and 2 drink tickets. (Valid proof of age is required to consume alcohol).***Thursday, May 15th | Senior Luncheon**

G76 140 West Building | 12:30 PM. - 2:00 P.M. | Price: FREE (MUST reserve ticket)*Join us for a graduate luncheon to celebrate the amazing Class of 2025! All graduating seniors will have access to various food options.***Thursday, May 15th | Senior Boat Cruise**

Chelsea Piers NYC | Boarding 7:30 P.M. | Set Sail 8:30 P.M. | Return 12:00 A.M.

Price: $160 for Fordham LC Seniors. No guest tickets will be available for purchase at this time. In the event that there is capacity on the boat, we *may* be able to release a limited amount of guest tickets on April 30th at the price of $200. *You’re invited to an exclusive private chartered yacht cruise along the Hudson with your Fordham friends, taking in the memorable skyline to commemorate your time spent at a University in New York City. Enjoy hors d’oeurves, a three-course dinner meal (dietary accommodations available), photo opportunities, and a live DJ! Dress up in your best cocktail attire and enjoy dinner, dancing, and an open bar (Valid proof of age is required to consume alcohol).*

**Friday, May 16th | Lincoln Center Baccalaureate Mass**

5:00 p.m. | Church of St. Paul the Apostle

Please join the graduates of the Class of 2025 at the celebration of the Baccalaureate Mass. Rev. Kevin Spinale, S.J., Principal Celebrant and Homilist

**Friday, May 16th | Family Reception**

Skadden Conference Center, Law School 2nd Floor | 6:15 P.M.

PRICE: Up to 3 complimentary tickets, including a Senior and two guests. Any additional guest tickets are $15. All seniors who want to attend must reserve their complimentary tickets. Additional guest tickets may also be purchased at the same time.*We are ecstatic to show our appreciation for the incredible families of our Class of 2025 at the Family Reception, which will immediately follow the Baccalaureate Mass. This event offers seniors and their families to gather and enjoy light hors d'oeuvres as well as an open bar (Valid proof of age is required to consume alcohol).***IMPORTANT NOTES:**

- Tickets will be sold only to Fordham College at Lincoln Center or Gabelli School of Business at Lincoln Center seniors who graduated in December 2024 or will be graduating in May or August 2025.
- All tickets for the listed events are non-refundable and non-transferable.
- Bring a valid state/government ID in order to consume alcohol at applicable events.
