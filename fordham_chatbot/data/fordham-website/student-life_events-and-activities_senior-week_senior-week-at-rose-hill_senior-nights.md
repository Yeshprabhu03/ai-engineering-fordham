# Senior Nights at Rose Hill

A popular Fordham tradition, Senior Nights bring the Senior class together in celebration of their time at Fordham - Rose Hill.

Themed and costumed, Senior Nights are held approximately three times in the Fall semester and three times in the Spring semester.

## Senior Nights Spring 2026 Dates

Thursday, February 5th, 2026: 100 Nights Until Graduation

Thursday, March 19th, 2026: Theme TBD

Thursday, April 16th, 2026: Theme TBD

### Who Can Attend?

Senior Nights are open to current seniors in the Gabelli School of Business at Rose Hill and Fordham College at Rose Hill who are graduating in December, May, or August.

We look forward to seeing you there!
