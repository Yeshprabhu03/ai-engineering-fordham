# Living on Campus Important Dates

### Lincoln Center

**Spring 2026 Semester **

**Spring Break**

McKeon Hall closes on **Saturday, March 7 at 12pm **

McKeon Hall re-opens on **Sunday, March 15 at 10am**

McMahon Hall remains open

**McKeon International First Year Students** that need to stay in McMahon Hall for** Spring Break** will need to find a McMahon first year host to stay on campus during the break(s). All McMahon roommates of the host will need to approve (**by signing the break stay form**) that the McKeon can stay in the apartment for the break. The break stay forms are located in the main office and available during business hours Monday thru Friday 10am-6pm.

**Easter Break **

Thursday, April 2 - Monday, April 6

The residence halls remain open

**Undergraduate Closing **

**Tuesday, May 12 by 12pm**

All non-graduating undergraduate students must vacate their room assignments 24 hours after their finals and no later than Tuesday, May 12 by 12pm.

**Graduating Seniors **

**Saturday, May 16 by 10pm**

All graduating undergraduate seniors must vacate their room assignments no later than Saturday, May 16 by 10pm. **There are no exceptions. **
