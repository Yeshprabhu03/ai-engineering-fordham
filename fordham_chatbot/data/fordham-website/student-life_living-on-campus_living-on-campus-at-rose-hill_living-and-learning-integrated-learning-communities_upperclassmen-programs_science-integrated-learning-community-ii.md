# Science Integrated Learning Community II

**Integrated Learning Community**

The Upperclass Science Integrated Learning Community (SILC2) is a partnership between the Dean's Office of Fordham College at Rose Hill and the Office of Residential Life that focuses on providing science-focused programming, such as:

- Discussion About the Pre-Health Program
- Peer Mentoring on How to Succeed in Science Coursework
- Review Sessions to Prepare for Exams, Midterms, and Finals
- Time Management Seminars
- Trips to Museums
- Academic Registration Guidance
- Special Topic Seminars from Faculty on Contemporary Science Issues
