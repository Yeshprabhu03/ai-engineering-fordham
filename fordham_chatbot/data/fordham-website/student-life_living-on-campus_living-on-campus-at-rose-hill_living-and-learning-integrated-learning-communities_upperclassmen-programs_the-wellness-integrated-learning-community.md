# The Wellness Integrated Learning Community

**Integrated Learning Communities**

The Wellness Integrated Learning Community explores various concepts of wellness, while providing tangible experiences to integrate these ideas into daily life practices.

The Wellness Integrated Learning Community will intentionally explore 8 embodiments of Wellness: Emotional Wellness, Social Wellness, Physical Wellness, Spiritual Wellness, Intellectual Wellness, Creative Wellness, Vocational Wellness, and Environmental Wellness! We are looking for residents who are interested in exploring all of these concepts together within the community.

The Wellness Integrated Learning Community is located on the 5th floor of Oâ€™Hare Hall Residential College and is available to both Sophomores and Juniors.

To be a member of the Wellness Integrated Learning Community, all residents will be required to sign a participation agreement that commits to living a substance free lifestyle (a lifestyle free from alcohol, tobacco, and illegal substances.), as well as attending at least 75% of the monthly Integrated Learning Community related events and programs.

What embodiments of Wellness do you value most and why?
