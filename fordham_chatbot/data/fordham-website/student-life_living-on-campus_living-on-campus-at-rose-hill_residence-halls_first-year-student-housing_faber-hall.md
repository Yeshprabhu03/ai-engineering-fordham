# Rose Hill Campus: Faber Hall

![Faber side](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Faber_Side.jpg)

Renovated in 2016, Faber Hall currently houses 70 new students -both first-year and transfer- consisting of primarily double rooms. Currently residents in Faber occupy two floors within the building, with the potential for expansion in the future. Faber has an elevator, a common lounge with a TV/DVD and a kitchenette, and laundry facilities. Air conditioning is available for a short time after the opening and again before the end of the academic year.

## Fast Facts

- Common Bathrooms
- Co-ed
- Storage space underneath a bed: 26 inches maximum
- Mattress size: Twin XL

## Features

- Large community lounge with kitchen
- Air Conditioning
- Laundry room

## Housing Rates

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities, cable television, telephone service, laundry, and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/). These rates do not include the residence hall activity fee. [View the rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/).

All residents in this dormitory are required to have a [meal plan](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans/Category?cat=Rose_Hill_Fall_Mandatory_Meal_Plans_9254&lid=9254). See the current room rates for Faber Hall. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.

Move-in dates vary, please see [Important Dates](/student-life/living-on-campus/important-dates/) for more information.
