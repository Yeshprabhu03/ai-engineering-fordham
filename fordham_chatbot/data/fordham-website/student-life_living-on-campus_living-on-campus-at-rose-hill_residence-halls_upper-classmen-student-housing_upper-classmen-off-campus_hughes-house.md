# Hughes House

![Hughes House, Rose Hill Residential Living](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/hughes_house.jpg)

Located at [2355-2357 Hughes Avenue](https://www.google.com/maps/place/2357+Hughes+Ave,+Bronx,+NY+10458/@40.8546925,-73.8874281,17z/data=!3m1!4b1!4m2!3m1!1s0x89c2f48735e31605:0xa2282ad0ddbea23f) in the [Belmont](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Belmont,_Bronx) neighborhood of the Bronx, Hughes House is home to upperclassmen students at Rose Hill. Hughes House residents have the opportunity to experience living in the urban environment with all the benefits of on-campus housing.

## Off-Campus Independence with On-Campus Convenience

Hughes House residents enjoy furnished apartments in University-operated buildings. Off-campus houses are staffed by a Resident Director and Resident Assistants and are maintained by the University. The Belmont Community Housing program offers similar services and benefits as those offered within the on-campus housing program, coupled with the independence of off-campus living.

This off-campus house accommodates about 5-8 students in each of the 9 apartments (bedrooms differ per apartment) in mostly single and some double bedrooms. Accommodations include, a living/dining area, kitchen, two full bathrooms, air conditioning, coin laundry machines and a small gym area.

**Free Off-Campus Shuttle Service**

Getting to your off-campus residence at night is made extremely safe through the [Late Night Shuttle Service](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/public-safety/services/campus-shuttle/off-campus-shuttle-information/), offered by the Security Department. Available every night from 10 p.m. until 5 a.m., this service is free for all Fordham students, faculty, and staff.

**Meal Plans**

[Meal plans](https://fordham.campusdish.com/MealPlans/Category?cat=Rose_Hill_Fall_Mandatory_Meal_Plans_9254&lid=9254) are **not** required but are available to off-campus students. The lively Belmont neighborhood offers plenty of [grocery shopping](http://fordhamram.com/2014/09/17/a-guide-for-your-grocery-shopping-needs/) and [restaurant ](http://fordhamram.com/2014/10/01/off-campus-dining-right-at-your-front-door/)options.

**Rooms and Rates**

Rates are for the nine-month academic year and include all utilities and membership to the [Lombardi Fitness Center](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/fitness-and-recreation/fitness-and-recreation-centers/vincent-t-lombardi-memorial-center/). These rates do not include the residence hall activity fee.

See the [current room rates](/student-life/living-on-campus/housing-rates/) for Hughes House. Room configurations and rates are subject to change.
