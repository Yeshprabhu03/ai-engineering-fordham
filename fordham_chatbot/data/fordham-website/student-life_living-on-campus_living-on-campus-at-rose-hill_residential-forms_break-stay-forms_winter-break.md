# Winter Break

**Stay Request Form**

Students wishing to stay on campus during Winter Break will need to complete the form emailed to their Fordham email account. Filling out the form does not guarantee that your request will be granted. Contact the Office of Residential Life at Rose Hill if you have any questions at [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#9be9fee8f7f2fdfee9f3dbfdf4e9fff3faf6b5feffee) or 718-817-3080.

*Form will be available via your student email account.*
