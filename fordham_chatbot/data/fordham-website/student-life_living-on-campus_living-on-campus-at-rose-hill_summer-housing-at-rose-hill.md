# Summer Housing at Rose Hill

## Summer Housing 2025

The following information is tentative and once finalized, this page will be updated.

### Summer Housing Deposit

- $50 and is non-refundable
- Payable on my.fordham.edu, between: Tuesday, April 1, 2025-Monday, April 14, 2025
**Deadline: Monday, April 14th, 2025**

### How to Make Your Deposit and Submit Application

- In order to deposit, students need to go to My.Fordham.edu and select Housing Deposit Payment in the housing channel. Please be sure to deposit for
**Summer 2025**and not another year. - Summer Housing Applications will open for students who deposited. Applications must be submitted via My.Fordham.edu. The housing application must be completed between Wednesday, April 16-Wednesday, April 30, 2025 at 5 PM
- Summer Housing will
**only**be guaranteed to students who deposited for Summer 2025 by the deadline. **Application is due Wednesday, April 30, 2025 at 5 PM**

### Other Important Information

- First Come - First Served: Rose Hill Summer Housing as well as Lincoln Center (different rates apply) are first come-first served; however, priority will be given to students taking classes.
**Current resident students at Rose Hill will need to be available on Friday, May 16th between 10am-3pm to move into their summer assignment.**If you cannot be available during this time frame, you will need to move out all of your belongings and return to campus on Friday, May 23, 2025 for Summer Session I move in.- Commuters will need to pay their $50 deposit with Student Accounts and then email the Office of Residential Life at
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#88fbfde5e5edfafaecfae0c8eee7faece0e9e5a6edecfd)in order to fill out the application. - Any student who submits a summer housing application and withdraws will lose their $50 deposit.
- Please note that you must cancel your housing 10 days prior to the start of the intended session. If you do not cancel 10 days prior, you are responsible for the full summer session charge.
- Lincoln Center students are not permitted to stay at RH for Intersession and must move out at the end of Summer Session II (no exceptions)
- On the flip side, Rose Hill students are
**not**permitted to stay at LC for Intersession and must move out at the end of Summer Session II (**no exceptions**).
