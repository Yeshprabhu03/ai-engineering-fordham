# Pride and Rainbow

Fordham University is home to two undergraduate student organizations that support the Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual, Transgender, Questioning, and Ally community. OMA coordinates ongoing communication with Rainbow and Pride leadership to offer support in student-led programming.

**PRIDE Alliance** is located at the Rose Hill campus

**Rainbow Alliance** is located at the Lincoln Center campus
