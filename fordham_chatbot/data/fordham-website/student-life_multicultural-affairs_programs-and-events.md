# Programs and Events from Multicultural Affairs

**Annual Heritage Month Programming and Collaborations **

**January/February**

MLK Week Programming/Black History Month

- Lincoln Center – Black Student Alliance (BSA)
- Rose Hill – ASILI: The Black Student Alliance

**March**

Women’s History Month/Fordham Advocates Cultural Enrichment (FACE) Series

**May**

Asian American and Pacific Islander Heritage Month

- Lincoln Center – Asian Pacific American Coalition (APAC)
- Rose Hill – Asian Cultural Exchange (ACE) and Fordham University's Philippine-American Club (FUPAC)

**September/October**

Latino Heritage Week/ Hispanic Heritage Month

- Lincoln Center - Student Organization of Latinos (SOL)
- Rose Hill – Academia Hispana & El Grito de Lares

**October**

LGBT History Month/Coming Out Week

- Lincoln Center – Rainbow Alliance
- Rose Hill – PRIDE Alliance

**November**

Native American History Month

**December**

Interfaith Celebration of the Seasons
