# Lincoln Center Fitness

![](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/fitness-center/JOboyle-20151014-0077-1000x480.jpg)


The RamFit at Lincoln Center located on the second floor of McMahon Hall is equipped with a weight room, a cardio room, and two studio spaces.

Hours of operation for the Cardio Room and Weight Room is as follows:

- Monday - Friday: 7:00 a.m. - 10:30 p.m.

Closed for deep cleaning: 11:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m. - Saturday and Sunday: 9:00 a.m. - 8:30 p.m.

Closed for deep cleaning: 11:00 a.m. - 12:00 p.m.

Hours of operation for Studio 1 and Studio 2:

- Monday - Friday: 8 a.m. - 6 p.m. Reservations on 25Live are required.
- Monday - Friday: 6 p.m. - 10:30 p.m. Open on a first come first serve basis.
- Saturday - Sunday: 9 a.m. - 8:30 p.m. Open on a first come first serve basis.

All current Fordham students, faculty, administrators, and staff are welcome to use RamFitLC. Guests are not allowed; all users are required to have their ID in their possession.

In addition, inside the cardio room, there is a locker room for males and females with two shower stalls. The lockers in the locker room are available for rent for a $50 fee per semester or $75 for the academic year; a Fall Semester locker will last from August 28, 2024 - December 20, 2024, and a Spring Semester locker will be from January 13, 2025 - May 12, 2025, and for the academic year August 28, 2024 - May 12, 2025. If you are extending your Spring semester locker rental into the summer, you must pay a $25 fee. Individuals must bring their own locks.

For more information on locker rentals, please contact the Office of Residential Life in McMahon 108. You can also request a locker via our Google form: [Locker Rental Request Form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1gNGNvf2e3-B0LVOsyBf_Yx9_IrKnruu364l7s0Wp_kA/viewform?edit_requested=true).
