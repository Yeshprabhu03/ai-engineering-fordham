# Medical Services

The Health Center offers a host of medical services for the Fordham community:

-
[Primary Care](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/primary-care/) -
[Physical Examinations](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/physical-examinations/) -
[Women's Health Care](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/womens-health-care/) -
[Men's Health Care](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/mens-health-care/) -
[LGBT Health Care](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/lgbt-health-care/) -
[Allergy Health Clinic](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/allergy-health-clinic/) -
[Laboratory Test and X-rays](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/laboratory-tests/) -
[Immunizations](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/immunizations/) -
[Special Referrals](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/special-referrals/) -
[Physical Therapy at Rose Hill Campus](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/health-center/medical-services/physical-therapy-at-rose-hill-campus/) -
[Emergency Medical Services](/student-life/safety-health-and-wellness/health-services/emergency-medical-services/)

The UHS does not participate in any insurance billing. There are out of pocket fees for some services provided in our office. Students can request a receipt to submit for claims purposes through their health insurance.
