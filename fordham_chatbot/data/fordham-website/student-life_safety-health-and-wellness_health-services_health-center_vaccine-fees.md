# Vaccine Fees

Fordham University Health Services does not bill insurance directly. Appointments are free of charge, but charges may occur from in-house procedures. All incurred charges are billed to the Bursar (student accounts). However, students can obtain a receipt after their visit and submit it as a claim through their insurance.
