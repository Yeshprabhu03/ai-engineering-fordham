# Criminal Activity

**In the event of any criminal activity:**

- Do not confront or attempt to apprehend the perpetrator.
- Call the Department of Public Safety and/or 911 immediately.

**When you have connected with the Department of Public Safety or 911, be prepared to provide as much information as possible:**

- Where/what is happening (or happened)
- Description of weapon, if any
- Description of individual(s)-sex, race, height, weight, clothing
- Direction of individuals fleeing the scene-and how they were fleeing
- Vehicle used: make, color, plate number, direction of travel

**Then:**

- Provide a call-back number so that the Department of Public Safety or the Police dispatcher can reach you for additional information.
- Move to a safe, well-lit area and advise the dispatcher of your description and location.
- If safe, remain to direct the Department of Public Safety or police.
