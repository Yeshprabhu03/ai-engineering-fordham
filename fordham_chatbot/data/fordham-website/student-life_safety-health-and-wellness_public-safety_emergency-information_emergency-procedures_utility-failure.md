# Utility Failure

**Emergency Power Failure:**

If there is an emergency power failure, or someone trapped in an elevator, it should be reported immediately to the Department of Public Safety.

**Minor Power Failure:**

Minor failures of electricity, water, and/or gas should be reported to Facilities Operations at the following numbers during weekday business hours. During off hours, notifications should be made to the Department of Public Safety numbers listed above.

Follow the directions given by fire wardens, the resident assistant/ director (RA/RD), or the Department of Public Safety. Fordham University is equipped with emergency generators capable of providing electricity to the campus telephone system and the following buildings at each campus.

- Rose Hill McGinley Center
- Lombardi Center
- Thebaud Hall Annex
- William D. Walsh Family Library
- Lincoln Center McMahon Hall
- McKeon Hall

Note: Temporary emergency lighting is provided in all other buildings and will remain lit long enough for the building to be evacuated. Once everyone is evacuated, the fire warden, RA/RD, or public safety personnel will direct you to an alternate location. Please ensure that all unnecessary appliances are disconnected or turned off to avoid overload when service is restored.
