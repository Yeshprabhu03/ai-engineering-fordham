# Public Safety: Policies and Procedures

University-wide public safety policies apply to all Fordham University campuses regardless of affiliation, and irrespective of whether those resources are accessed from on-campus or off-campus locations. For more information, speak with a member of Residential Life, Student Leadership and Community Development, Student Health Services, Counseling and Psychological Services and the Department of Public Safety.
