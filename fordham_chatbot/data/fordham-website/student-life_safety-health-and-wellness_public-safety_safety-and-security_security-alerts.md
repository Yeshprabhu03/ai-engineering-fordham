# Security Alerts

Current Status

There are no alerts at this time.

**Reporting an Emergency**

Twenty Four emergency blue-light phones are located within the indoor parking facility at Rose Hill. Twenty-four emergency blue-light phones are located outdoors throughout the Rose Hill campus and are identified on the [Rose Hill campus map](/about/maps-and-directions/rose-hill-campus/). One emergency blue light phone is located on the Plaza at the Lincoln Center campus. Emergency floor phones are located on each floor of the Law School and Lowenstein Building at the Lincoln Center campus. Emergency phones go directly to public safety. Three emergency phones are located outside on the Westchester campus

- Dial 718-817-2222 or x2222 from a Rose Hill phone to reach the Rose Hill campus Department of Public Safety.
- Dial 212-636-6076 or x6076 to reach the Lincoln Center campus public safety desk.
- Dial 914-367-3001 or x3001 to notify the Westchester campus public safety person.

**Campus Emergency Information**

During a major emergency, information can be found on this web site or by calling 877-375-HELP (4357), 800-280-SNOW (7669), 212-636-7777 and/or 718-817-5555.

**Non-Emergency Reporting**

Dial 718-817-2222 or x2222 to reach the Rose Hill campus Department of Public Safety.

Dial 212-636-6076 or x6075 to reach the Lincoln Center campus Public Safety desk.

Dial 914-367-3001 to notify the Westchester campus public safety person or x3001 to reach Westchester campus Public Safety Office.
