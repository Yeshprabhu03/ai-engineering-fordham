# Parking Facilities

We provide parking for students, faculty, administrators, staff, and visitors in all three campuses. Parking at Fordham is based on a user fee schedule.

All students, faculty, administrators, staff and visitors that require space must pay the posted parking fee. Parking is charged each time a vehicle enters campus unless an annual permit for daytime or overnight parking has been obtained from the Department of Public Safety.
