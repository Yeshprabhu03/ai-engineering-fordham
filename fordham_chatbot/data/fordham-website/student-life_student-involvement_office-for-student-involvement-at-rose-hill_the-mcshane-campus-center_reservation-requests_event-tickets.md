# McShane Center: Event Tickets

To assist with selling tickets at events, members of the campus community are able to purchase available tickets for campus events in advance [online](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/).

To request to sell tickets for an upcoming campus event [online](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/), please submit the applicable ticket request form below. Events sponsored by university departments will need to also submit a space request through [25Live](https://25live.collegenet.com/fordham/) to reserve the location on campus and undergraduate student organizations will need to submit an event request through the [Rams Involved Community page](https://communities.fordham.edu/).

Once the location is reserved, please complete the below ticket request form in order for it to be listed on the [ticket website](https://fordham.universitytickets.com/). For general admission events, please submit the request form no later than 5 business days prior to the start date of the ticket sales. Please note, if your event requires reserve seating you will have to submit a seating chart no later than 15 business days prior to the start date of the ticket sales.
