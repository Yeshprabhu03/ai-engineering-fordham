# Lincoln Center Student Organizations

There are 70+ student organizations at Fordham Lincoln Center. We provide students with meaningful interactions outside of the classroom that connects them with their peers and with other members of the University community.

All students are welcome to join any club here at Fordham. Learn more about [our mission](https://www.fordham.edu/about/mission-statement/) and [our strong commitment to access and non-discrimination](https://www.fordham.edu/about/leadership-and-administration/administrative-offices/title-ix-office/non-discrimination-policy/).

Check out the list of organizations to see the many ways you can get involved today! Current FCLC & GSB LC undergraduate students can also log into our online Rams Involved platform to join student organizations here: [communities.fordham.edu](http://communities.fordham.edu/)
