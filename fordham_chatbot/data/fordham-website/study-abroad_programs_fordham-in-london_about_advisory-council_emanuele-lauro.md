# Emanuele Lauro

![Emanuele A. Lauro - London Centre Advisory Board](/media/home/departments-centers-and-offices/study-abroad/Lauro-image.jpg)

Emanuele Lauro is the Chief Executive Officer of Scorpio Holdings Limited.

He is the founder, Chairman and CEO of Scorpio Tankers Inc. (NYSE: STNG) and the Vice Chairman of Cadeler (NYSE:CDLR). He has chaired and held executive roles in various US listed as well as private entities in the maritime industry.

He is the Vice President of the Monaco Chamber of Shipping and a member of the London Advisory Council of Fordham University NY, USA. He has also served as non-executive director of The Standard P&I Club.
