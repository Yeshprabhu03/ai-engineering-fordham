# Matthew Holland, Ex Officio

Matthew presently serves as Interim Senior Director of Fordham London. He has served in a leadership capacity at Fordham London since 2016, where he played a key role in developing much of the programming, staffing, policies and procedures currently in place. Matthew has been instrumental in building student engagement, community relationships and student support.

Having worked primarily within Jesuit universities for the past decade, Matthew’s emphasis on the needs of students personifies cura personalis: He has overseen the expansion of Fordham’s housing requirements in London, modernized student services and represents Jesuit values in all that he does. Matthew holds an MA in Philosophy and Religion from Heythrop College (2017) and an MBA in Educational Leadership & Management from Anglia Ruskin University (2022). He also plays a significant role in the study abroad sector, having chaired the London Student Affairs Network since its formation in 2018.
