# Summer Programs

![London Study Abroad Street](/media/review/content-assets/migrated/images/Week_1___Street_View_from_Bus__banner.jpg)


London, UK

Fordham runs Summer Study Abroad Programs for four to ten weeks and allow those who cannot commit to a semester or full-year abroad to incorporate a global experience into their education!

*Fordham's summer programs are open to undergraduates from any discipline currently seeking a degree at a US institution. Applicants must have a GPA of 3.0 or above, and a clear disciplinary record. Seniors are not permitted to study abroad in the summer following graduation.*

*Schedule a Short-term advising appointment to meet with an advisor:*

## Fordham London

Located in the heart of one of the most vibrant and multicultural cities in the world, Fordham London in Clerkenwell district in the Borough of Camden, is the site of an exciting and innovative summer study abroad program. You can choose from business courses, liberal arts courses, or a program that combines academics with an invaluable internship experience.

## VART 3500: Documentary Photography: Italy

Located in the heart of one of the most vibrant, artistic, and historic cities in the world, Fordham in Rome, Italy, is the site of an exciting and innovative summer study abroad program. Through Documentary Photography, the program will challenge your academic knowledge, opening up a whole world of cultural, societal, and historic experiences.## ARHI 2229: Japanese Art and Visual Culture in Tokyo

Located in the buzzing city of Tokyo, Japan, students have the opportunity to dive into Japanese culture, arts, and history. Japanese Art and Visual Culture will allow students to challenge themselves academically to learn more about ancient arts in Japan to contemporary arts, while having the chance to explore Tokyo.

## STEM Summer Research Projects

Fordham University has partnered with Arcadia University's College of Global Studies to offer summer [STEM research programs abroad](https://fordham-ip.terradotta.com/index.cfm?FuseAction=Programs.ViewProgramAngular&id=11476). The program will challenge your academic knowledge and critical thinking skills while opening up a whole world of cultural, societal and historic experiences to you. Choose from a variety of summer research programs available through Arcadia Abroad and international partner institutions in England, Spain, Ireland and Scotland.

## Bard Berlin Theater Intensive

The program will foster your creativity and allow for you to work with multidisciplinary artists in Berlin. ## Global Internships

Over the summer, students have the opportunity to apply for internships abroad through partner institutions like, [CIEE](https://www.ciee.org/go-abroad/college-study-abroad/summer-college-study-abroad), [IES](https://www.iesabroad.org/study-abroad/summer), [Boston University](https://www.bu.edu/abroad/), and more. Students will need to complete the online [Study Abroad application](https://fordham-ip.terradotta.com/index.cfm?FuseAction=Programs.ViewProgram&Program_ID=10300) as well as an application through the University or organization that runs the internship program.

## External Summer Courses

If you are interested in applying to take a summer course outside of those offered by Fordham University, you must complete the online [Study Abroad application](http://fordham-ip.terradotta.com/index.cfm?FuseAction=Programs.ViewProgram&Program_ID=10300) as well as an application through the University or organization that runs your program. Many of [Fordham's semester study abroad program partners also offer summer programs](https://fordham-ip.terradotta.com/index.cfm?FuseAction=Programs.ViewProgram&Program_ID=10300), so we encourage you to review the many options available through Fordham's existing partnerships.
