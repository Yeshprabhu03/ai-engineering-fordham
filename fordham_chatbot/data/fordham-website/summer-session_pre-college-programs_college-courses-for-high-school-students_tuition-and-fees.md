# Summer Session Tuition and Fees

## Pre-College Program

For a 3-credit class: $3,402

For a 4-credit class: $4,536

Tuition prices include the $75 application fee (which will be returned to those not accepted into the program), and a $300 registration deposit, which will be required after you are accepted to hold your seat. Please note that some courses require lab or materials fees, which are noted in the individual course descriptions.

## Cancellations/Withdrawal from Program

Accepted students who withdraw before classes officially begin may receive a full tuition refund (minus the application deposit), by notifying the Office of Summer Session via email ([[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#f685839b9b93849a939792938485b6909984929e979bd8939283)) on or before June 30. This request must be made in writing. After this date, Pre-College program participants will follow the [Summer Session refund schedule](/summer-session/registration-and-housing/policies-and-procedures/withdrawals-and-refunds/).
