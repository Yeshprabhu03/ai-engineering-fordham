# Summer Leaders Academy Courses


![](/media/home/commonly-used-images/698A7687-500x500.jpg)

In the Fordham Summer Leaders Academy, students select one course to pursue, which meets daily in the morning and afternoon in one and two-week sessions through July.

In the late afternoons and weekends, students will have the opportunity to bond and prepare for their college and professional journeys.

Activities will include:

[Sessions on the college application process](/summer-session/pre-college-programs/summer-leaders-academy/courses/college-application-prep/)given by Fordham's Undergraduate Team.[Career exploration](/summer-session/pre-college-programs/summer-leaders-academy/courses/career-exploration/)through Fordham's Career Services office.- Making the most of Fordham's incredible new campus center and athletic facilities.
- Community-building social activities with Fordham's Student Engagement team.
- Chaperoned off-campus excursions around New York City!
