# Summer Leaders Academy Career Exploration

## Enjoy the Journey

![Students and teacher working together](/media/home/commonly-used-images/admission-advice/pexels-kindel-media-6775122_sq.jpg)

Exploring your passions and discovering where they intersect with the good you can do in the world is at the heart of a Jesuit education. Each Summer Leaders Academy Immersion includes time with career counselors from Fordham's Career Center who will lead you through exercises to help you:

**Identify your natural strengths**and interests**Focus on career paths**that align with your personal values**Prepare**for fulfilling careers**Cultivate**job-ready skills
