# How to Apply to the Summer Leaders Academy

### Application Deadlines

- April 30: Resident Application Deadline
- May 1: Commuter Application Deadline for Immersion 1
- May 15: Resident Registration Consents, Deposits, and Medical Forms Due
- May 29: Commuter Application Deadline for Immersion 2 and 3
- May 31: Commuter Registration Consents, Medical Forms, and Deposits Due

### Credit-Bearing Courses

Credit-bearing courses require a G.P.A. of 3.0 or better, and are primarily for rising seniors. Rising juniors may apply with a GPA of 3.5 or better, a letter of recommendation, and the documents below.

To apply, you will need:

- an analytical writing sample from a class
- an unofficial copy of your high school transcript
- your fall report card if the grades are not reflected on your transcript
- $75 application deposit and $300 tuition deposit to hold your seat. All payments will be applied to your final bill.

### Non-Credit Courses

Non-credit courses are open to high schoolers who are rising sophomores through rising seniors. Courses are recommended for those with a 3.0 or better. No prior experience with the subject matter is required.

To apply, you will need:

- an unofficial copy of your high school transcript.
- your fall report card if the grades are not reflected on your transcript.
- If you are applying for the Musical Theatre Workshop, an audition song is required.
- $75 application deposit and $300 tuition deposit to hold your seat. All payments will be applied to your final bill.
