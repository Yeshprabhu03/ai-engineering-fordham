English Language Placement Evaluation

All new international students are required to take the Fordham English Language Test (FELT) prior to orientation and the start of classes.

**Transfer Students**

Students with previous international coursework must have their international university transcripts evaluated by [World Education Services](http://www.wes.org/) (WES) to receive a Fordham University transfer credit evaluation.

**Global Transition**

All new incoming international students and other new students coming to Fordham from outside the USA should plan to attend [Global Transition](https://www.fordham.edu/gt).

Global Transition for Spring 2026 will be held Thursday, January 8, 2026, followed by New Student Orientation on Friday, January 9. Spring 2026 classes will begin on Monday, January 12, 2026.

Dates for Global Transition and Fall New Student Orientation for Fall 2026 are coming soon.

### US Citizens and Permanent Residents

**How to Enroll at Fordham University**

To make a deposit, please return to your applicant portal at [admission.fordham.edu/status](https://admission.fordham.edu/status) and fill out the Enrollment Form, found under the section "Reply Forms." Once you submit the Enrollment Form, your applicant portal will display a new link for making the deposit (and if applicable, housing) payment.

[Financial Aid](/undergraduate-admission/admitted-students/financial-aid/)

If you have submitted all required financial aid information on the FAFSA and CSS PROFILE, a financial package will be prepared for you. You can access your financial aid package through the admitted student portal.

### Registration

**Spring Admitted Student Course Registration**Further information on your Spring course schedule will be sent to students confirmed to attend Fordham by early December.

**Fall Admitted Student Course Registration**Further information on your Fall course schedule will be sent to students confirmed to attend Fordham by early June.

**Fordham ID number (FIDN)**

You will find your Fordham ID (FIDN) in your admission letter on your [Online Applicant Portal](https://admission.fordham.edu/account/login?r=https%3a%2f%2fadmission.fordham.edu%2fapply%2fstatus).

**Additional Note**

If you require a paper admission letter for your F-1 student visa interview, you can print the 'Admit Letter' from your [Fordham applicant portal](https://admission.fordham.edu/account/login?r=https%3a%2f%2fadmission.fordham.edu%2fapply%2fstatus) after submitting your deposit.
