# Digital Signage Best Practices

Please familiarize yourself with the following for creating an effective message for Fordham University’s digital signage system. [Submit your message via this form](https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSe_ziGQlNixRulks09fWMLryub0Xl1AtIgvt_V8FpO66JZw9Q/viewform?usp=sf_link).

[Digital Signage Governance](https://docs.google.com/document/d/19d3Bi8HTY1NecQlu33XEFgL2g6ixutYCPOiI15aoHiY/edit?usp=sharing) provides specifications for the configuration of digital signage hardware and the management, design, and` dissemination of digital signage content at Fordham University.`


For questions about creating a message and anything else about digital signage, contact [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#95f1fcf2fce1f4f9e6fcf2fbf4f2f0d5f3fae7f1fdf4f8bbf0f1e0).

## Dimensions

Submit 2 versions of your message slide to accommodate both portrait and landscape screens.

Screen aspect ratio: 9:16 and 16:9

File dimensions:

-
Portrait: 900 x 1600 pixels
-
Landscape: 1600 x 900 pixels

### File formats

-
PNG or JPG format (1MB maximum file size)
-
Submit 2 files, one each for portrait and landscape orientations

### Text

Keep your message simple and use efficient phrasing. When planning out your message, think of it as a billboard. Messages will show for approximately 7 seconds. Focus on the most important information. Less is more.

-
Use the 3 by 5 rule: No more than 3 lines of text with 5 words each

OR -
5 lines of text with 3 words each

Always proofread and double check your text!

### Checklist for events

-
Event name
-
Sponsor
-
Date
-
Time
-
Location (physical or virtual)

### Fonts

-
Use one of these sans serif typefaces: Arial, Roboto, or Verdana
-
Use
**bold**only to emphasize important information -
Avoid
*italics*(sans serif italic is visually weaker, less legible than regular or bold)

Recommended text sizes in applications used to generate slides:

Adobe Apps (InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop)

180 point maximum / 60 point minimum

(see TechHelpBee examples)

Microsoft Apps (Word, Powerpoint)

100 point maximum / 36 point minimum

Google Apps (Docs, Slides)

100 point maximum / 36 point minimum

### Colors

For optimal legibility (readability), use a white or light-colored background and dark colors for text. (Note that on the screens, the template surrounding the message area is Fordham maroon)

### Restricted colors

-
Shades of red or purple that compete with Fordham maroon (#900028)
-
Athletics maroon #700429 (use only for Athletics-related messages)

### Memorable URLs

Use a friendly URL, such as Fordham.edu/example. If you need one made, contact [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#90e7f5f2e3f9e4f5f6f5f5f4f2f1f3fbd0f6ffe2f4f8f1fdbef5f4e5).

### Images and Video

-
Use images with a minimum resolution of 72 ppi.
-
Videos should be no longer than 20 seconds in length. Campus screens cannot play audio.
-
Use copyrighted images ONLY WITH consent. See section
[VII.C.o in the Governance document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/19d3Bi8HTY1NecQlu33XEFgL2g6ixutYCPOiI15aoHiY/). -
If images/videos include individuals, you must have those individuals’ consent before using them in public. See section
[VII.C.p in the Governance document](https://docs.google.com/document/d/19d3Bi8HTY1NecQlu33XEFgL2g6ixutYCPOiI15aoHiY/). -
Video must be closed captioned.
-
If you need assistance finding and using appropriate imagery, please contact Marketing and Communications (
[[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#543935263f31203d3a3314323b26303c35397a313021)) -
Using multiple images and graphics on one slide can cloud your message. Usually one image delivers the most impact.
-
Do not use QR codes!

### Use of Fordham Seal and Logo

-
Do not use the Fordham seal
- If your message is using a Fordham logo, please visit the
[brand standard site](https://live.standards.site/fordham-university/logo)to ensure you are using the correct version.
