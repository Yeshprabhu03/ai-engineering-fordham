# News, Events, and Other Timely Content

- News and Events pages should be current; there is no need for a Past Events page...the web is not a file cabinet!
- Avoid using dates in file names or on pages to avoid content appearing out-of-date, i.e. Fitness Schedule instead of Fitness Schedule Fall 2020.
- Avoid creating dated news documents on your website.
