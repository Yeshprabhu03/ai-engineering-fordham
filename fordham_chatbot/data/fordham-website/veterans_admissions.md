# Veterans Admissions

## Applying to Fordham as a Student Veteran? We can help!

If you are not sure where or how to apply, what to include, or what program to pursue, we can help! Contact Andie Marais, Director of Military and Veteran Higher Education Engagement and Transition: [[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) (718) 817-3843.

Fordham is supportive of those who have served our country and we fully support their educational journey. We are pleased to offer waived application fees and a streamlined admissions application process for veterans.
